info General Questions

What is Ilaaj AI and how does it work?

expand_more
Ilaaj AI is a Pakistan-centric digital healthcare platform that uses advanced AI to provide preliminary medical diagnostics and connect users with PMDC-verified doctors. Our AI analyzes your symptoms based on vast medical databases, providing potential causes and guiding you to the right specialist for a formal consultation.

Is the service available across all cities in Pakistan?

expand_more
Yes — our digital consultation and AI diagnostic services are available nationwide. Physical services like medicine delivery and lab sample collection are currently operational in Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, and Faisalabad. We're rapidly expanding to other regions.

How affordable is Ilaaj AI?

expand_more
The AI consultation and doctor verification are completely free. You only pay for medicines, lab tests, or in-clinic appointments if you choose them. No hidden charges, no consultation fees.

verified_user Data & Privacy

Is my medical data secure?

expand_more
Your privacy is our priority. We use bank-grade AES-256 encryption for all data storage and transmission. Ilaaj AI follows international health data standards (HIPAA principles) and local data protection regulations. Your medical history is only accessible to you and the doctors you choose to share it with.

Who can see my diagnostic results?

expand_more
Only you have primary access. If you book a consultation, the assigned PMDC-verified doctor receives temporary access to your relevant health history and AI diagnostic summary to provide an accurate second opinion.

Can I use it for sensitive health topics?

expand_more
Absolutely. You can chat anonymously without revealing your name or phone number. This is especially helpful for sensitive concerns where you want a private second opinion before approaching a clinic.

medical_services Medical & Doctors

How does the doctor verification work?

expand_more
Every doctor on Ilaaj AI is independently verified against the Pakistan Medical & Dental Council (PMDC) registry. We confirm their license number, specialty, and active practicing status before they can review any case. You can verify any doctor's PMDC number directly on the official PMDC website.

What languages can I chat in?

expand_more
English, Urdu (Nastaleeq script), and Roman Urdu — all three. The AI auto-detects which language you used and replies in the same one. You can mix freely too: "Mujhe sar dard hai aur fever bhi hai" works perfectly.

How do I add family members?

expand_more
From the chat sidebar, click "Add family member". Enter their name, age, and relationship — that's it. Each member has their own chat thread and medical history, all under your single account.

prescriptions Prescription & Delivery

How long does prescription approval take?

expand_more
Typically 15-30 minutes during peak hours, often faster. Once approved by a PMDC doctor, your digital prescription is delivered as a downloadable PDF directly in your chat thread.

What should I do in an emergency?

expand_more
Ilaaj AI is not a replacement for emergency services. For life-threatening symptoms (severe chest pain, breathing difficulty, loss of consciousness, severe bleeding) call 1122 or your nearest emergency room immediately.
