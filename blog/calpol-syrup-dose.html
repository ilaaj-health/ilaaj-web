Calpol Syrup — Bachon Ke Liye Correct Dose, Use aur Warnings

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Calpol = Paracetamol syrup, bachon ke liye safest
  • Dose: 15 mg/kg har 4-6 ghante baad
  • Different concentrations: 120 mg/5ml (Pediatric), 250 mg/5ml (6+)
  • 3 mahine se kam baby mein bukhar = ER
  • 24 ghante mein 4 doses se zyada nahi

Calpol Syrup Kya Hai?

Calpol GSK ka brand name hai bachon ke liye banaya gaya paracetamol syrup. Pakistan mein bachon ki sab se common bukhar aur pain ki dawai. Bilkul Panadol jaisi hai magar liquid form mein, bachon ke liye taste add ke saath.

Calpol — Available Forms
Calpol Pediatric Suspension120 mg/5 ml — 3 months to 6 years
Calpol 6+250 mg/5 ml — 6+ years
Calpol Drops100 mg/ml — newborns (sirf doctor mashware se)
Calpol Tablets500 mg — 12+ years
Pakistan price~80-150 PKR per 60 ml bottle

Calpol Kis Liye Use Hoti Hai?

  • Bukhar — viral, vaccination ke baad, teething
  • Sir dard
  • Dant nikalne ka dard (teething)
  • Kaan dard
  • Body aches
  • Vaccination ke baad fussiness
  • Sore throat

Calpol Pediatric Dose (120 mg / 5 ml) — Sab Se Common

Dose 15 mg/kg har 4-6 ghante baad:

Important Rules

  1. Maximum 4 doses per 24 hours
  2. Doses ke beech kam az kam 4 ghante
  3. 5 din se zyada na dein bina doctor
  4. Always weight ke hisab se dose calculate karein, sirf umer pe nahi
  5. Measuring spoon istemal karein, kitchen spoon nahi
  6. Bottle istemal se pehlay shake karein

3 Mahine Se Kam Baby

3 mahine se kam baby mein bukhar (100.4°F+) MEDICAL EMERGENCY hai!

  • Calpol khud na dein
  • Foran ER ya pediatrician
  • Newborn mein bukhar serious bacterial infection ka sign ho sakti
  • Doctor sirf consultation ke baad Calpol drops dega (75 mg single dose)

Side Effects (Bohat Rare)

Recommended dose pe Calpol bohat safe hai. Magar:

  • Allergic rash (rare)
  • Mitli (rare)
  • Liver damage — overdose se

Overdose — Sab Se Bara Khatra

Calpol overdose Pakistan mein common pediatric ER admission hai — galti se zyada dose, ya parents alag dawai bhi de rahe hote (Panadol + Calpol both = paracetamol).

Symptoms (24-72 hours mein):

  • Mitli, ulti
  • Pet ke upar dard
  • Sustpan
  • Yellow skin/eyes (jaundice — late)

Action:

Foran ER. Antidote (N-acetylcysteine) 8 ghante mein effective hai. Symptoms na hone pe bhi go.

Pediatrician Se Mashwara

Important: Combination Mat Karein

Yeh sab paracetamol contain karte hain — saath na dein:

  • Calpol
  • Panadol
  • Disprol
  • Pyromol
  • Hilton
  • Cold/flu syrups (check label)

Bachay Ko Calpol Dene Ka Tareeqa

  1. Bottle achi tarah shake karein
  2. Measuring spoon ya syringe use karein
  3. Bachay ko upright (sitting) position mein dein
  4. Cheek ki taraf se slowly
  5. Foran kuch khane/peene ko dein
  6. Time aur dose note karein (galti se repeat na ho)

Sponging — Bukhar Mein Bonus

  • Gungune (warm) pani se sponging karein
  • Thande pani se BILKUL nahi (shivering, ulta bukhar)
  • Ice nahi
  • 15-20 minute, har 1-2 ghante
  • Calpol ke saath additional madad

Kab Doctor Ko Foran?

  • 3 mahine se kam baby mein bukhar
  • Bukhar 102°F+ aur 3 din se zyada
  • Daur (febrile seizure)
  • Bacha sust ho jaye, doodh chod de
  • Sans tezi se le raha
  • Neela par jaye
  • Pesab kam ho jaye (dehydration)
  • Skin par red ya purple dane
  • Gardan akarna

Conclusion

Calpol bachon ke liye safest pain killer aur antipyretic hai jab correct dose pe use ho — weight ke hisab se 15 mg/kg har 4-6 ghante baad, max 4 doses per day. Multiple paracetamol products saath na dein. 3 mahine se kam baby mein bukhar emergency hai. Ilaaj AI pe pediatric doctor consultation, ghar baithe.

Frequently Asked Questions

Calpol syrup ki bachay ko kitni dose dein?

Dose weight pe depend karta hai — 15 mg/kg har 4-6 ghante baad. Calpol Pediatric (120 mg/5 ml): 3-6 mahine (4-7 kg) 2.5 ml; 6-12 mahine (7-10 kg) 5 ml; 1-2 saal 5 ml; 2-4 saal 7.5 ml; 4-6 saal 10 ml. Calpol 6+ (250 mg/5 ml): 6-8 saal 5 ml, 8-10 saal 5-7.5 ml, 10-12 saal 10 ml. Maximum 4 doses per 24 hours.

3 mahine se kam baby ko Calpol de sakte hain?

Bilkul nahi bina doctor consultation ke. 3 mahine se kam baby mein bukhar (100.4°F+) medical emergency hai — turant ER ya pediatrician ko dikhayen. Newborn mein bukhar serious bacterial infection (sepsis, meningitis) ka sign ho sakti. Doctor sirf consultation ke baad Calpol drops (75 mg single dose) dega.

Calpol aur Panadol saath de sakte hain?

Bilkul nahi. Calpol aur Panadol dono mein same drug (paracetamol) hai — saath dene se overdose aur liver damage ka khatra. Yeh sab paracetamol contain karte hain: Calpol, Panadol, Disprol, Pyromol, Hilton. Cold/flu syrups bhi check karein. Dose calculate karte waqt total paracetamol dekhna chahiye, brand alag bhi ho.

Calpol ke saath sponging karein?

Haan, bukhar utaarne mein madad. Magar gungune (warm) pani se, thande pani se BILKUL nahi — shivering hoti hai aur ulta bukhar barhta hai. Ice bhi nahi. 15-20 minute har 1-2 ghante. Maathay aur gardan pe geela kapra, bagal aur ghutno ke peeche bhi. Bohat fluids dete rahein. Bacha shiver kare to ruk jaein.

Bukhar mein Calpol ya Brufen syrup behtar hai?

Calpol (paracetamol) safer aur first-line hai — 3 mahine+ bachon mein, khaali pet OK, dengue mein safe. Brufen syrup (ibuprofen) sirf 6 mahine+ bachon mein, khaane ke saath, anti-inflammatory bhi (joint pain mein behtar), magar dengue mein bilkul nahi (bleeding risk). High persistent bukhar mein doctor dono alternate kar sakte hain. Aspirin bachon ko bilkul nahi (Reye's syndrome).
