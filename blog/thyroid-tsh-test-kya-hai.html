TSH Test Kya Hai?

TSH (Thyroid Stimulating Hormone) ek hormone hai jo pituitary gland (brain ke base mein) banata hai — is ka kaam thyroid gland ko signal dena hai ke woh kitna T3/T4 hormone banaye. TSH blood test thyroid function ka sab se accurate aur pehla indicator hai.

TSH Kaise Kaam Karta Hai?

Yeh ek feedback loop hai: Jab thyroid hormones (T3, T4) kam hote hain, pituitary zyada TSH banata hai thyroid ko "push" karne ke liye. Jab thyroid hormones zyada hote hain, TSH kam ho jata hai. Is liye:

  • High TSH = thyroid underactive hai (hypothyroidism) — pituitary "shout" kar raha hai
  • Low TSH = thyroid overactive hai (hyperthyroidism) — pituitary "quiet" ho gaya hai

TSH Test Kab Order Hota Hai?

  • Thyroid symptoms — thakawat, weight change, sardi/garmi sensitivity
  • Routine health screening — khaskar 35+ umar mein
  • Family history of thyroid disease
  • Pregnancy planning ya pregnancy ke douran
  • Existing thyroid patients ki monitoring — dose adjustment ke liye
  • Baal jhadna, irregular periods jaisi non-specific symptoms

Agar TSH Abnormal Aye To?

Sirf TSH abnormal hone se full diagnosis nahi hoti — doctor aksar Free T3, Free T4, aur kabhi kabhi Anti-TPO antibodies bhi order karta hai complete picture ke liye. Yeh confirm karta hai ke thyroid disorder ka exact type kya hai aur treatment plan kya hoga.

Doctor Se Report Discuss Karein

Test Ki Tayyari

Fasting ki zarurat nahi — TSH kisi bhi waqt test kiya ja sakta hai. Agar aap Levothyroxine (thyroid medicine) le rahe hain, to apni dose lene se pehle ya baad test karwane ke bare mein doctor se confirm karein taake accurate result mile.

Pregnancy Mein TSH Ki Importance

Untreated thyroid disorders pregnancy mein miscarriage aur baby ke brain development ko affect kar sakti hain. Isi liye pregnancy planning karne walon aur pregnant khawateen ko TSH test karwana strongly recommend kiya jata hai.

Kab Doctor Ko Milein?

  • Persistent thakawat, weight change bila wajah
  • Gale mein swelling ya lump
  • Dil ki dhadkan mein changes
  • Pregnancy plan kar rahi hain aur family history hai

Conclusion

TSH test thyroid function ka gold-standard screening tool hai — simple, affordable, aur bohat informative. Agar aap thyroid symptoms mehsoos kar rahe hain ya routine checkup chahte hain, TSH test se shuruaat karein. Ilaaj AI pe doctor se apna result discuss karein.

Frequently Asked Questions

TSH high hone ka kya matlab hai?
High TSH ka matlab hai ke pituitary gland zyada TSH bana raha hai thyroid ko 'push' karne ke liye — yeh hypothyroidism (kam active thyroid) ka ishara hai. Doctor further tests (T3, T4) order kar sakta hai confirm karne ke liye.
TSH test ke liye fasting zaruri hai?
Nahi, TSH test kisi bhi waqt kiya ja sakta hai, fasting ki zarurat nahi. Agar thyroid medicine le rahe hain to timing ke bare mein doctor se confirm karein accurate result ke liye.
TSH aur T3/T4 test mein kya farq hai?
TSH pituitary gland ka hormone hai jo thyroid ko regulate karta hai — pehla screening test hai. T3 aur T4 actual thyroid hormones hain jo directly measure karte hain thyroid kitna active hai. Dono mil kar complete picture dete hain.
Pregnancy mein TSH test kyun important hai?
Untreated thyroid disorders pregnancy mein miscarriage ka khatra barha sakti hain aur baby ke brain development ko affect kar sakti hain. Isi liye pregnancy planning aur pregnancy ke douran TSH test strongly recommend kiya jata hai.
TSH normal range kya hai?
Approximate normal range 0.4-4.0 mIU/L hai, magar yeh lab se lab thora vary kar sakta hai aur pregnancy mein target range different hoti hai. Apni report par diya gaya reference range dekhein aur doctor se discuss karein.
