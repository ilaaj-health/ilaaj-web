Bachon ki Bimari: AI se Fori Mashwara

Pakistan mein bacha bimar ho to maa-baap ki neend ud jati hai — raat ke 2 baje bukhaar barh raha, koi pediatrician available nahi. Yahan AI mashwara real value deta hai. Janein bachon ki common bimari, fori treatment, aur kab Doctor ko foran milein.

Bachon Ka Bukhaar — Complete Guide

Normal temperature: 97-99°F. Bukhaar 100.4°F (38°C) se upar.

Calpol Syrup Dose (15 mg/kg har 6 ghante):

Umar Weight Calpol Pediatric (120 mg/5ml)
3-6 mahine 4-7 kg 2.5 ml
6-12 mahine 7-10 kg 5 ml
1-2 saal 10-12 kg 5 ml
2-4 saal 12-16 kg 7.5 ml
4-6 saal 16-22 kg 10 ml

Important:
- Maximum 4 doses per 24 hours
- 5 din se zyada bina doctor nahi
- 3 mahine se kam baby mein bukhaar = EMERGENCY, foran ER
- Aspirin (Disprin) bachon ko BILKUL NAHI — Reye's syndrome ka khatra
- Brufen syrup 6 mahine+ bachon mein safe

Bachon Mein Dast — ORS Sab Se Zaruri

ORS Recipe (agar packet nahi):
- 1 liter clean pani
- 6 chamach cheeni
- 1/2 chamach namak

Dose: Har loose motion ke baad 1/4-1/2 cup. Doodh continue (breastfeeding).

Zinc supplement: WHO recommends 10-20 mg/day for 10-14 days during diarrhea.

Bachon ko BILKUL NAHI:
- Imodium (loperamide) — toxic megacolon ka khatra
- Antibiotics apni marzi se

Foran doctor:
- 3 din+ chal raha
- Khoon ya kaali stool
- Bukhaar ke saath
- Bacha sust, doodh chod de
- Ankhein dhansi, aansoo nahi (dehydration)
- Pesab kam ho jaye

Bachon Mein Khansi

1 saal+ bachon ko safe:
- Sone se pehlay 1/2 chamach honey
- Steam (gentle, supervise)
- Saline nasal drops
- Plenty of fluids
- Calpol bukhaar ke saath

4 saal se kam ko OTC cough syrups recommended NAHI

Foran doctor:
- Sans tezi se le raha
- Neela par jaye
- Sans ki seeti (wheeze)
- Khoon wali khansi
- 2 hafte+ persistent cough

Bachon Ki Vaccination Schedule (Pakistan EPI)

  • Birth: BCG, OPV-0, Hepatitis B
  • 6 weeks: Pentavalent-1, OPV-1, PCV-1, Rotavirus-1
  • 10 weeks: Pentavalent-2, OPV-2, PCV-2, Rotavirus-2
  • 14 weeks: Pentavalent-3, OPV-3, PCV-3, IPV
  • 9 months: Measles-1, IPV-2
  • 15 months: Measles-2

EPI free hai government centers pe.

Kab AI Mashwara Use Karein vs Hospital?

AI mashwara safe hai:
- Bukhaar 99-101°F
- Halki khansi/zukam
- 1 din ka loose motion
- Halka skin rash
- Constipation 1-2 din
- General queries (vaccines, milestones, feeding)
- Medication doses

Hospital foran:
- 3 mahine se kam baby mein bukhaar
- Bukhaar 102°F+ aur 3 din+
- Sans tezi se, neela par jaye
- Doray (febrile seizures)
- Severe dehydration (sunken eyes, no tears)
- Bachay ki awareness kam
- Continuous severe pain
- Khoon kahin bhi (stool, vomit, urine)

Pakistan Mein Common Pediatric Issues

  • Iron deficiency anemia — common, screening at 9-12 months
  • Vitamin D deficiency — sun exposure low
  • Stunting/malnutrition — 40% under-5 affected
  • Pneumonia — leading cause of under-5 mortality
  • Diarrhea — second leading cause

Conclusion

Bachon ki bimari mein parents ki anxiety high hoti hai — magar most common conditions safely manageable hain at home. Sahi dose, hydration, monitoring zaruri. Ilaaj AI par female pediatricians 24/7 — raat ko bukhaar ho ya weekend pe rash, foran mashwara mil sakta hai.

Frequently Asked Questions

Bachay ko bukhaar mein kya dein?
Calpol syrup weight ke hisab se (15 mg/kg har 6 ghante). 3 mahine se kam baby mein emergency. Aspirin bilkul nahi.
Bachay ko dast mein kya dein?
ORS sab se zaruri — har motion ke baad 1/4-1/2 cup. Doodh continue. Zinc 10-20 mg 10-14 days. Imodium NAHI.
1 saal ke bachay ko shahed safe hai?
Haan, 1 saal+ bachon ko shahed safe aur khansi mein effective. 1 saal se kam = botulism ka khatra, bilkul nahi.
Vaccine miss ho gayi to?
Foran nearest EPI center jaayein. Late vaccinations bhi catch up schedule pe lagti hain. Free hai government centers pe.
Bachay ka rash kab serious hai?
Bukhaar ke saath, jism par phailta hua, breathing difficulty ke saath, ya purple/non-blanching = foran ER.
