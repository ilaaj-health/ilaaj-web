Disprin (Aspirin) — Uses, Dosage, Side Effects Pakistan

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Disprin = aspirin (acetylsalicylic acid)
  • Sir dard, dard aur bukhar mein OTC
  • Dil ke patients mein 75 mg daily blood thinner
  • Bachon ko BILKUL nahi (Reye's syndrome)
  • Dengue mein BILKUL nahi (bleeding risk)

Disprin Kya Hai?

Disprin Reckitt Benckiser ka brand name hai aspirin (acetylsalicylic acid) ke liye. Yeh oldest aur most studied NSAID hai. Pakistan mein soluble form mein available — paani mein dissolve hoti hai, faster onset.

Disprin — Quick Facts
Generic nameAspirin (Acetylsalicylic Acid)
ClassNSAID + antiplatelet
Strengths75 mg, 100 mg, 150 mg, 300 mg, 500 mg
FormSoluble tablets, regular tablets, enteric-coated
OTCAvailable without prescription
Pakistan price~10-30 PKR per strip

Disprin Kis Liye?

1. Sir Dard, Dant Dard, Body Pain

Soluble form fast acting hai — 15-30 minute mein.

2. Bukhar (Adults Only)

Antipyretic effect, magar Panadol pehlay choice.

3. Heart Attack aur Stroke Prevention

Low dose (75-100 mg) daily — blood platelets ko clump hone se rokta hai.

4. Acute Heart Attack

Chest pain ke shuru mein 300 mg chabakar — life-saving.

5. Anti-Inflammatory

Arthritis, soft tissue injuries — magar zyadatar Brufen aur Diclofenac better.

6. Some Cancer Prevention (Research)

Long-term low-dose aspirin colorectal cancer risk kam kar sakti hai (under research).

Dosage

UseDose
Pain / fever (adults)300-900 mg every 4-6 hours
Heart attack prevention75-100 mg once daily
Acute chest pain300 mg chewed immediately
Maximum per day (pain)4,000 mg (4g)
Best timeKhaane ke baad

Soluble Disprin: Glass paani mein dissolve karein, foran piyo. Faster than regular tablets.

BACHON KO BILKUL NAHI!

Reye's Syndrome — aspirin bachon (16 saal se kam) mein viral infection ke saath dimagh aur liver ka damage kar sakti hai. Potentially fatal. Pakistan mein bukhar wale bachon ko Disprin dena bohat khatarnak hai.

Bachon ke liye sirf:

  • Calpol (paracetamol) — 3 months+
  • Brufen syrup — 6 months+

Dengue Mein BILKUL Nahi!

Dengue mein platelets pehlay se kam ho jate hain. Aspirin platelets ko aur kam karta hai aur bleeding ka khatra bohat barha deta — naak, masoode, internal bleeding. Pakistan mein dengue season mein yeh galti jaan-leva ho sakti hai.

Dengue mein sirf Panadol — Brufen aur Aspirin bilkul nahi.

Side Effects

Common:

  • Pet kharab, acidity
  • Mitli
  • Halki bleeding tendency (cuts mein khoon zyada)
  • Tinnitus (kaan mein awaaz — high dose)

Serious:

  • Stomach ulcer aur GI bleeding
  • Kidney damage
  • Reye's syndrome (bachon mein)
  • Allergic reactions (asthma sufferers)
  • Hemorrhagic stroke risk (rare, high dose)

Kab Avoid Karein?

  • Children under 16 (viral infection ke saath)
  • Dengue fever
  • Active stomach ulcer ya GI bleeding
  • Bleeding disorders (hemophilia)
  • Severe asthma (NSAID-sensitive)
  • Pregnancy 3rd trimester
  • Aspirin allergy
  • Severe kidney/liver disease
  • Surgery se 1 hafte pehlay (bleeding risk)
  • Gout flare (low dose ulta worsens)

Doctor Se Mashwara

Low-Dose Aspirin (75 mg) — Heart Protection

Pakistan mein bohat se patients 75 mg aspirin daily lete hain. Ye secondary prevention ke liye proven hai:

  • Pichla heart attack ho chuka
  • Pichla stroke ho chuka
  • Coronary artery disease confirmed
  • Stent placement ke baad
  • Diabetes + multiple risk factors

Primary prevention (no prior heart event) mein routinely recommend nahi — bleeding risk benefit se zyada ho sakta hai. Doctor se discuss karein.

Drug Interactions

  • Warfarin, DOACs: Bleeding risk bohat barha
  • NSAIDs (Brufen, Voltral): Stomach ulcer risk doubles
  • Steroids: Bleeding risk barha
  • SSRIs (depression dawai): GI bleeding risk
  • ACE inhibitors: BP control reduce
  • Methotrexate: Toxicity
  • Alcohol: Stomach bleeding

Pregnancy aur Breastfeeding

  • Low dose (75 mg) kuch high-risk pregnancies (pre-eclampsia prevention) mein doctor parchee se
  • 3rd trimester full dose bilkul avoid — fetal heart issues
  • Breastfeeding: Generally avoid

Pakistan Mein Brands

  • Disprin (Reckitt Benckiser) — soluble
  • Loprin 75 mg — low dose
  • Aspirin generic
  • Aspart 75/150 mg
  • Cardiprin 100 mg

Heart Attack Symptoms — Foran Action

Chest pain (especially crushing, radiating to left arm/jaw), sans phoolna, pasina:

  1. 1122 call karein
  2. 1 Disprin 300 mg chabakar nigalein (jab tak allergy nahi)
  3. Aaram se baith jaein
  4. Tight clothes loosen karein
  5. Hospital tak wait karein

Yeh single tablet life-saving ho sakti hai.

Conclusion

Disprin (aspirin) Pakistan mein dual purpose hai — pain killer (adults only) aur heart attack/stroke prevention. Magar bachon mein, dengue mein, ulcer mein, aur 3rd trimester pregnancy mein BILKUL nahi. Heart patients ke liye low-dose 75 mg daily life-saving ho sakti hai — sirf doctor parchee se shuru karein. Frequently Asked Questions

Disprin kis liye use hoti hai?
Disprin (aspirin) NSAID + antiplatelet hai. Adults mein sir dard, dant dard, body pain, bukhar mein use hoti hai. Sab se important use: heart attack aur stroke prevention (low dose 75 mg daily) un patients mein jinhein pichla heart event ho chuka. Acute chest pain mein 300 mg chabakar life-saving ho sakti hai. Bachon (16 saal se kam) ko bilkul nahi.
Disprin bachon ko de sakte hain?
BILKUL NAHI. Aspirin bachon (16 saal se kam) mein viral infection ke saath Reye's syndrome ka cause banti hai — dimagh aur liver ka damage, potentially fatal. Pakistan mein bachon ko bukhar mein Disprin dena bohat khatarnak galti hai. Bachon ke liye sirf Calpol (paracetamol, 3 months+) ya Brufen syrup (6 months+) safe hain.
Dengue mein Disprin le sakte hain?
BILKUL NAHI. Dengue mein platelets pehlay se kam ho jate hain. Aspirin platelets ko aur kam karta hai — bleeding ka khatra bohat barha deta (naak, masooro, internal bleeding). Brufen aur Voltral bhi bilkul avoid. Dengue mein sirf Panadol (paracetamol) safe hai. Pakistan mein dengue season (Aug-Nov) mein yeh galti jaan-leva.
Heart patients ke liye Disprin daily?
75-100 mg low-dose aspirin daily heart attack aur stroke prevention mein effective hai un patients mein jinhein pichla heart event, stroke, ya stent placement ho chuki ho. Yeh "secondary prevention" hai. Primary prevention (no prior event) mein routinely nahi — bleeding risk weigh karna parta hai. Sirf doctor parchee se shuru karein. Khaane ke baad lein, surgery se 1 hafte pehlay band.
Acute chest pain mein Disprin kaise lein?
Heart attack ke shuru mein (chest pain crushing, left arm/jaw mein radiate, sans phoolna, pasina): 1122 call karein, phir 1 Disprin 300 mg chabakar nigalein (jab tak aspirin allergy nahi). Yeh blood clot ko stable karta hai aur damage limit karta hai. Aaram se baith jaein, tight clothes loosen karein, hospital tak wait karein. Yeh single tablet life-saving ho sakti hai.
