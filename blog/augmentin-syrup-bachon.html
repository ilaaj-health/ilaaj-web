Augmentin Syrup Bachon Ke Liye — Correct Dose, Use Aur Side Effects

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Augmentin (amoxicillin + clavulanate) bachon ki sab se zyada prescribe ki jane wali antibiotic hai
  • 3 strengths: 156 mg/5ml (regular), 312 mg/5ml (forte), 457 mg/5ml (DS)
  • Dose 25-45 mg/kg per day, 2-3 doses mein — sirf doctor decide kare
  • Pora course (5-7 din) hamesha mukammal karein
  • Viral khansi-zukam mein bilkul mat dein — sirf bacterial infections mein

Augmentin Syrup Kya Hai?

Augmentin Pakistan mein bachon ke bacterial infections ke liye sab se zyada prescribe ki jane wali antibiotic syrup hai. Is mein do dawai milti hain — Amoxicillin (jo bacteria ko maarti hai) aur Clavulanic Acid (jo amoxicillin ko bacteria ki defense se bachata hai). Yeh combination usay simple amoxicillin se zyada strong banata hai, khaaskar aise infections mein jahan bacteria resistance develop kar chuke hon.

Augmentin Syrup — Quick Facts
Active ingredientsAmoxicillin + Clavulanate potassium
Regular strength156 mg/5ml (125 mg amoxicillin + 31 mg clavulanate)
Forte strength312 mg/5ml (250 mg amoxicillin + 62 mg clavulanate)
DS (Double Strength)457 mg/5ml (400 mg amoxicillin + 57 mg clavulanate)
Standard dose25-45 mg/kg/day in 2-3 divided doses
Course duration5-7 din (severe cases 10 din)

Augmentin Kab Use Hoti Hai?

Augmentin sirf bacterial infections ke liye hai, viral nahi. Pakistan mein doctors aksar yeh infections mein prescribe karte hain:

  • Kaan ki infection (Otitis media): Choti umr ke bachon mein sab se aam wajah
  • Galay ki infection (Strep throat, tonsillitis): Jab throat swab positive ho
  • Seenay ki infection (Pneumonia, bronchitis): Bacterial chest infection
  • Sinusitis: Naak aur chehray ki haddi mein infection
  • Skin infections: Cellulitis, infected wounds
  • UTI: Pesab ki nali ki bacterial infection

Sahih Dose — Wazan Ke Hisab Se

Augmentin ki dose hamesha bache ke wazan pe nikalti hai. Mild-moderate infection ke liye 25-45 mg/kg/day, aur severe infections mein doctor 80-90 mg/kg/day tak bhi de sakta hai. Yeh khud calculate karne ki cheez nahi — doctor bottle aur strength dono guide karega.

Aam taur pe approximate guideline (sirf reference, doctor ki dose follow karein):

  • 5-9 kg (4-12 mahine): 156 mg/5ml — 2.5 ml din mein 2 dafa
  • 10-18 kg (1-5 saal): 312 mg/5ml — 5 ml din mein 2 dafa
  • 19-29 kg (6-9 saal): 312 mg/5ml — 7.5 ml din mein 2 dafa, ya 457 mg/5ml — 5 ml din mein 2 dafa
  • 30 kg se zyada: Adult tablet (Augmentin 625 mg) consider karein

Syrup Tayar Karne Ka Tareeqa

  1. Bottle pe nishan tak boiled, thanda paani (mineral water bhi theek hai) daalein
  2. Cap band kar ke 1-2 minute zoor se hilain, jab tak powder mukammal ghul jaye
  3. 5 minute rakh dein, agar level neechay chala gaya to thoda aur paani daal kar dobara hilain
  4. Har dose se pehlay bottle ko achhi tarah hilana zaruri hai
  5. Reconstitute karne ke baad fridge mein rakhein aur 7 din ke andar use karein

Augmentin Ke Side Effects

Augmentin aam tor pe safe hai magar kuch side effects ho sakte hain:

  • Dast (sab se common): Clavulanate ki wajah se hota hai. Halka dast normal hai, magar khoon wala ya bohat zyada dast doctor ko bataein
  • Diaper rash: Choti umr ke bachon mein common — barrier cream lagaein
  • Pet ki kharabi, ulti: Khaane ke saath dene se kam hoti hai
  • Yeast infection (oral thrush): Munh mein safed dhabbe
  • Allergy rash: Jild pe surkh dhabbe — dawai foran band karein
  • Saans ki takleef, chehra/zubaan ki soojan: Serious allergy — emergency, foran hospital

Important Safety Rules

  • Pora course mukammal karein: 2-3 din mein bacha theek lagne lage to bhi dawai band na karein. Adha course resistance paida karta hai
  • Doodh ya roti ke saath dein: Pet ki kharabi kam hoti hai aur absorption behtar hoti hai
  • Penicillin allergy: Agar bache ko ya family mein kisi ko penicillin se allergy hai to Augmentin bilkul na dein — alternative azithromycin ya cefixime hain
  • Time fix rakhein: 12 ghante ke faasla pe (BID) ya 8 ghante (TID) — bohat zaruri
  • Doosri dawaiyon ke saath: Paracetamol (Calpol) safe hai. Brufen bhi dia ja sakta hai — antibiotic se taluq nahi
  • Probiotic dahi: Dast kam karne ke liye din mein 1 cup dahi madadgar hai

Augmentin Kab NAHI Deni Chahiye

  • Viral cold, flu, saadi khansi ya bukhar — antibiotic kaam nahi karti
  • Doctor ki guidance ke baghair — purana prescription dobara use na karein
  • Penicillin allergy ki history
  • Mononucleosis (glandular fever) — rash ka khatra
  • Pichli dafa Augmentin se jaundice ya liver issue hua ho

Conclusion

Augmentin bachon ki bacterial infections ke liye safe aur effective antibiotic hai, magar sirf doctor ke prescription pe. Sab se zaruri 3 baatein: (1) sahih wazan-based dose, (2) 5-7 din ka pora course, aur (3) viral infections mein bilkul use na karna. Frequently Asked Questions

Augmentin syrup bache ko kitni denai chahiye?
Augmentin syrup ki dose bache ke wazan (weight) pe depend karti hai — aam tor pe 25-45 mg/kg per day, 2 ya 3 doses mein. Misal ke tor pe 10 kg ka bacha 312 mg/5ml strength ka 5 ml din mein 2 dafa lay sakta hai. Asal dose doctor decide karega, ghar pe weight base pe na maapain.
Augmentin syrup ko fridge mein rakhna zaruri hai?
Haan. Powder ko pani mila kar tayar karne ke baad (reconstitution) syrup ko fridge mein rakhein aur 7 din ke andar use karein. Har dose se pehlay bottle ko achhi tarah hila lein. 7 din ke baad agar dawai bachay to phenk dein — asar khatam ho jata hai.
Augmentin syrup ke aam side effects kya hain?
Sab se aam side effect dast (loose motion) hai — yeh clavulanate ki wajah se hota hai. Diaper rash, ulti, aur halki pet ki kharabi bhi ho sakti hai. Agar bachay ko rash, soojan ya saans ki takleef ho to dawai foran band kar ke doctor ko dikhain — yeh allergy ki nishani ho sakti hai.
Kya viral khansi-zukam mein Augmentin dena chahiye?
Bilkul nahi. Augmentin antibiotic hai, sirf bacterial infections (ear infection, strep throat, niemonia, sinusitis) mein kaam karti hai. Viral cold, flu, ya saadi khansi mein deni se faida nahi hota balke antibiotic resistance barhti hai. WHO aur DRAP Pakistan dono ne bachon mein bay ja antibiotic use ko khatarnak qarar diya hai.
Agar bacha Augmentin ka course adha chod de to kya hoga?
Course adha chodna sab se badi galti hai. 2-3 din mein bacha theek lag jata hai magar bacteria mukammal khatam nahi hote — wapas aate hain aur is dafa antibiotic ka asar nahi hota (resistance). Hamesha 5-7 din ka pora course mukammal karein, chahe bacha bilkul theek lag raha ho.
