Pregnancy Ka Doosra Mahina — Symptoms, Care Aur Diet

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Doosra mahina = pregnancy weeks 5 se 8 — baby ke organs banne ka critical waqt
  • Folic acid 400-800 mcg roz lazmi — neural tube defects rokta hai
  • Morning sickness peak 8-10 weeks ke aas paas hota hai — choti meals madadgar
  • Brufen/Aspirin avoid karein — Panadol safe painkiller hai
  • Heavy bleeding, ek taraf shadeed dard, ya bohat zyada ulti — foran ER jaaein

Doosra Mahina — Kya Ho Raha Hai?

Pregnancy ka doosra mahina yaani weeks 5 se 8 sab se nazuk waqt hota hai. Andar baby (medical naam embryo) tezi se grow kar raha hai — heart dharakna shuru ho jata hai (week 6 ke aas paas), brain aur spinal cord ki shakl banti hai, haath paaon ki kaliyaan ubharti hain. Yahi waqt hai jab folic acid, achi diet, aur teratogenic (baby ko nuksaan dene wali) cheezon se parhez sab se zaruri hai.

Common Symptoms — Doosra Mahina

  • Matli aur ulti (morning sickness — magar aksar saare din ho)
  • Thakawat (fatigue) — body progesterone ke wajah se sleepy rehti hai
  • Breasts sensitive aur taza hone ka ehsaas
  • Bar-bar peshab — uterus mathaane par dabaata hai
  • Mood swings aur emotional
  • Food cravings ya aversions — kuch khaane par ulti, kuch khaane ki shadid khwahish
  • Halka spotting (light pink/brown) — aksar normal magar heavy bleeding red flag
  • Halka pet mein khinchaao (cramping) — ligaments stretch hone ki wajah se

Zaruri Supplements — Pakistan Mein

1. Folic Acid 400-800 mcg Roz

Yeh sab se important hai — baby ki neural tube (spinal cord ka shuruwati structure) week 4 se week 6 ke beech band hoti hai. Agar folic acid nahi hai to spina bifida ya brain malformations ka khatra hota hai. Pakistan mein Folik, Folvite, ya Vintage Folic 5 mg aam milti hain. Agar pregnancy plan kar rahi hain to 3 mahine pehlay shuru karein.

2. Iron Supplement

Pakistan mein 50% se zyada aurato mein anemia (khoon ki kami) hoti hai. Doosre mahine ke aas paas iron shuru kar dena chahiye — Ferotabs, Sangobion, Conviron ya doctor ki chosen. Khaali pet ke saath orange juice ya Vitamin C ke saath behtar absorb hota hai. Chai/coffee ke saath bilkul nahi.

3. Vitamin D

Pakistan mein bohat zyada aurato mein Vitamin D deficient hai — clothing aur kam dhoop ki wajah se. D-Caltrate, Vitamin D-3 (Sunny D), ya Indrop-D common hain.

4. Calcium aur DHA (Omega-3)

Calcium baby ki haddiyon aur dant ke liye, DHA brain development ke liye. Doctor mashware se shamil karein.

Morning Sickness — Ghareloo Tareeqay

  • Choti meals har 2-3 ghante — kabhi pet khaali na ho
  • Subah bistar se uthne se pehlay dry crackers, rusk, ya khusk biscuit khaayein
  • Adrak chai, adrak ki halki goli, ya nimbu pani — matli reduce karta hai
  • Strong smells (perfume, masala fry, oily food) se door rahein
  • Vitamin B6 (Pyridoxine) 25 mg + doxylamine — yeh Diclectin ke naam se mileta hai, pregnancy safe
  • Khaane ke saath paani na piyo — pehlay ya baad mein
  • Thanda khaana zyada manageable hota hai kyun ke khusboo kam hoti hai

Agar ulti itni hai ke kuch andar nahi tikta, peshab gehra peela hai, ya 24 ghante mein 2 kg weight gir gaya — yeh hyperemesis gravidarum hai aur IV fluids ke liye hospital jaana hai.

Khaane Ki Cheezein — Kya Khaaein, Kya Nahi

Khaaein:

  • Achi pakki sabzi, dal, anday (poori tarah pakka)
  • Phal — kela, seb, anar, mosambi (achi tarah dho kar)
  • Dahi, doodh (pasteurized), lassi
  • Saalan ke saath roti, biryani (less spicy), khichri
  • Dry fruits — badaam, akhrot, chuhare (kam matra)
  • Pakka hua gosht aur murghi

Avoid:

  • Kachi/halki pakki paya, maghaz, ya kalegi
  • Soft cheeses (feta, brie) — Listeria
  • Unpasteurized doodh, kachi mithai (khoya wali)
  • Shark, swordfish, king mackerel — mercury high
  • Deli meats jab tak garam karke na khaayein
  • Caffeine 200 mg/din se zyada (1-2 cup chai max)
  • Smoking aur sharab — bilkul band
  • Cat litter aur garden mitti — Toxoplasma

Abhi Doctor Se Milein

Safe Medicines — Doosra Mahina

Koi bhi dawai khud se nahi — pehlay doctor se confirm karein:

  • Safe: Paracetamol (Panadol, Calpol) 500 mg buyhar/dard ke liye — minimum dose
  • Safe (with caution): Antacids (Gaviscon, Mylanta) — acidity ke liye
  • Safe antibiotic options: Penicillin, Amoxicillin, Augmentin (doctor likhe to)
  • AVOID: Brufen (ibuprofen), Aspirin (Disprin), Ponstan — first/third trimester mein
  • AVOID antibiotics: Tetracycline, Doxycycline, Ciprofloxacin (high dose)
  • AVOID: Retinoids (acne creams jaisi Tretinoin, Roaccutane), ACE inhibitors (BP)

Pehli Prenatal Visit — Kya Hoga?

Week 8 tak ek gynecologist ko milna zaruri hai. Pehli visit mein yeh tests hote hain:

  • Confirmatory ultrasound — heartbeat, gestational age, location confirm
  • Blood tests: CBC, blood group + Rh, Hb, blood sugar, hepatitis B&C, HIV, syphilis
  • Rubella immunity aur thyroid (TSH)
  • Peshab test — UTI aur protein check
  • Folic acid + iron prescription confirm
  • BP, weight, height — baseline

Foran ER Kab Jaayein — Red Flags

  • Heavy bleeding (period jaisi ya zyada) — miscarriage ka shubha
  • Pet ke ek taraf shadeed dard — ectopic pregnancy emergency
  • Persistent ulti — peshab kam, kamzori, dehydration
  • Bukhar 101°F se zyada
  • Shadeed sar dard ya nazar dhundhli
  • Behoshi ya chakkar aane
  • Peshab mein jalan aur kamar dard (UTI — pregnancy mein kidney tak phail sakta)

Conclusion

Doosra mahina chhupi hui pregnancy ka sab se important phase hai — outside dunia ko ab tak nahi pata magar andar baby ki neenv rakhi ja rahi hai. Folic acid lein, ach se khaayein, ulti se ghabrayein nahi, aur red flags ko nazar mein rakhein. Frequently Asked Questions

Pregnancy ke doosre mahine mein konse vitamins zaruri hain?
Sab se zaruri folic acid 400-800 mcg roz hai — yeh baby ki spinal cord (neural tube) defects rokta hai aur week 12 tak chahiye. Iron supplement Pakistan mein zaruri hai kyun ke aurat mein anemia bohat aam hai. Vitamin D bhi most Pakistanis mein deficient hota hai. Calcium aur Omega-3 (DHA) bhi madadgar hain. Yeh sab gynecologist se prescription par lein, khud se nahi.
2nd month pregnancy mein morning sickness ka ilaj kya hai?
Choti choti meals har 2-3 ghante mein lein, kabhi pet khaali na rakhein. Subah uthne se pehlay bistar par dry crackers ya rusk khaayein. Adrak chai ya adrak ki goli matli reduce karti hai. Vitamin B6 + doxylamine combination (Diclectin) doctor se mil sakti hai. Strong smells, oily aur masaledar khaane se parhez karein. Agar ulti rok nahi paati ya peshab kam aa raha hai — hyperemesis gravidarum hai, foran doctor.
Pregnancy mein konse khaane avoid karne chahiyein?
Kachi machhli aur sushi, kacha ya halki pakka anda, kachi paya/maghaz, raw meat, unpasteurized doodh aur kachi mithai (jaise khoya wali), soft cheeses (feta, brie), high-mercury machhli (shark, swordfish), aur deli meats — Listeria aur Toxoplasma ke khatre se. Caffeine 200 mg/din se kam (1-2 cup chai). Smoking aur sharab bilkul band. Billi ke litter aur garden mitti se bhi door rahein.
Pregnancy mein konsi painkiller safe hai?
Paracetamol (Panadol/Calpol) 500 mg pregnancy mein safe hai jab zarurat ho — magar minimum dose, minimum time. Brufen (ibuprofen), Disprin (aspirin), aur baaki NSAIDs first trimester aur third trimester mein avoid karein. Antibiotics mein tetracycline aur doxycycline NOT safe. Retinoid (acne) aur ACE inhibitors (blood pressure) bhi avoid. Koi bhi dawai shuru karne se pehlay gynecologist ko zaroor batayein.
Pregnancy ke doosre mahine mein kab emergency hospital jaaein?
Foran ER jaaein agar heavy bleeding ho rahi ho, pet ke ek taraf shadeed dard (ectopic pregnancy ka shubha) ho, bukhar 101°F se zyada, persistent ulti ke saath dehydration (peshab kam, kamzori), shadeed sar dard ya nazar dhundhli, behoshi mehsoos ho. Halka spotting aksar normal hai magar bleeding heavy ho aur clots aayein to safe rehne ke liye fauran doctor dikhayein.
