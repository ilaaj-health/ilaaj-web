Nimesulide Bachon Ko Kyun Nahi Deni Chahiye — Liver Aur Reye Syndrome

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Nimesulide 12 saal se kam bachon ke liye globally banned hai (USA, Europe, India)
  • Wajah: fulminant liver failure aur bachon mein deaths reported
  • Pakistan mein Nise, Nims, Nimica, Aulin ke naam se milti hai — bachon ke liye NA
  • Safer alternatives: Paracetamol (Calpol) aur Brufen syrup (>6 mahine)
  • Agar galti se di gayi ho to LFT karwayein aur jaundice signs pe foran hospital

Nimesulide Kya Hai?

Nimesulide ek non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) hai jo dard, soojan aur bukhar kam karne ke liye use hoti hai. Pakistan mein yeh aam tor pe Nise, Nims, Nimica, Aulin aur Coxidol ke brand names se medical stores pe milti hai — kabhi tablet, kabhi suspension, aur aksar "khansi+dard" combo packs mein bhi. Adults mein kuch conditions ke liye DRAP allow karta hai magar bachon ke liye yeh dawai duniya ke kai mulkon mein banned hai aur Pakistan mein bhi pediatric use ki sifarish nahi hoti.

Nimesulide Bachon Mein Kyun Khatarnak Hai?

Nimesulide ka sab se bara khatra liver toxicity (hepatotoxicity) hai. Bachon ka liver abhi mukammal develop nahi hua hota aur is dawai ko process karne ki capacity adults se kam hoti hai. Yeh kuch din mein hi sehatmand bache ke liver ko mukammal fail kar sakti hai — is condition ko fulminant hepatitis kehte hain. Duniya bhar mein bachon ki deaths is dawai se report hui hain.

2007 mein WHO ne nimesulide pe pediatric safety warning issue ki. Iske baad USA, Canada, Finland, Spain, Belgium aur kai mulkon mein yeh adults ke liye bhi withdraw kar di gayi. India ne 2011 mein officially nimesulide 12 saal se kam bachon ke liye ban kar di. FDA ne USA mein kabhi approve hi nahi ki.

Khaaskar Khaufnak Cases

  • Fulminant hepatic failure: Sehatmand bacha 1-2 hafte mein liver failure mein chala gaya — kai cases mein liver transplant zaruri
  • Jaundice (yarqaan): Aankhein peeli, jild peeli, peshab gehra peela — alarming signs
  • Stevens-Johnson Syndrome: Jild aur munh par shadeed allergy reaction, jild utarna
  • Kidney damage: Pesab ki mqdar kam hona, dehydration ke saath
  • Gastrointestinal bleeding: Pet aur ant mein khoon bhana

Pakistan Mein Status — Aap Ko Kya Pata Hona Chahiye

DRAP (Drug Regulatory Authority of Pakistan) nimesulide ko balig logon ke liye registered rakhta hai magar pediatric use ki ijazat nahi deta. Magar zameeni haqeeqat alag hai — bohat se medical stores walay aur ghair-pesh-war "hakeem" yeh dawai bachon ke liye recommend kar dete hain, khaaskar "khansi+dard ka combo" packs mein. Walidain ko khud aware hona zaruri hai.

Combo dawai khareedte waqt label dekhain. Agar in mein se koi naam likha ho — nimesulide, nimes, nise, nims — aur dawai bache ke liye hai, to foran wapas kar dein aur safer dawai mangwaein.

Safer Alternatives — Bachon Ke Liye Best Choices

1. Paracetamol (Calpol, Panadol Syrup, Tylenol)

Sab se safe option. 3 mahine se barhe har bache ke liye, dose 10-15 mg/kg har 4-6 ghante baad. Bukhar aur halki dard mein first-line dawai.

2. Ibuprofen (Brufen Syrup, Nurofen)

6 mahine se barhe bachon ke liye safe — dose 5-10 mg/kg har 6-8 ghante baad. Soojan aur dard mein zyada asar daar. Khaane ke saath dein.

3. Combination Therapy (Doctor Ki Guidance Mein)

Shadeed bukhar mein doctor kabhi paracetamol aur ibuprofen ko alternate karwata hai — magar yeh apni marzi se na karein.

Kab Doctor Se Foran Milein?

Agar bache ko galti se nimesulide di gayi ho to in alamaat pe foran hospital lay jaein:

  • Ankhon ya jild ka peelapan (jaundice)
  • Peshab gehra peela ya bhura
  • Pet ke daye taraf upar shadeed dard
  • Mutawatir ulti, khaane se inkaar
  • Sustness, ghuneem aana, behoshi
  • Jild pe khoon ki larkein ya bohat zyada rash
  • Saans ki takleef

Walidain Ke Liye Aham Rules

  • Bina prescription apni marzi se bache ko painkiller na dein
  • "Khansi+dard" ya "bukhar+badan dard" ke combo packs ka label parhain
  • Medical store walay ki bajaye PMDC-registered doctor se prescription lein
  • Ghar mein nimesulide rakhi hai to door kar dein — galti se bachay ko jaa sakti hai
  • Padosi/rishtedaar ki "yeh dawai mere bache ko di thi" wali baat na manein — har bache ki dawai alag hoti hai

Conclusion

Nimesulide adults ke liye kabhi prescribed ki ja sakti hai magar bachon ke liye kabhi nahi. Pakistan mein iska aam istemal aik khamoshi se chal raha public health khatra hai. Bukhar aur dard mein bachon ke liye sirf paracetamol ya ibuprofen safe hain — wo bhi sahih dose mein. Frequently Asked Questions

Kya nimesulide bachon ke liye safe hai?
Nahi. Nimesulide 12 saal se kam bachon ke liye globally banned hai — USA, Canada, Europe, India aur kai mulkon mein. Wajah liver ki shadeed kharabi (fulminant hepatitis) hai jo deaths tak pohanchi hai. WHO ne 2007 mein iska warning issue kiya. Pakistan mein bhi DRAP ki guidelines bachon mein use ki ijazat nahi deti.
Pakistan mein nimesulide kin naamon se milti hai?
Pakistan mein nimesulide aam brands: Nise, Nims, Nimica, Aulin, Coxidol, Nimes ke naam se medical stores pe milti hai. Yeh tablet, suspension aur kabhi "khansi+dard" combo packs mein bhi hoti hai. Combo dawai khareedte waqt label zaroor parhain — agar nimesulide likha ho aur dawai bache ke liye hai to band kar dein.
Agar bache ko galti se nimesulide de di to kya karein?
Foran dawai band karein aur doctor se rabta karein. Aane wale dino mein bache ki ankhon aur jild ka rang (jaundice ke liye), peshab ka rang (gehra peela), pet ke daye taraf upar dard, ulti, aur sustness pe khaaskar nazar rakhein. Agar yeh kuch bhi nazar aaye to foran hospital lay jaein aur Liver Function Test (LFT) karwayein.
Bukhar aur dard mein bachon ke liye safer alternatives kya hain?
Paracetamol (Calpol, Panadol syrup, Tylenol) sab se safe hai — 3 mahine se barhe har bache ke liye. 6 mahine se barhe bachon ko ibuprofen (Brufen syrup) bhi di ja sakti hai. Aspirin (Disprin) aur Nimesulide bachon mein NEVER. Sahih dose hamesha wazan ke hisab se calculate hoti hai.
Reye syndrome kya hai aur nimesulide se kaisey juda hai?
Reye syndrome ek rare magar jaanleva bimari hai jis mein liver aur dimagh kharab ho jate hain. Yeh asal mein aspirin ke saath bachon mein zyada juda hai magar nimesulide bhi bachon mein liver toxicity aur fulminant hepatitis paida karti hai jo Reye syndrome ke jaisi pesh aati hai. Dono dawai bachon mein no-go hain — sirf paracetamol ya ibuprofen safe hain.
