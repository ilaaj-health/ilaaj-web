Cholesterol Ka Ilaj — High Cholesterol Diet Aur Medicine Pakistan Mein

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • High cholesterol ke koi symptoms NAHI hote — sirf blood test se pata chalta hai
  • LDL (bad cholesterol) 100 se kam hona chahiye
  • Ghee, dalda, fried food — yeh sab LDL barhate hain
  • Olive oil, oats, fish, almonds — yeh cholesterol kam karte hain
  • 30 minute daily walk bohat zaroori hai
  • Agar diet se kaam na bane to statin medicine safe aur effective hai

Cholesterol Kya Hota Hai?

Cholesterol ek waxy, chikna madda hai jo aap ke khoon mein hota hai. Jism ko cholesterol ki zarurat hai — yeh cells banata hai, hormones banata hai, aur khaana hazm karne mein madad karta hai. Magar jab cholesterol zyada ho jaye to yeh arteries (khoon ki naaliyon) mein jama hone lagta hai, unhe tang karta hai, aur heart attack ya stroke ka khatra barhata hai.

Cholesterol Types — Samjhein
LDL (Bad Cholesterol)Arteries mein jama hota hai — kam hona chahiye (<100 mg/dL)
HDL (Good Cholesterol)LDL ko saaf karta hai — zyada hona chahiye (>40 men, >50 women)
TriglyceridesExtra calories fat mein convert — kam hona chahiye (<150 mg/dL)
Total CholesterolSab ka sum — <200 mg/dL normal

Pakistan Mein Cholesterol Ka Masla

Pakistan mein heart disease maut ki sabse barri wajah hai — aur high cholesterol iske peeche sabse barra factor hai. Wajuhaat:

  • Ghee aur dalda ka zyada istamal: Desi khaane mein ghee, dalda (vanaspati — trans fat) bohat zyada use hota hai
  • Fried food culture: Samose, pakore, parathay, biryani — fried aur oily khaana Pakistan mein roz ka hai
  • Exercise ki kami: Walking culture kam hai, zyada tar log gaari/rickshaw mein chalte hain
  • Smoking: Cigarette HDL (good cholesterol) kam karti hai
  • Diabetes: Pakistan mein 33 million diabetics hain — diabetes triglycerides barhata hai
  • Family history: Dil ki bimari gharaney mein ho to risk aur barh jata hai

High Cholesterol Ke Symptoms — NAHI Hote!

Yeh samajhna bohat zaruri hai: high cholesterol ke koi symptoms nahi hote. Aap ko bilkul normal feel hoga, koi dard nahi, koi nishani nahi. Isliye ise "silent killer" kehte hain. Cholesterol arteries mein saalon tak jama hota rehta hai bina kisi alamat ke — aur phir achanak heart attack ya stroke hota hai.

Sirf lipid profile blood test se pata chalta hai. Isliye har 5 saal mein test zaruri hai — agar risk factors hain to zyada baar.

Lipid Profile Test — Kaise Aur Kab?

  • Test ka naam: Lipid Profile ya Fasting Lipid Panel
  • Fasting: Test se 12 ghante pehle kuch na khayen (pani pi sakte hain)
  • Qeemat: Pakistan mein Rs 800-1500
  • Kab karwayein: 20 saal ki umar ke baad har 5 saal mein. Diabetes, BP, ya family history ho to saalana
  • Results mein kya dekhein: Total cholesterol, LDL, HDL, triglycerides, VLDL

Doctor Se Report Review Karwayein

Diet Changes — Kya Khayen, Kya Avoid Karein

Khayen (Cholesterol Kam Karne Wale Foods):

  • Olive oil / Canola oil: Cooking mein ghee/dalda ki jagah — monounsaturated fats HDL barhate hain
  • Oats aur jaai: Soluble fiber LDL cholesterol absorb karta hai — roz nashte mein khayen
  • Fish: Salmon, sardines, tuna — omega-3 fatty acids triglycerides kam karte hain
  • Badam (Almonds) aur akhrot (Walnuts): Handful daily — healthy fats aur fiber
  • Fruits: Seb, narangi, angoor — pectin fiber cholesterol kam karti hai
  • Sabziyan: Bhindi, baingan (eggplant) — soluble fiber se bharpur
  • Daalein aur channay: Plant protein aur fiber — gosht ki jagah
  • Lehsan (Garlic): Cholesterol thoda kam karne mein madad karta hai

Avoid Karein:

  • Dalda / Vanaspati ghee: Trans fat — sabse khatarnak, bilkul band karein
  • Fried foods: Samose, pakore, french fries — deep frying se trans fat banti hai
  • Red meat zyada: Haftay mein 1-2 baar se zyada nahi
  • Full-fat dairy excess: Makkhan, cream — moderation mein
  • Bakery items: Biscuits, pastries, cakes — hidden trans fat
  • Sugary drinks: Cold drinks triglycerides barhate hain

Exercise — Kitni Zaruri Hai?

Exercise cholesterol control mein utni hi zaruri hai jitni diet:

  • 30 minute daily walk: Minimum — tez chaal se (brisk walking)
  • HDL (good cholesterol) 5-10% tak barh sakta hai regular exercise se
  • Triglycerides 20-30% tak kam ho sakte hain
  • Weight loss — har 5 kg wajan kam hone se cholesterol numbers behtar hote hain
  • Swimming, cycling, jogging — koi bhi cardio exercise fayde-mand hai

Statin Medicines — Kab Zaruri Hain?

Doctor statin medicine tab likhta hai jab:

  • LDL 190 mg/dL se zyada hai (chahe koi aur risk na ho)
  • LDL 70-189 hai MAGAR diabetes hai
  • Pehle se heart attack ya stroke ho chuka hai
  • 10-year heart risk 7.5% se zyada hai (calculator se)
  • 3 mahine diet/exercise se kafi farq nahi para

Common Statins Pakistan Mein:

  • Atorvastatin (Lipitor): 10-80 mg — sabse zyada prescribed
  • Rosuvastatin (Crestor): 5-40 mg — bohat effective, LDL 50%+ kam
  • Simvastatin: Purani magar still used

Statins raat ko lein — jism raat ko zyada cholesterol banata hai. Dawai band mat karein agar numbers normal aa jayein — statins rokne se cholesterol wapas barh jata hai.

Statin Side Effects

  • Muscle pain (myalgia): 5-10% patients mein — doctor ko batayein
  • Liver enzymes: Thode barh sakte hain — doctor monitoring karta hai
  • Diabetes risk: Thoda barh sakta hai magar heart protection isse kahin zyada hai
  • Serious side effects: Rhabdomyolysis (severe muscle damage) — bohat rare hai

Yaad rakhein: Statins ke fayde side effects se KAHIN zyada hain. Heart attack aur stroke prevention mein yeh sabse proven dawai hai.

Heart Attack Ke Warning Signs

High cholesterol ki wajah se heart attack ho sakta hai. Yeh signs dekhein:

  • Seene mein dard ya dabao — beech mein ya bayen taraf
  • Dard bayen baazu, gardan, jabre mein jaaye
  • Sans phoolna
  • Pasina chhootna, matli
  • Khawateen mein: thakawat, peeth mein dard — typical symptoms na hon

Yeh signs dikhen to 1122 call karein ya foran hospital jayein — time waste na karein!

Conclusion

High cholesterol ek khamosh bimari hai jo saalon tak bina alamat ke arteries kharaab karti hai. Lipid profile test karwayein, diet mein ghee/dalda kam karein, roz walk karein, aur agar doctor medicine likhe to regularly lein. Frequently Asked Questions

High cholesterol ke koi symptoms hote hain?
Nahi — high cholesterol ke koi symptoms NAHI hote. Yeh 'silent killer' hai. Aap ko bilkul normal feel hoga magar arteries mein cholesterol jama hota rehta hai. Sirf lipid profile blood test se pata chalta hai. Isliye 20 saal ki umar ke baad har 5 saal mein test karwana chahiye. Agar family mein heart disease hai to pehle aur zyada baar test karwayein.
Kya ghee khane se cholesterol barhta hai?
Haan — desi ghee saturated fat se bharpoor hai jo LDL (bad) cholesterol barhata hai. Magar thodi miqdar (1-2 tablespoon daily) itni nuqsan-deh nahi jitna samjha jata hai. Asal masla tab hota hai jab ghee zyada miqdar mein khayi jaaye aur saath exercise bilkul na ho. Dalda/vanaspati ghee (trans fat) desi ghee se BHI zyada khatarnak hai. Olive oil ya canola oil behtar options hain cooking ke liye.
Statin medicine ke side effects kya hain?
Zyada tar log statins bilkul safely lete hain. Kuch logon mein muscle pain (myalgia) ho sakta hai — 5-10% patients mein. Liver enzymes barh sakte hain magar serious liver damage bohat rare hai. Doctor shuru mein liver function test karwata hai. Agar muscle pain ho to doctor ko batayein — dose adjust ya dawai change ho sakti hai. Statins ke fayde (heart attack/stroke prevention) side effects se kahin zyada hain.
Cholesterol normal kitna hona chahiye?
Total cholesterol 200 mg/dL se kam hona chahiye. LDL (bad cholesterol) 100 se kam, HDL (good cholesterol) mardon mein 40 se zyada aur khawateen mein 50 se zyada. Triglycerides 150 se kam. Agar diabetes ya heart disease hai to LDL 70 se bhi kam rakhna target hota hai. Lipid profile test (fasting 12 ghante) se yeh sab check hota hai — Pakistan mein Rs 800-1500 mein.
Kya sirf diet se cholesterol kam ho sakta hai?
Haan — agar cholesterol thoda barha hua hai (borderline high) to diet aur exercise se 10-20% tak kam ho sakta hai. Magar agar LDL bohat zyada hai (190+), ya diabetes/heart disease hai, to akeli diet kafi nahi — statin medicine zaruri hoti hai. 3 mahine diet aur exercise try karein, phir lipid profile repeat karein. Agar kafi farq na ho to doctor medicine shuru karega.
