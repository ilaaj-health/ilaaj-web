Pregnancy Ka Teesra Mahina — Baby Ki Growth, Symptoms Aur Care

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Teesra mahina weeks 9-12 hota hai — first trimester ka aakhri hissa
  • Baby 3 inch ka ho jata hai, ungliyan banti hain, dil 150-170 bpm pe dharkta hai
  • Morning sickness is mahine peak pe hoti hai — week 12 ke baad aksar behtar
  • NT scan (nuchal translucency) 11-14 weeks pe zaruri hai — Down syndrome screening
  • Folic acid 400mcg + iron 60mg daily zaruri supplements hain
  • Bleeding, severe cramping, ya high fever ho to foran doctor ko milein

Teesra Mahina Kya Hota Hai? (Weeks 9-12)

Pregnancy ka teesra mahina week 9 se week 12 tak hota hai — yeh aap ke first trimester ka aakhri hissa hai. Is waqt tak aap ke baby ke tamam major organs banne shuru ho chuke hain aur ab woh rapidly grow kar rahe hain. Bohat si maaon ke liye yeh mushkil mahina hota hai kyunke morning sickness apne peak pe hoti hai — magar good news yeh hai ke week 12 ke baad aksar yeh symptoms behtar hone lagte hain.

Is mahine mein aap ka pehla major ultrasound (dating scan) hota hai jis mein baby ki heartbeat sunai deti hai — yeh bohat emotional aur exciting moment hota hai.

Baby Ki Development — Teesre Mahine Mein Kya Hota Hai?

Teesre mahine mein aap ka baby ek aakhrot ke size se badh kar lagbhag 3 inches (7.5 cm) ka ho jata hai. Yeh development ka bohat critical phase hai:

  • Ungliyan aur paon ki ungliyan (fingers and toes) properly ban jaati hain
  • Dil ki dharkan 150-170 beats per minute pe hoti hai — Doppler se sunai de sakti hai
  • Brain ka rapid growth — har minute mein hazaron naye neurons bante hain
  • Kidneys, liver, aur intestines develop ho rahe hain
  • Baby ke nails banne shuru hote hain
  • Chehra zyada human shape le raha hai — naak, hoont, aur ears ban rahe hain
  • Baby haath pair hilana shuru karta hai — magar abhi aap mehsoos nahi kar saktin
  • Reproductive organs develop ho rahe hain (magar ultrasound pe gender abhi nazar nahi aata)
Teesre Mahine Ki Key Milestones
Baby ka size~3 inches (lime ke barabar)
Weight~1 ounce (28 grams)
Heartbeat150-170 bpm
Major developmentOrgans, brain, fingers/toes
Miscarriage riskWeek 12 ke baad significantly kam

Maa Ke Symptoms — Teesre Mahine Mein Kya Mehsoos Hota Hai?

Teesra mahina aksar pregnancy ka sab se mushkil hissa hota hai symptoms ke lihaaz se. Yeh common symptoms hain:

Morning Sickness (Matlipan/Ulti)

Morning sickness is mahine mein apne peak pe hoti hai. "Morning" naam misleading hai — yeh din ke kisi bhi waqt ho sakti hai. Kuch auraton ko sirf matli hoti hai, kuch ko baarish aati hai. Week 12 ke baad aksar yeh behtar hone lagti hai kyunke hCG hormone levels stabilize hote hain.

Thakawat (Fatigue)

Shadeed thakawat first trimester ki hallmark hai. Aap ka jism baby ke liye placenta bana raha hai aur blood volume barh raha hai — yeh sab energy leta hai. Zyada se zyada aaram karein — raat ko jaldi soyen aur agar mumkin ho to din mein bhi short nap lein.

Frequent Urination

Baarha washroom jana parna — yeh growing uterus bladder pe pressure dalti hai aur blood volume barhne se kidneys zyada kaam karti hain.

Breast Changes

Breasts mein swelling, tenderness, aur nipples ka rang gehra hona. Yeh sab hormonal changes ki wajah se hota hai aur normal hai.

Mood Swings

Achanak khush hona, phir rona — hormones (progesterone aur estrogen) ki wajah se mood zyada fluctuate karta hai. Yeh bilkul normal hai.

Food Cravings aur Aversions

Kuch khaano ki achanak bohat craving hona aur kuch khaano ki smell se matli aana. Har aurat ka experience different hota hai.

First Trimester Screening — Zaruri Tests

Teesra mahina wo waqt hai jab kuch bohat important screening tests hote hain:

NT Scan (Nuchal Translucency) — 11-14 Weeks

Yeh ultrasound baby ki gardan ke peeche ki fluid thickness measure karta hai. Agar yeh normal se zyada ho to Down syndrome (Trisomy 21) aur kuch doosre chromosomal abnormalities ka risk barh jata hai. Yeh screening test hai, diagnosis nahi — matlab yeh batata hai ke risk zyada hai ya kam, magar confirm nahi karta.

Blood Tests (Double/Triple Marker)

NT scan ke saath blood tests (PAPP-A aur free beta-hCG) bhi hote hain jo Down syndrome aur Edwards syndrome ke risk ko aur accurately calculate karte hain.

Dating Scan

Yeh scan baby ki due date confirm karta hai aur check karta hai ke baby properly grow kar raha hai. Heartbeat confirm hoti hai aur multiple pregnancy (twins) bhi pata chalti hai.

Routine Blood Work

  • CBC (Complete Blood Count) — anaemia check
  • Blood group aur Rh factor — bohat zaruri hai
  • Hepatitis B aur C screening
  • HIV screening
  • Blood sugar (fasting/random)
  • Urine routine — infection check
  • Thyroid function (TSH)

Doctor Se Abhi Mashwara Karein

Zaruri Supplements — Teesre Mahine Mein Kya Lein?

Pregnancy mein sahi supplements lena maa aur baby dono ke liye bohat zaruri hai:

Folic Acid — 400mcg Daily (Zaruri!)

Folic acid baby ke neural tube defects (brain aur spine ki problems) se bachaati hai. Yeh pehle trimester mein sab se zyada zaruri hai. Ideally pregnancy se 3 mahine pehle shuru karein. Doctor aksar pregnancy ke end tak continue karte hain.

Iron — 60mg Daily

Pakistan mein anaemia bohat aam hai pregnant women mein. Iron supplement blood banane mein madad karta hai kyunke pregnancy mein blood volume 50% tak barh jata hai. Iron ko orange juice ya vitamin C ke saath lein taake absorption behtar ho. Chai/doodh ke saath na lein.

Calcium

Baby ki haddiyan banne ke liye calcium zaruri hai. Agar aap dairy products kam khaati hain to supplement lein. 1000mg daily recommended hai.

Vitamin D

Vitamin D calcium ki absorption mein madad karta hai. Pakistan mein despite sunny weather, Vitamin D deficiency bohat aam hai. Doctor se check karwayein.

Diet Aur Khaana — Matli Mein Kya Khaein?

Teesre mahine mein morning sickness ki wajah se khaana mushkil ho sakta hai. Yeh tips follow karein:

Morning Sickness Ke Liye Diet Tips

  • Chhoti magar frequent meals lein — din mein 5-6 baar thoda thoda khaein
  • Subah khali pet na uthein — bedside pe dry crackers/biscuits rakhein, uthne se pehle khaein
  • Adrak (ginger) wali chai matli mein faydemand hai
  • Bohat masaledar, oily, ya strong smell wala khaana avoid karein
  • Cold foods aksar behtar tolerate hote hain (smell kam hoti hai)
  • Khaane ke beech mein pani piyen, khaane ke saath nahi

Kya Khaein?

  • Protein-rich: anday, daal, chicken, dahi, paneer
  • Iron-rich: palak, chana, kaleji (cooked), kishmish
  • Calcium-rich: doodh, dahi, cheese, lassi
  • Fruits: kelay, seb, narangi, amrood
  • Whole grains: roti (atta wali), brown rice, daliya
  • Pani: 8-10 glasses rozana — dehydration se bachna zaruri hai

Kya Avoid Karein — Pregnancy Mein Mana

Teesre mahine mein (aur puri pregnancy mein) yeh cheezein avoid karein:

  • Raw/undercooked meat aur eggs — Salmonella aur Toxoplasmosis ka khatra
  • Unpasteurized dairy — Listeria infection ho sakti hai
  • Papaya (kaccha) aur pineapple zyada — kuch research mein uterine contractions se jora gaya hai
  • Bhari weight uthana — first trimester mein khaaskar avoid karein
  • X-rays — agar zaruri ho to doctor ko pregnancy ke baare mein zarur batayein
  • Koi bhi dawai bina doctor ke — even painkiller ya cold medicine
  • Smoking aur alcohol — bilkul band
  • Caffeine zyada — 200mg/day se kam (1-2 cups chai okay hai)
  • Hot tubs/sauna — body temperature zyada barhna baby ke liye harmful hai

Exercise Aur Physical Activity

Pregnancy mein exercise bilkul safe aur recommended hai — magar kuch precautions ke saath:

Safe Exercises:

  • Walking — sab se behtareen aur safest exercise. Rozana 20-30 minutes
  • Prenatal yoga — flexibility aur breathing mein madad karta hai
  • Swimming — joints pe kam pressure, comfortable rehti hai
  • Light stretching — body ko flexible rakhta hai

Avoid Karein:

  • Contact sports (football, basketball)
  • Heavy weight lifting
  • High-intensity exercise (agar pehle se karti nahi thin)
  • Pet ke bal laitne wali exercises
  • Bohat garmi mein bahar exercise

Rule of thumb: Exercise ke dauran baat kar sakein to intensity theek hai. Sans phoolne lage to slow karein.

Red Flags — Kab Doctor Ko Foran Milein?

Kuch symptoms aise hain jin mein foran doctor se milna zaruri hai:

  • Bleeding (khoon aana) — chahe thoda bhi ho, doctor ko zarur batayein
  • Severe cramping — halki cramping normal hai, magar shadeed dard nahi
  • High fever (101°F+) — infection ka sign ho sakta hai
  • Symptoms achanak gayab ho jaein — breast tenderness aur matli achanak band hona kabhi kabhi missed miscarriage ka sign hota hai
  • Severe vomiting — kuch bhi andar na reh raha ho (hyperemesis gravidarum)
  • Burning/pain in urination — UTI ka sign
  • Dizziness ya behoshi

Pakistan mein emergency: Agar bleeding heavy ho ya shadeed dard ho to nearest hospital emergency jaein. Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad mein major hospitals mein 24/7 OB-GYN emergency hoti hai.

Abhi Doctor Se Poochhein

Pakistan Mein Antenatal Care — Pehli Visit

Agar aap ne abhi tak OB-GYN (lady doctor) ko nahi dikhaya to teesra mahina pehli visit ka ideal waqt hai. Pakistan mein antenatal care ke key steps:

Pehli Visit Mein Kya Hoga:

  • Complete medical history — pehle ki pregnancies, koi bimari, family history
  • Physical examination — weight, blood pressure, general health
  • Blood tests — blood group, Rh factor, CBC, hepatitis B/C, HIV, blood sugar
  • Urine test — infection aur protein check
  • Ultrasound — baby ki heartbeat aur growth confirm
  • Supplements prescription — folic acid, iron, calcium

Pakistan Mein Costs:

Government hospitals (Lady Aitchison Lahore, Jinnah Hospital Karachi, PIMS Islamabad) mein antenatal care bohat sasti ya free hoti hai. Private mein pehli visit Rs. 1500-5000 tak hoti hai depending on city aur hospital. NT scan Rs. 3000-8000 ke beech hota hai.

Visits Ka Schedule:

Normal pregnancy mein doctor aksar yeh schedule follow karte hain:

  • Week 8-12: Pehli visit + dating scan
  • Week 11-14: NT scan
  • Week 18-22: Anomaly scan (detailed ultrasound)
  • Har 4 weeks mein checkup (28 weeks tak)
  • Har 2 weeks (28-36 weeks)
  • Har week (36 weeks ke baad delivery tak)

Emotional Wellbeing — Apna Khayal Rakhein

Pregnancy mein sirf physical health nahi, mental health bhi zaruri hai:

  • Mood swings normal hain — apne aap ko blame na karein
  • Partner aur family se baat karein — feelings share karein
  • Zyada stress na lein — meditation ya deep breathing try karein
  • Miscarriage ka dar hona normal hai — magar week 12 ke baad risk bohat kam ho jata hai
  • Agar sadness ya anxiety bohat zyada ho to doctor se zarur batayein — perinatal depression real hai

Conclusion

Pregnancy ka teesra mahina challenging hai — morning sickness, thakawat, aur anxiety sab mil kar mushkil lagte hain. Magar yeh bhi wo waqt hai jab aap ka baby tezi se grow kar raha hai, ungliyan ban rahi hain, dil dharak raha hai. Week 12 ke baad aksar symptoms behtar hote hain aur second trimester ka comfortable phase shuru hota hai.

Apni antenatal visits schedule pe rakhein, supplements regularly lein, healthy khaein, aur koi bhi red flag ho to doctor se milein. Frequently Asked Questions

Pregnancy ke teesre mahine mein kya kya hota hai?
Baby 3 inch ka ho jata hai, ungliyan/paon ki ungliyan banti hain, dil ki dharkan 150-170 bpm, organs develop hote hain. Maa ko morning sickness peak, thakawat, frequent urination hoti hai.
Teesre mahine mein kaunse tests zaruri hain?
NT scan (nuchal translucency) 11-14 weeks pe, blood tests Down syndrome risk ke liye, dating scan due date confirm karne ke liye, CBC, blood group, hepatitis screening, urine routine.
Morning sickness ka kya ilaj hai?
Chhoti magar frequent meals lein, subah khali pet na uthein — dry crackers rakhein, adrak wali chai piyen, vitamin B6 doctor ki advice pe, zyada masaledar khaana avoid karein. Week 12 ke baad aksar behtar hoti hai.
Pregnancy mein kaunsi cheezein avoid karni chahiyen?
Raw/undercooked meat aur eggs, unpasteurized dairy, papaya/pineapple zyada, bhari weight uthana, X-rays, koi bhi dawai bina doctor ke, smoking/alcohol/caffeine zyada.
Folic acid kitne din tak leni chahiye?
Folic acid 400mcg rozana pehle trimester bhar zaruri hai — ideally pregnancy se pehle shuru karein. Yeh baby ke neural tube defects (brain/spine) se bachaati hai. Doctor aksar pregnancy ke end tak continue karte hain.
