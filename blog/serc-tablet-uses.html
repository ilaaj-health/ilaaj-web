Serc Tablet Uses — Chakkar, Kaanon Ki Seeti Aur Meniere's Ka Ilaj Pakistan

Mukhtasar — Ahem Baatein
  • Serc = betahistine dihydrochloride 8mg/16mg — inner ear blood flow badhata hai
  • Vertigo (chakkar), tinnitus (kaanon ki seeti) aur Meniere's disease ke liye
  • Long-term prevention ke liye hai — acute attack mein fast-acting nahi; Stemetil/Stugeron zyada jaldi kaam karte hain
  • Neend nahi aati (cinnarizine ki tarah) — driving wale le sakte hain
  • Asthma aur peptic ulcer mein احتیاط — doctor se poochhna zaroori

Serc Kya Hai?

Serc betahistine dihydrochloride ki tablet hai — Pakistan mein 8mg aur 16mg forms mein available hai. Yeh inner ear (vestibular system) mein blood flow badhati hai aur endolymphatic pressure kam karti hai — jo chakkar aur hearing problems ka sabab hota hai.

Betahistine histamine ka synthetic analog hai — histamine H1 receptors activate karta hai (vasodilation) aur H3 receptors block karta hai (neurotransmitter release). Yeh combination inner ear ko stabilize karta hai.

Serc Ke Uses

1. Meniere's Disease

Serc ka sab se established use Meniere's disease hai — sudden severe vertigo attacks, progressive hearing loss, tinnitus aur ear fullness ka combination. Betahistine endolymphatic hydrops (inner ear mein fluid buildup) kam karta hai.

2. Vestibular Vertigo

Inner ear se related chakkar ke attacks — peripheral vertigo — mein Serc long-term prevention ke liye kaam aata hai. Attacks ki taqaadud aur shaddat dono kam hoti hain regular use se.

3. Tinnitus

Kaanon mein seeti ya ghant bajaane wali awaaz — Meniere's ya vascular tinnitus mein Serc helpful hai. Sensorineural hearing loss se related tinnitus mein bhi try kiya jaata hai.

Khulaasa

Serc (betahistine) Pakistan mein chakkar aur Meniere's disease ke liye ENT aur neurologist ka first-choice option hai. Long-term prevention ke liye hai — acute attack mein Stemetil ya Stugeron jaldi kaam karte hain. Non-sedating hone ki wajah se driving/work ke dauran le sakte hain. Asthma ya peptic ulcer mein doctor ki advice zaroori hai.

Aksar Pooche Jane Waale Sawaalat

Serc chakkar (vertigo) mein kitne time mein kaam karti hai?

Long-term prevention ke liye hai — 4-8 hafte regular use mein attacks kam hote hain. Acute attack mein Stemetil (prochlorperazine) ya Stugeron jaldi kaam karte hain.

Serc asthma walon ke liye safe hai?

احتیاط zaroori hai — betahistine histamine analog hai aur bronchospasm theoretically possible hai. Stable asthma mein usually safe magar severe asthma mein doctor se poochhein.

Serc 8mg aur 16mg mein kya farq hai?

8mg starting dose, 16mg common maintenance. 24mg (3 baar) tak dose badh sakti hai severe cases mein — doctor titration karte hain.

Kya Serc se neend aati hai?

Nahi — Serc non-sedating hai (Stugeron/cinnarizine ki tarah drowsiness nahi). Driving aur office kaam karne walon ke liye better choice hai.

BPPV mein Serc faida karta hai?

BPPV ke liye Epley maneuver (physiotherapy) first-line hai, Serc nahi. Serc Meniere's aur vestibular migraine mein better kaam karta hai. ENT ya neurologist se diagnosis zaroori hai.
