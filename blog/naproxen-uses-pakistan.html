Naproxen 500 mg — Uses, Dosage, Pakistan Mein Price

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Naproxen ek NSAID painkiller hai jo Brufen se zyada der tak (12 ghante) asar karti hai
  • Standard dose: 500 mg din mein 2 baar khaane ke saath
  • Joint pain, gout, period cramps aur kamar dard mein bohat asar daar
  • Pakistan mein Synflex 500 (Searle) aur Atrosan ke naam se milti hai
  • Dengue, hamla khwateen (3rd trimester), aur ulcer ke marizon ke liye sakht mana

Naproxen Kya Hai?

Naproxen ek Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drug (NSAID) hai — yani yeh dard, sujan aur bukhar teeno cheezon mein kaam karti hai. Pakistan mein yeh sab se aam tor pe Synflex 500 (Searle) aur Atrosan ke naam se milti hai. Iska sab se bara faida yeh hai ke iska asar lambay arsay tak — taqreeban 10 se 12 ghante — chalta hai, jab ke Brufen (ibuprofen) ka asar sirf 4-6 ghante hota hai.

Naproxen Ke Asal Uses

1. Joint Pain aur Arthritis

Naproxen ka sab se aam istemaal ghutnay, kandhe aur ungliyon ke joint pain mein hota hai. Rheumatoid arthritis aur osteoarthritis ke marizon mein doctors yeh dawai routine pe likhte hain kyun ke yeh sujan (inflammation) bhi kam karti hai.

2. Period Cramps (Mahwari Ka Dard)

Aurato mein periods ke dauran neeche pet ka shadeed dard Naproxen se kaafi behtar hota hai. Period start hone se 1 din pehlay 500 mg shuru karein aur 2-3 din le sakti hain.

3. Kamar Aur Gardan Ka Dard

Acute back pain, muscle strain, aur disc problems mein Naproxen pehli line ki dawai hai. Inflammation kam hone se nerve pressure bhi reduce hota hai.

4. Gout

Gout ke acute attack mein (jab paaon ke angootha shadeed dard karta hai aur sooj jaata hai) Naproxen 500 mg 2 baar din mein 5-7 din ke liye behtar option hai.

5. Dental aur Post-Surgery Pain

Dant nikalwane ke baad ya minor surgery ke baad ka dard control karne ke liye dentists Naproxen tajweez karte hain.

Naproxen Ka Dose Aur Tareeqa

  • Adults: 500 mg har 12 ghante baad — pehli dose acute pain mein 750 mg bhi ho sakti hai
  • Maximum: 1000 mg per day se zyada nahi (kuch cases mein doctor 1500 mg short-term tak likh sakta hai)
  • Bachay: 12 saal se kam bachon ko routine pe na dein — sirf doctor ki specific advice pe
  • Buzurg: 60 saal se upar ko half dose (250 mg) shuru karna behtar hai
  • Khaane Ke Saath: Hamesha khaane ke beech ya doodh ke saath — pet ulcer ka khatra kam hota hai

Abhi Doctor Se Milein

Side Effects Aur Khatraat

  • Pet ki kharabi: Acidity, jalan, mateelana, ulcer — sab se aam masla
  • Kidney pe asar: Lambay use se gurde ka function kam ho sakta hai, khaaskar buzurgon mein
  • Blood pressure barhna: Hypertension ke marizon mein BP control mushkil ho sakti hai
  • Khoon patla hona: Cuts se khoon zyada nikalta hai — surgery se 1 hafte pehlay band karein
  • Tang mein sujan (edema): Dil ke marizon mein khatarnaak ho sakti hai
  • Asthma attack: Aspirin-sensitive asthmatics mein attack ho sakta hai

Kab Naproxen Bilkul Na Lein

  • Dengue / viral bukhar: Platelets gir sakte hain, internal bleeding ka khatra — sirf Panadol
  • Hamla khwateen (3rd trimester): Bachay ke dil pe asar parta hai — sakht mana
  • Active pet ulcer ya khoon ki ulti ki history
  • Severe kidney ya liver disease
  • Dil ka dora (heart attack) ki recent history
  • Aspirin ya kisi NSAID se allergy

Naproxen vs Brufen — Kaunsi Lein?

Yeh aam sawal hai. Choti, halki cheezon — sar dard, halka bukhar, halki muscle ache — ke liye Brufen 400 mg kaafi hai. Magar agar dard lambi der tak chalta hai (jaise period cramps, gout attack, ya joint inflammation), Naproxen 500 mg behtar hai kyun ke aap ko din mein sirf 2 baar leni parti hai. Buzurg patients ke liye Naproxen ka cardiovascular risk Brufen se kam maani jaati hai magar pet ka risk thora zyada.

Pakistan Mein Price Aur Availability

Naproxen Pakistan mein over-the-counter milti hai magar pharmacist se hamesha brand aur strength check karein:

  • Synflex 500 (Searle): ~PKR 280-350 per strip of 10
  • Atrosan 500: ~PKR 250-300 per strip
  • Naprosyn: ~PKR 350-400 per strip
  • Synflex 250: Halki dose ke liye, ~PKR 180-220

Smog season (October-January) mein Lahore, Karachi aur KP mein joint pain badh jata hai — is waqt Naproxen ki demand ziada hoti hai magar self-medication na karein.

Conclusion

Naproxen ek bohat asar daar painkiller hai magar har dard ke liye nahi. Joint pain, period cramps, gout aur lambay arsay ke inflammation mein behtarin choice hai. Khaali pet, dengue, hamla aur ulcer ke marizon ke liye khatarnak. Frequently Asked Questions

Naproxen 500 mg din mein kitni baar le sakte hain?
Adults ke liye standard dose 500 mg din mein 2 baar (har 12 ghante baad) khaane ke saath hai. Total 1000 mg per day se zyada na lein. Naproxen ki khasiyat yeh hai ke iska asar Brufen se zyada lamba (10-12 ghante) chalta hai, is liye din mein sirf 2 doses kaafi hoti hain. Bina doctor mashware ke 7-10 din se zyada use na karein.
Naproxen aur Brufen mein kya farq hai?
Dono NSAIDs hain magar Naproxen ka asar zyada lamba chalta hai (12 ghante vs Brufen ke 6 ghante). Yani Naproxen din mein 2 baar lena parta hai jab ke Brufen 3-4 baar. Joint pain, gout aur period cramps mein Naproxen aksar behtar maani jati hai. Halki cheezon (sar dard, halka bukhar) ke liye Brufen 400 mg kaafi hai.
Naproxen ke side effects kya hain?
Sab se aam side effect pet ki kharabi hai — acidity, jalan, ulcer aur shadeed cases mein khoon ki ulti. Lambay arsay ke use se gurde (kidney) per asar parta hai, blood pressure barh sakti hai, aur tang mein sujan ho sakti hai. Khaali pet hargiz na lein — hamesha khaane ke saath ya doodh ke saath istemaal karein.
Dengue mein Naproxen le sakte hain?
BILKUL nahi. Dengue, malaria ya kisi bhi viral bukhar ki halat mein Naproxen, Brufen, aspirin jaisi NSAIDs khaana khatarnak hai — yeh khoon ko patla karti hain aur platelets gir sakte hain jis se internal bleeding ho sakti hai. Dengue mein sirf Panadol (paracetamol) safe hai. Garmi aur barsat (July-October) mein khaaskar yeh ehtiyaat zaruri hai.
Pakistan mein Naproxen ka price kya hai?
Pakistan mein Naproxen mukhtalif brands mein milti hai: Synflex 500 mg (Searle), Atrosan 500 mg, aur Naprosyn. Ek strip (10 tablets) ki keemat lagbhag PKR 250-400 ke darmiyan hoti hai brand aur strength ke hisaab se. 250 mg ki tablets bhi available hain jo halki dard ke liye behtar hain.
