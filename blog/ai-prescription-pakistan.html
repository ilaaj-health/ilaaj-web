AI Prescription Kya Hai?

AI prescription mein artificial intelligence aap ke symptoms structured tareeqe se collect karti hai aur draft prescription generate karti hai — phir licensed PMDC doctor review aur approve karta hai. Yeh digital prescription pharmacy se dawai laane ke liye legally valid hoti hai.

Why Pakistan Needs Online Prescriptions

  • Doctor shortage: Especially specialists, dehati ilaqon mein
  • Time: Office workers, mothers, students ke liye clinic visit difficult
  • Cost: Rs. 1500-5000 per consultation prohibitive
  • Privacy: Mental health, sexual health, gynecology ke liye
  • Refills: Chronic conditions (BP, diabetes) ke liye repeated visits
  • Travel: Specialists usually big cities mein

Step 1 — Symptom Description

Kin Conditions Ke Liye Prescription?

Acute Conditions:

  • Bukhar (viral, bacterial)
  • Sir dard, migraine
  • Pet dard, gastritis
  • Loose motion
  • Khansi-zukam, sore throat
  • UTI
  • Skin infections
  • Period cramps
  • Allergic reactions

Chronic Conditions (Refills):

  • Hypertension (BP medications)
  • Diabetes (Metformin, insulin guidance)
  • Thyroid (Eltroxin)
  • Cholesterol (statins)
  • Acid reflux (Risek, Pantop)
  • Asthma (inhalers)
  • Mental health (anti-depressants — careful monitoring)

Lifestyle Conditions:

  • Birth control (gynec consultation)
  • Erectile dysfunction (men's health)
  • Hair loss (Finasteride, minoxidil)
  • Acne (topical retinoids)

What Ilaaj AI WON'T Prescribe

Some prescriptions need in-person evaluation:

  • Controlled substances (opioids, benzodiazepines long-term)
  • Chemotherapy
  • Strong sedatives
  • Hormones requiring physical exam
  • Initial diagnosis of serious conditions (cancer, autoimmune)
  • Surgical interventions

In cases mein in-person specialist consultation zaruri hai.

Frequently Asked Questions

Online AI prescription Pakistan mein legal hai?

Haan, Ilaaj AI prescriptions legally valid hain Pakistan mein. Har prescription PMDC (Pakistan Medical Commission) registered doctor verify karta hai apna license number aur digital signature ke saath. Pakistan ke har pharmacy chain (Dawaai, Servaid, D.Watson, Fazaldin, etc.) Ilaaj AI prescriptions accept karte hain. Digital ya printed format mein use kar sakte hain.

AI prescription kitne der mein milti hai?

Ilaaj AI pe verified prescription 5-15 minute mein milti hai. AI structured intake 5-7 minute, doctor review aur approval 5-10 minute. Busy hours (raat ya weekend) mein thori der lag sakti hai magar generally bohat fast hai compared to clinic (2-4 ghante including travel + waiting).

Ilaaj AI prescription pharmacy accept karti hai?

Haan, Pakistan ke har pharmacy chain Ilaaj AI prescriptions accept karte hain — Dawaai, Servaid, Saylani, D.Watson, Fazaldin, aur local pharmacies. Prescription mein doctor's full name, qualifications, PMDC license number, digital signature, aur prescription ID hota hai jo pharmacist verify kar sakte hain. Phone pe show karein ya print karein.

Refills ke liye doctor consultation dobara zaruri hai?

Chronic conditions (BP, diabetes, thyroid, cholesterol) ke liye Ilaaj AI easy refill process hai. Pichli prescription chat history mein available hai. "Refill" button click karein, doctor briefly review karke renew kar deta hai. Refill pricing initial consultation se kaafi reduced. Major changes ya new symptoms ho to fresh consultation zaruri hai.

Kin medications ke liye Ilaaj AI prescription nahi degi?

Ilaaj AI yeh categories mein prescription nahi deta: controlled substances (opioids, long-term benzodiazepines like Lexotanil), chemotherapy, strong sedatives, hormones requiring physical exam, ya serious conditions (cancer, autoimmune) ka initial diagnosis. In cases mein in-person specialist consultation zaruri hai. Ilaaj AI clearly aap ko refer kar deta hai jab in-person evaluation needed ho.
