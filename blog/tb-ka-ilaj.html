TB Ka Ilaj — Tuberculosis Symptoms Aur Treatment Pakistan

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • TB Mycobacterium tuberculosis bacteria se hoti hai — phephron ko sabse zyada affect karti hai
  • Pakistan WHO ke high burden countries mein hai — 6 lakh+ cases har saal
  • 2 hafton se zyada khansi + bukhar + wazan girna = TB test zaruri
  • DOTS treatment 6 mahine ka hai — Pakistan mein FREE available hai
  • Course adhura chorna = MDR-TB ka khatra — bilkul mat chorna
  • TB curable hai — 95%+ patients theek hote hain pura course se

TB (Tuberculosis) Kya Hai?

Tuberculosis (TB) ek serious infectious disease hai jo Mycobacterium tuberculosis bacteria se hoti hai. Yeh sabse zyada phephron (lungs) ko affect karti hai — isko pulmonary TB kehte hain. Magar TB jism ke kisi bhi hisse mein ho sakti hai — haddiyon mein (spinal TB), gurdon mein, dimagh mein (TB meningitis), lymph nodes mein, aur pet mein. Pakistan duniya ke un countries mein hai jahan TB sabse zyada hai — WHO ke mutabiq har saal 6 lakh se zyada naye cases samne aate hain. Yeh koi purani bimari nahi — aaj bhi bohat active hai.

TB Kaise Failti Hai?

TB hawa (airborne) ke zariye failti hai. Jab active pulmonary TB ka marreez khansay, cheenkay, baat karay ya gaaye to bacteria hawa mein aate hain aur doosre log saans ke zariye inhale karte hain. Crowded jagahon pe zyada risk — jaise public transport, madrassay, hostels, jails.

Important: Sirf ACTIVE pulmonary TB contagious hai. Latent TB (infected magar beemar nahi) se doosron ko infection NAHI lagta. Ek active TB patient agar ilaj na karay to saal mein 10-15 logon ko infect kar sakta hai.

TB Ki Symptoms — Kaise Pehchanen?

Pulmonary TB ki classic symptoms:

  • Khansi 2 hafton se zyada — khaaskar balgham (sputum) ya khoon wali
  • Shaam ko halka bukhar jo raat ko barhta hai
  • Raat ko shadeed pasina aana (night sweats)
  • Wazan girna bina kisi wajah ke
  • Bhook kam hona aur kamzori
  • Seene mein dard ya saans lene mein takleef

Kuch logon mein symptoms dheere dheere barhte hain — mahino tak halka bukhar aur khansi ignore karte hain. Agar 2 hafton se zyada khansi ho to TB test LAZMI karwaein.

TB Ki Diagnosis — Kaunse Tests Hote Hain?

  • GeneXpert test — sabse best aur fast test, 2 ghanton mein result, drug resistance bhi batata hai (Pakistan mein government hospitals mein FREE)
  • Sputum microscopy — purana magar still useful, 2-3 samples subah ka balgham
  • Chest X-ray — TB ka pattern dikhata hai magar confirm nahi karta akela
  • Mantoux/TST (TB skin test) — latent TB detect karta hai
  • Blood tests (IGRA) — latent TB ke liye
  • CT scan — complicated cases mein

Pakistan mein agar khansi 2+ hafton se hai to pehla qadam sputum test + chest X-ray hai.

Doctor Se Foran Mashwara

TB Ka Ilaj — DOTS Treatment

Standard TB treatment 6 mahine ka hai aur isko DOTS (Directly Observed Treatment, Short-course) kehte hain:

Pehle 2 mahine (Intensive Phase): 4 dawaiyan — Rifampicin (R), Isoniazid (H), Pyrazinamide (Z), Ethambutol (E) = RHZE.

Agle 4 mahine (Continuation Phase): 2 dawaiyan — Rifampicin + Isoniazid = RH.

Pakistan mein yeh treatment GOVERNMENT SE FREE milta hai — NTP (National TB Control Program) ke through. DOTS mein health worker ya family member patient ko roz dawai khilata/dekhta hai — yeh isliye ke course pura ho.

TB Ka Course Adhura Chorna — Kitna Khatarnak?

Yeh sab se ZARURI baat hai: TB ki dawai beech mein chorna bohat KHATARNAK hai. Jab patient 2-3 mahine dawai kha kar behtar feel karta hai to aksar sochta hai "theek ho gaya" aur dawai chhor deta hai. Isse bacteria resistant ho jata hai aur MDR-TB (Multi-Drug Resistant TB) ban jaata hai.

MDR-TB ka ilaj 9-20 mahine ka hota hai, dawaiyan zyada aur mushkil (injections bhi), side effects zyada, aur success rate kam. Pakistan mein MDR-TB barh rahi hai — yeh national health crisis hai.

FULL 6 MAHINE KA COURSE PURA KAREIN — chahe 2 mahine mein acha feel ho.

TB Ki Dawaiyon Ke Side Effects

TB ki dawaiyan overall safe hain magar kuch side effects ho sakte hain:

  • Rifampicin — pesab/aansu/pasina orange/red ho sakta hai (NORMAL hai, darna nahi)
  • Isoniazid — peripheral neuropathy (pair mein sunn/tingling) — isliye Pyridoxine (Vitamin B6) saath dete hain
  • Pyrazinamide — joint pain, uric acid barhna
  • Ethambutol — nazar mein masla (rare) — color vision check
  • Liver enzymes barhna — doctor regular LFTs check karta hai

Agar shadeed ulti, yarkan (peeli ankhein), ya nazar kamzor ho to FORAN doctor ko batayein.

Latent TB — Infected Magar Beemar Nahi

Kuch log TB bacteria se infected hote hain magar unka immune system bacteria ko control mein rakhta hai — yeh latent TB hai. Latent TB mein:

  • Koi symptoms nahi
  • Doosron ko infection nahi lagta
  • Mantoux test ya IGRA positive aata hai
  • Chest X-ray normal hota hai

Magar latent TB kisi bhi waqt active ho sakti hai — khaaskar agar immune system kamzor ho (HIV, diabetes, malnutrition, steroids). Doctor latent TB ka preventive treatment de sakta hai — Isoniazid 6-9 mahine.

TB Ke Baare Mein Ghalat Fehmiyan (Myths)

  • TB sirf ghareebon ki bimari hai — GALAT. TB kisi ko bhi ho sakti hai — ameer, ghareeb, doctor, engineer.
  • TB curable nahi hai — GALAT. Pura course karne se 95%+ patients theek hote hain.
  • TB patients se milna ya baat karna khatarnak hai — Exaggerated. Treatment shuru hone ke 2-3 hafton baad patient mostly non-contagious ho jaata hai.
  • TB hereditary hai — GALAT. Yeh infection hai, genetic nahi.
  • Sirf phephron ki TB hoti hai — GALAT. TB haddi, brain, kidney, lymph nodes — kahin bhi ho sakti hai.

TB Se Bachao

  • TB patient ka ilaj hi sabse bara bachao hai — treated patient doosron ko infect nahi karta
  • BCG vaccine — Pakistan mein paidaish ke waqt lagti hai (serious childhood TB se bachati hai)
  • Ventilation — kamron mein hawa ka intezaam rakhein
  • Active TB patient pehle 2-3 hafton mein mask pehne
  • Khansi ki adab (cough etiquette) — mooh dhank kar khansein
  • Crowded jagahon pe zyada waqt na guzarein agar immune system weak hai
  • Screening — TB patient ke ghar walon ka test zaruri

Conclusion

TB ek serious magar CURABLE bimari hai. Pakistan mein treatment FREE available hai — sirf zarurat hai awareness aur pura course karne ki. Frequently Asked Questions

TB ka ilaj kitne din ka hota hai?
Standard TB ka ilaj 6 mahine ka hai — pehle 2 mahine 4 dawaiyan (RHZE) aur agle 4 mahine 2 dawaiyan (RH). MDR-TB mein 9-20 mahine lag sakte hain. Course beech mein chorna bilkul GALAT hai — bacteria resistant ho jaata hai. Pakistan mein yeh treatment government se FREE milta hai.
TB kaise failti hai?
TB hawa ke zariye failti hai — jab active TB ka marreez khansay ya cheenkay to bacteria hawa mein aate hain. Crowded jagahon pe risk zyada hai. Sirf active pulmonary TB contagious hai. Treatment shuru hone ke 2-3 hafton baad patient mostly non-contagious ho jaata hai. Latent TB se doosron ko infection nahi lagta.
Kya TB ka test free hai Pakistan mein?
Haan, Pakistan mein NTP (National TB Control Program) ke through sputum test, GeneXpert, aur chest X-ray FREE hain government hospitals mein. Dawai bhi 6 mahine ka pura course FREE milta hai. Apne qareeb ke government hospital ya TB clinic mein jaein.
TB ki dawai ke side effects kya hain?
Common side effects mein pesab/pasina orange hona (Rifampicin se — normal hai), pair mein sunn hona (Isoniazid se — Vitamin B6 saath lein), joint pain, aur liver enzymes barhna shamil hain. Zyada tar side effects manageable hain. Agar shadeed ulti, yarkan, ya nazar kamzor ho to foran doctor se milein.
Kya TB dubara ho sakti hai?
Haan, TB dubara ho sakti hai — khaaskar agar pehli baar course adhura chora ho ya immune system weak ho (HIV, diabetes). Isliye pura course karna bohat zaruri hai. Reinfection bhi ho sakti hai naye exposure se. Agar dubara symptoms aayein to turant test karwaein.
