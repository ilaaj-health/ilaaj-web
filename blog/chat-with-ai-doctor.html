Chat Workflow

1. Initial Greeting

Aap likhte hain: "Salam. Mera sir 3 din se dard kar raha hai aur halki bukhar bhi hai."

AI: "Walaikum salam. Mujhe khushi hai aap ne contact kiya. Aap ki taklif samajhne ke liye chand sawalat puchta hun:"

2. Structured Questions

AI 5-7 focused questions puchta hai (one at a time, with chip options):

  • "Sir dard sir ke kis hisse mein hota hai?" [chips: maathay, peeche, ek taraf, pure sir mein]
  • "Bukhar kitna degree?" [chips: 99-100, 100-101, 101-102, 102+]
  • "Aur kya symptoms hain?" [chips: ulti, light se taklif, gardan akarna, koi nahi]

3. Free Text Option

Har question mein "Apne lafzon mein" pill — agar chips se zyada batana ho.

4. AI Assessment

Saari information ke baad AI:

  • Most likely diagnosis explain karta hai
  • Self-care advice deta hai
  • Red flags identify karta hai
  • Doctor consultation option suggest karta hai

5. Doctor Verification (Optional)

Verified prescription chahiye to one-click upgrade. Payment ke baad PMDC doctor 5-10 minute mein review karta hai.

6. Prescription Delivery

Doctor approval ke baad prescription chat mein PDF format mein deliver hoti hai.

Limitations

Honest hone ke liye, chat-based AI doctor:

  • Physical examination nahi kar sakta
  • Direct emergency response nahi (1122 call karein)
  • Imaging interpret nahi kar sakta (X-ray photos analyze ho sakti hain magar limited)
  • Surgery decisions nahi le sakta
  • Some specialized conditions in-person evaluation chahiye
