Hepatitis B Aur C Ka Ilaj — Liver Infection Treatment Pakistan Mein

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Pakistan mein 12 million+ log Hepatitis B ya C se mutasir hain — aksariyat ko pata bhi nahi
  • Hepatitis C ab 95%+ cases mein dawai se CURE ho jata hai (3-6 mahine ka course)
  • Hepatitis B ki vaccine free hai — EPI mein shamil hai
  • Unsafe needles, barber ka blade, aur contaminated blood sab se bari wajah hain
  • Bina ilaj ke liver cirrhosis aur liver cancer ho sakta hai
  • HBsAg aur Anti-HCV test Rs. 300-500 mein ho jata hai — har adult ko ek baar zarur karwana chahiye

Hepatitis Kya Hai?

Hepatitis ka matlab hai liver ki sujan (inflammation). Yeh virus se hoti hai aur agar ilaj na ho to liver permanently damage ho jata hai. Pakistan mein sab se aam Hepatitis B aur Hepatitis C hain — dono blood se phelte hain aur dono liver cancer ki wajah ban sakte hain.

WHO ke mutabiq Pakistan mein 12 million se zyada log Hepatitis B ya C se mutasir hain. Sab se khatarnak baat yeh hai ke aksariyat ko pata hi nahi ke unhe hepatitis hai — kyunke shuru mein koi symptoms nahi hote. Jab tak pata chalta hai, liver ko kaafi nuqsaan ho chuka hota hai.

Hepatitis Kaise Phelta Hai? — Pakistan Mein Common Wajuhat

Pakistan mein hepatitis itna aam hone ki wajuhat samajhna zaruri hai taake aap apna aur apne ghar walon ka bachao kar sakein:

Unsafe Needles Aur Syringes

Pakistan mein quack doctors aur desi clinics mein ek hi syringe kai logon pe use hoti hai. Yeh hepatitis C ki sab se bari wajah hai. Drip lagane walay, dental clinics, aur tattooing se bhi infection ho sakta hai.

Barber Ka Blade

Nai ki dukaan pe ek hi blade kai logon ki shave ke liye use hota hai. Blade pe micro-cuts se khoon lagta hai aur virus aglay insaan ko lag jata hai. Yeh Pakistan mein hepatitis phailne ki doosri sab se bari wajah hai.

Contaminated Blood Transfusion

Unscreened blood transfusion se hepatitis B aur C dono lag sakte hain. Hamesha screened blood hi lein — registered blood bank se.

Mother-to-Child (Maa Se Bachay Ko)

Hepatitis B positive maa se delivery ke waqt bachay ko virus lag sakta hai. Isliye pregnancy mein HBsAg test zaruri hai — agar positive ho to bachay ko birth dose vaccine aur immunoglobulin di jaati hai.

Unsafe Dental Procedures

Jo dentist instruments properly sterilize nahi karta, usse hepatitis lag sakta hai. Hamesha reputable dental clinic jaein jahan autoclave sterilization hoti ho.

Hepatitis Ki Symptoms — Kaise Pehchanein?

Hepatitis ki sab se khatarnak baat yeh hai ke shuru mein koi symptoms nahi hote — virus saalon tak chup-chaap liver damage karta rehta hai. Jab symptoms aate hain to aksar liver ko kaafi nuqsaan ho chuka hota hai:

  • Yarkan (Jaundice): Aankhon aur jild ka peela hona — yeh sab se classic sign hai
  • Shadeed thakawat aur kamzori jo aaram karne se bhi theek na ho
  • Pet ke daayein hisse mein dard (liver ki jagah)
  • Bhook na lagna, wajan girna
  • Dark brown pesab (cola-colored urine)
  • Halki ya safaid rang ki potty
  • Ulti aur matli
  • Joints mein dard
  • Pet mein pani bharna (ascites) — yeh advanced stage ki nishani hai

Hepatitis Ka Test Kaise Hota Hai?

Har Pakistani adult ko zindagi mein kam az kam ek baar hepatitis ka test zarur karwana chahiye:

Screening Tests:

  • HBsAg (Hepatitis B Surface Antigen): Hepatitis B ke liye — Rs. 300-500
  • Anti-HCV (Hepatitis C Antibodies): Hepatitis C ke liye — Rs. 300-500
  • Yeh dono tests blood test hain — kisi bhi lab mein ho jate hain

Agar Screening Positive Aaye:

  • PCR Test (Quantitative): Virus ki exact miqdar batata hai — Rs. 3,000-5,000
  • LFTs (Liver Function Tests): Liver kitna damage hua hai — Rs. 800-1,200
  • Fibroscan ya Ultrasound: Liver ki halat dekhne ke liye
  • Genotype Test: Hepatitis C mein virus ka type pata karna (treatment decide karne ke liye)

Government hospitals mein yeh tests free ya bohat saste hain — JPMC Karachi, Mayo Hospital Lahore, PIMS Islamabad mein facilities hain.

Hepatitis C Ka Ilaj — Ab Cure Mumkin Hai!

Pehle hepatitis C ka koi permanent ilaj nahi tha — magar ab DAA (Direct-Acting Antiviral) dawaiyon se 95% se zyada cases cure ho jaate hain. Yeh Pakistan ke liye bohat bari khush-khabri hai.

Treatment:

  • Sofosbuvir + Daclatasvir: Pakistan mein sab se common combination — 12-24 hafton ka course
  • Sofosbuvir + Velpatasvir: Naya combination, sab genotypes pe kaam karta hai
  • Pakistan mein yeh dawaiyan bohat sasti hain — monthly Rs. 3,000-5,000 mein mil jaati hain (America mein $84,000+)
  • Government programs mein free treatment bhi milta hai — Punjab Hepatitis Control Program
  • Course poora karna zaruri hai — beech mein chorna se virus resistant ho sakta hai

Treatment Ke Dauran:

  • Sharab bilkul nahi peeni
  • Doctor ke bataye schedule pe dawai leni hai — dose miss mat karein
  • Regular blood tests karwate rahein — PCR repeat hoga
  • 12 hafte baad PCR negative aaye (SVR) = CURED!

Hepatitis B Ka Ilaj

Hepatitis B ka ilaj thora different hai — yeh har case mein cure nahi hota magar manage ho jata hai:

  • Acute Hepatitis B: 90% adults mein khud theek ho jata hai 6 mahine mein
  • Chronic Hepatitis B: Agar 6 mahine baad bhi HBsAg positive rahe to chronic kehlaata hai
  • Dawaiyan: Tenofovir ya Entecavir — yeh virus ko dabaye rakhti hain, magar lifelong leni par sakti hain
  • Regular monitoring zaruri hai — har 6 mahine LFTs, viral load, aur liver ultrasound
  • Liver cancer ka screening: Chronic Hep B mein har 6 mahine ultrasound aur AFP test karwayein

Hepatitis B Ki Vaccine — Prevention

Hepatitis B ki vaccine sab se effective prevention hai:

  • Pakistan ke EPI program mein free hai — har bachay ko 3 doses milti hain
  • Adults jo vaccine nahi lagwa chuke unhe 3 doses lagwani chahiyen (0, 1, 6 mahine)
  • 95% se zyada protection deti hai — 20+ saal tak immunity rehti hai
  • Healthcare workers, hepatitis patient ke partners, aur dialysis patients ko zarur lagwani chahiye
  • Vaccine bohat safe hai — side effects sirf injection ki jagah pe halka dard

Prevention — Hepatitis Se Kaise Bachein?

  • Apna blade/razor rakhein — barber ko apna blade dein ya use nahi hone dein
  • Disposable syringes hi use karein — reuse bilkul nahi
  • Blood transfusion hamesha screened blood se karwayein
  • Dental clinic mein sterilization ka poochein
  • Hepatitis B vaccine lagwayein
  • Pregnancy mein HBsAg test zarur karwayein
  • Ear/nose piercing sterilized equipment se karwayein
  • Kisi ke toothbrush, nail cutter ya razor share mat karein

Kab Doctor Ko Foran Milein?

  • Aankhon ya jild ka peela hona (yarkan)
  • Pet mein shadeed dard (daayein taraf)
  • Khoon ki ulti ya kaali potty
  • Pet mein pani bharna (pet phoolna)
  • Confusion ya behoshi — liver encephalopathy ki nishani
  • Shadeed thakawat jo hafton se theek na ho

Frequently Asked Questions

Hepatitis B aur C mein kya farq hai?
Hepatitis B DNA virus hai jo blood, sexual contact aur maa se bachay ko lagta hai — iske liye vaccine mojud hai. Hepatitis C RNA virus hai jo zyada tar unsafe needles, barber ke blade aur blood transfusion se phelta hai — iski vaccine abhi tak nahi bani magar ab yeh 95%+ cases mein dawai se cure ho jata hai. Dono liver ko damage karte hain aur bina ilaj ke cirrhosis ya liver cancer ho sakta hai.
Hepatitis C ka ilaj kitne din mein hota hai?
Hepatitis C ka ilaj ab DAA (Direct-Acting Antivirals) dawaiyon se hota hai — course 12-24 hafton (3-6 mahine) ka hota hai. Sofosbuvir aur Daclatasvir combination Pakistan mein sab se zyada use hota hai. Cure rate 95% se zyada hai. Pakistan mein yeh dawaiyan bohat sasti hain — government hospitals mein free bhi milti hain. Course poora karna zaruri hai.
Hepatitis B ki vaccine kab lagwani chahiye?
Hepatitis B ki vaccine Pakistan ke EPI (Expanded Programme on Immunization) mein shamil hai — har bachay ko paidaish ke baad 3 doses milti hain (6, 10, aur 14 hafte pe). Adults jo vaccine nahi lagwa chuke, unhe bhi 3 doses ka course lagwana chahiye — khaaskar healthcare workers, hepatitis patient ke ghar walon, aur multiple partners walon ko. Vaccine bohat safe aur effective hai — 95%+ protection deti hai.
Hepatitis ka test kaise hota hai aur kitne ka hai?
Hepatitis B ka test HBsAg (Hepatitis B Surface Antigen) hai — Rs. 300-500 mein ho jata hai. Hepatitis C ka test Anti-HCV hai — yeh bhi Rs. 300-500 ka hai. Agar yeh positive aayein to PCR test (Rs. 3,000-5,000) karwana parta hai jo virus ki exact quantity batata hai. LFTs (Liver Function Tests) bhi karwayein — Rs. 800-1,200. Government hospitals mein yeh tests free ya bohat saste hain.
Kya hepatitis C wapas ho sakta hai ilaj ke baad?
Hepatitis C ka ilaj complete hone ke baad agar SVR (Sustained Virological Response) aa jaye — yani 12 hafte baad PCR negative ho — to yeh cure maana jata hai aur wapas nahi aata. Magar dubara infection ho sakta hai agar unsafe needles ya contaminated blood se dobara virus lag jaye. Isliye ilaj ke baad bhi prevention zaruri hai — apna blade/razor rakhein, disposable syringes use karein.
