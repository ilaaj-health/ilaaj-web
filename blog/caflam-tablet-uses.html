Caflam Tablet Uses — Dard, Period Cramps Aur Dental Pain Ka Ilaj Pakistan

Mukhtasar — Aham Baatein

  • Caflam ka generic naam diclofenac potassium 50mg hai — ye tez dard ki fast-acting NSAID (dard ki dawa) hai
  • Voltaren se jaldi kaam karta hai — 30 minute mein asar shuru, kyunki potassium salt paani mein jaldi ghulta hai
  • Dental pain, period cramps, renal colic, post-surgery dard, aur sports injuries mein khaas tor par moassir hai
  • Hamesha khaane ke saath lein — khaali pait lene se maeday mein jalan ya ulti ho sakti hai
  • Sirf short-term use ke liye — dil, kidney ya maeday ki bimari mein doctor se zaroor poochein

Caflam Kya Hai?

Caflam Pakistan mein Novartis ki mashhoor dard ki dawa hai jis ka generic naam diclofenac potassium 50mg hai. Ye NSAID (non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug) category mein aati hai — wohi category jis mein Brufen (ibuprofen), Synflex (naproxen) aur Ponstan (mefenamic acid) bhi hain. Lekin Caflam in sab se alag hai kyunki isme diclofenac ka potassium salt istemal hua hai jo paani mein tezi se ghulta hai aur blood mein jaldi pahunch jaata hai — is liye asar sirf 30 minute mein shuru ho jaata hai, jabke aam diclofenac sodium (Voltaren) ko 1-2 ghante lagte hain.

Pakistan mein Caflam dant ke dard, period cramps, sports injuries, post-surgical dard, aur renal colic (gurde ke pathri ka dard) mein bohot istemal hoti hai jahan tez aur jald rahat darkar hoti hai. Ye ek prescription dawa hai lekin Pakistan mein pharmacies par aam tor par milti hai.

Caflam Kaise Kaam Karti Hai?

Caflam COX (cyclooxygenase) enzymes — khaas tor par COX-1 aur COX-2 — ko block karti hai. Yahi enzymes prostaglandins banate hain jo dard, sujan aur bukhar paida karte hain. Prostaglandins kam ho jaayein to dard aur sujan dono dono ghante mein behtar ho jaati hai. Potassium salt hone ki wajah se Caflam maeday mein tezi se ghulti hai aur khoon mein 30 minute mein pahunch jaati hai — ye isi wajah se period cramps, dental pain jaise halat mein zyada kaam aati hai jahan jald rahat chahiye hoti hai.

Caflam Ke Istemal — Pakistan Mein

Dant Ka Dard (Dental Pain)

Pakistan mein dant ke dard ke liye Caflam sab se zyada istemal hone wali dawaoyon mein se ek hai. Dant mein infection, cavity, ya wisdom tooth extraction ke baad ka dard — sab mein 50mg Caflam tez rahat deti hai. Dentist aksar extraction ke baad Caflam 8 ghante ke fark par likhte hain. Khaali pait kabhi nahi leni — hamesha thoda khaana khaane ke baad lein.

Period Cramps (Haiz Ka Dard)

Primary dysmenorrhoea yaani haiz ke dard mein Caflam bohot moassir hai. Prostaglandins uterus ki maansapeshiyon ko contract karate hain — Caflam unhe block karke dard kam karti hai. Period shuru hone se 1-2 din pehle ya shuru hote hi lena sab se behtar kaam karta hai, baad mein lena utna moassir nahi hota. 2-3 din se zyada nahi leni chahiye bina doctor ke mashware ke.

Renal Colic (Gurde Ke Pathri Ka Dard)

Gurde ki pathri ki wajah se hone wala tez colic pain ek emergency hoti hai. Pakistan mein hospitals aur doctors renal colic mein diclofenac injection ya suppository istemal karte hain — oral tablet bhi kaam karta hai lekin injection ya suppository jaldi asar karta hai. Agar aap ko aisa dard ho to foran doctor ke paas jaayen — sirf tablet pe na rahein.

Sports Injuries Aur Musculoskeletal Dard

Muscle strain, ankle sprain, ya sports injuries mein Caflam dard aur sujan dono ko theek karti hai. Aam tor par 3-5 din leni chahiye aur agar sujan zyada ho to barfani sek (ice pack) ke saath mili kar lena faidemand hai. 7 din se zyada lene par doctor ko zaroor dikhaayein.

Aham baatein: Caflam hamesha khaane ke saath ya foran baad lein. Pani ke saath poori goli nigal lein. Dose miss ho to jald se lein lekin agar agla dose ka waqt qareeb ho to chhod dein. Sharaab aur Caflam saath nahi leni chahiye — GI bleeding ka khatra badh jaata hai.

Caflam Ke Side Effects

Aam Side Effects

Sab se aam masla maeda ki takleef hai — ulti, jalan, acidity ya dast. Ye zyada tar tab hota hai jab khaali pait li jaaye. Sar dard, chakkar, aur halki susti bhi ho sakti hai. Ye sab aam tor par halke hote hain aur khaane ke saath lene se bohot kam ho jaate hain.

Sanjeedah Side Effects (Kam Aam Lekin Zaroori Janna)

  • GI Bleeding (Maeday Mein Khoon): Kaala ya khoon wala latrine, ya khoon ki ulti — ye emergency hai — foran hospital jaayen
  • Kidney Pe Asar: Lambe arsa ya zyada dose se gurdon ka function kam ho sakta hai — paani zyada piyein
  • Dil Ka Khatra: Lambe arsa lene se heart attack ya stroke ka khatra thoda barh jaata hai — dil ki bimari mein doctor se poochein
  • Allergy: Jild par daane, chehra sujan, saans mein takleef — foran dawa band karein aur doctor ke paas jaayen

Kise Caflam Nahi Leni Chahiye?

  • Peptic Ulcer Ya Maeday Ki Khoon Aane Ki History: NSAIDs ulcer bigaad sakte hain ya naya ulcer bana sakte hain
  • Aspirin Sensitivity Wala Asthma: Aspirin ya NSAIDs se asthma attack ho sakta hai — is group mein bilkul nahi
  • Dil Ka Dora (Heart Attack/Stroke) Ho Chuka Ho: NSAIDs se dobara khatra barh jaata hai
  • Gurde Ya Jigar Ki Sakht Bimari: Caflam in organs pe boj daalti hai
  • Hamal Ka Teesra Mahina (Third Trimester): Is waqt bilkul mana hai — baby ke dil ko nuqsaan pahuncha sakti hai
  • 12 Saal Se Chhote Bachon Ko: Is umar mein istemal safe nahi mana jaata

Aksar Poochhe Jane Wale Sawal

Caflam aur Voltaren mein kya farq hai?

Dono mein diclofenac hai lekin alag salt: Caflam mein diclofenac potassium hai aur Voltaren mein diclofenac sodium. Potassium salt paani mein jaldi ghulta hai is liye Caflam ka asar 30 minute mein shuru ho jaata hai jabke sodium salt ko 1-2 ghante lagte hain. Tez dard mein Caflam behtar option hai. Dono ki GI risk aur dil par asar ek jaisi hai.

Kya Caflam period ke dard mein kaam karti hai?

Han, Caflam primary dysmenorrhoea mein bohot moassir hai. Ye prostaglandin hormone ko kam karti hai jo uterus ko contract karata hai aur dard deta hai. Period shuru hone se pehle ya shuru hote hi 50mg lena sab se zyada faidemand hota hai. Khaane ke baad lein. Zyada tor par 3-5 din se zyada nahi leni chahiye.

Caflam ke saath khaana khaana zaroori hai kya?

Han, Caflam hamesha khaane ke saath ya khaane ke foran baad lein. Khaali pait lene se maeday mein jalan, ulti, ya acidity ho sakti hai. Agar lambe arsa leni ho to doctor omeprazole bhi likh sakte hain maeday ki hifazat ke liye.

Caflam roz kitni baar le sakte hain?

Baaligon ke liye aam dose 50mg din mein 2 ya 3 baar hai — maximum 150mg roz tak. Hamesha khane ke saath. 5-7 din se zyada lene par doctor se zaroor rayezan karein — lambe arsa NSAIDs dil, kidney aur maeday ke liye nuqsandah ho sakti hain.

Caflam Pakistan mein bina parchi ke milti hai?

Qanoonan Caflam prescription wali dawa hai, lekin kai pharmacies de dete hain. Sirf thodi dair ke tez dard mein ek aadhé tablet se zyada masla nahi, lekin 2-3 din se zyada zaroorat ho, ya aapko heart, kidney ya maeday ki bimari ho to doctor se zaroor milein pehle.
