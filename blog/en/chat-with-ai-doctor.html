Chat Workflow

1. Initial Message

You write: "My head has been hurting for 3 days and I have a slight fever."

AI: "Thank you for reaching out. To understand your situation better, let me ask a few questions:"

2. Structured Questions

The AI asks 5–7 focused questions (one at a time, with quick-select chips):

  • "Which part of your head hurts?" [chips: forehead, back, one side, all over]
  • "How high is the fever?" [chips: 99–100°F, 100–101°F, 101–102°F, 102°F+]
  • "Any other symptoms?" [chips: nausea, sensitivity to light, stiff neck, none]

3. Free Text Option

Every question has an "In your own words" option — for when you need to say more than the chips allow.

4. AI Assessment

After gathering all information, the AI:

  • Explains the most likely diagnosis
  • Provides self-care advice
  • Identifies red flags
  • Suggests a doctor consultation option

5. Doctor Verification (Optional)

If you need a verified prescription, upgrade with one click. After payment, a PMDC doctor reviews your case within 5–10 minutes.

6. Prescription Delivery

After doctor approval, the prescription is delivered in your chat as a PDF.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is chatting with an AI doctor free?

Yes, chatting with Ilaaj AI and receiving an AI symptom assessment is completely free. You can describe your symptoms as many times as you like, receive AI recommendations, get OTC medication suggestions, and access self-care guidance. Only a doctor-verified prescription requires a nominal payment. The free chat still provides significant value.

Does the AI doctor chat in real time?

Yes, Ilaaj AI responds in real time — instant, like WhatsApp. You send a message and receive a response within seconds. During the doctor verification phase, the PMDC doctor reviews your case within 5–10 minutes (slightly longer during busy hours). Available 24/7 — nights, weekends, and holidays.

Is Ilaaj AI available on WhatsApp?

Yes, Ilaaj AI's WhatsApp number is 0324-8199663. You can start a chat directly on WhatsApp. However, for the full feature experience (family profiles, prescription history, voice messages), a registered account at app.ilaaj.ai is better. WhatsApp is accessible for quick queries and first-time users.

Can I send voice messages to the AI doctor?

Yes, voice messages are fully supported in the Ilaaj AI app. If your symptom description is long or typing is difficult, just speak in Roman Urdu, Urdu, or English — the AI understands and performs a structured analysis. This feature is especially helpful for older users and less-literate Pakistanis. The AI responds in text format.

Does my chat history remain private?

Absolutely. All conversations are end-to-end encrypted. Family members (even on shared accounts) cannot access your private chats. Doctors only see relevant chat history. Mental health, gynecology, and men's health topics are treated with extra confidentiality. You can delete your chat history at any time. We follow a HIPAA-style privacy framework.
