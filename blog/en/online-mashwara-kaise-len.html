Why Use Ilaaj AI for a Consultation?

Visiting a clinic in Pakistan can be time-consuming and costly — travel, waiting rooms, and consultation fees all combine to make even a simple health issue difficult. Ilaaj AI provides a doctor-verified prescription from home, in your own language, at an affordable rate.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Create an Account

Go to app.ilaaj.ai and quickly sign up. Provide basic information — name, age, contact number. You only do this once, then can add family members too.

Step 2: Describe Your Symptoms

Just like talking to a friend, type your symptoms — in Roman Urdu, Urdu, or English. Example: "I've had a fever for 2 days and also a cough" — that's enough to get started. If typing is difficult, you can also send a voice message.

Step 3: Answer the AI's Questions

The AI will ask you 5-7 focused questions — with tap-to-select options to make answering easy:

  • How severe are the symptoms
  • When did they start
  • Any associated symptoms
  • Past illnesses or allergies
  • Any medications currently being taken

Step 4: The AI Creates a Draft Prescription

After collecting all the information, the AI creates a structured draft prescription — including possible diagnosis, recommended medications, and lifestyle advice.

Step 5: Payment and Doctor Approval

Make a nominal payment to unlock the verified prescription — much cheaper than a clinic visit. After payment, a licensed PMDC doctor reviews your entire chat history and AI draft, then approves or modifies it.

Try your first online consultation — get a doctor-verified prescription in 15-20 minutes.

Start Your Consultation

Step 6: Receive Your Prescription

The prescription is delivered in your chat with the doctor's digital signature and PMDC number. It is legally valid and can be filled at any pharmacy in Pakistan.

Tips — For the Best Consultation

  • Be specific — instead of "stomach pain," say "pain in the upper right side of my stomach for 2 days"
  • Give a timeline — when the symptom started, how long it's lasted
  • Mention past medical history — allergies, chronic conditions
  • Tell them your current medications — to avoid interactions
  • Attach photos if it's a skin issue — helps with visual assessment

Best Use Cases for Ilaaj AI

  • Fever, cough, cold
  • Stomach pain, acidity
  • Skin issues, allergies
  • Follow-up consultations
  • Medication refills
  • Lab test interpretations

When Ilaaj AI Isn't Enough — What to Do in an Emergency

For chest pain, severe bleeding, breathing difficulty, stroke symptoms, or any life-threatening situation, call 1122 (Rescue) immediately or go to the nearest hospital ER — online consultation is not a replacement for emergency care.

Conclusion

Getting an online consultation via Ilaaj AI is very simple — describe, answer questions, and receive a prescription, all in 15-20 minutes. Feel comfortable using Roman Urdu — a PMDC-verified doctor checks every prescription. Try your first consultation today.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to use Ilaaj AI?

Just a smartphone or computer and an internet connection. Go to app.ilaaj.ai, create a quick account, and start describing your symptoms — no complex setup needed.

Can I use Roman Urdu on Ilaaj AI?

Absolutely, Ilaaj AI is built specifically for Pakistan and naturally understands Roman Urdu, Urdu script, and English — just like talking to a friend.

How long does the entire consultation process take?

On average, everything — describing symptoms, answering the AI's questions, and receiving the final doctor-approved prescription — is completed in 15-20 minutes.

Which pharmacies accept the prescription?

Every prescription is approved by a licensed PMDC doctor, making it legally valid and fillable at any pharmacy in Pakistan — whether a chain or local medical store.

Can I get separate consultations for family members?

Yes, you can add family profiles under one account — for a spouse, children, or parents — with each member's health history maintained separately.
