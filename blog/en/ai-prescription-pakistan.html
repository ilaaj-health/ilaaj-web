What Is an AI Prescription?

An AI prescription works by having artificial intelligence collect your symptoms in a structured way and generate a draft prescription — which a licensed PMDC doctor then reviews and approves. This digital prescription is legally valid for collecting medication from any pharmacy in Pakistan.

Why Pakistan Needs Online Prescriptions

  • Doctor shortage: Especially specialists, and particularly in rural areas
  • Time: Clinic visits are difficult for office workers, mothers, and students
  • Cost: Rs. 1,500–5,000 per consultation is prohibitively expensive
  • Privacy: Essential for mental health, sexual health, and gynecology
  • Refills: Chronic conditions (hypertension, diabetes) require repeated visits
  • Travel: Specialists are usually located only in major cities

The Ilaaj AI Prescription Process

Step 1 — Describe Your Symptoms

Start a chat in Urdu, Roman Urdu, or English. Voice messages are also accepted.

Step 2 — AI Structured Intake (5–7 Questions)

The AI asks focused, relevant questions. No overwhelming forms to fill out.

Step 3 — AI Draft Prescription

The AI generates:

  • Most likely diagnosis
  • Recommended medications, dosage, and duration
  • Lifestyle advice
  • Follow-up timing
  • Red flag warnings

Step 4 — Payment Gate

To unlock the verified prescription. Pakistan-friendly pricing.

Step 5 — Doctor Review

An assigned PMDC doctor:

  • Reviews the full chat history
  • Approves or modifies the AI prescription
  • Adds their PMDC license number and digital signature
  • Adds patient-specific notes

Step 6 — Verified Prescription Delivered

Download the PDF from your chat. Show it at a pharmacy to collect your medication.

Lifestyle Conditions:

What Ilaaj AI Will NOT Prescribe

Some prescriptions require an in-person evaluation:

  • Controlled substances (opioids, long-term benzodiazepines)
  • Chemotherapy
  • Strong sedatives
  • Hormones requiring a physical examination
  • Initial diagnosis of serious conditions (cancer, autoimmune diseases)
  • Surgical interventions

In these cases, an in-person specialist consultation is required.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is an online AI prescription legal in Pakistan?

Yes, Ilaaj AI prescriptions are legally valid in Pakistan. Every prescription is verified by a PMDC (Pakistan Medical Commission) registered doctor with their license number and digital signature. All major pharmacy chains in Pakistan — Dawaai, Servaid, D.Watson, Fazaldin, etc. — accept Ilaaj AI prescriptions. You can use them in digital or printed format.

How quickly do I receive an AI prescription?

You receive a verified prescription on Ilaaj AI within 5–15 minutes. AI structured intake takes 5–7 minutes; doctor review and approval takes 5–10 minutes. During busy hours (late night or weekends) it may take slightly longer, but it is still far faster than a clinic visit (2–4 hours including travel and waiting).

Do pharmacies accept Ilaaj AI prescriptions?

Yes, all major pharmacy chains in Pakistan accept Ilaaj AI prescriptions — Dawaai, Servaid, Saylani, D.Watson, Fazaldin, and local pharmacies. The prescription includes the doctor's full name, qualifications, PMDC license number, digital signature, and prescription ID that the pharmacist can verify. Show it on your phone or print it out.

Do I need a new doctor consultation for refills?

For chronic conditions (hypertension, diabetes, thyroid, cholesterol), Ilaaj AI has an easy refill process. Your previous prescription is available in your chat history. Click the "Refill" button and a doctor briefly reviews and renews it. Refill pricing is significantly reduced compared to an initial consultation. A fresh consultation is required if there are major changes or new symptoms.

What medications will Ilaaj AI not prescribe?

Ilaaj AI does not prescribe: controlled substances (opioids, long-term benzodiazepines like Lexotanil), chemotherapy, strong sedatives, hormones requiring a physical exam, or initial diagnoses of serious conditions (cancer, autoimmune diseases). In these cases, an in-person specialist consultation is required. Ilaaj AI will clearly refer you when an in-person evaluation is needed.
