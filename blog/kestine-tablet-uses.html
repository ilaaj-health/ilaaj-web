Kestine Tablet Uses — Allergy, Naak Behna Aur Hives Ka Ilaj Pakistan

Mukhtasar — Aham Baatein

  • Kestine ka generic naam ebastine 10mg hai — ye second-generation antihistamine hai jo allergy ke symptoms rokti hai
  • Truly non-drowsy hai — cetirizine (Rigix/Softin) ke muqablay mein neend bilkul nahi aati, fexofenadine (Fexet) jaisi
  • Allergic rhinitis (naak behna, cheenkein) aur chronic urticaria (chhapaki) mein bohot moassir hai
  • Dose: 10mg roz ek baar — ek goli poore 24 ghante kaam karti hai
  • Pakistan mein badi pharmacies par available; cetirizine se thodi mehenga lekin alertness-sensitive logon ke liye behtar

Kestine Kya Hai?

Kestine Abbott Pakistan ki antihistamine dawa hai jis ka generic naam ebastine 10mg hai. Ye second-generation H1-receptor antagonist category mein aati hai — wahi category jis mein cetirizine (Rigix, Softin), fexofenadine (Fexet) aur loratadine hain. Lekin ebastine ki khaas baat ye hai ke ye truly non-sedating hai — matlab neend bilkul nahi aati, ya sirf 1-2% logon mein ho sakti hai jo placebo jaisi hai. Pakistan mein Kestine un logon ke liye khaas tor par achi hai jo din mein gaari chalate hain, kaam karte hain ya exam dete hain aur cetirizine se neend aayi ho.

Ebastine ek piperidine-class antihistamine hai jo blood-brain barrier ko minimal cross karti hai — isi wajah se CNS par asar bahut kam hota hai aur neend ka masla nahi hota. Ye allergic rhinitis aur chronic urticaria ke liye WHO aur international guidelines mein recommended hai.

Kestine Kaise Kaam Karti Hai?

Jab aapka jism kisi allergen (dhool, pollen, janwar ke baal) se milta hai to mast cells histamine release karti hain. Histamine naak, aankhon, jild aur airways mein H1 receptors se milke allergy ke symptoms — naak behna, cheenkein, khujli, aankhon mein paani — paida karta hai. Kestine (ebastine) H1 receptors ko block karti hai taake histamine attach na ho sake aur symptoms na ho sakein.

Ebastine ka ahem faida ye hai ke ye blood-brain barrier bohot kam cross karti hai — is liye CNS (dimaag) mein H1 receptors ko block nahi karti aur neend ka asar nahi hota. Ye usi wajah se "truly non-sedating" hai. Cetirizine thoda zyada blood-brain barrier cross karti hai is liye 10-15% logon mein halki neend aa sakti hai — ebastine mein ye masla practically nahi hota.

Kestine Ke Istemal — Pakistan Mein

Allergic Rhinitis (Naak Ki Allergy)

Allergic rhinitis — mausami ya saal bhar — mein Kestine bohot effective hai. Pakistan mein dhool, smog, pollen (khaas tor par spring mein) aur ghar ki gard se naak behna, cheenkein, naak band hona aam masla hai. Kestine 24 ghante tak in symptoms ko control karti hai. Jo log din mein gaari chalate hain, school ya university mein hain, ya office mein kaam karte hain aur cetirizine se neend ka masla hua ho unhe Kestine zyada moafiq aati hai.

Chronic Urticaria (Chhapaki / Sheeri)

Chronic idiopathic urticaria — 6 hafte se zyada rehne wali chhapaki — mein Kestine first-line treatment hai. Jild par surkh, ubhre hue, khaarishtaar daane jo kahin bhi nikal aayein — ebastine in sab ko control karta hai. Agar 10mg se pura fayda na ho to doctor 20mg tak barha sakte hain. Chronic urticaria mein mahino tak lena pad sakta hai — ebastine is ke liye safe hai.

Hay Fever Aur Mausami Allergy

Spring mein pollen ya kisi khaas allergy ke mausam mein roz ek Kestine lena symptoms ko poore din control karta hai. Gaari chalane wale ya pilots ke liye khaas tor par moafiq hai kyunki alertness par koi asar nahi padta.

Kestine Ke Side Effects

Kestine in general bohot safe hai. Neend na aana is ki sab se bari khoobee hai. Kuch logon mein:

  • Munh khushk hona — paani zyada piyein
  • Halka sar dard — aam tor par kuch dinon mein theek ho jaata hai
  • Pet mein halki takleef — bohot kam
  • QT prolongation — 10mg standard dose par practically nahi hota; sirf 20mg+ par ehtiyaat aur cardiac history mein doctor se poochein

Serious side effects — jaise severe allergic reaction — bohot nadir hain. Agar chehra ya gala sujan jaaye ya saans mein mushkil ho to foran emergency mein jaayen.

Aksar Poochhe Jane Wale Sawal

Kestine aur Rigix mein kya farq hai?

Kestine mein ebastine hai jabke Rigix mein cetirizine. Sab se ahem farq neend ka hai: Kestine truly non-sedating hai — neend bilkul nahi aati. Rigix se 10-15% logon ko halki neend aa sakti hai. Dono allergy aur hives mein effective hain. Driver, student ya office worker ke liye Kestine behtar option hai.

Kya Kestine roz lena safe hai?

Han, roz lena safe hai. Chronic urticaria ya perennial allergic rhinitis mein doctors mahino tak lene ki salah dete hain. Ebastine aadat nahi dalti. Lambe arsa lene se pehle doctor se confirm karein.

Kestine ka asar kitni dair mein shuru hota hai?

Kestine ka asar 1 ghante mein shuru ho jaata hai aur poore 24 ghante tak rehta hai. Roz ek hi waqt par lena behtar hai. Asar dheeray shuru hota hai lekin 24 ghante ka consistent cover deta hai.

Kestine Pakistan mein kaahan milti hai?

Kestine Abbott Pakistan ki product hai aur badi pharmacies — Fazal Din's, D.Watson, Shaheen Chemist — par milti hai. Chhooti pharmacies par kam aam hai — wahan cetirizine zyada milti hai. Online pharmacy par bhi order kar sakte hain.

Kya Kestine se neend aati hai?

Nahi — ebastine truly non-sedating hai. Blood-brain barrier bahut kam cross karti hai is liye dimaag par asar nahi. Clinical studies mein sirf 1-2% logon mein neend — jo placebo jaisi hai. Drivers, students, aur daytime workers ke liye ideal antihistamine hai.
