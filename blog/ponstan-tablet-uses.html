Ponstan 250/500 mg — Uses, Dosage, Pakistan Price

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Ponstan = mefenamic acid, NSAID class
  • Period cramps mein bohat effective
  • 250 mg three times daily, max 7 din
  • Khaane ke saath lein — stomach safety
  • Pregnancy 3rd trimester aur kidney issues mein avoid

Ponstan Kya Hai?

Ponstan Pfizer ka brand name hai mefenamic acid ke liye. Yeh NSAID (Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drug) class ki dawai hai jo specifically period cramps ke liye bohat effective hai. Pakistan mein ladies ki sab se common period painkiller.

Ponstan Kis Liye?

1. Period Cramps (Sab Se Common)

Dysmenorrhea mein gold-standard. Period start hone se 1-2 din pehlay shuru karein for best results.

2. Heavy Periods (Menorrhagia)

Bleeding 30-50% kam karta hai inflammation reduce kar ke.

3. Sir Dard / Migraine

Tension headache aur migraine mein.

4. Dant Dard

Especially post-extraction pain.

5. Joint aur Muscle Pain

Mild arthritis, sports injuries.

6. Bukhar

Antipyretic effect bhi hai magar Panadol/Brufen pehlay choice.

7. Inflammation

Soft tissue injuries, sprains.

Period Cramps Specific Tip:

Period start hone se 1-2 din pehlay shuru karein, period ke 2-3 din chalayein. Pain barhne ka wait na karein — preventive use behtar hai.

Side Effects

Common:

  • Pet dard, mitli
  • Acidity, heartburn
  • Loose motion ya constipation
  • Sir dard (ironic)
  • Drowsiness
  • Skin rash

Serious (Rare):

  • Stomach ulcer aur bleeding
  • Kidney damage
  • Liver issues
  • Severe allergic reaction
  • Asthma exacerbation
  • Stevens-Johnson syndrome

Kab Avoid Karein?

  • Pregnancy 3rd trimester — fetal heart issues
  • Active stomach ulcer
  • Severe kidney disease
  • Heart failure
  • Aspirin/NSAID allergy
  • Severe asthma
  • Bleeding disorders
  • 14 saal se kam bachon

  4. Hot bath

  6. Caffeine kam

  8. Severe ho to gynecologist (endometriosis check)

Frequently Asked Questions

Ponstan tablet kis liye use hoti hai?

Ponstan (mefenamic acid) NSAID hai jo specifically period cramps ke liye Pakistan mein most prescribed hai. Bleeding bhi 30-50% kam karta hai (heavy periods mein). Aur uses: sir dard, dant dard, joint pain, muscle pain, mild arthritis, post-surgery pain. Adults aur 14+ saal ke liye, pregnancy 3rd trimester mein avoid.

Ponstan period cramps mein kab leni chahiye?

Best results ke liye period start hone se 1-2 din pehlay Ponstan shuru karein, period ke pehlay 2-3 din chalayein. Starting dose 500 mg, phir 250 mg har 6 ghante baad. Khaane ke saath lein. Pain barhne ka wait na karein — preventive use behtar hai. Pad/cloth, garam pati aur hydration combine karein.

Ponstan ki ek din mein kitni tablet?

Adults ke liye 250-500 mg three times daily. Maximum 1,500 mg per day (3 tablets of 500 mg ya 6 tablets of 250 mg). Period cramps mein starting 500 mg, phir 250 mg har 6 ghante baad. Khaane ke saath lein — stomach safety. Maximum 7 din without doctor consultation.

Ponstan ke side effects kya hain?

Common: pet dard, mitli, acidity, heartburn, loose motion ya constipation, sir dard, drowsiness. Serious (rare): stomach ulcer, kidney damage, liver issues, severe allergic reaction, asthma exacerbation. Risk kam karne ke liye khaane ke saath lein, paani bohat, 7 din se zyada use na karein, kidney/liver issues mein avoid.

Pregnancy mein Ponstan safe hai?

Pregnancy ke 3rd trimester mein BILKUL nahi — fetal heart (ductus closure), kidney damage aur labor delay ka risk. 1st trimester mein bhi avoid karein. 2nd trimester mein sirf doctor mashware se short-term use OK ho sakta hai. Pregnancy mein Panadol (paracetamol) safest painkiller hai. Breastfeeding mein Ponstan generally safe short-term.
