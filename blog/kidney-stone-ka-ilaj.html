Kidney Stone Ka Ilaj — Gurde Ki Pathri Symptoms Aur Treatment Pakistan Mein

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Pakistan mein kidney stones bohat aam hain — garmi aur kam pani peena sab se bari wajah
  • Pathri ka dard waves mein aata hai — kamar se neeche pet/groin tak
  • 5mm se choti pathri aksar khud nikal jaati hai — bohat zyada pani + Tamsulosin
  • Badi pathri ke liye lithotripsy (ESWL), ureteroscopy ya surgery zaruri
  • Roz 2.5-3 litre pani peena sab se best prevention hai
  • Pathri + bukhar = EMERGENCY — foran hospital jaein (infection ka khatra)

Kidney Stone (Gurde Ki Pathri) Kya Hai?

Kidney stone ya gurde ki pathri ek sakht, patthri jaisi cheez hai jo gurde (kidney) mein minerals aur salts ke jam hone se banti hai. Jab yeh pathri gurde se nikal ke ureter (pesab ki nali) mein aati hai, to shadeed dard hota hai — medical terms mein ise renal colic kehte hain.

Pakistan mein kidney stones duniya ke muqable mein kaafi zyada aam hain. Wajah simple hai — garmi zyada, pani kam. Sindh aur Punjab ke garm ilaqon mein har 10 mein se 1-2 log zindagi mein kam az kam ek baar pathri ka shikar hote hain.

Kidney Stone Ki Symptoms

Chhoti pathri aksar bina dard ke pesab mein nikal jaati hai. Magar jab pathri ureter mein phansti hai to zindagi ka sab se shadeed dard ho sakta hai:

  • Shadeed dard kamar ke ek taraf — waves mein aata-jaata hai (colicky pain)
  • Dard neeche pet, groin aur genital area tak phelta hai
  • Pesab mein khoon (pink, laal ya brown pesab)
  • Pesab karte waqt shadeed jalan
  • Bar bar pesab ka ehsaas magar thori thori aati hai
  • Ulti aur matli — dard ki shaddat se
  • Bechain hona — let ke, baith ke koi position aaram nahi deti
  • Kabhi kabhi bukhar aur khanpein — yeh infection ki nishani hai (EMERGENCY)

Pakistan Mein Kidney Stones Kyun Zyada Hain?

  • Extreme garmi: Pasine se pani nikal jata hai aur pesab concentrated ho jati hai — minerals jam hote hain
  • Kam pani peena: Bohat se log roz sirf 4-5 glass pani peete hain — zarurat 10-12 glass ki hai
  • Zyada chai: Pakistan mein roz 6-8 cup chai aam hai — chai mein oxalate pathri banata hai
  • High-protein diet: Gosht, daalein zyada — yeh uric acid barhate hain
  • Namak zyada: Pakistani khaanon mein namak bohat zyada hota hai — calcium excretion barhata hai
  • Genetics: Agar family mein kisi ko pathri hui hai to aap ko bhi hone ka khatra zyada

Kidney Stone Ka Diagnosis

Doctor pathri ki tashkhees ke liye yeh tests karwata hai:

  • Ultrasound (KUB): Sab se pehla test — Rs. 1,000-2,000, painless, kidney mein pathri dikhata hai
  • CT Scan (Non-contrast): Sab se accurate — har size ki pathri dikhata hai, Rs. 5,000-10,000
  • X-ray KUB: Calcium stones dikhata hai magar choti ya uric acid stones miss ho sakti hain
  • Urine Test: Khoon, infection, aur crystals check karta hai
  • Blood Tests: Creatinine (kidney function), uric acid, calcium, phosphorus

Doctor Se Foran Mashwara

Kidney Stone Ka Ilaj — Size Ke Hisab Se

Choti Pathri (5mm se kam):

Yeh aksar khud nikal jaati hai — sirf patience aur pani chahiye:

  • Pani: Roz 3 litre+ pani piyo — pesab saaf aur zyada aani chahiye
  • Tamsulosin (Flomax): Ureter ko relax karta hai — pathri aasani se neeche aati hai
  • Pain management: Diclofenac injection ya tablet sab se effective hai; Brufen bhi kaam karta hai
  • Aksar 1-3 hafte mein nikal jaati hai

Medium Pathri (5-20mm):

  • ESWL (Lithotripsy): Bahar se shock waves se pathri todhna — non-invasive, Rs. 30,000-80,000
  • Ureteroscopy (URS): Peshab ke raaste camera aur laser se pathri todhna — Rs. 50,000-120,000

Badi Pathri (20mm+):

  • PCNL (Percutaneous Nephrolithotomy): Kamar mein chota sa soorakh kar ke pathri nikali jaati hai
  • Open surgery ab bohat rare hai — sirf bohat badi ya complicated cases mein

Pathri Ke Dard Ka Fori Ilaj

Pathri ka dard sab se shadeed dard mein se ek hai — yeh tips foran relief de sakti hain:

  • Diclofenac injection (75mg IM): Sab se effective — 20-30 minute mein relief
  • Brufen 400-600mg: Ghar pe available ho to le lein
  • Buscopan: Spasm kam karta hai
  • Garam pani ki bottle kamar pe rakhein — muscles relax hote hain
  • Pani piyo — dard ke bawajud pani peete rahein
  • Paracetamol akela kafi nahi — pathri ke dard mein NSAIDs zyada effective hain

Kidney Stone Se Bachao — Prevention

Agar ek baar pathri ho chuki hai to 50% chance hai ke 5-10 saal mein dubara hogi. Prevention bohat zaruri hai:

Pani — Sab Se Important:

  • Roz 2.5-3 litre pani piyo — pesab halka peela ya saaf hona chahiye
  • Garmi mein 4 litre+ zaruri hai
  • Subah uth ke 2 glass pani piyo
  • Raat ko bhi 1 glass pani pi ke soyen

Khana Peena:

  • Nimbu pani: Citrate pathri rokta hai — roz 1-2 glass nimbu pani piyo
  • Namak kam: Roz 5 gram (1 chammach) se zyada nahi
  • Gosht moderate: Red meat haftay mein 2-3 baar se zyada nahi
  • Oxalate-rich foods kam: Palak, chocolate, chai, dry fruits — moderate rakhein
  • Calcium normal miqdar mein: Doodh, dahi khayein — calcium supplements zyada na lein
  • Cola/soft drinks avoid: Phosphoric acid pathri bana sakta hai

Kab Emergency Hai?

Yeh situations mein foran hospital jaein — delay jaan-leva ho sakta hai:

  • Pathri + bukhar + khanpein: Yeh obstructive infection hai — kidney damage ho sakta hai hours mein
  • Dono kidneys block: Pesab bilkul band ho jaye — acute kidney failure
  • Ek kidney wale insaan mein pathri: Serious situation
  • Dard pain killers se control na ho
  • Lagatar ultiyan — dawai kha bhi nahi sakte
  • Pregnant khatoon mein pathri ka dard

Frequently Asked Questions

Kidney stone ka dard kaisa hota hai?
Kidney stone ka dard (renal colic) sab se shadeed dard mein se ek hai — log kehte hain yeh delivery ke dard jaisa hai. Yeh kamar ke ek taraf se shuru ho ke neeche pet aur groin tak jata hai. Dard waves mein aata hai — kabhi shadeed, kabhi halka. Saath mein pesab mein khoon, jalan, ulti aur bechain hona aam hai. Dard itna shadeed ho sakta hai ke insaan let bhi nahi sakta.
Choti pathri khud nikal sakti hai?
Haan, 5mm se choti pathri 80% cases mein khud nikal jaati hai agar bohat zyada pani piya jaye (roz 3 litre+). Doctor Tamsulosin (Flomax) dawai dete hain jo ureter ko relax karti hai aur pathri ko neeche aane mein madad karti hai. Aksar 1-3 hafte lagte hain. Magar agar dard control mein na aaye, bukhar ho, ya pesab band ho jaye to surgery ki zarurat pad sakti hai.
Lithotripsy (ESWL) kya hoti hai aur kitni safe hai?
ESWL (Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy) mein bahar se shock waves de ke pathri ko chote tukron mein todh diya jata hai — phir yeh tukray pesab ke raaste khud nikal jaate hain. Yeh non-invasive hai — koi cheera nahi lagta. 5-20mm ki pathriyon ke liye best hai. Success rate 70-80% hai. Side effects mein halka dard, pesab mein khoon (temporary), aur kabhi kabhi tukray phans sakte hain. Pakistan mein Rs. 30,000-80,000 mein ho jaati hai.
Pathri se bachne ke liye kya khayein aur kya na khayein?
Sab se zaruri cheez: roz 2.5-3 litre pani piyo — pesab halka peela ya saaf hona chahiye. Nimbu pani faydemand hai (citrate pathri rokta hai). Zyada namak, gosht, palak, chocolate, chai, aur dry fruits (oxalate walay foods) kam khayein. Calcium walay foods (doodh, dahi) normal miqdar mein lein — calcium supplements se zyada nahi. Soft drinks (cola) avoid karein.
Kidney stone ke saath bukhar ho to kya karein?
Kidney stone ke saath bukhar hona EMERGENCY hai — iska matlab hai ke pesab ki nali mein infection ho gaya hai (obstructive pyelonephritis). Yeh jaan-leva ho sakta hai agar foran ilaj na ho. Hospital mein IV antibiotics aur emergency stent ya nephrostomy lagani parti hai taake pesab ka raasta khula ho. Ghar pe mat baitho — foran emergency jaein. Yeh delay nahi bardasht karta.
