Hydryllin Cough Syrup — Uses, Dose, Bachon Ke Liye Guide

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Hydryllin Pakistan mein sab se aam likhi jaane wali cough syrup hai — diphenhydramine + ammonium chloride
  • Khushk khansi, allergy aur halki naak behne ke liye behtar — neend bhi laati hai
  • Adults: 10 ml har 6 ghante, 6-12 saal: 5 ml — 2 saal se kam ke bachon ko HARGIZ nahi
  • Driving aur exam wale din avoid karein — drowsiness ka khatra
  • 5 din se zyada use na karein, khansi 1 hafte se zyada chale to doctor

Hydryllin Kya Hai?

Hydryllin Pakistan ki sab se mashhoor cough syrups mein se ek hai. Iski 2 main contents hain: diphenhydramine HCl (ek "first-generation" antihistamine jo neend laata hai) aur ammonium chloride (jo balgham (mucus) ko patla karta hai taake aasaani se nikal sake). Yeh combination Pakistan mein khaaskar mausam badalne, smog aur allergy season mein bohat istemaal hoti hai.

Hydryllin Ke Asal Uses

1. Khushk Khansi (Dry Cough)

Khushk khansi jis mein balgham nahi nikalta — galay mein khujli, raat ko khansi se neend kharab — yahan Hydryllin ka sab se behtar role hai. Diphenhydramine cough reflex ko halka karta hai aur neend mein madad deta hai.

2. Allergy Ki Wajah Se Khansi

Garmi aur barsat mein dust aur pollen ki wajah se naak aur galay mein khujli hoti hai, chheenkein lagti hain, aur khansi shuru hoti hai. Hydryllin ka antihistamine effect yahan kaam aata hai.

3. Common Cold Ke Sath Khansi

Mausami nazla zukam ke saath jab khansi shuru hoti hai to Hydryllin nazla bhi sukhati hai aur khansi bhi kam karti hai.

4. Smog Aur Pollution Ka Khansi

Lahore aur Karachi mein October se January tak smog se irritant cough barhta hai. Halki cases mein Hydryllin madadgar hai magar smog ke shadeed cases mein steam aur N95 mask zyada zaruri hain.

5. Allergic Rhinitis aur Hay Fever

Naak behna, chheenkein, aankhon mein khujli — diphenhydramine in sab mein asar daar hai.

Dose Aur Tareeqa

  • Adults (12+): 10 ml (2 chai ke chamach) har 6 ghante baad — max 40 ml/day
  • 6-12 saal bachay: 5 ml har 6 ghante — max 20 ml/day
  • 2-6 saal bachay: Doctor ki advice se 2.5-5 ml har 6 ghante
  • 2 saal se kam: HARGIZ NAHI — FDA/CHMP ki warning
  • Khaane se pehlay ya baad: Dono theek hai, magar pet kharab ho to khaane ke saath
  • Duration: 5 din se zyada na lein bina doctor mashware ke

Abhi Doctor Se Milein

Bachon Mein Safety — Zaruri Maloomat

  • 2 saal se kam: Hargiz nahi — saans rukna aur seizure ka risk
  • 2-6 saal: Sirf doctor ki advice — over-the-counter na khareedein
  • Wazan ke hisaab se: Paediatrician sab se accurate dose deta hai (1.25 mg/kg)
  • Paradoxical reaction: Kuch bachay neend ke bajaye chid-chide aur over-active ho jate hain — yeh dawai band karne ka signal hai
  • Asthma ke bachay: Diphenhydramine balgham ko gaarha bhi kar sakti hai — bachne ki advice
  • Bukhar ke saath khansi: Pehlay doctor — antibiotic ya viral test ki zarurat ho sakti hai

Side Effects

  • Drowsiness: Sab se aam — driving aur school exam wale din avoid
  • Dry mouth aur throat: Paani zyada piyo
  • Dizziness aur dhundla nazar: Buzurgon mein zyada
  • Qabz aur peshab mein dushwari: Khaaskar buzurg mardon (prostate) mein
  • Bachon mein over-activity: Paradoxical excitement
  • Tachycardia (dharkan tez): Rare, magar dil ke marizon ke liye

Kab Hydryllin Na Lein

  • 2 saal se kam bachay (absolute contraindication)
  • Glaucoma ke patients
  • Severe asthma ya COPD jab balgham gaarha ho
  • Prostate enlargement (BPH) wale buzurg
  • Hamla khwateen — pehlay doctor se baat
  • MAOI dawaiyaan le rahe hain (purani depression dawai)
  • Sharab ya neend ki goliyan ke saath (additive sedation)

Better Alternatives Aur Tips

  • Steam inhalation: Garam pani ki bhaap din mein 2-3 baar — bachon aur adults sab ke liye safe
  • Honey (shehd): 1 saal se upar bachon mein 1 chai ka chamach raat ko
  • Hot fluids: Adrak chai, neem garam pani
  • Saline nasal drops: Choti age ke bachon mein safe
  • Humidifier ya geela towel: Sone ke kamre mein, khushk mausam mein
  • Non-sedating antihistamines: Bigger kids aur drivers ke liye Telfast ya Zyrtec behtar

Pakistan Mein Price Aur Availability

  • Hydryllin (GSK): 120 ml — PKR 220-280
  • Hydryllin DM: Dextromethorphan version bhi available — drowsiness kam
  • Generic versions: PKR 150-200, ek hi formula

Yeh syrup pharmacy mein bina prescription milti hai magar self-medicate karna khaaskar bachon ke liye theek nahi. Smog season aur mausam badalte waqt har ghar mein iska istemaal barhta hai — magar 5 din ke andar farq na pare to doctor zaruri.

Conclusion

Hydryllin halki khushk khansi aur allergy ke liye Pakistan mein time-tested syrup hai. Magar yeh "khansi ka safe ghareloo upaay" nahi — bachon mein khaaskar carefully aur doctor ki advice ke saath istemaal karein. Choti khansi se shadeed problem kabhi bhi badh sakti hai. Frequently Asked Questions

Hydryllin syrup kis kaam aati hai?
Hydryllin Pakistan mein sab se zyada likhi jaane wali cough syrups mein se ek hai. Yeh diphenhydramine aur ammonium chloride se bani hai. Use cases: khushk khansi (dry cough), allergy ki wajah se khansi, naak behna, chheenkein, aur halki cough cold ki shuruat. Yeh sedating antihistamine hai jo neend bhi laati hai is liye raat ke waqt khaaskar useful hai.
Hydryllin syrup ki dose kya hai?
Adults (12 saal aur upar): 10 ml har 6 ghante baad, max 4 doses per din. 6-12 saal bachon ke liye: 5 ml har 6 ghante. 2-6 saal bachon ke liye sirf doctor ki advice se 2.5-5 ml. 2 saal se kam bachon ko HARGIZ na dein — FDA aur paediatric guidelines mein clear warning hai. Total 5 din se zyada use na karein.
Hydryllin syrup ke side effects kya hain?
Sab se aam side effect drowsiness (neend ka aana) hai — is liye driving aur machinery operate karne se pehlay na lein. Doosray side effects: dry mouth, dry eyes, qabz, peshab mein dushwari (khaaskar buzurg mardon mein), aur kabhi-kabhi bachon mein ulta asar (chid-chidapan aur over-activity). Asthma ke patients aur glaucoma walon ko ehtiyaat karni chahiye.
Kya Hydryllin syrup 1 saal ke bachay ko de sakte hain?
Nahi, 2 saal se kam ke bachon ko Hydryllin ya koi bhi sedating cough syrup dena khatarnaak hai — FDA aur WHO ki sakht warning hai. Choti umar mein yeh saans ka problem aur seizure paida kar sakti hai. 6 months se 2 saal bachon ki khansi mein gunghuna pani, breastfeeding badhana, saline nasal drops, aur humid kamra (steam) sab se safe hain. Doctor ko foran dikhain agar bukhar, dam ya feed lena band ho.
Hydryllin syrup smog mein khansi ke liye theek hai?
Smog season (October-January) mein Lahore, Karachi aur KP mein khansi badh jati hai magar yeh aksar irritant cough hoti hai. Hydryllin halki allergic khansi mein madadgar hai magar smog ki shadeed khansi mein steam, N95 mask aur inhaler zyada zaruri hain. Agar khansi 1 hafte se zyada chal rahi ho, balgham mein khoon ho, ya saans phule to doctor ko dikhain — TB aur asthma ke chances barh jate hain.
