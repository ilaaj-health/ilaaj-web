Arinac Forte — Uses, Dosage, Pakistan Mein Price

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Arinac Forte = Ibuprofen 400 mg + Pseudoephedrine 60 mg
  • Cold, flu, sinusitis ke saath naak band ke liye
  • Hypertension aur heart disease mein avoid (pseudoephedrine)
  • Khaane ke baad lein — stomach safety
  • 5 din se zyada bina doctor mashware nahi

Arinac Forte Kya Hai?

Arinac Forte Abbott Pakistan ki combination dawai hai jo do active ingredients rakhta hai:

  • Ibuprofen 400 mg — pain killer + anti-inflammatory + bukhar
  • Pseudoephedrine 60 mg — nasal decongestant (naak khulta)

Arinac Forte Kis Liye?

1. Common Cold + Flu

Cold ke saath sir dard, body aches, naak band ho to ek tablet sab kuch handle karti hai.

2. Sinusitis

Sinus infection mein dard, pressure aur congestion — bohat effective.

3. Allergic Rhinitis

Mausmi allergy ke saath naak band aur sir dard.

4. Upper Respiratory Infections

Bronchitis ke shuru'aati phase mein.

5. Ear Pressure / Eustachian Tube Dysfunction

Flight travel mein kaan band ho jayein.

Side Effects

Ibuprofen Se:

  • Pet kharab, acidity
  • Stomach ulcer (long-term)
  • Kidney issues

Pseudoephedrine Se:

  • Ghabrahat, restlessness
  • Neend mein dushwari
  • Dil ki dharkan tez
  • BP barhna
  • Munh sukha
  • Dizziness

Kab Avoid Karein?

  • Hypertension (high BP) — pseudoephedrine BP barhata hai
  • Heart disease — angina, arrhythmia
  • Diabetes — uncontrolled
  • Hyperthyroid
  • Glaucoma
  • Prostate enlargement (pesab mein dushwari)
  • Pregnancy — avoid karein
  • Breastfeeding — milk supply kam
  • Bachon (12 saal se kam)
  • Stomach ulcer history
  • Severe kidney disease

Doctor Se Mashwara

Drug Interactions

  • BP medicines: Effectiveness kam (especially beta blockers, ACE inhibitors)
  • MAO inhibitors: BP spike, hypertensive crisis
  • Caffeine: Stimulant effect double
  • Other NSAIDs: Bleeding risk
  • Aspirin: Combination avoid

Tips for Safe Use

  • Khaane ke baad lein
  • Bohat paani piyo
  • Raat ko avoid karein (pseudoephedrine sleep disturb)
  • 5 din se zyada na lein bina doctor
  • Cold ke 7-10 din ke baad bhi naak band ho to bacterial infection

Pakistan Mein Brands aur Alternatives

  • Arinac Forte (Abbott) — original
  • Coldrex
  • Solpadeine Plus
  • Generic ibuprofen + pseudoephedrine combinations

Conclusion

Arinac Forte cold/flu/sinusitis mein convenient combination hai jo pain aur congestion dono target karta hai. Magar pseudoephedrine ki wajah se hypertension, heart issues aur pregnancy mein avoid. 5 din se zyada use bina doctor consultation nahi. Frequently Asked Questions

Arinac Forte kis liye use hoti hai?
Arinac Forte mein ibuprofen 400 mg + pseudoephedrine 60 mg hain — common cold, flu, sinusitis, allergic rhinitis aur upper respiratory infections mein use hoti hai. Pain (sir dard, body aches), bukhar, aur naak band sab ek saath treat karti hai. 12+ saal ke liye, hypertensives aur heart patients ko avoid karna chahiye.
Arinac Forte ki ek din mein kitni tablet?
Adults aur 12+ saal ke liye 1 tablet 3 times daily, maximum 4 tablets per 24 hours. Khaane ke baad lein — stomach safety. 5 din se zyada bina doctor mashware nahi. Bachon (12 saal se kam) ke liye recommended nahi. Raat ko sone ke 4-6 ghante pehlay last dose — pseudoephedrine sleep disturb karta hai.
Arinac Forte hypertension mein safe hai?
Nahi. Pseudoephedrine BP significantly barha sakta hai aur heart rate tez kar sakta hai. Hypertension, heart disease, diabetes, hyperthyroid, glaucoma aur prostate enlargement mein bilkul avoid karein. BP medicines ki effectiveness bhi reduce karta hai. Plain Brufen + saline nasal spray safer alternative hai in patients ke liye.
Arinac Forte ke side effects kya hain?
Ibuprofen se: pet kharab, acidity, stomach ulcer (long-term). Pseudoephedrine se: ghabrahat, restlessness, neend mein dushwari, dil ki dharkan tez, BP barhna, munh sukha, dizziness. Risk kam karne ke liye khaane ke baad lein, paani bohat, raat ko avoid, aur 5 din ke andar use khatm karein.
Pregnancy mein Arinac Forte safe hai?
Bilkul nahi. Ibuprofen 3rd trimester mein fetal heart aur kidney issues, pseudoephedrine pregnancy mein placental blood flow kam karta hai aur birth defects ka risk. Breastfeeding mein bhi avoid — milk supply kam karta hai aur baby ko ghabrahat. Plain paracetamol (Panadol) + saline nasal spray pregnancy ke liye safe options hain.
