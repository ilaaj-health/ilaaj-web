Bawaseer Ka Ilaj — Symptoms, Ghareloo Aur Best Medicine Pakistan

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Bawaseer (hemorrhoids) Pakistan mein bohat aam hai — almost 50% adults ko zindagi mein kabhi na kabhi hota hai
  • Internal bawaseer mein zyadatar dard nahi hota magar chamakdar laal khoon aata hai; external mein dard, khujli aur lump hota hai
  • Ghareloo ilaj: sitz bath 10 minute din mein 2 baar, isabgol/fiber, 3-4 liter paani aur walking
  • Common medicines: Daflon 500 mg, Anusol ointment, Proctosedyl suppository
  • Heavy bleeding, lump jo wapis andar na jaye, ya shadeed dard mein foran doctor se milein

Bawaseer Kya Hai?

Bawaseer (hemorrhoids / piles) anus aur lower rectum mein moti aur sooji huyi naseinh (swollen veins) ko kehte hain. Pakistan mein yeh bohat aam masla hai — andaza hai ke har 2 mein se 1 baligh shakhs ko zindagi mein kabhi na kabhi bawaseer hota hai. Garmi, masaledar khaana, kam paani aur ghanton baithay rehne wali jobs is ko aur barhati hain. Sharm ki wajah se loag aksar baat nahi karte, magar bawaseer ka ilaj seedha aur effective hai.

Bawaseer — Quick Facts
InternalRectum ke andar — dard nahi, chamakdar laal khoon
ExternalAnus ke bahar — dard, khujli, lump mehsoos hota hai
Grade 1Sirf bleeding, koi prolapse nahi
Grade 2Paikhana ke waqt bahar aata hai, khud wapis chala jata hai
Grade 3Bahar aata hai, haath se push karna parta hai
Grade 4Hamesha bahar, wapis nahi jata — surgery zaruri

Bawaseer Ki Wajuhat

  • Qabz (constipation): Sab se badi wajah — sakht potty aur zor lagana naseinh ko phaata hai
  • Ghanton baithay rehna: Office jobs, driving aur toilet pe phone use karna
  • Garmi mein paani kam peena: Khaaskar Karachi aur Lahore ki garmi mein dehydration qabz banata hai
  • Pregnancy: Uterus ka pressure pelvic veins pe parta hai
  • Heavy weight lifting: Gym mein bina technique ke dead-lift karna
  • Kam fiber wali diet: Sirf naan, roti, chawal — kam sabzi aur phal
  • Family history aur bharti umar (40+)

Bawaseer Ki Symptoms

  • Paikhana ke saath ya baad mein chamakdar laal khoon — tissue pe ya toilet bowl mein
  • Anal area mein khujli aur jalan
  • Anus ke bahar lump (ghulti) mehsoos hona — khaaskar baithne ke baad
  • Paikhana ke baad bhi pet bhara mehsoos hona (incomplete evacuation)
  • External bawaseer mein shadeed dard agar khoon jam jaye (thrombosed pile)
  • Mucus discharge aur kapron pe halki staining

Bawaseer Ka Ghareloo Ilaj

1. Warm Sitz Bath

Sab se zyada effective ghareloo remedy. Neem garam pani mein 10-15 minute baithna — din mein 2-3 baar, khaaskar paikhana ke baad. Sojish, khujli aur dard mein fori aaram. Pani mein kuch zaruri nahi.

2. Fiber Barhao (Isabgol / Phal-Sabzi)

Roz raat ko 1-2 chamach isabgol (psyllium) ek glass pani mein ghol kar peena potty narm karta hai. Sath mein keela, papita, palak, methi, chana aur dalia diet mein lazmi shamil karein.

3. Paani — 3 Se 4 Liter Roz

Garmi mein khaaskar — dehydration qabz aur bawaseer dono ko bigarti hai. Subah uthtay hi 2 glass neem garam pani.

4. Masaledar Aur Fried Khaane Se Parhez

Mirch-masala, biryani, fried food, achaar aur chai/coffee bawaseer ki jalan ko barhate hain. 2-3 hafte ke liye band karein.

5. Walking Aur Toilet Mein Kam Waqt

Roz 30 minute walking circulation theek karti hai. Toilet pe 5 minute se zyada na baithein — phone aur kitaab andar mat le jain.

Bawaseer Ki Best Medicines (Pakistan Mein)

Yeh dawai pharmacies pe milti hain magar 1 hafte se zyada bina doctor ke use na karein:

  • Daflon 500 mg: Oral tablet — pehle 4 din mein 6 tablets daily (acute), phir 2 tablets daily — bleeding aur sojish mein effective
  • Anusol ointment/suppository: Local application — khujli aur halki bleeding ke liye
  • Proctosedyl (steroid + anesthetic): Shadeed dard aur sojish mein — 5-7 din se zyada nahi
  • Faktu / Xyloproct: Anesthetic ointments — local numbing
  • Lactulose syrup ya Movicol sachets: Potty narm karne ke liye
  • Isabgol (Metamucil / Konsyl): Roz ki fiber supplement

Abhi Doctor Se Milein

Surgery Aur Procedures

Grade 3-4 ya recurrent bawaseer mein conservative ilaj kafi nahi hota. Common options:

  • Rubber band ligation: Out-patient procedure — pile ki base pe band lagaya jata hai, 7-10 din mein khud girr jata hai. Bohat common aur effective.
  • Sclerotherapy: Injection se pile ko shrink karte hain
  • Infrared coagulation: Heat se shrink karna
  • Hemorrhoidectomy: Surgical removal — grade 4 ke liye, magar recovery lambi hai
  • Stapled hemorrhoidopexy: Naya minimal-invasive option

Pregnancy Mein Bawaseer

Hamla khwateen mein bohat common hai khaaskar 3rd trimester aur delivery ke baad. Safe ilaj: sitz bath, isabgol, paani, walking. Daflon aur kuch ointments doctor ke mashware se safe hain. Khush khabri: delivery ke 4-6 hafton mein zyadatar bawaseer khud theek ho jate hain. Bina doctor poochay koi bhi dawai mat lein pregnancy mein.

Kab Doctor Se Foran Milein?

  • Khoon zyada ya har baar paikhana ke saath aa raha ho
  • Chakkar aana, kamzori — anemia ka shubha
  • Lump bahar aa kar wapis andar nahi ja raha
  • Shadeed dard, bukhar aur sojish (thrombosed pile ya infection)
  • Kaali ya tarry potty — yeh upper GI bleeding hai, bawaseer nahi
  • 50 saal se zyada umar aur naya bleeding (colon cancer screening zaruri)
  • 3 hafte ke ghareloo ilaj ke baad bhi farq na pare

Conclusion

Bawaseer ek aam masla hai aur uska ilaj mojood hai — sharm na karein, baat karein. Zyadatar cases (grade 1-2) sirf fiber, paani aur sitz bath se theek ho jate hain. Magar khoon, dard ya 3 hafte se zyada chalti symptoms ko ignore na karein. Frequently Asked Questions

Bawaseer ki best medicine kaunsi hai Pakistan mein?
Daflon 500 mg din mein 2 baar (oral) bleeding aur sojish kam karta hai. Local relief ke liye Anusol ya Proctosedyl ointment/suppository — khujli, dard aur jalan mein farq daalti hai. Saath mein fiber (Isabgol/psyllium 1 chamach raat ko), 3-4 liter pani aur sitz bath sab se zaruri hain. 1 hafte tak farq na ho ya khoon zyada ho to doctor se milein.
Bawaseer mein khoon aaye to kya karein?
Chamakdar laal khoon paikhana ke saath ya tissue pe nazar aana grade 1-2 bawaseer ki aam nishani hai. Fiber barhain, paani zyada piyo, Daflon shuru karein aur sitz bath lein. Magar agar khoon zyada ho, chakkar aayein, ya kaali potty ho (yeh upper GI bleeding ho sakti hai), to foran doctor ko dikhain — colonoscopy ki zarurat ho sakti hai.
Sitz bath kaise karein bawaseer ke liye?
Ek bare tub ya basin mein neem garam pani (itna garam ke haath bardasht kar le) bhar lein. Us mein 10-15 minute baith jaain taa ke anal area pani mein doob jaye. Yeh din mein 2-3 baar karein — khaaskar paikhana ke baad. Sojish, dard aur khujli mein fori aaram milta hai. Pani mein namak ya betadine zaruri nahi.
Bawaseer ki surgery kab zaruri hoti hai?
Grade 3 aur 4 bawaseer (jab piles bahar nikal aaye aur khud andar na jaye, ya haath se push karna pare), recurrent heavy bleeding, ya 3 mahine ke conservative ilaj ke baad farq na pare — surgery zaruri ho jati hai. Common procedures: rubber band ligation (out-patient), sclerotherapy aur hemorrhoidectomy. Surgeon se consult karein.
Pregnancy mein bawaseer ka ilaj kya hai?
Pregnancy mein bawaseer bohat aam hai — uterus ka pressure aur qabz dono wajah hain. Safest options: sitz bath, fiber diet (sabzi, phal, isabgol), 3 liter pani roz, walking. Daflon aur kuch ointments doctor ke mashware se safe hain magar bina poochay koi dawai na lein. Khush khabri yeh hai ke delivery ke baad zyadatar khud theek ho jate hain.
