Rovista Tablet Uses — Cholesterol Kam Karne Ki Rosuvastatin Pakistan

Mukhtasar — Ahem Baatein
  • Rovista = rosuvastatin calcium, Searle Pakistan — Pakistan ki high-potency statin
  • LDL (bura cholesterol) kam, HDL (acha cholesterol) barhaata hai — atorvastatin se zyada potent
  • Pakistan/Asian patients mein 5mg se shuru karein — hamare genes rosuvastatin zyada sensitively process karte hain
  • Muscle pain + dark urine = emergency — foran band karein aur doctor se milein
  • Pregnancy mein bilkul nahi — FDA category X (fetal harm)

Rovista Kya Hai?

Rovista rosuvastatin calcium ki tablet hai jo Searle Pakistan banaata hai — Pakistan mein 5mg, 10mg aur 20mg forms mein available hai. Rosuvastatin HMG-CoA reductase inhibitor (statin) hai — liver mein cholesterol synthesis rokta hai.

Rosuvastatin Pakistan mein available sabse potent statin hai — milligram per milligram atorvastatin se zyada LDL ghataata hai. Asian patients ke liye specially 5mg starting dose recommend hoti hai — pharmacogenomic studies dikhati hain ke Asian patients rosuvastatin zyada sensitively process karte hain.

Rovista Ke Uses

1. High Cholesterol (Hyperlipidaemia)

LDL (low-density lipoprotein — bura cholesterol) kam karna Rovista ka primary use hai. Pakistan mein high-fat khana aur inactive lifestyle ki wajah se hyperlipidaemia bohot common hai. LDL 50-60% tak kam ho sakti hai high-dose rosuvastatin se.

2. Heart Attack aur Stroke Prevention

Rosuvastatin sirf cholesterol nahi ghataata — arterial plaque stabilize karta hai aur inflammation kam karta hai. High-risk patients mein (diabetes, hypertension, smoking) rosuvastatin heart attack aur stroke ka risk significantly kam karta hai.

3. After Heart Attack / Stroke

Heart attack ke baad high-intensity statin (rosuvastatin 20-40mg) zaroori hai — secondary prevention ke liye. Yeh evidence-based hai aur Pakistan mein routine practice hai.

4. Familial Hypercholesterolaemia

Genetic condition jisme cholesterol bahut zyada hota hai wirasat se — Rovista high dose mein effective hai. Kuch patients mein ezetimibe ya PCSK9 inhibitors bhi add kiye jaate hain.

Emergency Warning: Rovista lene ke baad severe muscle dard, weakness ya dark/cola-colored urine = rhabdomyolysis. Foran band karein aur emergency room jaayein.

Khulaasa

Rovista (rosuvastatin, Searle Pakistan) Pakistan ki high-potency statin hai — cholesterol kam karne aur heart disease rokne ke liye. Asian patients mein 5mg se shuru karein — hamare genetics zyada sensitivity dikhate hain. Lifelong leni hai — band karne se cholesterol wapas aata hai. Aksar Pooche Jane Waale Sawaalat

Rovista aur atorvastatin mein kya farq hai?

Rosuvastatin zyada potent hai — 10mg rosuvastatin ≈ 20mg atorvastatin LDL reduction mein. HDL bhi zyada badhata hai. Same LDL reduction ke liye lower dose chahiye.

Statin se muscle mein dard kyun hoti hai?

Statin myopathy common side effect hai. Severe muscle dard + dark urine = rhabdomyolysis emergency — foran band karein aur doctor se milein.

Kya Rovista lene ke baad cholesterol normal ho jaye to band kar sakte hain?

Nahi — Rovista akeli wajah hai ke cholesterol normal hai. Band karne se wapas barh jaata hai. Lifelong leni hai high-risk patients mein.

Rovista pregnancy mein kyon nahi leni chahiye?

FDA Category X — absolutely contraindicated. Fetal cholesterol synthesis rokti hai, abnormalities aur miscarriage ka risk. Pregnancy plan ho to pehle doctor se band karein.

Rovista kab leni chahiye — subah ya raat ko?

Anytime — rosuvastatin subah ya raat dono theek. Atorvastatin ki tarah sirf raat tak mahduud nahi. Consistency zaroori hai — roz ek waqt pe lein.
