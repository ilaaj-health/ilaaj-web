Breast Lump — Wajah, Tashkhees Aur Pakistan Mein Self-Exam

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Pakistan mein breast cancer rate South Asia mein sab se zyada — har 9 mein 1 aurat
  • ~80% breast lumps benign hote hain — magar har lump doctor ko dikhana zaruri hai
  • Monthly self-exam period ke 5-7 din baad — sab adult aurato ko karna chahiye
  • Sakht, painless, mobile na hone wali lump + nipple change + dimpling = foran screening
  • Pink Ribbon, SKM, Aga Khan, aur government clinics free screening offer karte hain

Pakistan Mein Breast Cancer — Aham Haqaiq

Breast cancer Pakistan mein aurato ki sab se aam cancer hai — aur South Asia mein hamari rate sab se zyada hai (lagbhag har 9 mein 1 aurat ki life mein ho sakta hai). Afsos ki baat yeh hai ke awareness ki kami aur stigma ki wajah se zyada cases stage 3 ya 4 par tashkhees hote hain — jab ilaj mushkil ho jata hai. Magar agar early detect ho jaye to 5-year survival rate 90% se zyada hai. Isi liye monthly self-exam aur warning signs janna har aurat ka haq aur zimmedari hai.

Breast Lump Ki Common Causes (Benign)

1. Fibroadenoma

Young aurato (teenage se 30s) mein sab se common. Smooth, mobile (haath se hilti hai), rubber jaisi gilti. Aksar painless. Hormonal change se grow ya shrink ho sakti hai. Aksar treatment ki zarurat nahi magar ultrasound se confirm karna zaruri hai.

2. Fibrocystic Changes

30-50 saal ki aurato mein common. Multiple choti choti gilti, aksar dono breasts mein, periods se pehlay painful aur swollen mehsoos hoti hain. Hormone-related — period ke baad behtar ho jati hai.

3. Cyst (Pani Wali Gilti)

Smooth, paani ya fluid se bhari, mobile, kabhi painful. Ultrasound se confirm hota hai. Bara cyst doctor needle se khaali kar deta hai (aspiration).

4. Mastitis aur Abscess

Breastfeeding mothers mein infection — red, sakht, painful, bukhar ke saath. Antibiotic aur warm compress se theek hota hai.

Cancer Ka Khatra Kab Barhta Hai?

  • Umar 40 ke baad — barhti umar ke saath risk barhta hai
  • Family history (maa, behen, ya beti mein breast cancer)
  • BRCA1 ya BRCA2 gene mutation
  • Pehla bacha 30 saal ke baad — ya bilkul nahi
  • Breastfeeding nahi karwana
  • Mota-pa, especially menopause ke baad
  • Early periods (under 12 yrs) ya late menopause (after 55 yrs)
  • Hormone replacement therapy (HRT) long-term
  • Smoking aur sharab

WARNING SIGNS — Foran Doctor Se Milein

  • Sakht, irregular, painless lump jo haath se hile nahi (fixed)
  • Skin dimpling — santare ki peel jaisi shakl (medical: peau d'orange)
  • Nipple retraction — nipple andar dhans jaye
  • Nipple discharge — khaaskar bina dabaaye, khoon wali ya saaf paani jaisi
  • Skin red, garam, ya thick ho jaye
  • Underarm (axillary) mein gilti — lymph node swelling
  • Ek breast doosri se bilkul mukhtalif lagne lage shakl ya size mein
  • Pehlay se chali aa rahi lump ka size badalna ya shape bigarna

Abhi Doctor Se Milein

Breast Self-Exam (BSE) — Step By Step

Kab karein: Mahine mein ek baar — periods khatm hone ke 5-7 din baad (jab breasts kam taza aur swollen hote hain). Menopause ke baad calendar ki ek date fix karein (har month ki 1st ya 5th).

Step 1 — Aainay Ke Samne (Shakal Dekhain)

  • Arms apne sides par — donon breasts ki size, shape, symmetry dekhain
  • Arms upar utha kar — koi dimpling, puckering, ya skin change
  • Hands hips par, chest tani hui — shape change check karein
  • Nipples ki position, color, ya direction badli ho to note karein

Step 2 — Letay Hue Ya Shower Mein (Mehsoos Karein)

  • Right haath sar ke neeche, left haath ki 3 ungliyon ke flat pads se right breast ko halki circular motion mein chuayein
  • 3 sections cover karein: collar bone se nipple tak, nipple se bra line tak, aur chest ke center se underarm tak
  • 3 pressure levels: light (skin), medium (middle), firm (deep tissue tak)
  • Phir doosri taraf repeat karein
  • Underarm zaroor check karein — lymph nodes wahin hoti hain
  • Shower mein soapy haath glide karta hai — bohat asaani hoti hai

Note: Self-exam clinical exam ya mammogram ko replace nahi karti — magar yeh aap ko apne body se waqif rakhti hai, taa ke koi naya change foran pakad sakein.

Tashkhees — Pakistan Mein Tests

  1. Clinical Breast Exam (CBE) — Doctor haath se examine karta hai
  2. Breast Ultrasound — 35 saal se kam mein zyada accurate (dense breast tissue)
  3. Mammogram — 35-40+ saal ke baad — X-ray based, calcifications detect karta hai
  4. FNAC ya Core Biopsy — Agar suspicious lump ho to tissue ka chota sample
  5. MRI — Selected cases mein (BRCA carriers, high risk)

Free aur Sasti Screening — Pakistan

  • Pink Ribbon Pakistan — Multiple cities mein free clinical exams aur awareness camps
  • Shaukat Khanum Memorial Cancer Hospital (SKM) — Lahore, Karachi, Peshawar. Hotline 1166 ya skm.org.pk
  • Aga Khan University Hospital — Karachi mein breast screening clinic
  • Government Hospitals — Jinnah Hospital Karachi/Lahore, Civil Hospital, PIMS Islamabad ki breast clinics
  • Sindh Institute of Urology and Transplantation (SIUT) aur Health Department ki monthly camps

Conclusion

Breast lump mile to ghabraana nahi — magar ignore karna sab se bara nuksaan hai. 80% gilti benign hoti hain, magar yeh confirm sirf doctor exam aur imaging se hi hota hai. Monthly self-exam Pakistan ki har baligh aurat ko karna chahiye. Frequently Asked Questions

Kya har breast lump cancer hota hai?
Nahi — research ke mutabiq lagbhag 80% breast lumps benign (non-cancerous) hote hain. Sab se aam wajuhat fibroadenoma (young aurato mein chala, smooth, mobile gilti), fibrocystic changes (multiple, hormonal, periods se pehlay painful), aur simple cysts hain. Magar yeh confirm sirf doctor examination aur ultrasound/mammogram se hi ho sakta hai — har naye lump ko check karwana lazmi hai.
Breast self-exam (BSE) kab aur kaise karein?
Mahine mein ek baar — periods khatm hone ke 5-7 din baad (jab breasts kam taza hote hain). Pre-menopause khwateen periods ke baad, post-menopause aurato calendar ki ek date par. Aainay ke samne arms down, arms up, hands on hips dekhein — koi shakl change ya skin change. Phir shower mein soapy haath se halki circular motion. Donon haath se 3 sections — clavicle se nipple, nipple se bra line, aur underarm tak. Underarm zaroor check karein.
Breast cancer ke warning signs kya hain?
Sakht, irregular, painless gilti jo mobile na ho. Skin pe dimpling (santare ki peel jaisi), nipple andar dhans jaye, nipple se discharge (khaaskar khoon wali), skin red ya tight ho jaye, ek breast doosri se bilkul mukhtalif lagne lage, ya underarm mein gilti — yeh sab signs hain. Pakistan mein late presentation aam hai — koi bhi naya change foran clinical breast exam karwayein.
Pakistan mein breast cancer screening kahan free milti hai?
Pink Ribbon (multiple cities), Shaukat Khanum Memorial Cancer Hospital (Lahore, Karachi, Peshawar), Aga Khan University Hospital, government breast clinics (Jinnah Hospital, Civil Hospital, PIMS), aur Health Department ki monthly screening camps — sab mein free ya kam paisay mein services hoti hain. Hotline 1166 (SKM) bhi useful hai. Self-exam koi replace nahi karta clinical exam aur ultrasound/mammogram ko.
Ultrasound aur mammogram mein kya farq hai — Pakistan mein konsa kab karwana chahiye?
35 saal se kam umar ki aurato mein breasts dense (gher) hote hain — ultrasound zyada accurate hai. 35-40 saal ke baad mammogram zyada effective hai aur small calcifications detect karta hai. Doctor aksar dono combine karte hain agar lump hai. Pakistan mein dono test private aur government hospitals mein available hain. Final diagnosis biopsy se hoti hai agar suspicious lump ho.
