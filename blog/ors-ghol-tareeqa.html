ORS Ghol Ka Sahi Tareeqa — Dehydration Aur Diarrhea Guide

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • ORS diarrhea aur dehydration mein zaroori salt-sugar balance restore karta hai
  • Sahi ratio zaruri — 1 sachet ko exact 1 litre clean water mein mix karein
  • Ghol banane ke 24 ghante ke andar use karein, phir zaya karein
  • Bachon mein dehydration jaldi hoti hai — ORS foran shuru karein
  • Severe dehydration ke signs mein foran hospital jayein
ORS — Quick Facts
Kya haiOral Rehydration Solution — salt aur sugar ka balanced mix
Ratio1 sachet : 1 litre clean/boiled water
Shelf life after mixing24 ghante (fridge mein bhi)
Pakistan mein availabilityHar pharmacy, sasta aur OTC
Best useDiarrhea, ulti, garmi mein dehydration

ORS Kya Hai?

ORS (Oral Rehydration Solution) ek simple magar life-saving mixture hai jo salt aur sugar ka scientifically balanced ratio rakhta hai — diarrhea ya ulti se lost fluids aur electrolytes ko quickly restore karta hai. Pakistan mein yeh har pharmacy aur medical store pe sasta available hai.

ORS Ghol Banane Ka Sahi Tareeqa

  1. 1 litre saaf, boiled (aur thanda kiya hua) pani lein — exact measurement zaruri hai
  2. ORS sachet ka poora powder is pani mein daalein
  3. Achi tarah mix karein jab tak powder pura ghul na jaye
  4. Clean, covered container mein rakhein

BOHAT ZARURI: Kabhi bhi 1 litre se kam ya zyada pani na dalein — galat concentration nuksan deh ho sakti hai, khaskar bachon mein.

ORS Kab Aur Kaise Piyein

  • Diarrhea/ulti ke har episode ke baad — thora thora pi kar continue karein
  • Sip by sip piyein — ek saath bara glass mat piyein, khaskar ulti ho rahi ho to
  • Adults: Har loose motion ke baad 200-400ml
  • Bachon ke liye: Doctor ki guidance ke mutabiq weight-based amount

ORS Ghol Kitni Der Tak Use Kar Sakte Hain?

Banaya hua ORS ghol 24 ghante ke andar use karein — fridge mein rakhne ke bawajood bhi is se zyada rakhna avoid karein, contamination ka khatra hota hai. 24 ghante baad bacha hua ghol zaya kar dein aur fresh banayein.

Bachon Mein Dehydration Ki Alamat

  • Muh khushk, aansu na ana
  • Peshab kam ya na ana (6+ ghante)
  • Sust ya irritable hona
  • Sunken eyes
  • Skin pinch karne pe slowly wapis ana

Doctor Se Mashwara Lein

ORS Ke Sath Kya Karein

  • Khana normal tarike se continue karein (chota bacha breastfeeding continue rakhein)
  • Zinc supplement diarrhea mein doctor ki advice se — recovery time kam karta hai
  • Fatty ya spicy khana avoid karein temporary tor pe

Garmi Ke Mausam Mein ORS

Pakistan ki shadeed garmi mein ORS sirf diarrhea ke liye nahi — heat exhaustion aur excessive pasine se hone wali dehydration mein bhi bohat faida-mand hai. Labourers aur outdoor workers ke liye especially important hai.

EMERGENCY — Foran Hospital Jayein

  • Bacha ORS pi hi nahi pa raha (baar baar ulti)
  • Severe dehydration signs — sunken eyes, no urine, extreme lethargy
  • Khoon wali diarrhea
  • High fever ke saath persistent diarrhea
  • Bacha behosh ho raha ho ya seizures

Conclusion

ORS ek simple, sasta, aur bohat effective solution hai dehydration se bachne ke liye. Sahi ratio aur timing important hai — 1 sachet 1 litre pani mein, aur 24 ghante ke andar use karein. Severe dehydration ke signs mein delay na karein, Ilaaj AI pe doctor se foran mashwara lein.

Frequently Asked Questions

ORS ghol banane ka sahi ratio kya hai?
Har sachet ko exact 1 litre saaf, boiled aur thanda kiya hua pani mein mix karein. Kabhi bhi kam ya zyada pani na dalein — galat concentration nuksan deh ho sakti hai, khaskar bachon mein sodium imbalance ka khatra.
Banaya hua ORS kitne waqt tak use kar sakte hain?
24 ghante ke andar use karein, fridge mein rakhne ke bawajood bhi. 24 ghante baad bacha hua ghol zaya karein aur fresh banayein contamination se bachne ke liye.
Kya ulti mein ORS de sakte hain?
Haan, magar sip by sip dein — ek saath bara amount na dein kyunke isse ulti barh sakti hai. Har 5-10 minute mein thora thora piyein, gradually amount barhayein.
Bachon mein dehydration ki alamat kya hain?
Muh khushk hona, aansu na ana, peshab kam ya na ana, sust ya irritable hona, aur sunken eyes dehydration ki alamat hain. Yeh signs dekhte hi foran ORS shuru karein aur agar severe ho to doctor se milein.
Kya ORS diarrhea ka ilaj hai?
Nahi, ORS diarrhea ko rokta nahi — yeh sirf fluids aur electrolytes ka loss replace karta hai jo dehydration se bachata hai. Diarrhea ki underlying wajah (infection, food poisoning) ka ilaj alag se doctor se discuss karein agar persist kare.
