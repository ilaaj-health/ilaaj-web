Teething — Bachon Ke Daant Nikalne Ka Time Aur Care

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Pehlay daant 4-7 month, saare 20 baby teeth 2.5-3 saal tak complete
  • Symptoms: rir behna, gum khujli, finger chewing, halki irritability
  • Teh dard ki tez bukhar (101°F+), dast ya ulti TEETHING NAHI — infection check karein
  • Safe relief: cold teething ring, gum massage, breastfeeding
  • AVOID: benzocaine teething gels, homeopathic tablets, amber necklaces

Teething Kya Hai Aur Kab Shuru Hoti Hai?

Teething yaani daant nikalna har bachay ki growth ka natural marhala hai. Pakistan mein walidain aksar fikar mein hote hain ke bacha bechain hai, kya yeh teething hai? Daant 4 se 7 month ki umar mein shuru hote hain, magar 3 month ya 12 month tak bhi range normal hai. Sab se pehlay niche ke do front incisors, phir upar ke do, phir lateral incisors aur molars.

Teething Ke Asli Symptoms

Yeh symptoms 3-5 din pehlay shuru hote hain aur daant nikalne ke baad theek ho jate hain:

  • Rir behna (excessive drool): Itna ke bib bhig jaye — sab se aam nishani
  • Gums sooj jana aur halki red: Cheek bhi red ho sakti hai
  • Mun mein cheez daalna: Ungli, khilona, jo bhi mile
  • Halki irritability aur chid-chid: Khaaskar shaam ko
  • Neend mein khalal: Bar bar uthna
  • Bhook thoda kam: Magar doodh peena chodna nahi chahiye
  • Halki bukhar (100°F se kam): Magar yeh debate hai, zyada tar studies kehti hain teething sirf temperature thoda barhati hai

Symptoms Jo Teething Nahi Hain

Yeh bohat important hai — Pakistan mein "daant nikal raha hai" keh kar serious symptoms ignore ho jaate hain. Teething yeh nahi karti:

  • Tez bukhar 101°F (38.3°C) se zyada — yeh infection ki nishani hai
  • Dast ya watery stool — gastroenteritis check karein
  • Ulti (vomiting) — alag wajah search karein
  • Khansi ya nak behna — yeh viral cold hai, teething nahi
  • Persistent rash poore jism pe — allergy ya infection
  • Bohat lethargy, doodh chodna — emergency, foran doctor

Yeh symptoms hone pe doctor ko zarur dikhain. Teething ke saath sath koi aur problem ho sakti hai jo treat karni zaruri hai.

Safe Relief — Kya Dein Bachay Ko

1. Cold Teething Ring

Silicone ya rubber ka teething ring fridge mein 30 minute thanda karein — freezer mein nahi, kyunke jamma hua plastic gums kaat sakta hai. Cold gums ko numb karta hai aur sojish kam karta hai.

2. Saaf Ungli Se Gum Massage

Apne hath achi tarah dho kar, ungli se gently 2 minute gums massage karein. Yeh sab se asaan aur effective tareeqa hai.

3. Soft Chewy Toy

BPA-free silicone teether jo bacha chaba sake. Choking hazard ka khayal rakhein — saare tukde fix hone chahiye.

4. Cold Washcloth

Saaf gele washcloth ko fridge mein 15 minute rakhein, phir bachay ko chabane ke liye dein.

5. Breastfeeding Aur Comfort

Maa ka doodh aur cuddle bachay ko sab se zyada sukoon dete hain. Yeh teething phase ka emotional support hai.

6. Calpol (Sirf Zaroorat Mein)

Agar bacha bohat bechain ho to Calpol syrup doctor ki guidance pe dose ke mutabiq de sakte hain. Brufen 6 month se zyada bachon ke liye. Disprin ya aspirin kabhi na dein.

Abhi Doctor Se Milein

Yeh Cheezein BILKUL Avoid Karein

1. Benzocaine Teething Gels (Bonjela, Mucopain)

US FDA ne 2018 mein 2 saal se kam bachon mein benzocaine ko ban kiya hai — methemoglobinemia naam ka serious aur jaan leva blood disorder ho sakta hai. Pakistan mein yeh gels milte hain magar bachon ko na lagayein.

2. Homeopathic Teething Tablets

FDA warning hai ke kuch homeopathic teething tablets aur gels mein belladonna ki inconsistent quantities thi jo bachon mein seizures aur deaths ka baais bani. Mukammal avoid karein.

3. Amber Teething Necklaces

Pakistan aur internationally bohat popular hain magar AAP aur dentists is se mana karte hain — strangulation aur choking ka serious hazard. Koi scientific evidence nahi ke yeh kaam karte hain.

4. Solid Foods Choking Hazard

Carrot, biscuit ya hard rusks jo tukde mein toot sakein — choking risk. Sirf supervised soft snacks dein agar weaning shuru ho.

5. Adult Painkillers

Disprin (aspirin) Reye's syndrome ka khatra. Nimesulide bachon mein liver damage. Sirf paracetamol/ibuprofen pediatric formulation.

Dental Care Shuru Karein

Pehlay daant ke saath hi dental care ki aadat dalein:

  • Gauze ya soft cloth ko paani mein bhigo kar gums saaf karein, har feed ke baad
  • Pehlay daant nikalne pe silicone finger brush use karein
  • 18 month ke baad baby toothbrush + chawal ke daane jitna fluoride toothpaste
  • Doodh ki bottle mun mein lekar sona bilkul band — "baby bottle tooth decay" ho jata hai
  • Pehla dental visit 1 saal ki umar tak ya pehlay daant ke 6 mahine andar
  • Meetha juice aur cookies kam, paani zyada

Walidain Ke Liye Reassurance

Teething temporary phase hai. Har bacha alag pace pe daant nikalta hai — apne bachay ko cousin ya bhai-behan se compare na karein. Agar 12 month tak koi daant nahi nikla to dentist ya pediatrician se rabta karein, magar yeh bhi mostly normal variation hoti hai. Kuch bachon mein 2 daant ek hi waqt nikalte hain, kuch mein gaps hote hain — sab acceptable.

Conclusion

Teething bachay ki normal development ka hissa hai — care aur sabar chahiye, panic nahi. Cold rings, gum massage, aur breastfeeding safe relief hain. Benzocaine gels aur amber necklaces ka chakar na karein — yeh nuksaan dete hain. Aur sab se zaruri: tez bukhar ya dast ko teething ki ilzaam dekar ignore na karein. Frequently Asked Questions

Bachon ke daant kab nikalna shuru hote hain?
Zyada tar bachon ke daant 4 se 7 month ki umar mein nikalna shuru hote hain. Niche ke do incisor (front teeth) pehlay aate hain, phir upar ke do. Saare 20 baby teeth aam tor pe 2.5 se 3 saal tak complete ho jaate hain. Kuch bachon mein daant 12 month tak nahi nikalte — yeh bhi normal range mein hai, fikr ki baat nahi.
Teething mein bukhar aur dast hota hai?
Yeh aam ghalat fehmi hai. Teething sirf halki bukhar (100°F se kam) wajah ban sakti hai, magar 101°F se zyada bukhar, dast, ya ulti teething ki nishani NAHI hai — yeh kisi infection ki taraf ishara karte hain. Aise mein doctor ko dikhain — teething ko ilzaam de kar bachay ka infection miss na karein.
Teething ki takleef mein kya dein?
Safe options: cold teething ring (fridge mein, freezer mein nahi), saaf ungli se gum massage, soft chewy toy, breastfeeding ya cuddle. Agar bohat bechain ho to Calpol (paracetamol) doctor ki guidance pe de sakte hain. Avoid karein: benzocaine wale teething gels (Bonjela), homeopathic teething tablets, aur amber teething necklaces — yeh sab FDA warnings rakhte hain.
Pehlay daant nikalne ke baad kya safai karein?
Pehlay daant ke saath hi dental care shuru karein. Gaaz ya soft cloth ko paani mein bhigo kar gums aur naye daant saaf karein subah-sham. 18 month ke baad chote bachon ka soft toothbrush + chawal ke daane jitna fluoride toothpaste (rice grain size) use karein. Doodh ki bottle mun mein le kar sona daant kharab kar deta hai — yeh aadat na dalein.
Pehli dental visit kab honi chahiye?
American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry ka rule hai: pehla dental visit pehlay daant nikalne ke 6 mahine ke andar ya 1 saal ki umar tak. Pakistan mein bhi kai pediatric dentists yeh service dete hain. Yeh visit mostly assessment aur parents ki guidance ke liye hoti hai — agay ki cavities aur dental problems ko rokne mein bohat madad karti hai.
