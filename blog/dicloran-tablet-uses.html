Dicloran Tablet Uses — Dard, Arthritis Aur Sujan Ka Ilaj Pakistan

Mukhtasar — Ahem Baatein
  • Dicloran = diclofenac sodium 50mg — Pakistan ki mashoor NSAID dard ki dawa
  • Arthritis, kamar dard, sports injury aur post-operative pain ke liye
  • Hamesha khane ke saath lein — khali pet stomach ulcer ka risk barh jaata hai
  • Heart disease, kidney problem ya peptic ulcer walon ke liye dangerous ho sakti hai
  • Caflam (diclofenac potassium) se slow-acting hai — acute dard mein Caflam better choice

Dicloran Kya Hai?

Dicloran Pakistan mein available diclofenac sodium 50mg ki tablet hai — ek Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drug (NSAID). Yeh prostaglandins ka synthesis rokti hai jo dard, sujan aur bukhaar ka sabab bante hain. Dicloran Pakistan ke sab se zyada prescribed pain killers mein se ek hai.

Diclofenac ki do main forms Pakistan mein milti hain: diclofenac sodium (Dicloran) — jo slow-release form hai — aur diclofenac potassium (Caflam) — jo fast-acting form hai. Dono ek hi drug hain magar alag speeds pe kaam karti hain.

Dicloran Ke Uses

1. Arthritis (Gathiya)

Rheumatoid arthritis aur osteoarthritis (knee, hip, fingers) mein sujan aur dard kam karne ke liye diclofenac first-line treatment hai. Lekin long-term use mein gastric protection (omeprazole) saath lena zaroori hai.

2. Kamar Dard (Back Pain)

Acute lower back pain mein diclofenac effective hai — muscle spasm, disc problem ya ligament injury se hone wali takleef mein kaam aati hai. Commonly 3–5 din ke short course ke liye di jaati hai.

3. Sports Injuries

Mashal (sprain), strain aur sports injuries mein sujan aur dard kam karne ke liye use hoti hai. Tablets ke saath Dicloran gel (topical) bhi available hai jo seedha takleef waali jagah pe lagayi jaati hai.

4. Dental Pain

Dant dard ke liye Pakistan mein commonly diclofenac use hoti hai — especially tooth extraction ke baad ya abscess mein. Antibiotic ke saath di jaati hai.

5. Post-Operative Pain

Operation ke baad dard control ke liye diclofenac effective hai — intravenous ya rectal form se hospital mein, aur oral form ghar pe discharge ke baad.

Side Effects

  • Stomach problems — nausea, ulcer, gastric bleeding (sab se common aur ahem risk)
  • Kidney problems — long-term ya high dose mein kidney damage
  • Heart risk — long-term use mein heart attack aur stroke ka risk barh jaata hai
  • Liver damage — rare magar possible
  • Headache, dizziness
Ahmiyat: Dicloran hamesha khane ke saath lein. Kabhi bhi khali pet nahi. Omeprazole ya antacid saath lena better hai.

Khulaasa

Dicloran (diclofenac sodium 50mg) Pakistan mein dard aur sujan ke liye sab se zyada prescribed NSAID hai. Short-term use mein effective aur safe hai lekin hamesha khane ke saath leni chahiye. Heart, kidney ya stomach problems walon ko doctor se poochhna zaroori hai. Caflam se slow magar longer-acting hai — chronic arthritis ke liye better choice.

Aksar Pooche Jane Waale Sawaalat

Dicloran aur Caflam mein kya farq hai?

Dicloran = diclofenac sodium (slow, 30–60 min). Caflam = diclofenac potassium (fast, 15–20 min). Jaldi dard ke liye Caflam; chronic arthritis ke liye Dicloran.

Kya Dicloran khali pet le sakte hain?

Nahi — hamesha khane ke saath lein. Khali pet ulcer aur gastric bleeding ka risk barh jaata hai.

Dicloran kitne din tak le sakte hain?

Short-term: 3–7 din. 2 hafte se zyada doctor ki supervision ke bagair nahi leni chahiye — kidney aur stomach risk barh jaata hai.

Kya Dicloran aur paracetamol saath le sakte hain?

Haan — yeh safe combination hai. Alag mechanisms se kaam karte hain — saath lene se dono ki dose kam rakh sakte hain.

Dicloran heart patients ke liye safe hai?

Nahi — heart attack ya stroke ke baad bilkul nahi. Hypertension aur kidney disease walon ko bhi احتیاط chahiye. Doctor se poochhein.
