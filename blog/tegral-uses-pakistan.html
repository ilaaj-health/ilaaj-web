Tegral (Carbamazepine) — Uses, Dose, Side Effects Pakistan

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Tegral (carbamazepine) Pakistan ki common neurology dawai hai — mirgi, trigeminal neuralgia
  • Yeh general painkiller NAHI hai — sirf specific nerve dard ke liye
  • Start 200 mg BD, slowly 800-1200 mg/day tak; khaane ke saath
  • Khatarnak rash (SJS) — Pakistan/South Asian mein HLA-B*1502 gene ka risk zyada
  • Hamla mein teratogenic; birth control pills kamzor karta hai — barrier method zaruri
  • Achanak band mat karo — status epilepticus ka khatra; doctor ki nigrani mein taper

Tegral Kya Hai?

Tegral (active ingredient: carbamazepine) Pakistan mein bohat purani aur trusted anti-epileptic / neuropathic pain dawai hai. Yeh dimagh aur nerves mein abnormal electrical signals ko control karti hai — is wajah se mirgi (epilepsy) ke dauray rokti hai aur chehre ke shadeed nerve dard (trigeminal neuralgia) mein farst-line treatment hai. Magar yeh ek "specialist" dawai hai — bina doctor ke prescription ke kabhi shuru nahi karni chahiye, kyun ke side effects sangeen ho sakte hain.

Tegral Ke Uses (Indications)

1. Mirgi (Epilepsy)

Partial seizures aur focal-to-bilateral tonic-clonic seizures ke liye first-line treatment. Generalised tonic-clonic ke liye bhi effective. Absence aur myoclonic seizures mein NAHI use hoti — yeh actually bigaar sakti hai.

2. Trigeminal Neuralgia

Chehre ke ek taraf shadeed, bijli jaisa dard jo kuch second se chand minute tak chalta hai — chabane, baat karne, mooh dhone se trigger. Tegral 70-80% patients mein bohat asardar hai — yeh gold standard ilaj hai.

3. Diabetic Neuropathy

Diabetes ki wajah se paon, pindliyon mein jalan, sui chubhne ka ehsaas, ya numbness. Gabapentin aur Pregabalin (Lyrica) ke baad option hai.

4. Bipolar Disorder Mania

Lithium ya Valproate alternative ke taur pe psychiatry mein use hota hai mood stabilizer ke taur par.

5. Aur Conditions

Post-herpetic neuralgia (shingles ke baad nerve pain), alcohol withdrawal seizures, glossopharyngeal neuralgia.

Tegral Ka Doze (How To Take)

  • Adults (epilepsy): 200 mg BD start, har hafte 200 mg barhao 800-1200 mg/day tak (divided)
  • Trigeminal neuralgia: 100-200 mg BD start, slowly 600-1200 mg/day tak
  • Tegral CR (controlled-release): Roz 1-2 baar — better sleep, kam side effects
  • Bachon mein: 10-20 mg/kg/day divided doses — pediatric neurologist decide karega
  • Khaane ke saath lein — pet ki kharabi kam hoti hai aur absorption better hai
  • Bhool jane par: 4 ghante ke andar yaad aaye to le lo, warna chhod do — double NEVER

Abhi Doctor Se Milein

Side Effects (Aam Aur Khatarnak)

Common (Pehle Haftay Mein)

  • Sustai (drowsiness), nazar dhundli, dohri nazar (diplopia)
  • Chakkar, unsteady gait (ataxia) — chalne mein ladkharana
  • Nausea, ulti, mooh ka swad badalna
  • Sir dard, halki kamzori

Yeh sab 1-2 hafton mein bahtar ho jate hain. Slowly dose barhana isi liye zaruri hai.

Khatarnak / Serious — Foran Doctor Ko Bataein

  • Skin rash — STOP IMMEDIATELY: Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS) aur Toxic Epidermal Necrolysis (TEN) jaan-leva hain. South Asian aur Han Chinese mein HLA-B*1502 gene ki wajah se risk zyada. FDA recommend karta hai Asians mein genetic testing pehlay. KOI bhi rash = foran stop aur ER.
  • Hyponatremia (kam sodium) — confusion, kamzori, dauray
  • Bone marrow suppression — low white cells, low platelets — periodic CBC zaruri
  • Liver damage — peeli aankhein, mitti ka rang potty, periodic LFTs check
  • Cardiac: heart block, slow heart rate — purani umar mein zyada
  • Allergic hepatitis ya pancreatitis (rare)

Pregnancy Aur Birth Control

Tegral pregnancy mein TERATOGENIC hai — neural tube defects (spina bifida), heart defects, cleft palate, aur developmental delay ka risk zyada hai. Agar pregnancy ka plan hai:

  • Neurologist se pehle baat karein — Lamotrigine usually safer hai mirgi mein
  • 5 mg folic acid roz, conception se 3 mahine pehlay aur first trimester
  • Achanak Tegral band NA karein — taper doctor ki nigrani mein
  • Birth control pills Tegral kamzor karta hai — pregnancy hone ka khatra. Condom (barrier method) ya copper IUD use karein

Drug Interactions — Ahem Warning

Tegral hepatic enzyme inducer hai — liver mein bohat si dawaiyon ko jaldi todta hai, unki asar kam karta hai. Bohat zyada interaction wali dawai hai:

  • Birth control pills — kamzor ho jati hain
  • Warfarin — INR change hota hai
  • Statins (cholesterol), oral steroids, Theophylline
  • HIV antiretrovirals, tacrolimus (transplant)
  • Erythromycin, Clarithromycin, Fluconazole — Tegral level barhate hain — toxicity risk
  • Grapefruit juice — avoid, Tegral level barhati hai
  • Alcohol — drowsiness aur seizure risk barhata hai

Har doctor ko aur har pharmacist ko hamesha bataye ke aap Tegral le rahay hain.

Monitoring (Lambay Arsay Ke Use Mein)

  • CBC (complete blood count) — har 3-6 mahine
  • LFTs (liver) — har 3-6 mahine
  • Electrolytes (sodium) — confusion ya kamzori mein
  • Carbamazepine drug level (4-12 mcg/ml therapeutic) — agar control nahi ya toxicity suspect

Stop Karne Ka Tareeqa

Tegral achanak band karna khaaskar mirgi ke patients mein status epilepticus (lambay dauray) ki wajah ban sakta hai — yeh jaan-leva emergency hai. Hamesha doctor ki nigrani mein 4-8 hafton mein taper karein.

Alternatives

  • Oxcarbazepine (Trileptal) — similar mechanism, kam drug interactions, better tolerability
  • Lamotrigine — pregnancy mein safer, mood mein bhi madadgar
  • Gabapentin / Pregabalin (Lyrica) — diabetic neuropathy aur post-herpetic mein
  • Levetiracetam (Keppra) — modern anti-epileptic, kam interactions

Pakistan Mein Availability

Tegral Novartis ka brand hai. 200 mg tabs Rs. 300-500/strip, CR 400 mg Rs. 600-800/strip, syrup bachon ke liye available. Generic carbamazepine bhi milti hai sasti rates pe. Bara medical store ya hospital pharmacy se asaani se mil jati hai — magar prescription ke baghair na khareedein.

Conclusion

Tegral (carbamazepine) Pakistan mein epilepsy aur trigeminal neuralgia ka ek effective ilaj hai, magar yeh "easy" dawai nahi hai — SJS rash ka risk Pakistanis mein khaas hai, pregnancy mein teratogenic hai, aur dozens of drug interactions hain. Sirf neurologist ya experienced GP ki nigrani mein lein, dose khud na badlein, aur koi bhi rash dekhain to foran band kar ke ER jaain. Frequently Asked Questions

Tegral kis bemari ka ilaj hai?
Tegral (carbamazepine) ka istemal hota hai: mirgi (epilepsy) — partial seizures aur tonic-clonic; trigeminal neuralgia — chehre ka shadeed bijli jaisa dard, yeh first-line treatment hai; diabetic neuropathy (peeshab/paon mein jalan); bipolar disorder mania; aur kabhi post-herpetic neuralgia aur alcohol withdrawal. Yeh general painkiller NAHI hai — sirf specific nerve conditions ke liye hai. Bina neurology consultation start mat karein.
Tegral ki sahi dose kya hai?
Adults mein start 200 mg din mein 2 baar (BD), khaane ke saath. Doctor slowly barhata hai 800-1200 mg/day tak, divided doses mein. Tegral CR (controlled-release) 200/400 mg din mein 1-2 baar mein milti hai — better tolerated. Bachon mein dose weight ke hisab se. Khud dose change mat karein — therapeutic drug level (4-12 mcg/ml) check hota hai blood test se. Bhool jaane par next dose chhod do, double mat karo.
Tegral ke side effects kya hain?
Common: drowsiness, dizziness, dohri nazar (diplopia), unsteady gait (ataxia), nausea — yeh hafton mein behtar ho jate hain. SERIOUS: Skin rash — Stevens-Johnson Syndrome (SJS) ka risk South Asian (Pakistan included) mein zyada hai HLA-B*1502 gene ki wajah se. Rash = foran band karo aur ER jao. Aur low sodium (hyponatremia), low blood counts (CBC monitor), liver damage (LFT monitor). Lambay arsay ke ilaj mein 6 mahine baad blood tests zaruri.
Hamil khwateen Tegral le sakti hain?
Tegral teratogenic hai — bachay mein neural tube defects (spina bifida) aur dusri congenital malformations ka risk hai. Hamla ho sakti hai to Tegral start na ho. Agar pregnancy planning hai, neurologist se safer alternative discuss karein (Lamotrigine commonly used). 5 mg folic acid roz, conception se 3 mahine pehlay. Birth control pills bhi Tegral kam asar karta hai — barrier method (condom) zaruri. Achanak band mat karein — mirgi mein status epilepticus ka jaan-leva khatra.
Tegral aur dusri dawaiyon mein interaction?
Tegral hepatic enzyme inducer hai — bohat si dawaiyon ki effectiveness kam kar deta hai. Khaaskar: birth control pills (pregnancy ka khatra, barrier method use karein), Warfarin (blood thinner), statins, oral steroids, kuch antibiotics aur HIV dawai. Aur grapefruit juice Tegral level barhata hai — avoid. Alcohol drowsiness barhata hai. Apne har doctor aur pharmacist ko hamesha bata dein ke aap Tegral le rahe hain — yeh sab se zyada interaction wali dawai mein se hai.
