Osnate-D Tablet Uses — Haddi Mazboot Karne Ki Calcium + D3 Tablet Pakistan

Mukhtasar — Ahem Baatein
  • Osnate-D = calcium carbonate 500mg + vitamin D3 400IU — GSK Pakistan
  • Haddi mazboot karne, osteoporosis aur pregnancy calcium ke liye widely prescribed
  • Hamesha khane ke saath lein — calcium carbonate ko stomach acid chahiye
  • Iron ke saath mat lein — calcium iron absorption 30-40% kam kar deta hai (2 ghante farq karein)
  • Pakistan mein vitamin D ki kami bohot common hai — 400IU maintenance ke liye kaafi, deficiency mein zyada chahiye

Osnate-D Kya Hai?

Osnate-D calcium carbonate 500mg + vitamin D3 (cholecalciferol) 400IU ki combination tablet hai jo GSK (GlaxoSmithKline) Pakistan banaati hai. Pakistan mein haddi kamzori, osteoporosis aur pregnancy supplement ke liye sab se zyada prescribed calcium tablets mein se ek hai.

Calcium aur vitamin D ko milana zaroori hai: akela calcium lene se absorption bahut kam hoti hai — vitamin D3 intestine mein calcium transport proteins banata hai jo calcium ko blood mein jaane dete hain.

Osnate-D Ke Uses

1. Osteoporosis Prevention aur Treatment

Menopause ke baad khawateen mein bone density tezi se ghatti hai — Osnate-D calcium aur D3 provide karta hai jo bones maintain karne ke liye zaroori hain. Bisphosphonate drugs (alendronate, ibandronate) ke saath bhi diya jaata hai.

2. Pregnancy aur Breastfeeding

Pregnancy mein baby ki haddiyan aur daant banane ke liye calcium zaroori hai — agar khaana se kaafi nahi milta to supplement zaruri hai. Breastfeeding mein bhi maternal bone loss rokne ke liye liya jaata hai.

3. Vitamin D Deficiency

Pakistan mein vitamin D ki kami bohot aam hai — 60-70% adults deficient hain. Symptoms: haddi aur muscle pain, thakaawat, depression. D3 deficiency ke saath calcium deficiency bhi common hoti hai — Osnate-D dono treat karta hai.

4. Steroid Use ke Saath

Long-term corticosteroids (prednisolone) calcium absorption ghataate hain aur bones kamzor karti hain. Steroid therapy ke saath Osnate-D routine supplement ke tor pe di jaati hai osteoporosis prevention ke liye.

Khulaasa

Osnate-D (calcium carbonate 500mg + D3 400IU) Pakistan mein haddi health aur pregnancy ke liye GSK ki standard supplement hai. Khane ke saath lena zaroori hai — calcium carbonate ko acid chahiye. Iron ke saath 2 ghante ka gap zaroori hai. Agar vitamin D deficiency zyada ho to sirf Osnate-D ki 400IU kaafi nahi — doctor se D3 level check karaayein aur zyada dose le sakte hain.

Aksar Pooche Jane Waale Sawaalat

Osnate-D khane ke saath leni chahiye ya khali pet?

Khane ke saath — calcium carbonate ko stomach acid chahiye absorption ke liye. Khali pet absorption 30-40% kam ho jaati hai.

Kya Osnate-D iron ke saath le sakte hain?

Nahi — calcium iron absorption rokta hai. Kam se kam 2 ghante ka farq rakhein — subah iron, raat ko Osnate-D.

Osnate-D se constipation kyun hoti hai?

Calcium carbonate ka common side effect hai. Zyada paani piyen, fiber khayen. Severe ho to calcium citrate form mein switch karen — doctor se poochhein.

Pakistan mein D3 ki kami kyun zyada hai?

Pardah, indoor lifestyle, high melanin skin. Studies mein 60-70% adults deficient hain — dhoop hone ke bawajood supplement zaroori hai.

Osnate-D mein D3 400IU kaafi hai?

Maintenance ke liye haan. Deficiency mein nahi — 1000-2000 IU ya doctor ki high dose. D3 blood test (25-OH Vitamin D) se check karaayein.
