Pregnancy ke Pahle Mahine: Kya Expect Karain

Pehla month pregnancy ka sab se important — baby ke main organs is dauran develop hote hain. Magar bohat si khwateen ko abhi pregnancy ka pata bhi nahi hota. Janein week 1-4 ka complete guide.

Pehla Month Kya Hota Hai?

Pregnancy ka calculation last menstrual period (LMP) se shuru hota hai. Iska matlab:

  • Week 1-2: Aap technically pregnant nahi — body ovulation ke liye prepare ho rahi
  • Week 2-3: Conception (jab anda fertilize hota hai)
  • Week 3-4: Implantation (uterus mein attach hona)
  • Week 4 end: Pregnancy test usually positive

Pehlay Mahine Ki Symptoms

Symptoms har woman mein different. Common signs week 4-6 ke around shuru hote hain:

Sab Se Pehlay:
- Periods miss
- Halki implantation bleeding (week 3-4 mein, light spotting)
- Breast tenderness
- Bohat thakawat
- Halki ulti ya mitli
- Pyaas zyada lagna
- Bar bar pesab

Hormonal Changes:
- Mood swings
- Smell sensitivity
- Food cravings ya aversions (kuch khaane se nafrat)
- Halka sir dard
- Constipation
- Halka pet phulna (bloating)

Baby Ka Development — Pehla Month

  • Week 4: Implantation complete, heart beat formation shuru
  • Week 5: Brain, spine, organs develop hone shuru
  • Week 6: Heart beat detect ho sakti
  • Size: Pea seed jitni (week 6 tak)
  • Crucial: Folic acid is dauran neural tube defects rokta

Pregnancy Test — Kab Karein?

  • Periods miss hone ke 1 hafte baad
  • Subah ki pehli pesab mein zyada accurate
  • Test home pregnancy strip se
  • Negative aur period nahi aaya — 1 hafte baad recheck
  • Confirm ke liye gynecologist se ultrasound + beta-HCG blood test (week 6+)

Foran Karne Ke Kaam

  1. Folic acid 400-800 mcg/day start karein — neural tube defects (spina bifida) rokta hai. Pakistan mein common deficiency.

  2. Prenatal vitamins shuru karein — folic acid + iron + calcium + vitamin D

  3. Gynecologist se appointment — week 6-8 mein first ultrasound

  4. Blood tests: CBC, blood group, HBsAg, HIV, syphilis, thyroid, blood sugar

  5. Smoking, alcohol band — completely

  6. Caffeine kam — max 200 mg/day (1-2 cups chai/coffee)

Diet — Foods to Eat

  • Folate-rich: Palak, methi, broccoli, beans, anjeer
  • Protein: Anday, skinless chicken, machli (low mercury), daal
  • Calcium: Doodh, dahi, paneer, palak
  • Iron: Palak, gosht (well-cooked), khajoor, anjeer
  • Vitamin C: Kinno, mosmi, capsicum (iron absorption)
  • Healthy fats: Badam, akhrot, olive oil
  • Whole grains: Atta roti, brown rice, oats

Foods to Avoid

  • Raw/undercooked: Sushi, raw eggs, unpasteurized dairy
  • High mercury: King mackerel, swordfish
  • Soft cheeses: Brie, Camembert (unpasteurized)
  • Liver: High vitamin A
  • Excess caffeine: >200 mg/day
  • Alcohol: Zero tolerance
  • Smoking, paan, gutka: Bilkul nahi
  • Bahar ka raw food: Salads, chutneys

Common Concerns

Morning sickness:
- Empty stomach pe nahi rehna — small frequent meals
- Adrak (ginger) chai
- Vitamin B6
- Crackers, dry toast subah

Constipation:
- Fiber zyada (sabzi, phal)
- 10+ glass pani
- Walking
- Isabgol safe in pregnancy

Acidity:
- Late dinner avoid
- Sone se 3 ghante pehlay
- Antacids (doctor mashware se)

Bleeding:
- Light spotting (implantation) — usually OK
- Heavy/red bleeding — foran doctor
- Cramping ke saath bleeding — emergency

Warning Signs — Foran Doctor

  • Heavy vaginal bleeding
  • Severe pet dard
  • Severe vomiting (fluids nahi rok sakte) — hyperemesis gravidarum
  • Burning urination (UTI)
  • Severe headache
  • Vision changes
  • High fever (>101°F)

Lifestyle — Pehla Month

Karein:
- Roz 30 min walk
- 7-9 ghante neend
- Stress management (namaz, meditation)
- Light yoga (prenatal)
- Family ko involve karein

Avoid:
- Heavy lifting
- Hot tubs/saunas (high temperature)
- Cat litter (toxoplasmosis)
- X-rays bina doctor
- Travel long-distance bina mashware

Conclusion

Pregnancy ka pehla month silently bohat important hota hai — baby ke organs develop hote, magar maa ko aksar pata nahi hota. Frequently Asked Questions

Pregnancy ki pehli alamat?
Periods miss, breast tenderness, thakawat, mitli, bar bar pesab, smell sensitivity. Test missed period ke 1 hafte baad.
Pehlay mahine mein safe medicines?
Paracetamol (Panadol) safe. Brufen, Voltral, Aspirin avoid. Kisi bhi nayi dawai se pehlay doctor.
Folic acid kab shuru karein?
Conception se 1 mahina pehlay ideal. Pregnancy confirm hote hi foran shuru — 400-800 mcg/day.
Pregnancy mein chai pi sakti hain?
1-2 cups (200 mg caffeine) safe. Excess caffeine miscarriage risk barhata hai.
Implantation bleeding kya normal hai?
Light spotting week 3-4 mein normal ho sakti hai. Heavy/red bleeding ya cramping = doctor.
