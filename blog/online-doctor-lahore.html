Online Doctor Lahore: Ghar Baithay Doctor

Lahore Pakistan ka cultural capital hai — magar healthcare access yahan bhi mushkil. Smog season mein bahar nikalna mushkil, traffic ki wajah se clinic visit half-day kha jaata, aur specialist appointments 1-2 hafte ka wait. Online doctor Lahore ne yeh sab badal diya hai.

Lahore Mein Healthcare Reality 2026

  • 13+ million residents
  • Smog season (Oct-Feb): AQI 200-400+ — outdoor exposure dangerous
  • Traffic: Mall Road, Liberty, Garden Town mein peak hours mein 1+ ghante
  • Clinic visit average: 2-4 ghante including travel
  • Specialist consultation: 3,000-7,000 PKR
  • Government hospital OPD: 1-3 ghante wait

Online Doctor Process — 5 Minute

Chahe aap DHA, Model Town, Johar Town, Gulberg, ya Liberty mein hon — process same:

  1. App.ilaaj.ai khole

  3. AI 5-7 questions puchta hai
  4. PMDC doctor verify karta hai — Lahore-licensed physician
  5. Prescription chat mein milti hai — kisi bhi Lahore pharmacy se dawai
