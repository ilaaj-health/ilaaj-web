UTI (Pesab Ki Infection) Ka Ilaj Pakistan

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • UTI = pesab ki nali ka infection. Pakistan ki 50% aurato ko zindagi mein kabhi hota hai
  • Symptoms: pesab mein jalan, frequent urination, lower abdomen pain, foul-smelling urine
  • Lower UTI (bladder) vs Upper UTI (kidney — bukhar + kamar dard) — kidney infection emergency hai
  • Antibiotics zaruri (Nitrofurantoin, Fosfomycin) — bina doctor ke khud na shuru karein
  • Bachao: 8-10 glass pani, intercourse ke baad pesab, wipe front-to-back, cotton underwear

UTI Kya Hai?

UTI (Urinary Tract Infection / pesab ki infection) tab hoti hai jab bacteria pesab ki nali (urethra) se andar ja kar bladder ya kidney mein infection paida kar deta hai. Pakistan mein yeh aurato ka sab se common infection hai — har 2 mein 1 ko zindagi mein kabhi na kabhi UTI hota hai, aur 25% ko bar bar.

UTI ka sab se aam wajah E. coli bacteria hai (80%+ cases) jo normally aant mein rehta hai. Klebsiella aur Staphylococcus saprophyticus bhi causes mein shamil hain.

UTI Ke Symptoms

Lower UTI (Bladder Infection / Cystitis)

  • Pesab mein jalan (dysuria) — sab se classic symptom
  • Bar bar pesab aana magar thori thori matra mein
  • Pesab ki urgency — abhi jana hai feel
  • Lower abdomen mein cramps ya pressure
  • Pesab cloudy, foul-smelling ya khoon ke saath (hematuria)
  • Halka bukhar (kabhi kabhi)

Upper UTI (Pyelonephritis — Kidney Infection)

Yeh SERIOUS hai aur emergency treatment chahiye:

  • Bukhar 102°F+ chills ke saath
  • Kamar dard (flank pain) — ek ya dono taraf
  • Ulti aur nausea
  • Kamzori, dehydration
  • Confusion (especially elderly mein)

UTI Ke Wajuhat Aur Risk Factors

Pakistan mein UTI common honay ki wajuhat:

  • Aurat ki anatomy: urethra chhoti (4 cm vs men ki 20 cm), anus ke qareeb — bacteria asani se pahonchte hain
  • Sexual intercourse — bacteria push hote hain
  • Diabetes — Pakistan ki 25% population diabetic, sugar pesab mein bacteria ke liye perfect environment
  • Dehydration — Pakistan ki garmi mein pani kam peene se pesab concentrated, bacteria flush nahi hote
  • Pesab rok kar rakhna — bacteria multiply hote hain
  • Pregnancy — hormonal changes ureter relax karte hain
  • Menopause — estrogen kam, vaginal flora change
  • Catheter use (hospital admit patients)
  • Kidney stones — obstruction infection asan banata hai

Diagnosis — Tests Kya Hote Hain?

  • Urinalysis (R/E): pesab test — WBCs, RBCs, nitrites, leukocyte esterase check — 15 min mein result. Symptoms + positive test = UTI likely.
  • Urine culture (C/S): GOLD STANDARD — bacteria identify aur antibiotic sensitivity batata hai. 24-48 hrs lagte hain. Yeh batata hai kaunsa antibiotic kaam karega.
  • Ultrasound KUB: recurrent UTI ya kidney involvement ka shubha hota to.
  • Blood tests: kidney infection mein CBC, creatinine.

Doctor Se Milein

UTI Ka Ilaj — Antibiotics

WARNING: UTI antibiotics khud se na lein. Pakistan mein antibiotic resistance bohat barh raha hai. Doctor ki advice par poora course mukammal karein.

Common Antibiotics (Pakistan Mein Available)

  • Nitrofurantoin (Macrobid) 100 mg BD x 5 din — first-line choice, pregnancy mein bhi safe (1st-2nd trimester)
  • Fosfomycin 3 g single dose — convenient, compliance acchi
  • Trimethoprim-Sulfamethoxazole (Septran) DS BD x 3 din — Pakistan mein resistance barh raha hai, culture ke baad use behtar. Pregnancy 3rd trimester avoid.
  • Ciprofloxacin 500 mg BD x 3-5 din — complicated UTI ya pyelonephritis ke liye. Pregnancy mein AVOID.
  • Cephalexin (Keflex) — pregnancy mein safe alternative

Supportive Care

  • Hydration: 8-10 glass pani roz — sab se zaruri (bacteria flush)
  • Paracetamol bukhar aur dard ke liye
  • Hot water bottle lower abdomen pe — cramps ke liye
  • Cranberry juice/extract — prevention ke liye kuch evidence (cure nahi)
  • Spicy, caffeine, alcohol avoid karein (bladder irritate)

Recurrent UTI — Bar Bar UTI Ka Ilaj

Agar saal mein 3+ UTI ho rahi hain (recurrent), to gynae ya urologist se discuss karein:

  • Post-coital antibiotic: intercourse ke baad single dose Nitrofurantoin
  • Low-dose prophylactic antibiotic: 6 mahine ke liye nightly low dose
  • Vaginal estrogen cream: postmenopausal women mein recurrent UTI 50% reduce
  • D-Mannose supplement — some evidence
  • Probiotic vaginal supplements

Pregnancy Mein UTI

Pregnancy mein UTI MUST treat — untreated UTI preterm labor, low birth weight, aur pyelonephritis ka khatra deti hai. Even "asymptomatic bacteriuria" (bina symptoms ke bacteria) ka ilaj zaruri hai. Routine prenatal visits mein urine test isi liye hota hai.

Safe antibiotics: Nitrofurantoin (1st-2nd trimester), Cephalexin, Amoxicillin-clavulanate. Avoid: Ciprofloxacin, Septran (3rd trimester).

UTI Se Bachao — Prevention

  • 8-10 glass pani roz — sab se important
  • Wipe front-to-back — pesab/potty ke baad (urethra to anus direction)
  • Intercourse ke baad pesab karein — bacteria flush out
  • Pesab rok kar mat rakhein — chand ghante se zyada nahi
  • Cotton underwear, tight jeans avoid
  • Scented hygiene products avoid — vaginal flora disturb karte hain
  • Diabetes control — sugar ka level theek rakhein
  • Khaali pesab karein — bladder fully empty

Foran ER Kab Jaein?

  • Bukhar 102°F+ + kamar dard (kidney infection)
  • Pregnancy + UTI symptoms
  • Khoon ki dhaar pesab mein + severe pain
  • Ulti jo nahi ruk rahi, kuch kha pi nahi sakte
  • Confusion, low BP, dizziness (sepsis signs)
  • Diabetic + UTI symptoms (rapid worsening)
  • 48 ghante antibiotics par bhi koi sudhar nahi

Conclusion

UTI Pakistan mein aurato ka sab se common infection hai magar asani se treatable hai agar waqt par diagnose ho. Pesab mein jalan ya bar bar pesab ko ignore na karein — early antibiotics se kidney involvement rok'ne mein madad milti hai. Hydration aur hygiene practices se 50% UTI prevent ho sakti hain. Frequently Asked Questions

UTI ki best dawai kaunsi hai?
Pakistan mein simple lower UTI ke liye Nitrofurantoin (Macrobid) 100 mg din mein 2 baar 5 din safe aur effective hai — pregnancy mein bhi (1st aur 2nd trimester). Fosfomycin 3g ek single dose convenient option hai. TMP-SMX (Septran) bhi use hota hai magar Pakistan mein resistance barh raha hai. SAB SE ZARURI: bina urine culture aur doctor ke antibiotics khud na shuru karein — resistance ka risk.
UTI khud theek ho jata hai ya antibiotics zaruri hain?
Kabhi kabhi bohat halki UTI 3-4 din mein pani zyada peene se theek ho jati hai, magar yeh risky game hai — infection kidney tak chal sakta hai jo serious hai. Behtar hai doctor ko dikhayein, urine test karwayein, aur agar UTI confirm ho to 3-5 din ka antibiotic course poora karein. Pregnancy mein UTI to bilkul ignore na karein.
UTI mein kab emergency room jaana chahiye?
Foran ER jaein agar yeh signs hon: bukhar 102°F se zyada + kamar dard (kidney infection — pyelonephritis), khoon ki dhaar pesab mein + severe pain, ulti jo nahi ruk rahi, pregnancy mein UTI + bukhar, ya confusion/low BP (sepsis ke signs). Yeh complications IV antibiotics aur admission demand kar sakti hain.
UTI bar bar kyun ho jata hai aurat ko?
Aurat ki pesab ki nali (urethra) chhoti hoti hai aur anus ke qareeb — bacteria ko bladder tak pohonchne mein asani. Recurrent UTI (saal mein 3+) ki wajah: sexual intercourse, dehydration, pesab rok kar rakhna, diabetes, menopause (estrogen kam se vaginal tissues thin), spermicides ka use. Roz 8-10 glass pani, intercourse ke baad pesab karna, aur cotton underwear bachao mein madad karte hain.
Pregnancy mein UTI ka ilaj kya hai?
Pregnancy mein UTI bilkul untreated nahi chhodna chahiye — yeh preterm labor aur kidney infection ka risk barhata hai. Safe antibiotics: Nitrofurantoin (1st aur 2nd trimester safe, 3rd mein avoid), Cephalexin (Keflex), Amoxicillin. AVOID: Ciprofloxacin (fetus ko nuksaan), Septran (3rd trimester mein). Hamesha gynae se confirm ke baad medicine lein aur poora course mukammal karein.
