Gala Kharab Ka Ilaj — Wajah, Ghareloo Tareeqay aur Best Medicine

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • 90% sore throat viral hote hain — antibiotics useless
  • Strep throat (bacterial) mein antibiotics zaruri
  • Namak wala garam pani gargle sab se proven home remedy
  • Nigalne mein bohat dushwari ya pus emergency
  • Bachon mein bukhar + sore throat ki test zaruri

Gala Kharab Ki Wajuhat

Gala kharab (sore throat) Pakistan mein mausam badalne pe ya smog ke dino mein bohat aam hai. Wajuhat:

Gala Kharab — Causes
Viral (90%)Common cold, flu, COVID
Bacterial (10%)Strep throat — antibiotics zaruri
AllergySmog, dust, pollen
Acid refluxStomach acid gala mein
Smoke / PollutionSmoking ya passive smoke
Vocal strainBohat awaaz lagana

Symptoms Pehchanein

  • Gala mein dard ya kharosh
  • Nigalne mein dushwari
  • Awaaz baithna ya hoarseness
  • Bukhar (bacterial mein zyada)
  • Tonsils sojay aur red
  • Tonsils pe safed/peela patches (strep)
  • Sir dard, body aches

Viral vs Bacterial Sore Throat

Viral (Antibiotics Useless):

  • Khansi, zukam, naak band
  • Halka ya darmiyana bukhar
  • Awaaz baithna
  • Aankhon mein paani
  • 3-7 din mein khud theek

Strep (Bacterial — Antibiotics Zaruri):

  • Achanak shadeed gala dard
  • Bukhar 101°F+
  • Tonsils par safed pus patches
  • Khansi nahi hoti
  • Gardan ki glands sojayi (lymph nodes)
  • Sir dard, pet dard (bachon mein)

Confirmation: Throat swab test ya rapid strep test se. Doctor symptoms se bhi kaafi hadd tak guess kar sakta hai.

Gala Kharab Ka Ghareloo Ilaj

1. Namak Wala Garam Pani Gargle

Sab se proven home remedy. 1 cup garam pani + 1/2 chamach namak. Din mein 3-4 baar 30-60 second gargle.

2. Shahed aur Adrak

1 chamach shahed + nimbu ka ras + adrak peess kar garam pani mein. Din mein 2-3 baar.

3. Garam Yakhni / Soup

Chicken yakhni gala soothe karti hai aur hydration bhi.

4. Beverage Hot/Cold

Garam green tea ya thanda ice cream — dono madadgar (preference pe).

5. Steam Inhalation

10 minute, 2 baar din mein. Rumal lagayein sir pe.

6. Hydration

Bohat zyada paani — gala moist rakhne ke liye.

7. Humidifier ya Geela Kapra

Kamre mein humidity rakhein, khaaskar AC ke saath.

Best OTC Medicine

  • Lozenges: Strepsils, Vicks Throat Drops, Difflam — instant soothing
  • Anti-inflammatory: Brufen 400 mg ya Panadol 500 mg — dard aur bukhar mein
  • Throat sprays: Difflam spray (benzydamine) — strong relief
  • Antihistamine: Allergy ki wajah ho to Telfast, Zyrtec
  • Ant-acid: GERD ki wajah ho to Risek 20 mg ya Pantop

Doctor Se Milein

Strep Throat Mein Antibiotics

Strep throat confirmed ho to:

  • Penicillin V (Calvepen): First line, 10 din ka course
  • Amoxicillin (Amoxil): 7-10 din
  • Augmentin 625 mg: Resistant cases mein
  • Erythromycin / Azithromycin: Penicillin allergy mein

Important: Antibiotic course pura karein, beech mein chodne se rheumatic fever ka khatra (heart valve damage). Pakistan mein rheumatic heart disease ki badi wajah untreated strep hai.

Tonsillitis

Tonsils mein recurrent infection (saal mein 5-7 baar) ho to tonsillectomy (tonsils nikalna) ki zarurat ho sakti hai. ENT specialist se evaluation.

Pakistan Mein Special Considerations

  • Smog season: N95 mask aur indoor air purifier
  • Roza: Hydration ka special khayal sehri/iftar mein
  • Singers/teachers: Vocal rest aur warm-up exercises
  • Acid reflux culture: Late dinner, masaledar khaane se gala issues

Gala Kharab Se Bachao

  • Roz hath dhona
  • Sick logon se distance
  • Smoking aur passive smoke avoid
  • Smog mein N95 mask
  • Hydration
  • Voice rest jab gala kharab ho
  • AC se direct hawa avoid
  • Toothbrush har 3 mahine change karein

Kab Foran Doctor Ko?

  • Sans lene ya nigalne mein dushwari
  • Munh kholne mein dushwari (tongue locked)
  • Awaaz "muffled" ho gayi (peritonsillar abscess)
  • Bukhar 101°F+ aur tonsils pe pus
  • Lal khansi ya khoon thookna
  • 3 din se zyada shadeed dard
  • 1 hafta se zyada koi sore throat
  • Dane jism pe (scarlet fever)

Conclusion

Zyadatar sore throat viral hota hai aur 5-7 din mein khud theek ho jata hai. Antibiotics sirf strep throat mein zaruri hain. Pakistan mein untreated strep rheumatic heart disease ka cause banta hai — isliye correct diagnosis zaruri hai. Frequently Asked Questions

Gala kharab ka best ilaj kya hai?

Sab se proven: namak wala garam pani gargle din mein 3-4 baar, garam fluids (chai, soup, shahed-adrak), aur Strepsils/Difflam lozenges. Dard ke liye Brufen 400 mg ya Panadol 500 mg. 90% sore throat viral hote hain aur 5-7 din mein khud theek. Antibiotics sirf strep throat mein zaruri hain — doctor se confirm karwana zaruri.

Sore throat mein antibiotics zaruri hain?

Sirf bacterial (strep throat) mein. Signs: achanak shadeed dard, bukhar 101°F+, tonsils pe pus patches, khansi NAHI hoti, gardan ki glands sojayi. Viral sore throat mein antibiotics useless hain aur side effects deti hain. Doctor throat swab ya rapid test se confirm karta hai. Strep ho to Calvepen ya Amoxil ka 10 din ka course zaruri.

Gala bethna kyun hota hai?

Awaaz baithne ki common wajuhat: viral infection (laryngitis), bohat awaaz lagana, smoking, acid reflux, allergies, ya rare cases mein vocal cord nodules. 1 hafte mein theek ho jana chahiye. Voice rest, hydration, aur smoking band karna sab se important. 3 hafte se zyada awaaz baithi rahe to ENT specialist se milein — vocal cord exam zaruri.

Bachon ke gala kharab ka ilaj?

Bachon mein bukhar + gala kharab ka combination strep ho sakta hai — doctor zaruri. Calpol bukhar aur dard ke liye safe. 6+ saal bachon ko salt water gargle, lozenges, garam shahed (1 saal+) safe. Khaane mein soft foods (yakhni, dahi, ice cream). Bohat fluids. Pus patches ya nigalne mein bohat dushwari ho to foran doctor.

Tonsils sojay hue hain kya karein?

Pehlay namak wala gargle, lozenges aur painkillers try karein. Tonsils pe safed pus patches, bukhar, ya breathing problem ho to bacterial infection — antibiotics zaruri. Recurrent tonsillitis (saal mein 5-7 baar) mein tonsillectomy ki zarurat ho sakti hai. ENT specialist se evaluation. Hot ya cold beverages dono soothing hain — preference pe.
