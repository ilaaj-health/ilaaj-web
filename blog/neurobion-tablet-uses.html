Neurobion Tablet Uses — Nerve Pain, Haath Paon Soun Hona Pakistan

Mukhtasar — Ahem Baatein
  • Neurobion = B1 100mg + B6 200mg + B12 200mcg — Merck Pakistan
  • Neuromet se farq: B6 200mg hai (Neuromet mein 100mg) — zyada B6 long-term risk rakhta hai
  • Diabetic neuropathy, haath paon ka soun hona, metformin patients ke liye
  • Severe deficiency mein injection zyada effective — tablet maintenance ke liye
  • Metformin lene waale diabetes patients mein B12 check aur supplement zaroori

Neurobion Kya Hai?

Neurobion Merck Pakistan ki mashoor neurological B vitamin complex hai: B1 (thiamine) 100mg + B6 (pyridoxine) 200mg + B12 (cyanocobalamin) 200mcg. Pakistan mein nerve pain aur neuropathy ke liye sab se zyada prescribed B complex tablets mein se ek hai.

Key differentiator: Neurobion mein B6 200mg hai — Neuromet se double. Yeh kuch conditions mein extra benefit deta hai magar long-term high B6 dose neuropathy risk bhi rakhti hai — doctor ki guidance zaroori hai.

Neurobion Ke Uses

1. Diabetic Neuropathy

Diabetes mein nerves ko nuksaan hota hai — haath paon soun hona, jalan, dard. B vitamins especially B12 nerve function maintain karne ke liye zaroori hain. Long-term metformin users mein B12 deficiency common hai — Neurobion prophylaxis ke tor par di jaati hai.

2. Peripheral Neuropathy

Alcohol, kidney disease ya B vitamin deficiency se related peripheral neuropathy mein Neurobion beneficial hai — nerves ki repair aur maintenance ke liye B1+B6+B12 teeno zaroori hain.

3. Kamar Dard Ka Nerve Component

Sciatica ya radiculopathy mein (jab nerve compression se dard hota hai) NSAIDs ke saath Neurobion add kiya jaata hai. Nerve repair ke liye B vitamins adjunct therapy hai.

4. Metformin Users

Metformin long-term lene waale Pakistan mein bohot hain. B12 absorption 20-30% kam hoti hai metformin se — annual check aur Neurobion/Methycobal routine supplement zaroori hai.

Aksar Pooche Jane Waale Sawaalat

Neurobion aur Neuromet mein kya farq hai?

Neurobion mein B6 200mg (Neuromet mein 100mg). B12 dono mein 200mcg. Long-term 200mg B6 roz sensory neuropathy risk — doctor supervision zaroori.

Neurobion tablet ya injection — kaunsa behtar hai?

Severe deficiency mein injection — GI absorption bypass hoti hai. Mild deficiency/maintenance ke liye tablet kaafi. Severe symptoms mein doctor IM injection shuru karte hain.

Neurobion se kamar dard theek hota hai?

Nerve component (sciatica) mein partial madad. Mechanical back pain mein NSAIDs + physiotherapy zyada effective hain.

Kya Neurobion pregnancy mein safe hai?

B1 aur B12 safe hain. B6 200mg long-term ke baare mein doctor se confirm karein — standard pregnancy supplement (folic acid + iron) zyada appropriate hai.

Neurobion diabetes patients ke liye zaroori hai?

Haan — khaas kar metformin users. Metformin B12 absorption 20-30% ghata deta hai. Annual B12 check aur supplement routine zaroori hai.
