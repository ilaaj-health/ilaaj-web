Prostate Ka Ilaj — BPH Symptoms Aur Mukammal Treatment Guide

TL;DR — Key Takeaways

  • Cancer nahi hai — magar symptoms similar ho sakte hain, test zaruri
  • Mild cases lifestyle changes aur dawaiyon se manage hote hain
  • Severe cases mein surgery ki zarurat par sakti hai

Prostate Aur BPH Kya Hai?

Prostate ek gland hai jo bladder ke neeche hota hai aur umar ke saath barh sakta hai — is condition ko BPH (Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia) kehte hain. Yeh cancer nahi hai, magar barha hua prostate urethra ko press karta hai aur peshab mein masla create karta hai.

Alamat

  • Peshab karne mein weak stream
  • Baar baar peshab ana, khaskar raat ko
  • Peshab shuru karne mein mushkil
  • Peshab poori tarah khali na hona ka ehsaas
  • Peshab rukna aur phir se shuru karna
  • Achanak zyada urgency mehsoos hona
  • Dribbling — peshab ke baad thora tapakna

Wajuhat

  • Umar — 50+ mein bohat common, 80 saal tak 90% mardon mein kuch degree
  • Hormonal changes — umar ke saath testosterone/estrogen balance shift
  • Family history
  • Obesity aur sedentary lifestyle

BPH Aur Prostate Cancer Mein Farq

BPH aur prostate cancer ke symptoms similar ho sakte hain, magar BPH cancerous nahi hai. Phir bhi, doctor tests karta hai (PSA blood test) taake dono ko differentiate kar sake — is liye symptoms ko ignore na karein aur proper evaluation karwayein.

Ilaj

Mild Symptoms — Lifestyle Changes:

  • Raat ko sone se pehle liquids kam pina
  • Caffeine aur alcohol kam karein — bladder ko irritate karte hain
  • Regular exercise
  • Bladder training — regular intervals pe peshab jana

Dawaiyan:

  • Alpha-blockers (Tamsulosin) — prostate aur bladder muscles ko relax karte hain, fori relief
  • 5-alpha reductase inhibitors (Finasteride) — prostate ka size kam karte hain, asar mahino mein
  • Combination therapy — dono types ek saath severe cases mein

Surgery (Severe Cases Mein):

  • TURP (Transurethral Resection of the Prostate) — sab se common surgical option
  • Laser therapy — modern, kam invasive alternative

Prostate Screening — Kab Karwana Chahiye?

  • 50+ umar — routine PSA test consider karein doctor se discuss kar ke
  • Family history walon mein — 40-45 saal se hi screening consider karein
  • Symptoms nazar aayein to fori consult karein, wait na karein

Yeh Symptoms Emergency Hain

  • Peshab bilkul nahi a raha (urinary retention)
  • Peshab mein khoon
  • Shadeed dard peshab karte waqt
  • Bukhar ke saath peshab ki takleef (infection ka khatra)

Kab Doctor Ko Milein?

  • Peshab pattern mein koi bhi change
  • Raat ko baar baar uthna neend disturb kar raha ho
  • Weak stream ya incomplete emptying ka ehsaas
  • 50+ umar mein routine checkup

Conclusion

Prostate enlargement (BPH) 50+ umar ke mardon mein ek bohat common aur manageable condition hai. Cancer se different hone ke bawajood proper evaluation zaruri hai. Frequently Asked Questions

Kya BPH prostate cancer hai?
Nahi, BPH (Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia) cancer nahi hai — yeh sirf prostate ka non-cancerous enlargement hai. Magar symptoms similar ho sakte hain, is liye doctor PSA test se dono ko differentiate karta hai.
Prostate problems ki pehli alamat kya hoti hai?
Zyada tar cases mein peshab ki takleef pehli alamat hoti hai — weak stream, baar baar peshab ana (khaskar raat ko), aur peshab shuru karne mein mushkil. Yeh gradually barhti hai umar ke saath.
Prostate enlargement ka ilaj kya hai?
Mild cases mein lifestyle changes (kam liquids raat ko, caffeine kam karna) kaafi hote hain. Moderate cases mein alpha-blockers ya 5-alpha reductase inhibitors jaisi dawaiyan di jati hain. Severe cases mein TURP jaisi surgery zaruri ho sakti hai.
Kis umar mein prostate screening karwani chahiye?
50+ umar mein routine PSA test consider karna chahiye doctor se discuss kar ke. Family history walon ko 40-45 saal se hi screening consider karni chahiye.
Prostate ki takleef mein kab foran doctor ke paas jana chahiye?
Agar peshab bilkul nahi a raha (urinary retention), khoon nazar aa raha ho, shadeed dard ho, ya bukhar ke saath ho to yeh emergency hai — foran medical attention lein.
