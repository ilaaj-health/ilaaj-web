Neuromet Tablet Uses — B1, B6, B12 Nerve Pain Ka Ilaj Pakistan

Mukhtasar — Ahem Baatein
  • Neuromet = B1 (thiamine 100mg) + B6 (pyridoxine 100mg) + B12 (cyanocobalamin 200mcg)
  • Diabetic neuropathy, haath paon soun hona, nerve pain ke liye Pakistan mein widely prescribed
  • Neurobion se farq: B6 100mg (Neurobion mein 200mg); Methycobal se farq: woh sirf B12 hai

  • Safe magar B6 ki zyada extra dose (200mg+) ulta nerve damage kar sakti hai

Neuromet Kya Hai?

Neuromet Pakistan mein available B vitamin complex tablet hai jisme teen neurological B vitamins hain: B1 (thiamine) 100mg + B6 (pyridoxine) 100mg + B12 (cyanocobalamin) 200mcg. Yeh combination specifically nervous system ki health ke liye hai — nerve conduction, myelin sheath maintenance aur nerve repair mein kaam aata hai.

Neuromet Ke Uses

1. Diabetic Neuropathy

Pakistan mein diabetes bohot common hai aur nerve damage (neuropathy) iska sabse common complication hai. Neuromet B vitamins provide karta hai jo nerve function ke liye zaroori hain — especially B12 jo myelin sheath (nerves ki protective coating) banata hai.

2. Haath Paon Ka Soun Hona (Numbness / Tingling)

Peripheral neuropathy ke symptoms — haath ya paon ka soun jaana, jalan, pins & needles — mein Neuromet beneficial hai. B12 deficiency in symptoms ki common wajah hai.

3. Alcohol-Related Neuropathy

Alcohol se B1 (thiamine) ki kami hoti hai jo Wernicke's encephalopathy aur peripheral neuropathy ka sabab banti hai. B1-B6-B12 combination is situation mein zaruri hai.

4. General Nerve Support

Metformin (diabetes ki dawa) B12 absorption kam karte hai — long-term metformin users ko Neuromet ya B12 supplement zaroori hai. Elderly logon mein bhi B12 malabsorption common hai.

Khulaasa

Neuromet (B1+B6+B12) Pakistan mein nerve pain aur neuropathy ke liye sabse zyada prescribed B complex hai. Diabetic neuropathy mein metformin ke saath routine supplement ke tor pe di jaati hai. Neurobion se B6 dose thoda kam hai; Methycobal se farq yeh hai ke Methycobal sirf B12 hai. 4-8 hafte ka poora course zaroori hai — symptoms improve hone pe bhi band mat karein.

Aksar Pooche Jane Waale Sawaalat

Neuromet aur Neurobion mein kya farq hai?

Dono B1+B6+B12 hain magar Neuromet mein B6 100mg, Neurobion mein B6 200mg hai. Methycobal sirf B12 hai — nerve repair ke liye targeted.

Neuromet kitne time mein kaam karti hai?

4-8 hafte mein improvement shuru hoti hai. Poora course complete karein — beech mein band karne se symptoms wapas aa sakte hain.

Kya Neuromet khali pet le sakte hain?

Haan — B vitamins khali pet ya khane ke saath dono theek hain. Nausea ho to khane ke saath lein.

Kya Neuromet diabetes ke liye best nerve tablet hai?

Mild cases ke liye helpful hai. Severe neuropathy mein pregabalin/gabapentin doctor prescribe karte hain — Neuromet akela kaafi nahi hota.

B6 ki zyada dose nuksaan kar sakti hai?

Haan — 200mg+ roz sensory neuropathy kar sakti hai. Prescribed dose safe hai magar extra B6 supplement doctor ke bagair nahi leni.
