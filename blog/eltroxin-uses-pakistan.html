Eltroxin — Thyroid Ka Ilaj, Dose Aur Side Effects

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Eltroxin (levothyroxine) synthetic thyroid hormone hai — hypothyroidism ka first-line ilaj
  • Pakistan mein 30-50 saal ki khwateen mein bohat aam masla
  • Subah khaali pet, nashtay se 30-60 minute pehlay, sirf paani ke saath
  • Calcium, iron aur doodh absorption ko bohat kam kar dete hain — gap rakhein
  • Aksar lifelong dawai — TSH har 6 mahine ya saal mein check karwayein

Eltroxin Kya Hai?

Eltroxin GSK ki banai hui synthetic levothyroxine (T4 hormone) hai jo body ki natural thyroid hormone ki kami poori karti hai. Hypothyroidism (under-active thyroid) Pakistan mein bohat aam problem hai — khaaskar 30-50 saal ki khwateen mein. Thyroid gland metabolism, body temperature, energy, mood, aur wazan — sab control karti hai. Jab yeh kaam karna chod de to pori body sust ho jati hai.

Pakistan Mein Thyroid Ki Beemari Kitni Aam Hai?

Pakistan Society of Endocrinology ke mutabiq 15-20% baligh khwateen ko life mein kabhi na kabhi thyroid ki problem hoti hai. Punjab aur KP ke kuch ilaqon mein iodine deficiency ki wajah se rate aur zyada hai. Khwateen mein hypothyroidism mardo se 5-8 guna zyada hota hai. Pregnancy ke baad (postpartum thyroiditis), 35 ke baad, aur family mein thyroid problem ho to risk barh jata hai. Karachi aur Lahore mein urban awareness barh rahi hai magar choti shehron aur dehi ilaqon mein ab bhi log "thakawat" ko sirf kaam ya umar samajh kar diagnose karwane mein dair karte hain.

Hypothyroidism Ke Symptoms

  • Bayhad thakawat, sustay, neend zyada
  • Wazan barhna bina khaane mein farq
  • Sardi zyada lagna, hath paaon thande
  • Qabz
  • Baalon ka jhadna, baal patle hone
  • Dry skin, nakhun toot-na
  • Mood low, depression jaisi feeling
  • Period mein irregularity, heavy bleeding
  • Yaadasht aur concentration mein kami ("brain fog")
  • Awaaz mein bharipan, gala phulna (goiter)
  • Bachay mein growth ruk jana

Diagnosis — TSH Test

Sab se reliable test TSH (Thyroid Stimulating Hormone) hai. Normal range 0.5-4.5 mIU/L. Hypothyroidism mein TSH zyada hota hai. Doctor saath mein Free T4 aur kabhi anti-TPO antibodies bhi test karte hain Hashimoto's diagnose karne ke liye. Pakistan mein test Rs. 800-1500 mein lab se ho jata hai.

Eltroxin Ki Sahi Dose Aur Tareeqa

  • Young, healthy adults: 50-100 mcg se shuru, full dose 1.6 mcg/kg/day
  • Buzurg ya heart patients: 25 mcg se shuru, ahista barhana
  • Hamla khwateen: Pre-pregnancy dose se 30-50% zyada
  • Bachay: Wazan ke hisab se, pediatric endocrinologist set karta hai

Bohat Important — Kaise Lein:

  • Subah uth kar khaali pet, sirf paani ke saath
  • Nashtay se 30-60 minute pehlay
  • Chai/coffee mein bhi 30-60 minute gap
  • Calcium, iron, multivitamin ke saath 4 ghante ka gap
  • Antacids (Risek, Gaviscon) se bhi 4 ghante
  • Soya products absorption kam karte hain

Abhi Doctor Se Milein

TSH Monitoring — Test Kab Karayein?

  • Dawai start ya dose change ke 6-8 hafte baad TSH test
  • Stable hone tak har 8 hafte test
  • Phir saal mein 1-2 baar kaafi
  • Pregnancy mein har 4-6 hafte
  • Symptoms wapas aa rahe ho to test pehlay
  • Naya khaane peenay ya nayi dawai shuru ho to bhi test

Eltroxin Ke Side Effects

Agar dose sahi hai to side effects nahi hote — yeh aap ki body ka apna natural hormone hai. Side effects mostly over-dosing se hote hain:

  • Dil ki tez dharkan, palpitations
  • Wazan kam hona, bhook bohat lagna
  • Garmi zyada lagna, paseena aana
  • Neend mein khalal
  • Bechaini, anxiety, hath kanpana
  • Dast
  • Period mein irregular

Yeh symptoms ho to TSH test karwayein — dose kam karne ki zarurat ho sakti hai. Bohat lambi over-treatment se osteoporosis aur atrial fibrillation ka risk barhta hai khaaskar bzurg khwateen mein.

Drug Interactions — Yeh Dawaiyan Absorption Kam Karti Hain

  • Calcium aur Iron supplements: 4 ghante gap
  • Antacids aur PPI (Risek, Nexium): 4 ghante gap
  • Multivitamins: 4 ghante gap
  • Sucralfate: Ulcer ki dawai — 4 ghante gap
  • Cholesterol dawaiyan (cholestyramine): Gap rakhein
  • Estrogen pills: Eltroxin ki zarurat barhati hain
  • Warfarin (blood thinner): Effect barhta hai — INR monitor

Lifestyle Tips Thyroid Patients Ke Liye

  • Iodized namak use karein (Pakistan mein iodine kam hai)
  • Seafood, machli, anday — natural T4 ka source
  • Roz exercise — wazan aur metabolism ke liye
  • Selenium aur Zinc kam diet ho to supplement (doctor se poochein)
  • Stress management — yoga, meditation, dua
  • Soya aur cruciferous sabziyaan (band gobhi, broccoli) zyada na khaayein — magar zaroori bhi nahi ke bilkul band karein
  • Smoking band — thyroid eye disease aur autoimmune problems barhati hai

Kab Doctor Se Foran Milein?

  • Dil ki bohat tez dharkan ya seenay mein dard
  • Saans phulna ya pair sojhna
  • Achanak shadeed bechaini, hath kanpana
  • Wazan tezi se kam ya barh raha
  • Pregnancy ki planning ya hamal confirm — dose review
  • 2 mahine se symptoms wapas aa rahe ho dawai ke bawajood

Conclusion

Eltroxin Pakistan mein hypothyroidism ka safe, effective aur sasti dawai hai. Magar yeh sirf tablet nigalna nahi — sahi timing, khaali pet, doosri dawaiyon se gap, aur regular TSH monitoring sab zaruri hain. Aksar yeh dawai lifelong leni hoti hai, magar agar sahi se ho to thyroid patient bilkul normal zindagi guzar sakta hai. Frequently Asked Questions

Eltroxin kab aur kaise lein?
Eltroxin subah uth kar khaali pet, nashtay se 30-60 minute pehlay, sirf paani ke saath lein. Chai, doodh, calcium ya iron ki dawai ke saath bilkul na lein — yeh thyroid hormone ka absorption 40-50% tak kam kar dete hain. Agar subah mushkil hai to sone se pehlay (akhri khaane ke 4 ghante baad) bhi le sakte hain.
Eltroxin ki dose kaise determine hoti hai?
Dose TSH level, umar, wazan aur baqi medical conditions par depend karta hai. Aam tor par 50-100 mcg shuruwati dose hai, aur 1.6 mcg per kg per day target. Buzurg aur dil ke marizo mein 25 mcg se shuru karte hain. Har 6-8 hafte TSH check kar ke doctor dose adjust karta hai jab tak TSH 0.5-2.5 mIU/L ke beech aa jaye, phir saal mein 1-2 baar test kaafi hai.
Hypothyroidism ke symptoms kya hain?
Pakistan mein 30-50 saal ki khwateen mein sab se aam — bayhad thakawat, wazan barhna, sardi zyada lagna, qabz, baalon ka jhadna aur patle hona, dry skin, mood low, period mein irregularity, aur memory mein kami. Bachon mein growth ruk jana aur taleem mein peechay rehna. Aksar yeh symptoms ahista ahista shuru hote hain isliye log dair se doctor ke pass jate hain. TSH test sab se reliable diagnosis hai.
Kya pregnancy mein Eltroxin safe hai?
Bilkul safe hai — balke hamla khwateen ke liye zaruri hai. Untreated hypothyroidism miscarriage, preterm birth aur bachay ki dimaagi nashunuma par bura asar daalti hai. Hamal mein zyadtar khwateen ko dose 30-50% barhane ki zarurat parti hai. TSH test har 4-6 hafte karwana zaruri hai, target TSH pehlay trimester mein 2.5 se kam. Apni gynecologist ko zaroor batayein agar Eltroxin chal rahi hai aur hamal ki planning kar rahi hain.
Eltroxin kitne arsay tak leni padti hai?
Zyadtar cases mein lifelong. Hypothyroidism aksar permanent condition hai — thyroid gland natural hormone nahi bana pati to bahar se replacement zaruri hai. Achanak band karne se thakawat, wazan, depression sab wapas a jate hain. Sirf kuch cases — jaise post-partum thyroiditis ya temporary thyroid inflammation — mein dawai 6-12 mahine baad band ho sakti hai, woh bhi doctor ki nigrani mein TSH dobara test kar ke.
