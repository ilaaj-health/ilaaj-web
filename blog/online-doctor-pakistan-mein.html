Online Doctor Se Ilaaj Kyu Zaruri Hai?

Pakistan mein health system kai challenges face kar raha hai:

  • Doctor-to-patient ratio bahut kum hai — 1 doctor per 1,000 patients se bhi kam
  • Shahari ilaqon mein specialist doctors milte hain, magar dihati ilaqon mein access mushkil hai
  • Consultation fees 1,500 se 5,000 rupay tak ja sakti hain
  • Waiting time ghanton tak ho sakta hai

Isi liye telemedicine aur AI health apps Pakistan ke liye ek game-changer hain.

Frequently Asked Questions

Kya online doctor se milna safe hai?

Haan, Ilaaj AI pe har consultation bilkul safe hai. Hamara platform PMDC-registered doctors se verified prescriptions provide karta hai. AI sirf initial triage karta hai, lekin final prescription ek licensed doctor approve karta hai. Aapka data bhi completely private aur secure rehta hai.

Ilaaj AI pe consultation ka kharcha kitna hai?

Ilaaj AI traditional clinic se kaafi sasta hai. Pakistan mein ek clinic visit 1,500 se 5,000 rupay tak ja sakti hai, jabke Ilaaj AI pe aap bohut kam mein doctor-verified prescription pa sakte hain. Pehli consultation ke liye hamari website pe latest pricing check karein.

Kya Urdu mein baat kar saktay hain?

Bilkul! Ilaaj AI Pakistan ke liye bana hai. Aap Urdu, Roman Urdu, ya English — kisi bhi zubaan mein apni symptoms bata sakte hain. Voice messages bhi support hote hain, taki typing mushkil ho to sirf bol dein. AI aapki zubaan samajhta hai aur usi mein jawab deta hai.

Doctor prescription kitni der mein milti hai?

Zyada tar cases mein, prescription 10 se 30 minute ke andar aa jati hai. AI pehle aapki symptoms analyze karta hai, phir ek licensed doctor usse review aur approve karta hai. Raat ko ya busy hours mein thodi zyada time lag sakti hai, magar aam tor par process bahut fast hai.

Kya yeh emergency ke liye hai?

Ilaaj AI common aur non-emergency issues ke liye best hai — jaise bukhaar, zukam, sar dard, pet dard, skin issues, aur follow-up consultations. Serious emergencies jaise heart attack, accident, ya severe pain mein turant 1122 (Rescue Pakistan) call karein ya nearest hospital jaein. Telemedicine emergency care ki jagah nahi le sakti.
