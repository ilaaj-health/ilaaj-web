Fatty Liver Ka Ilaj — Wajuhat, Diet Aur Reversal Guide

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Fatty liver mein jigar mein zyada charbi jama ho jati hai
  • Zyada tar cases mein koi alamat nahi hoti — ultrasound se accidental pata chalta hai
  • Obesity, diabetes, aur alcohol sab se badi wajuhat hain
  • Weight loss aur diet change se fatty liver reverse ho sakta hai
  • Untreated chhorne se cirrhosis aur liver failure ka khatra

Reversible?Haan, early stage mein diet aur weight loss se

Fatty Liver Kya Hai?

Fatty liver disease tab hoti hai jab jigar (liver) ke cells mein zyada fat jama ho jata hai — normal liver mein thoda fat hota hai, magar 5% se zyada ho jaye to yeh fatty liver kehlata hai. Pakistan mein obesity aur diabetes ki wajah se yeh bohat common hai.

Types

  • NAFLD (Non-Alcoholic Fatty Liver Disease) — Pakistan mein sab se common, alcohol se related nahi
  • Alcoholic Fatty Liver — zyada alcohol consumption se
  • NASH (Non-Alcoholic Steatohepatitis) — advanced stage jahan inflammation bhi shuru ho jati hai

Alamat

Zyada tar logon ko koi alamat nahi hoti — routine ultrasound mein accidental pata chalta hai. Advanced stages mein:

  • Thakawat
  • Right side pait mein halka dard ya bhaari pan
  • Weakness
  • Weight kam hona (advanced cases)
  • Skin ya aankhein peeli hona (jaundice — severe cases)

Wajuhat

  • Obesity/overweight — sab se badi wajah
  • Type 2 diabetes/insulin resistance
  • High cholesterol/triglycerides
  • Zyada alcohol consumption
  • Fast, oily, aur sugary diet
  • Sedentary lifestyle
  • Kuch dawaiyan (long-term steroids)

Diagnosis

Ultrasound pehla test hai jo aksar routine checkup mein fatty liver reveal karta hai. LFT (Liver Function Test) se liver enzymes check hote hain. Advanced cases mein FibroScan ya biopsy se liver damage ka level pata chalta hai.

Ilaj — Weight Loss Sab Se Effective

  • 7-10% body weight kam karna — fatty liver ko significantly kam kar sakta hai
  • Gradual weight loss — 0.5-1kg per week, achanak crash diet avoid karein
  • Regular exercise — hafte mein 150 minute moderate activity
  • Sugar aur processed food kam karein
  • Alcohol completely band karein

Fatty Liver Diet

Khayein:

  • Sabzi, phal (khaskar low-sugar)
  • Whole grains — daliya, brown rice
  • Lean protein — machli, chicken (bina skin)
  • Olive oil, nuts (moderate)
  • Green tea

Avoid Karein:

  • Fried aur oily khana
  • Sugar, mithai, soft drinks
  • White bread, white rice zyada
  • Red meat zyada
  • Alcohol

Doctor Se Mashwara Lein

Kab Doctor Ko Milein?

  • Right upper pait mein persistent dard
  • Bila wajah weight loss
  • Jaundice (peeli skin ya aankhein)
  • Pait mein sujan (fluid accumulation)
  • LFT mein liver enzymes high

Conclusion

Fatty liver early stage mein fully reversible hai sirf lifestyle changes se — weight loss, healthy diet, aur exercise. Ignore karne se yeh NASH, fibrosis, aur eventually cirrhosis mein badal sakta hai. Ilaaj AI pe doctor se apna diet plan aur regular monitoring discuss karein.

Frequently Asked Questions

Kya fatty liver theek ho sakta hai?
Haan, early stage fatty liver bilkul reversible hai — 7-10% body weight kam karne se, healthy diet, aur regular exercise se liver mein fat significantly kam ho sakta hai. Advanced stages (fibrosis, cirrhosis) mein damage permanent ho sakta hai.
Fatty liver ke liye best diet kya hai?
Low-sugar, low-fried khana — zyada sabzi, phal, whole grains, aur lean protein. Sugar, soft drinks, fried food, aur alcohol completely avoid karein. Mediterranean-style diet fatty liver ke liye behtarin samjhi jati hai.
Kya fatty liver sirf motay logon ko hota hai?
Nahi, zyada tar cases obesity se related hain magar normal weight walon ko bhi 'lean NAFLD' ho sakta hai, khaskar agar diabetes ya insulin resistance ho. Genetics bhi role play karti hai.
Fatty liver aur liver cirrhosis mein kya farq hai?
Fatty liver early aur reversible stage hai jahan sirf fat jama hota hai. Ignore karne se NASH (inflammation) aur phir fibrosis (scarring) ho sakti hai, aur end stage mein cirrhosis — jo permanent aur life-threatening hai.
Fatty liver ki tashkhees kaise hoti hai?
Zyada tar routine ultrasound mein accidental pata chalta hai. Liver Function Test (LFT) se enzymes check hote hain. Confirm diagnosis aur severity ke liye FibroScan ya rarely biopsy ki zarurat par sakti hai.
