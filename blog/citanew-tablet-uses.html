Citanew Tablet Uses — Depression, Anxiety Aur OCD Ka Ilaj Pakistan

Mukhtasar — Aham Baatein

  • Citanew ka generic naam escitalopram hai — ye sabse selective SSRI antidepressant hai
  • Depression, generalized anxiety disorder (GAD), panic disorder, social anxiety aur OCD ke liye istemal hoti hai
  • Aam dose: 10mg roz aik baar — doctor 2-4 hafte baad 20mg tak barha sakta hai
  • Asar 2-4 hafte mein shuru hota hai — pehle band mat karein ye soch kar ke fayda nahi ho raha
  • Achanak band mat karein — 2-4 hafte mein dheere dheere kam karein. Ye addictive nahi aur controlled substance bhi nahi

Zaroori Baat: Asar Aane Mein Waqt Lagta Hai — Aur Achanak Band Mat Karein

Citanew (escitalopram) addictive nahi hai aur ye controlled substance bhi nahi hai — lekin har mareez ko ye do baatein zaroor samajhni chahiye:

  • Asar 2-4 hafte mein aata hai: Pehle do hafton mein "fayda nahi ho raha" soch kar dawa band mat karein. Pakistan mein antidepressant ilaj fail hone ki sabse badi wajah yahi jaldi band karna hai. Poora fayda 6-8 hafte mein nazar aata hai.
  • Kabhi achanak band mat karein: Escitalopram achanak band karne se discontinuation syndrome ho sakta hai — chakkar, bijli ke jhatke jaisi sensation (brain zaps), flu jaise symptoms, matli, chirchirapan aur anxiety ka wapas aana. Dose hamesha doctor ki nigrani mein 2-4 hafte (ya zyada) mein dheere dheere kam karein.
  • Shuru mein apne mood par nazar rakhein: Pehle kuch hafton mein — khaas tor par 25 saal se kam umar ke logon mein — anxiety, beqaraari ya bohot kam logon mein khudkushi ke khayal aasakte hain. Agar mood aur kharab ho ya khudkushi ke khayal aayen to foran doctor se rabta karein.

Pehle mahine mein aur jab bhi dose badle, apne doctor se regular rabta rakhein.

Citanew Kya Hai?

Citanew ek mashoor brand naam hai jis ka generic naam escitalopram hai. Ye ek SSRI (selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor) antidepressant hai — duniya bhar mein sabse zyada likhi jane wali antidepressant classes mein se aik. Escitalopram dar-asal citalopram ka active (S-) enantiomer hai aur ise tamam SSRIs mein sabse zyada "selective" mana jaata hai — yani ye khaas tor par serotonin system par kaam karti hai. Yahi selectivity is wajah hai ke ye aam tor par achi tarah bardasht ho jaati hai.

Pakistan mein Citanew aksar psychiatrists aur general physicians depression aur mukhtalif anxiety disorders ke liye likhte hain. Ye samajhna zaroori hai ke ye painkiller ki tarah "foran asar" wali goli nahi — escitalopram kuch hafton ke arsa mein mood aur anxiety disorders ki bunyadi chemical imbalance ko dheere dheere theek karti hai, aur ye sab se behtar tab kaam karti hai jab is ke saath therapy, lifestyle changes aur regular follow-up bhi ho.

Citanew Kaise Kaam Karta Hai?

Serotonin dimagh ka aik neurotransmitter (chemical messenger) hai jo mood, anxiety, neend, bhook aur sukoon ke ehsaas ko control karta hai. Depression aur anxiety disorders mein nerve cells ke darmiyan serotonin ka signal kam ya dysregulated ho jaata hai.

Jab serotonin aik nerve cell release karti hai to ye normally wapas usi cell mein absorb (reuptake) ho jaata hai. Escitalopram (Citanew) serotonin transporter ko selectively rokti hai aur is reuptake ko ruk jaati hai. Is ke nateeje mein zyada serotonin synapse (nerve cells ke darmiyan jagah) mein mojood rehta hai aur signal jaari rakhta hai. Kuch hafton mein ye dimagh ke circuits mein dheere dheere tabdeeli laata hai — yahi wajah hai ke antidepressant asar dheere dheere banta hai, raat o raat nahi aata.

Kyunki escitalopram serotonin ke liye itni selective hai aur doosre receptors (histamine, acetylcholine, noradrenaline) par bohot kam asar karti hai, is liye is mein purane antidepressants (jaise amitriptyline) wali neend, wazan aur khushki jaise side effects kam hote hain.

Citanew Ke Istemal — Pakistan Mein

Depression (Udaasi Ki Bimari)

Citanew moderate se severe depression ka first-line ilaj hai. Pakistan mein depression ka diagnosis aur ilaj kaafi kam hota hai, kuch had tak mental health ke baare mein stigma ki wajah se. Symptoms mein hamesha udaasi, kisi cheez mein dilchaspi na rehna, neend kharab, energy kam, dhyaan na lagna, ehsas-e-jurm, aur kabhi khudkushi ke khayal shamil hain. Escitalopram kuch hafton mein mood, neend, energy aur himmat ko behtar karti hai aur mareez ko wapas normal zindagi ki taraf le aati hai.

Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)

GAD mein insaan ko rozmarra ki cheezon ki behad aur mustaqil fikr rehti hai jise control karna mushkil hota hai — saath mein beqaraari, jism ka tanaao, thakaan, chirchirapan aur kharab neend. Escitalopram GAD ke liye sabse effective aur achi tarah bardasht hone wale long-term ilaj mein se aik hai — aur benzodiazepines (jaise alprazolam) se kahin zyada mehfooz option hai kyunki ye dependence nahi banati.

Panic Disorder Aur Social Anxiety

Panic disorder mein achanak shadeed khauf ke daure parte hain — dil ki dhadkan tez, saans phoolna, seene mein jakdan, chakkar aur "kuch bura hone wala hai" ka ehsaas. Citanew waqt ke saath panic attacks ki tadaad aur shiddat dono kam karti hai. Ye social anxiety disorder mein bhi effective hai jahan logon ke saamne ya judgement ke darr se insaan mehfilon se bachne lagta hai. In dono mein dose aksar kam shuru ki jaati hai (taake shuru mein anxiety na barhe), phir dheere dheere barhayi jaati hai.

OCD (Waswasay Ki Bimari)

OCD mein baar baar aane wale na-pasandeeda khayalat (obsessions) aur unhe kam karne ke liye baar baar kiye jane wale amal (compulsions) hote hain. Escitalopram samet SSRIs OCD ke ilaj ka bunyadi hissa hain, magar OCD mein aksar zyada dose (20mg tak) aur zyada lamba trial (10-12 hafte) chahiye hota hai is se pehle ke poora fayda andaza lagaya jaye. Behtreen nateeje ke liye ise CBT (cognitive behavioural therapy) ke saath istemal kiya jaata hai.

Citanew ki dose bimari aur mareez ke mutabiq alag hoti hai. Hamesha doctor ki taay karda dose par rahein. Ye aam dose schedule hai:

Aham baatein: Citanew roz aik hi waqt par lein — subah ya shaam, jo aap ko theek lage. Khane ke saath ya baghair dono tarah se li ja sakti hai; agar pet kharab kare to khane ke saath lein. Agar aik khurak bhool jayen to yaad aane par le lein, lekin agar agle dose ka waqt qarib ho to chhod dein — double dose mat lein. Band karna ho to hamesha doctor ki nigrani mein 2-4 hafte mein dheere dheere kam karein.

Citanew Ke Side Effects

Aam Side Effects

Escitalopram aam tor par achi tarah bardasht hoti hai. Kai side effects halke hote hain aur pehle 1-2 hafte mein jism ke adjust hone par behtar ho jaate hain: matli (jo aksar 1-2 hafte mein theek ho jaati hai — khane ke saath lene se kam hoti hai), sir dard, neend aana ya na aana (timing badalne se madad milti hai), shuru mein anxiety ya beqaraari ka thoda barhna, mun khushk hona aur paseena aana.

Sexual Side Effects

SSRIs ke saath libido (jinsi khwahish) kam hona ya orgasm mein dair hona aam hai aur jaari reh sakta hai. Ye ek aam masla hai jis par sharmane ki zaroorat nahi — apne doctor ko zaroor bataayein, kyunki dose adjust karke ya dawa badal kar is ka hal ho sakta hai.

Serious Side Effects — Foran Doctor Se Milein

Kuch side effects serious hain aur foran medical madad chahiye:

  • Serotonin syndrome: Beqaraari, confusion, dil ki dhadkan tez, tez bukhar, muscles ki akarhan, kapkapi — ek emergency hai, khaas tor par doosri serotonergic dawaoyon ke saath
  • Khudkushi ke khayal: Khaas tor par naujawanon mein shuru ke hafton mein — foran doctor ko bataayein
  • Dil ki dhadkan par asar (QT prolongation): Zyada dose (20mg se upar) ya doosri QT-prolonging dawaoyon ke saath — dil ke mareezon mein khaas dhyan
  • Khoon mein sodium kam hona (hyponatraemia): Khaas tor par bozurgon mein — confusion, kamzori, sir dard
  • Ghair maamooli bleeding ya neel: SSRIs bleeding ka khatra thoda barha sakti hain, khaas tor par brufen ya aspirin jaise painkillers ke saath

Kise Citanew Nahi Leni Chahiye Ya Ehtiyaat Karni Chahiye?

Kuch halaat mein Citanew lena mana hai ya bohot ehtiyaat se lena chahiye. Doctor ko pehle ye sab bataana zaroori hai:

  • MAOI dawayein: Escitalopram kabhi MAOI antidepressants ke saath nahi leni — beech mein kam az kam 14 din ka wakfa zaroori hai
  • Dil ki dhadkan ka masla (QT prolongation): Ehtiyaat aur dose ki had — 20mg se upar aur doosri QT-prolonging dawaoyon se parhiez
  • 25 saal se kam umar ke naujawan: Shuru ke hafton mein qareebi nigrani zaroori hai (khudkushi ke khayal ka thoda khatra)
  • Bipolar disorder: Akela antidepressant mania la sakta hai — escitalopram sirf mood stabiliser ke saath istemal honi chahiye
  • Hamal aur doodh pilana: Faisla doctor ke saath karein — band na karein khud se, kyunki untreated depression ka bhi nuqsaan hota hai
  • Jigar (liver) ki kamzori aur bozurg afraad: Kam dose istemal ki jaati hai

Citanew Aur Doosri Dawaoyon Ka Interaction

Escitalopram ke kuch aham interactions hain. Doctor ko apni tamam dawaoyon ki list zaroor dikhayein:

  • MAOIs (phenelzine, selegiline, linezolid): Bilkul mana — jaanleva serotonin syndrome ka khatra
  • Doosri serotonergic dawayein (tramadol, doosre SSRI/SNRI, migraine ke triptans, St John's Wort): Serotonin syndrome ka khatra barhta hai
  • NSAIDs aur aspirin (brufen, disprin): Pet mein bleeding ka khatra — zarorat ho to gastric protection lein
  • QT-prolonging dawayein (kuch antibiotics, antipsychotics): Dil ki dhadkan par asar barhta hai
  • Shraab: Behtar hai parhiez karein — mood kharab kar sakti hai aur neend barhati hai
  • Khoon patla karne wali dawa (warfarin): Bleeding ke khatre ki wajah se monitoring zaroori ho sakti hai

Pakistan Mein Kahan Milegi Aur Kitnay Ki Hai?

Citanew aur doosre escitalopram brands Pakistan ki zyada tar pharmacies mein milte hain aur inhe doctor ki parchi ke saath kharidna chahiye. Qeemat strength aur brand ke mutabiq alag hai — Citanew ki ek strip taakhmeeni Rs. 150 se Rs. 400 ke darmiyan milti hai, jabke imported brands jaise Lexapro aur Cipralex zyada mehengi hain. Kyunki ilaj aam tor par kam az kam 6-12 mahine (aur baar baar hone wali bimari mein zyada) jaari rakha jaata hai, is liye Citanew jaise local brand ki kam qeemat se mustaqil, long-term ilaj Pakistani mareezon ke liye zyada mumkin ho jaata hai.

Ek bohot aham baat — mental health ki dawa hamesha PMDC-registered doctor ki nigrani mein lein. Apni dose ya dawa khud se mat badlein, aur jab band karna ho to doctor ke taay karda taper plan par chalein. Mental health ki bimari aik haqeeqi, qaabil-e-ilaj medical masla hai — madad mangna kamzori nahi balke himmat ki nishani hai.

Aksar Poochhe Jane Wale Sawal

Citanew ka asar kitne dair mein shuru hota hai?

Citanew (escitalopram) ka asar foran nahi hota. Zyada tar logon ko 2-4 hafte mein mood, neend aur energy mein behtari mehsoos hoti hai, aur poora fayda 6-8 hafte mein nazar aata hai. Pehle 2 hafton mein ye soch kar band mat karein ke fayda nahi ho raha — Pakistan mein yahi sabse aam wajah hai jis ki bina par log ilaj jaldi chhod dete hain. Kuch logon ko pehle hafton mein anxiety kam hone ya neend behtar hone ki shakal mein jaldi farq mehsoos hota hai. Sabr zaroor rakhein aur bina doctor ki ijazat ke dose mat badhaayein.

Citanew achanak band karne se kya hota hai?

Citanew achanak band karna takleef-deh ho sakta hai. Discontinuation syndrome ke symptoms mein chakkar, bijli ke jhatke jaisi sensation (brain zaps), flu jaise symptoms, matli, chirchirapan, neend na aana aur anxiety ka wapas aana shamil hain. Ye aam tor par khatarnaak nahi lekin bohot pareshan-kun hote hain. Is liye is dawa ko hamesha doctor ki nigrani mein 2-4 hafte (ya zyada) mein dheere dheere kam karein. Kabhi khud faisla karke band mat karein, chahe aap ko lagta ho ke ab zaroorat nahi.

Kya Citanew, Lexapro aur Cipralex aik hi cheez hain?

Han — Citanew, Lexapro aur Cipralex sab mein aik hi active ingredient hai: escitalopram. Lexapro aur Cipralex original brands (Lundbeck) hain jabke Citanew ek Pakistani brand hai. Aik hi dose par ye clinically barabar hain — efficacy aur safety mein koi farq nahi. Bunyadi farq sirf qeemat ka hai — Citanew Pakistan mein aksar kafi sasti hoti hai. Doosre escitalopram brands bhi barabar dose par utne hi effective hain.

Kya Citanew ki aadat (addiction) pad sakti hai?

Nahi — escitalopram (Citanew) addictive nahi hai aur ye controlled substance bhi nahi hai. Is mein woh craving, tolerance ya compulsive use nahi hota jo Xanax ya Lexotanil jaise benzodiazepines mein hota hai. Lekin jism is ka aadi ho jaata hai, is liye ise achanak band karne ke bajaye dheere dheere kam karna zaroori hai taake discontinuation symptoms na hon. Ye jismani adjustment hai, addiction nahi — is liye taper hamesha doctor ki nigrani mein karein.

Kya Citanew doosri dawaoyon ke saath le sakte hain?

Kuch combinations khatarnaak hain. Citanew kabhi MAOI antidepressants ke saath nahi leni chahiye, aur doosri serotonergic dawaoyon (tramadol, doosre SSRI/SNRI, migraine ke triptans, St John's Wort) ke saath ehtiyat zaroori hai kyunki serotonin syndrome ka khatra hota hai. 20mg se zyada dose QT prolongation ki wajah se nahi di jaati. Citanew shuru karne se pehle apne doctor ko apni tamam dawaoyon (aur supplements) ki list zaroor dikhayein.
