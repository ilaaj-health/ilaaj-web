Anxiety Ka Ilaj — Ghabrahat Aur Panic Attack Treatment Pakistan Mein

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Anxiety ek medical condition hai — kamzori ya pagalpan NAHI
  • Pakistan mein 30%+ adults kisi na kisi waqt anxiety se guzarte hain
  • Panic attack heart attack NAHI hai — 10-20 minute mein khatam ho jata hai
  • 4-7-8 breathing technique panic attack mein foran kaam karti hai
  • CBT therapy 60-80% cases mein effective hai
  • SSRIs (Escitalopram/Sertraline) safe aur affordable hain — Rs. 300-800/month

Anxiety Disorder Kya Hai?

Anxiety ya ghabrahat ek normal jazbat hai — exam se pehle, interview mein, ya kisi mushkil waqt pe har insaan ko hoti hai. Magar jab yeh ghabrahat bina wajah ho, roz ho, aur itni shadeed ho ke roz ki zindagi mutasir ho, to ise Anxiety Disorder kehte hain — yeh ek medical condition hai jiska ilaj hota hai.

Pakistan mein mental health ka sab se bada masla stigma hai — log anxiety ko "waham", "kamzori", ya "jinn ka asar" samajhte hain. Haqeeqat mein yeh brain chemistry ka masla hai — bilkul diabetes ya BP jaisi bimari hai. WHO ke mutabiq Pakistan mein 30% se zyada adults kisi na kisi waqt anxiety symptoms mehsoos karte hain.

Anxiety Ki Symptoms

Anxiety sirf "tension" nahi — iske jismani (physical) symptoms bhi hote hain jo log aksar dil ya pet ki bimari samajhte hain:

Jismani Symptoms:

  • Dil ka tez dhakna (palpitations) — sab se aam shikayat
  • Seene mein dard ya bharipan — heart attack ka dar
  • Sans phoolna ya dam ghutna
  • Haath paon ka kaampna
  • Pasina aana (khaaskar hathaliyon mein)
  • Pet kharaab — dast, gas, IBS
  • Sir dard aur gardan/kandhon mein akran
  • Chakkar aana
  • Neend na aana ya beech mein jaag jana

Zehn (Mental) Symptoms:

  • Lagatar fikar — "kuch bura hone wala hai"
  • Concentrate na kar pana
  • Chidchidapan aur bechain
  • Marne ka dar ya "control kho dene" ka ehsaas
  • Har cheez mein worst-case scenario sochna
  • Social gatherings se bachna

Panic Attack Kya Hai? — Heart Attack Se Farq

Panic attack ek achanak shadeed dar ka dora hai jo 10-20 minute mein peak pe hota hai. Log aksar ise heart attack samajh ke emergency aate hain.

Panic Attack Ki Alamat:

  • Achanak shadeed ghabrahat — "main mar jaonga/jaongi"
  • Dil bohat tez dhakta hai
  • Seene mein dard ya pressure
  • Sans mushkil se aati hai
  • Haath paon sunn ya jhunak
  • Jism kaamp raha hai
  • Lag raha hai jaise haqeeqat nahi — "main kahin aur hoon"

Farq Kaise Karein:

  • Panic attack: 10-20 minute mein kam hota hai, dard seene ke beech mein, koi lasting damage nahi
  • Heart attack: Dard baazu/jabray/peeth tak phelta hai, kam nahi hota, pasina aur ulti — EMERGENCY
  • Pehli baar ho to HAMESHA hospital jaein — doctor ECG se confirm karega

Doctor Se Abhi Mashwara Karein

Anxiety Ki Wajuhat

Anxiety ki koi ek wajah nahi — yeh kai factors mil ke banti hai:

  • Genetics: Agar parents ya siblings ko anxiety hai to aap ko hone ka khatra 2-3 guna zyada hai
  • Brain Chemistry: Serotonin aur GABA neurotransmitters ka imbalance
  • Stress/Trauma: Financial problems, rishtay, job loss, accident, childhood trauma
  • Pakistan-specific factors: Load shedding, mehngai, unemployment, joint family conflicts, social media pressure
  • Medical conditions: Thyroid problems, vitamin deficiencies (B12, D), anemia
  • Caffeine/chai excess: Roz 6+ cup chai anxiety symptoms barhati hai
  • Sleep deprivation: Kam neend se anxiety 3-4 guna barh jaati hai

Anxiety Ka Ilaj — Therapy

CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy):

CBT anxiety ke ilaj ka gold standard hai — research se sabit hai ke 60-80% cases mein effective hai:

  • Negative thoughts ko pehchanna aur challenge karna sikhata hai
  • Dar ka saamna karna (exposure therapy) — dheere dheere
  • Aksar 12-16 sessions mein significant improvement
  • Pakistan mein trained CBT therapists major cities mein available hain
  • Online CBT bhi effective hai — ghar baithe therapy

Breathing Techniques — Foran Relief:

4-7-8 Technique:

  • 4 second — naak se sans andar lein
  • 7 second — sans rok ke rakhein
  • 8 second — moonh se dheere dheere bahar chhorein
  • 3-4 baar repeat karein — 2-3 minute mein anxiety kam hone lagti hai

Progressive Muscle Relaxation:

  • Pair ki ungliyon se shuru karein — 5 second tense karein, phir relax
  • Dheere dheere upar aayein — paindli, rani, pet, haath, kandhay, chehra
  • Har muscle group ko tense-then-relax karein
  • Sone se pehle karein — neend bhi achi aayegi

Anxiety Ki Dawaiyan

Dawai sirf doctor ke mashware se lein — kabhi apni marzi se shuru ya band na karein:

SSRIs (First-Line Treatment):

  • Escitalopram (Lexapro): 10-20mg — sab se zyada prescribed, kam side effects
  • Sertraline (Zoloft): 50-200mg — anxiety + depression dono mein effective
  • Kaam karne mein 2-4 hafte lagte hain — sabr zaruri hai
  • Aadat NAHI banti — safe long-term dawaiyan hain
  • Cost: Rs. 300-800 per month — bohat affordable

Benzodiazepines (Short-Term Only):

  • Alprazolam (Xanax): Fast relief magar aadat ban sakti hai
  • Clonazepam: Thora lamba asar — magar yeh bhi habit-forming
  • Doctor sirf 2-4 hafte ke liye dete hain
  • KABHI apni marzi se na lein — Pakistan mein bina prescription milti hain magar khatarnak hai

Lifestyle Changes — Anxiety Kam Karne Ke Tareeqay

  • Exercise: Roz 30 minute walk/jogging — endorphins release hote hain jo naturally anxiety kam karte hain
  • Screen time kam: Social media roz 1 ghante se zyada nahi — comparison anxiety barhata hai
  • Caffeine kam: Chai/coffee roz 2 cup se zyada nahi — caffeine anxiety trigger karta hai
  • Sleep routine: Roz ek hi waqt soyen aur uthein — 7-8 ghante zaruri
  • Journaling: Roz apne thoughts likh ke rakhein — dimagh halka hota hai
  • Social connection: Dost aur family se milte rahein — isolation anxiety barhata hai
  • Limit news: Din mein ek baar news dekhein — 24/7 news anxiety barhati hai

Pakistan Mein Mental Health Ka Stigma

Pakistan mein mental health ke baare mein bohat si ghalat-fehmiyan hain:

  • Ghalat: "Anxiety kamzor logon ko hoti hai" → Sahi: Yeh brain chemistry ka masla hai — kisi ko bhi ho sakti hai
  • Ghalat: "Dawai pagal banati hai" → Sahi: SSRIs safe hain aur brain ko normal function karne mein madad karti hain
  • Ghalat: "Namaz/dua se theek ho jayega" → Sahi: Spiritual practice helpful hai magar medical treatment bhi zaruri hai — dono saath mein chalein
  • Ghalat: "Mard ko anxiety nahi hoti" → Sahi: Mardon mein bhi utni hi aam hai magar wo report nahi karte

Kab Professional Help Zaruri Hai?

  • Anxiety 6 mahine se zyada ho
  • Roz ki zindagi mutasir ho rahi ho — kaam, padhai, rishtay
  • Panic attacks aa rahe hon
  • Neend bilkul na aaye
  • Sharab ya nashe se anxiety "manage" kar rahe hon
  • Khud ko nuqsaan pahunchane ke khayal aa rahe hon
  • Ghar se bahar nikalna mushkil ho gaya ho

Emergency: Agar aap ya koi apna khud ko nuqsaan pahunchane ka soch raha hai to foran Umang Helpline 0317-4288665 pe call karein.

Frequently Asked Questions

Panic attack aur heart attack mein kya farq hai?
Panic attack mein seene mein dard, dil ka tez dhakna, sans phoolna aur marne ka dar hota hai — bilkul heart attack jaisa lagta hai. Magar panic attack mein dard aksar seene ke beech mein hota hai aur 10-20 minute mein kam ho jata hai. Heart attack mein dard baayen baazu, jabde ya peeth tak phelta hai aur halka nahi hota. Agar pehli baar aisa ho to HAMESHA hospital jaein — doctor ECG se confirm karega. Ek baar heart ruled out ho jaye to aglay episodes mein breathing exercises try karein.
Kya anxiety ki dawai aadat ban jaati hai?
SSRIs (Escitalopram, Sertraline) aadat nahi banate — yeh safe long-term dawaiyan hain jo doctor ki nigrani mein leni chahiyen. Benzodiazepines (Alprazolam/Xanax, Clonazepam) zarur aadat bana sakte hain agar lambe waqt tak ya bina doctor ke liye jayen. Doctor inhe sirf short-term (2-4 hafte) dete hain. Kabhi apni marzi se anxiety ki dawai shuru ya band na karein — achanak band karne se withdrawal symptoms ho sakte hain.
4-7-8 breathing technique kya hai?
4-7-8 breathing panic attack aur anxiety ke liye bohat effective technique hai: 4 second naak se sans andar lein, 7 second sans rok ke rakhein, 8 second moonh se dheere dheere sans bahar chhorein. Yeh cycle 3-4 baar repeat karein. Yeh technique nervous system ko calm karti hai aur 2-3 minute mein anxiety kam hone lagti hai. Roz practice karein taake zarurat ke waqt automatically yaad aaye.
Pakistan mein mental health treatment kitna mehnga hai?
Private psychiatrist ki fees Rs. 2,000-5,000 per visit hai. Government hospitals (JPMC, Mayo, PIMS) mein free ya Rs. 100-200 mein milta hai. SSRIs ki monthly cost Rs. 300-800 hai — bohat affordable. Online therapy platforms bhi available hain. Ilaaj AI pe bhi mental health ke baare mein doctor se mashwara kar sakte hain — affordable aur ghar baithe.
Kya anxiety apne aap theek ho sakti hai?
Halki anxiety (exam stress, naye kaam ki tension) aksar waqt ke saath theek ho jaati hai. Magar agar anxiety 6 mahine se zyada ho, roz ki zindagi mutasir ho rahi ho (kaam, rishtay, neend), ya panic attacks aa rahen hon to professional help zaruri hai. CBT therapy 60-80% cases mein kaam karti hai. Dawai ke saath to success rate aur zyada hai. Sharam mat karein — anxiety ek medical condition hai, kamzori nahi.
