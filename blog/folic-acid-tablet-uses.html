Folic Acid Tablet Uses — Hamal, Khoon Ki Kami Aur Neural Tube Defect Pakistan

Mukhtasar — Ahem Baatein
  • Folic acid = vitamin B9 — Pakistan mein pregnancy ki sab se zaruri supplement
  • Neural tube defects (brain aur spinal cord ki paidaishi kharaabi) rokti hai
  • Hamal se 3 mahine pehle shuru karein — pehle 28 din mein baby ka brain banta hai
  • Normal dose: 400–500mcg/day; high risk (diabetes, epilepsy drugs): 5mg/day
  • Zyada folic acid vitamin B12 ki kami chhupa sakti hai — B12 check karaana zaroori hai

Folic Acid Kya Hai?

Folic acid vitamin B9 ki synthetic form hai. Yeh DNA synthesis, cell division aur red blood cell banaane ke liye zaroori hai. Pakistan mein yeh pregnancy ki sab se ahem preventive supplement hai — neural tube defects (NTDs) rokne ke liye.

Neural tube defects brain aur spinal cord ki paidaishi kharabiyaan hain — jaise anencephaly (brain ka andar nahi banana) aur spina bifida (spinal cord ki problem). Yeh hamal ke pehle 28 dinon mein hoti hain — aksar jab hamal ka pata bhi nahi hota.

Folic Acid Ke Uses

1. Neural Tube Defect Prevention (Sab Se Ahem Use)

WHO aur Pakistan ke tamam gynecologists ki recommendation: hamal se pehle aur hamal ke pehle 12 hafte tak roz folic acid lena zaroori hai. Research sabit karta hai ke yeh NTDs ka risk 70% tak kam kar deti hai.

2. Megaloblastic Anaemia (Khoon Ki Kami)

Folic acid ki kami se megaloblastic anaemia hoti hai — jisme bade magar andar se kharaab red blood cells bante hain. Pregnancy mein bhi yeh common hai. Symptoms: kamzori, thakaawat, sans lene mein takleef.

3. Methotrexate Therapy

Methotrexate (rheumatoid arthritis, psoriasis) folate metabolism rokti hai. Folic acid saath dene se side effects (mouth ulcers, nausea) kam hote hain.

4. Homocysteine Kam Karna

Folic acid blood mein homocysteine level kam karti hai — jo heart disease ka risk factor hai. Folate + B6 + B12 milkar homocysteine metabolism karte hain.

Khulaasa

Folic acid Pakistan mein har pregnant khatoon ke liye zaroori supplement hai. Neural tube defects rokne ke liye hamal se teen mahine pehle shuru karna chahiye. Normal risk mein 400–500mcg, high risk mein 5mg. Zyada folic acid B12 ki kami chhupa sakti hai — isliye B12 check zaroori hai. Akela folic acid kaafi nahi — iron aur calcium bhi pregnancy mein zaroori hain.

Aksar Pooche Jane Waale Sawaalat

Folic acid hamal mein kab shuru karni chahiye?

Hamal se 3 mahine pehle. Baby ka brain pehle 28 dinon mein banta hai — jab hamal ka pata nahi hota. Isliye pre-conception se shuru karna zaroori hai.

Folic acid sirf pregnancy ke liye hai?

Nahi — anaemia, methotrexate therapy, homocysteine control ke liye bhi use hoti hai. Lekin pregnancy mein role sab se ahem aur proven hai.

Folic acid kitni dose leni chahiye pregnancy mein?

Normal risk: 400–500mcg/day. High risk (diabetes, epilepsy drugs, pehle NTD): 5mg/day — doctor ki prescription se.

Kya folic acid se vitamin B12 ki kami chhup jaati hai?

Haan — zyada folic acid B12 deficiency ki anaemia theek kar deti hai magar nerve damage jaari rehti hai. Folic acid shuru karne se pehle B12 check karaayein.

Folic acid khaane ke saath leni chahiye ya khali pet?

Dono theek hai — water-soluble vitamin hai. Nausea ho to khane ke saath behtar. Subah breakfast ke saath routine ke liye aasaan hai.
