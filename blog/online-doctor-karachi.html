Online Doctor Karachi: 24/7 Free Consultation

Karachi is Pakistan's largest city — and despite having more doctors per capita than smaller cities, getting timely medical care still means hours of traffic, long clinic queues, and 1,500-5,000 PKR per visit. Online doctors solve all of this.

Karachi's healthcare reality in 2026:
- 22+ million residents
- Average commute to a clinic: 45-90 minutes one-way
- Average clinic wait time: 1-3 hours
- Specialist consultation: 3,000-7,000 PKR
- Frequent traffic snarls in Saddar, Tariq Road, II Chundrigar
- Long working hours leave little time for clinic visits

For most acute, common conditions, online consultation is faster, cheaper, and equally effective.

When Online Won't Work — Go to a Clinic or Hospital

For these situations, online won't help:

Heart attack symptoms:
Crushing chest pain, sweating, pain radiating to left arm or jaw — call 1122 immediately or rush to:
- Aga Khan University Hospital (AKUH) — 24/7 emergency
- Liaquat National Hospital
- South City Hospital (Clifton)

Stroke signs:
Face droop, arm weakness, slurred speech — also 1122/AKUH/LNH ER.

Severe trauma, severe bleeding, anaphylaxis — emergency only.

Initial cancer workup, fractures, lumps — require physical exam.
