Bachon Ko Thos Ghiza Kab Aur Kaise Dein — Weaning Guide

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Thos ghiza (solids) sirf 6 mahine complete hone ke baad shuru karein — WHO/AAP ki guideline
  • Pehle thin puree (seb, kela, khichri), phir 9 mahine pe mashed family food
  • 4-day rule: ek nayi cheez de kar 4 din allergy reaction observe karein
  • 1 saal se pehle shehed, whole nuts, cow milk as drink, namak/cheeni avoid karein
  • Maa ka doodh ya formula minimum 1 saal tak jari rakhain

Weaning Kya Hai Aur Kab Shuru Karein

Weaning ka matlab hai bachay ko sirf doodh se thos ghiza (solids) ki taraf le jaana. World Health Organization (WHO) aur American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP) dono kehte hain: pehle 6 mahine exclusive breastfeeding — sirf maa ka doodh, paani bhi nahi. 6 mahine ke baad solids shuru karein, magar doodh band na karein.

4 mahine se pehle solids dene se gut ready nahi hota, choking aur food allergies ka risk barhta hai, aur kidneys par bojh parta hai. Bilkul 6 mahine ka intezar karna safest hai.

Weaning Stages — Quick Reference
0-6 mahineSirf maa ka doodh / formula
6 mahineThin purees, single-ingredient, 1-2 chamche
6-9 mahineThicker purees, mashed food, soft finger food
9-12 mahineMashed family food, chote soft tukde
1+ saalFamily food chopped, full diet, cow milk allowed

Bachay Ki Readiness Ki Nishaniyaan

Sirf umar dekh kar shuru na karein — yeh signs bhi check karein:

  • Bachay ka sir control hai aur sahara le kar beth sakta hai
  • Tongue thrust reflex khatm — chamcha muh mein daalo to bahar nahi nikalta
  • Khaane mein dilchaspi — aap khate ho to dekhe, muh kholay, haath barhaaye
  • Bhook barh gayi — sirf doodh se pet nahi bharta, raat ko zyada uthe
  • Wazan double ho gaya hai paida hone ke wazan se

6 Mahine: Pehla Khaana Kaise Dein

Pehli baar bohot patli puree dein — ek chamcha thin apple puree, mashed kela paani milake, ya patli khichri (sirf chawal + thora paani, ghee nahi). Single-ingredient rakhain taake agar reaction ho to wajah samajh aaye.

4-Day Rule: Ek nayi cheez de kar 4 din tak doosri nayi cheez na dein. Is dauran rash, ulti, dast, ya saans ki taqleef check karein. Reaction nahi to next item try karein.

Iron-rich foods 6 mahine se zaruri hain kyunke maa ke doodh mein iron 6 mahine ke baad kam parta hai. Daal ki patli puree, anday ki zardi mashed, palak puree, aur khajoor mashed — yeh sab acche choices hain.

6-9 Mahine: Thicker Food Aur Finger Foods

Ab puree thori thick kar sakte hain. Mashed kela, dahi (plain, no sugar), avocado, sabzi ki khichri, mashed aloo, daal-chawal mash — sab try karein. Finger food bhi shuru — soft pakaye huey gajar ke tukde, kelay ke pieces, ubla anda yolk.

Important: allergens (anda, peanut paste thin layer roti par, dairy) early introduce karna — 6-9 mahine — allergy ka risk kam karta hai, late introduction se nahi. Yeh purani guideline change ho chuki hai.

9-12 Mahine: Family Food Ki Taraf

Ab Pakistani family meals work karte hain — sirf mashed ya choti tukron mein. Dal-chawal mashed, sabzi ka qeema, mashed roti paani mein bhigo kar, dahi, soft khichri ghee ke saath, anda fully cooked. Thora ghee theek hai, lekin namak aur masala minimal rakhain.

Cup se paani peena bhi sikhayein. Doodh ka feeder dheere dheere kam karne ki koshish karein magar maa ka doodh 1 saal tak jari rakhain.

Abhi Doctor Se Milein

Yeh Cheezein 1 Saal Se Pehle Bilkul Mat Dein

  • Shehed: Botulism ka khatra — bohot khatarnaak
  • Whole nuts (badam, akhrot, pista): Choking risk — paste form mein de sakte hain
  • Gaye/bhains ka doodh as drink: Iron absorption block karta hai, anemia hoti hai (cooking mein thori amount theek)
  • Namak aur cheeni: Kidneys aur taste buds par bura asar
  • Fruit juice: Sirf cheeni, no fiber — dant kharab, motapa
  • Adha pakka anda / kacha doodh: Salmonella infection
  • Chai, coffee, soft drinks: Bilkul nahi

Choking Aur Allergy Se Bachao

Bachay ko hamesha upright bitha kar khilayein, lait kar nahi. TV ya phone band rakhain — distraction se choking hoti hai. Grapes, popcorn, hard candy, whole nuts 4 saal tak avoid karein.

Allergic reaction ki nishaniyaan: muh ya chehre par rash, hont sujna, ulti baar baar, saans phoolna, sust ho jana. Saans ki taqleef ya sujan ho to foran hospital lay jaein — yeh anaphylaxis ho sakta hai.

Pakistani Context — Aam Sawal

Ghutti aur janam ghutti: Modern medicine support nahi karti — gut bacteria disturb hote hain. Pehle 6 mahine sirf maa ka doodh.

Lassi: 1 saal ke baad theek — pehle nahi, kyunke yeh cow milk based hai.

Roti/naan: 9 mahine ke baad chote soft tukde paani mein bhigo kar dein.

Dahi: 6 mahine ke baad plain dahi (no sugar) safe aur acchi probiotic source hai.

Ghee: 9 mahine ke baad thori quantity (1/4 chamcha) khichri ya daal mein add kar sakte hain.

Kab Doctor Se Milein

  • Bachay solids reject karta hai 2 hafte se zyada
  • Allergic reaction — rash, sujan, breathing trouble
  • Wazan barh nahi raha
  • Persistent ulti ya dast solids shuru karne ke baad
  • Choking episode hua ho — review karwain feeding technique
  • Bachay anemic lage (pale, sust)

Conclusion

Weaning aaram aaram ka safar hai — har bachay ki speed alag. 6 mahine se start karein, signs follow karein, 4-day rule lagaein, aur 1 saal tak doodh jari rakhain. Frequently Asked Questions

Bachay ko thos ghiza kab shuru karwani chahiye?
WHO aur AAP ki guidelines ke mutabiq 6 mahine complete hone ke baad solids shuru karwain. Is se pehle sirf maa ka doodh ya formula. 4 mahine se pehle thos ghiza ka gut tayar nahi hota aur allergy ya choking ka risk barhta hai. Har bachay ki readiness alag hoti hai — signs check karein.
Bachay ki readiness ki nishaniyaan kya hain?
Bachay ka sir control ho (sahara se beth jaye), tongue thrust reflex khatm ho gaya ho (chamcha bahar na nikalay), khaane mein dilchaspi (aap khaate waqt dekhe ya muh kholay), bhook zyada lagti ho aur doodh kafi na ho. Yeh sab 5-7 mahine ke darmiyan zahir hote hain.
Pehli baar konsa khaana dein? 4-day rule kya hai?
Pehli baar single-ingredient thin puree dein — seb (apple) puree, kelay (banana) ki mashed, patli khichri, ya rice cereal. 4-day rule yeh hai ke ek nayi cheez de kar 4 din wait karein taake agar koi allergy reaction (rash, ulti, dast) ho to pata chal jaye. Phir doosri cheez try karein.
1 saal se kam bachon ke liye kaunsa khaana khatarnaak hai?
Shehed (honey) 1 saal se pehle bilkul mat dein — botulism ka khatra hai. Whole nuts (badam, akhrot) choking risk. Gaye ka doodh as a drink 1 saal se pehle anemia laata hai (cooking mein thori quantity ok). Namak aur cheeni 1 saal se pehle minimal — kidneys par bojh. Fruit juice 1 saal se pehle bilkul nahi. Adha pakka anda yolk salmonella risk.
Pakistani family food bachay ko kab dena shuru karein?
9 mahine ke baad family food mashed kar ke dein — dal-chawal mash, sabzi ka qeema, dahi, roti ke chote tukde paani mein bhigo kar. Thori ghee theek hai. Namak aur masala kam rakhain. 1 saal ke baad lassi aur cooked cow milk add kar sakte hain. Mirchi, achaar, chai aur soft drinks bilkul avoid karein. Iron-rich foods (anday, palak, daal, khajoor) 6 mahine se zaruri hain.
