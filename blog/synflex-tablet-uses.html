Synflex Tablet Uses, Dosage and Side Effects in Pakistan

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Synflex = naproxen sodium, fast-acting NSAID painkiller
  • Brufen se zyada der tak asar — 8-12 ghante
  • Joint pain, period cramps, muscle pain, daant dard mein use
  • Khaane ke saath lein — stomach ki hifazat
  • Kidney disease, ulcer, aur pregnancy ke teesre mahine mein avoid karein

Synflex Kya Hai?

Synflex naproxen sodium ka brand name hai — jo naproxen ka sodium salt hai aur jald absorb hoti hai. Yeh ek NSAID (dard aur sojan ki dawai) hai jo prostaglandins block karke kaam karti hai. Brufen (ibuprofen) ki compared mein jo 6 ghante tak karti hai, Synflex 8-12 ghante asar karti hai — isliye din mein sirf do baar leni parti hai.

Synflex Tablet Kis Liye Use Hoti Hai?

1. Muscular Pain Aur Sports Injury

Muscles ka dard, moch, strain, aur ziada mehnat se aane wala dard.

2. Joro Ka Dard

Osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, aur gout ke flares mein bohat effective.

3. Mahwari Ka Dard (Dysmenorrhea)

Period cramps mein bohat effective — prostaglandins kam karta hai jo dard ka sabab bante hain. Mahwari shuru hone se 1-2 din pehle shuru karein behtar nataij ke liye.

4. Daant Dard

Dental procedure ke baad aur toothache mein use hoti hai.

5. Kamar Dard Aur Gardan Dard

Reeth aur gardan ka dard — acute aur chronic dono mein.

6. Sir Dard Aur Migraine

Tension headache aur migraine dono mein rahat de sakti hai.

7. Bukhar (Secondary)

Bukhar kam karne ki salahiyat bhi hai — lekin sirf bukhar ke liye paracetamol behtar hai.

Side Effects

Aam:

  • Pet kharab, matli, seenay mein jalan
  • Sir dard, chakkar
  • Thodi sojan (especially paaon mein)
  • Neend aana

Khatarnak:

  • GI bleeding / peptic ulcer — ziada muddat tak lene ka sabse bada khatra
  • Gurde ka nuqsan — ziada dose ya paani ki kami mein
  • Dil ka khatra — chronic use mein heart attack/stroke ka risk barh sakta hai
  • Allergy — rash, sojan, saans lene mein takleef
  • Jigar ka nuqsan — nadir

Warning Signs — Foran Doctor Se Milein

  • Kaala ya tar pakhana, ya qay mein khoon (GI bleed)
  • Achanak seenay mein shadeed dard (cardiac event)
  • Chehra, honth, ya gale ki sojan (allergy)
  • Peshab mein bohot kami (gurde ka nuqsan)
  • Aankhon ya jild ka peela hona (jigar)

Kab Avoid Karein?

  • Active peptic ulcer ya GI bleeding
  • Shadeed kidney disease (GFR < 30)
  • Shadeed liver disease
  • Heart failure
  • Pregnancy ka teesra teemaha (bachche ki blood vessel band kar sakti hai)
  • Aspirin-sensitive asthma
  • Kisi bhi NSAID se allergy
  • 12 saal se kam umra ke bachche

Stomach Ki Hifazat

PPI (Risek 20, Pantop) saath lena zaroori hota hai jab:

  • Umra 60 saal se zyada ho
  • 7 din se zyada le rahe hon
  • Khoon patla karne ki dawaiyaan (aspirin, warfarin) bhi le rahe hon
  • Pehle peptic ulcer hua ho
  • Saath mein steroids bhi le rahe hon

Drug Interactions

  • Aspirin / Loprin: Saath mat lein — GI bleeding ka khatra doghna aur dono ki asar kami
  • Warfarin: Khoon behne ka khatra barh jata hai — INR monitor karein
  • Lithium: Naproxen lithium ka level barha deta hai — toxicity ka khatra
  • ACE inhibitors / ARBs: Blood pressure control mein kami, gurde ka khatra
  • Diuretics: Paani ki dawai ki asar kam ho jati hai
  • Methotrexate: Toxicity ka khatra
  • SSRIs: GI bleeding ka khatra barh jata hai

Khaas Halaat Mein Istemal

  • Pregnancy: Pehle aur doosre teemahi mein thodi muddat ke liye doctor ki rai se le sakte hain; teesre teemahi mein bilkul mana hai
  • Breastfeeding: Avoid karein — dudh mein dakhil hoti hai
  • Buzurg (60+): Sab se kam mufeed dose lein aur PPI zaroor shamil karein; gurde kamzor hote hain
  • Dengue: DENGUE MEIN SYNFLEX YA KOI BHI NSAID HARGIZ MAT LEIN — andar se khoon behne ka shadeed khatra; sirf paracetamol lein

Conclusion

Synflex (naproxen sodium) Pakistan ki ek behad mufeed painkiller hai — Brufen se zyada der tak asar karti hai aur joro ka dard, period cramps, aur musculoskeletal injuries mein effective hai. Magar iske GI, gurde, aur dil ke serious khatray hain — khaas taur par ziada muddat tak lene mein. Hamesha khaane ke saath lein, kidney disease aur ulcer mein avoid karein, aur hafte se zyada lena ho toh PPI bhi shamil karein. Sahi tashkhees aur personalized ilaj ke liye Ilaaj AI use karein.

Frequently Asked Questions

Synflex 275 kis liye use hoti hai?

Synflex 275 mg halke se darmiyana dard ke liye use hoti hai — joro ka dard (arthritis, gout), muscle pain, period cramps, daant dard, kamar dard, aur sir dard. Yeh ek NSAID hai jo prostaglandins kam karke kaam karti hai. 275 mg Pakistan mein bina nuskhe ke milti hai.

Synflex aur Brufen mein kya farq hai?

Dono NSAID hain lekin Synflex (naproxen sodium) 8-12 ghante kaam karti hai jabke Brufen (ibuprofen) 4-6 ghante. Isliye Synflex din mein do baar aur Brufen teen baar leni parti hai. Period pain aur arthritis ke liye Synflex zyada pasand ki jati hai. Magar Synflex ka zyada waqt blood mein rehna stomach ke liye thoda zyada risk deta hai.

Kya Synflex khali pait le sakte hain?

Nahi — hamesha khaane, doodh, ya antacid ke saath lein. NSAIDs stomach ki lining ko irritate karti hain aur khali pait lene se ulcer ho sakta hai. Agar heartburn ya kaale rang ka pakhana aaye toh foran band karein aur doctor se milein.

Kya Synflex pregnancy mein safe hai?

Synflex pregnancy mein generally avoid ki jati hai. Pehle aur doosre teemahi mein sirf doctor ki dekhrekh mein le sakte hain. Teesre teemahi (28 hafte ke baad) mein Synflex bilkul mana hai — yeh bachchay ki ek khas blood vessel (ductus arteriosus) band kar sakti hai. Pregnancy mein paracetamol sab se safe painkiller hai.

Kya Synflex dengue mein le sakte hain?

Hargiz nahi — dengue mein Synflex ya koi bhi NSAID bilkul mat lein. Dengue mein platelets kam ho jate hain aur NSAIDs andar se khoon behne ka khatra shadeed barha deti hain. Sirf paracetamol use karein.
