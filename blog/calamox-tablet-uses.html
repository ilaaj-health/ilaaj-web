Calamox Tablet Uses, Dosage and Side Effects in Pakistan

TL;DR — Aham Baatein
  • Calamox = amoxicillin + clavulanic acid (Augmentin jaisi dawai, alag manufacturer)
  • Chest, kaan, throat, UTI, skin infection ke liye
  • Dast (diarrhea) bohat aam — khaane ke saath lein
  • Penicillin allergy mein kabhi nahi leni
  • Poora course complete karein — beech mein mat rokein

Calamox Kya Hai?

Calamox Hilton Pharma ki taraf se banai jati hai aur isme do active ingredients hain:

  1. Amoxicillin — ek broad-spectrum penicillin antibiotic jo bacteria ki cell wall synthesis ko rokkar unhe maarta hai
  2. Clavulanic acid — ek beta-lactamase inhibitor jo amoxicillin ko resistant bacteria se bachata hai

Bohat se bacteria beta-lactamase enzymes banate hain jo simple amoxicillin ko tor dete hain. Clavulanic acid in enzymes ko block karta hai aur amoxicillin ki effectiveness kaafi zyada bacteria ke khilaf restore ho jati hai. Calamox pharmacologically Augmentin (GSK) se bilkul ek jaisi hai — ek hi dawai, alag manufacturer.

Calamox Tablet Uses

1. Lower Respiratory Tract Infections (Seene Ki Infection)

Community-acquired pneumonia (halki se darmiyani), chronic bronchitis ka acute worsening.

2. Upper Respiratory Tract Infections (Naak Aur Gale Ki Infection)

Acute bacterial sinusitis, tonsillitis jab doosri antibiotics kaam na karein.

3. Otitis Media (Kaan Ki Infection)

Khaas tor par jab treatment fail ho ya beta-lactamase paida karne wale organisms hon.

4. Urinary Tract Infections (UTI)

Plain amoxicillin se resistant organisms ki wajah se hone wali uncomplicated aur complicated UTIs.

5. Skin Aur Soft Tissue Infections

Cellulitis, zakhm ki infection, infected eczema.

6. Dental Infections (Daant Ki Infection)

Dental abscess, periodontal infection (jahan bacteria beta-lactamase banate hon).

7. Janwar Ka Kaatna (Animal Bites)

Kuttay aur billi ke kaatne ke liye first-line choice (8 ghante ke andar prophylactic ilaaj).

8. Diabetic Foot Infections (Halki Se Darmiyani)

Wisi ilaaj ke hisse ke tor par.

Calamox Se Dast Kyun Itni Zyada Hoti Hai?

Calamox plain amoxicillin se zyada dast paida karti hai — iska sabab khaas tor par clavulanic acid hai, jo:

  • Gut microbiome ko akela amoxicillin se zyada disturb karta hai
  • Gut mein direct osmotic asar rakhta hai
  • Gut motility ko stimulate karta hai

Dast kam karne ke tareeqe:

  • Hamesha khaane ke saath lein
  • Saath mein probiotic lein (Lactobacillus GG, Saccharomyces boulardii) — evidence mojood hai
  • Pani zyada piyen
  • Agar dast shadeed ho, mein khoon aa raha ho, ya band hone ke 3 din baad bhi ho toh doctor se milein (C. difficile rule out karein)

Side Effects

Aam:

  • Dast (sabse zyada — 30% tak users mein)
  • Matli, ulti
  • Pait mein dard
  • Rash (halka morbilliform rash)
  • Khawateen mein vaginal yeast infection (antibiotic vaginal flora ko disturb karta hai)

Sanjeedah:

  • Shadeed allergic reaction / anaphylaxis — medical emergency; penicillin users mein 0.01–0.05%
  • Stevens-Johnson syndrome — rare, shadeed skin reaction
  • Clostridioides difficile (C. diff) infection — shadeed antibiotic-associated diarrhea
  • Cholestatic jaundice — Calamox (co-amoxiclav) drug-induced liver injury ki sabse aam antibiotic hai — band karne ke baad aam tor par theek ho jata hai
  • Agranulocytosis — bohat rare, white blood cells kam ho jati hain

Penicillin Allergy Warning

Agar aapko penicillin se allergy hai toh Calamox bilkul mat lein. Penicillin allergy ki nishaniyan: pehle use ke baad rash, hives, sujan, ya saans lene mein takleef. True penicillin allergy utni zyada nahi jitni report hoti hai (bohat log drug rash ko allergy samajh lete hain), lekin confirmed allergy mein yeh bilkul mana hai. Mutabadil antibiotics: azithromycin (Azomax), doxycycline, ya fluoroquinolones infection ki nau par munhasir.

Calamox Kise Nahi Leni Chahiye

  • Penicillin ya beta-lactam allergy
  • Pehle co-amoxiclav ki wajah se jaundice ya liver masail
  • Shadeed kidney ki kamzori (dose adjustment zaroor hai)
  • Mononucleosis (glandular fever) — aminopenicillins is condition mein rash paida karte hain

Drug Interactions

  • Warfarin: Khoon patla karne ka asar barh jata hai — INR monitor karna zaroor hai
  • Methotrexate: Excretion kam hoti hai — toxicity ka khatra
  • Oral contraceptive pills: Effectiveness thodi (chhoti) kam ho sakti hai — backup contraception use karein
  • Doosri antibiotics (fluoroquinolones): Ek hi infection ke liye aam tor par combine nahi karte; doctor se salah lein
  • Allopurinol (gout ki dawai): Ampicillin/amoxicillin combinations ke saath skin rash ki possibility barh jati hai

Poora Course Complete Karein

Yeh bohat zaroor hai. Theek mehsoos hone par Calamox jaldi band karna:

  • Tamam bacteria nahi maarta (adhuri eradication)
  • Resistant bacteria bach jate hain — aur mushkil infections banate hain
  • Relapse ho sakta hai jisme zyada strong antibiotics ki zaroorat pad sakti hai

Hamesha 5–7 din (ya sinusitis/dental ke liye 10–14 din) ka poora course complete karein.

Khulasa

Calamox (co-amoxiclav) Pakistan ki sabse effective broad-spectrum antibiotics mein se ek hai — clavulanic acid amoxicillin ki taqat resistant bacteria ke khilaf wapis dilata hai. Yeh chest, kaan, throat, UTI, skin infections, aur dental infections ka kaargir ilaaj karta hai. Lekin dast bohat aam hai (khaane ke saath aur probiotic lein), penicillin allergy mein bilkul mana hai, aur poora course complete karna lazmi hai. Resistant ya shadeed infections mein khud dawai lene ki bajaye doctor se milein. Sahi assessment ke liye Ilaaj AI use karein.

Aksar Puchhe Jane Wale Sawalat

Calamox 625 kis kaam aati hai?

Calamox 625 mg (amoxicillin 500 mg + clavulanic acid 125 mg) bacterial infections ke liye use hoti hai jisme community-acquired pneumonia, sinusitis, kaan ki infection (otitis media), plain amoxicillin se resistant UTIs, skin aur soft tissue infections, dental abscess, aur janwar ke kaatne ka ilaaj shamil hai. Yeh broad-spectrum antibiotic un organisms ke khilaf kaam karti hai jo simple amoxicillin ke khilaf resistance develop kar chuke hain.

Calamox se dast kyun hoti hai?

Calamox plain amoxicillin se zyada dast paida karti hai kyunki isme clavulanic acid hoti hai jo gut bacteria ko disturb karti hai aur gut motility par direct asar karti hai. 30% tak users ko dast hoti hai. Calamox khaane ke saath lene se yeh kaafi kam ho jati hai. Probiotic (Lactobacillus ya Saccharomyces boulardii) saath lena bhi faydemand hai. Agar dast mein khoon ho ya bohat shadeed ho toh doctor se milein.

Kya Calamox aur Augmentin ek hi dawai hai?

Haan — pharmacologically dono bilkul ek jaise hain. Dono mein amoxicillin + clavulanic acid (co-amoxiclav) ek hi matra mein hoti hai. Augmentin GSK ki original brand hai jabke Calamox Hilton Pharma Pakistan ki hai. Dono ki efficacy barabar hai. Calamox aam tor par sasti hoti hai.

Calamox kitne din leni chahiye?

Zyada tar infections mein 5–7 din ka course hota hai. Dental aur sinus infections mein 10–14 din lag sakte hain. Theek mehsoos hone par bhi beech mein mat rokein — adhura course tamam bacteria nahi maarta aur resistant relapse ho sakta hai. Hamesha poora prescribed course complete karein.

Kya penicillin allergy mein Calamox le sakte hain?

Nahi — agar aapko penicillin allergy confirmed hai toh Calamox (ya koi bhi amoxicillin/ampicillin antibiotic) bilkul nahi leni chahiye. Calamox ek penicillin-class antibiotic hai aur usi tarha ki allergic reaction trigger kar sakti hai, jisme jaan-lewa anaphylaxis bhi shamil hai. Koi bhi antibiotic shuru karne se pehle apne doctor ya pharmacist ko drug allergies zaroor batayein. Alternatives mein azithromycin, doxycycline, ya fluoroquinolones shamil hain infection ki nau par munhasir.
