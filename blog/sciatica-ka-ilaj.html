Sciatica Ka Ilaj — Kamar Se Paon Tak Dard Ka Treatment

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Sciatica sciatic nerve dabne se hota hai — kamar se paon tak shooting dard
  • Slipped/herniated disc sabse aam wajah hai
  • 90% cases surgery ke baghair theek hote hain — 6-12 hafton mein
  • Physiotherapy aur exercises sabse important treatment hai
  • Prolonged bed rest GALAT hai — halki harkat zaruri
  • Cauda equina (pesab/potty control loss) = EMERGENCY — foran hospital

Sciatica Kya Hai?

Sciatica ek aisi condition hai jis mein sciatic nerve — jism ki sabse lambi aur moti nerve — dabti ya irritate hoti hai, jis se kamar ke nichle hisse se hip, thigh, calf aur paon tak dard jata hai. Yeh dard aksar ek taraf (one side) hota hai aur shooting, burning, ya electric shock jaisa feel hota hai. Sciatica khud koi bimari nahi — yeh ek SYMPTOM hai kisi aur underlying maslay ka, aksar slipped disc ka. Pakistan mein kamar dard bohat common hai magar log aksar sciatica ko samajh nahi paate aur galat ilaj karte hain.

Sciatica Ki Wajuhaat (Causes)

Herniated/Slipped Disc — sabse common cause. Kamar ke nechle hisse (L4-L5, L5-S1) ki disc apni jagah se khisak kar nerve pe pressure dalti hai. Spinal Stenosis — reedh ki haddi ki nali tang ho jaati hai (age-related). Piriformis Syndrome — hip ke andar ki muscle (piriformis) sciatic nerve ko dabata hai. Bone Spur (Osteophyte) — haddi ki abnormal growth nerve pe pressure. Spondylolisthesis — ek vertebra aage khisak jaata hai. Rare causes: tumor, infection, injury.

Sciatica Ki Symptoms

  • Kamar ke nichle hisse se hip ke zariye thigh, calf aur paon tak dard — aksar ek taraf
  • Shooting, burning, ya tingling (jaise current lag raha ho)
  • Baith kar ya khara ho kar worse hota hai
  • Khansne ya cheenkne pe bhi dard barh sakta hai
  • Pair mein ya paon mein sunn hona (numbness)
  • Kamzori — pair uthane mein mushkil
  • Kuch logon mein sirf kamar mein dard hota hai, kuch mein sirf pair mein — pattern har case mein alag ho sakta hai

Risk Factors

  • Desk jobs — lambi der baith kar kaam karna
  • Heavy lifting — galat technique se (kamar jhuka kar uthana)
  • Age 30-50 — disc herniation is age group mein zyada
  • Diabetes — nerve damage ka risk barhata hai
  • Motapa — extra weight spine pe pressure dalta hai
  • Sedentary lifestyle — exercise na karna
  • Pakistan mein risk factors: office culture, manual labor, aur physiotherapy ki awareness kam

Sciatica Ki Diagnosis

Doctor clinical exam se aksar diagnose kar leta hai. Straight Leg Raise (SLR) test — seedha pair uthane pe dard hona sciatica confirm karta hai. MRI — disc herniation ya spinal stenosis clearly dikhata hai (best imaging test). X-ray — haddi ka structure dekhne ke liye magar disc nahi dikhata. EMG/NCS — nerve damage ki severity check (agar zarurat ho). Important: Har kamar dard sciatica NAHI hota. Doctor se proper diagnosis zaruri hai.

Sciatica Ka Ilaj — Bina Surgery (Conservative Treatment)

90% sciatica cases surgery ke baghair 6-12 hafton mein theek hote hain.

Medicines:

  • Naproxen/Ibuprofen (NSAIDs) — inflammation kam karte hain
  • Pregabalin/Gabapentin — nerve pain ke liye best
  • Muscle relaxants — agar muscle spasm ho
  • Short course oral steroids — severe cases mein

Physiotherapy — SABSE IMPORTANT:

  • McKenzie exercises — disc ko wapas position mein lane mein madad
  • Bird-dog exercise — core stability
  • Knee-to-chest stretch — nerve tension kam
  • Piriformis stretch
  • Walking — halka chalna bohat faida mand

Hot/cold therapy — pehle 48 ghanton mein ice, phir heat. Epidural Steroid Injection — severe cases mein jab medicines kaam na karein.

Bed Rest Ka Myth — Galat Hai!

Pakistan mein sabse bari ghalat fehmi yeh hai ke sciatica mein "complete bed rest" karna chahiye. Yeh GALAT hai. Prolonged bed rest muscles ko aur kamzor karta hai aur recovery slow karti hai. Pehle 1-2 din agar dard bohat shadeed ho to rest theek hai. Uske baad halki harkat, walking, aur gentle exercises shuru karni chahiye. Active rehna recovery ko TEZI deta hai.

Surgery Kab Zaruri Hai?

Surgery sirf in situations mein consider hoti hai:

  • Cauda Equina Syndrome — EMERGENCY: pesab ya potty ka control kho jana, genital area mein sunn hona. Agar yeh symptoms hon to FORAN hospital jaein — delay se permanent damage ho sakta hai.
  • Progressive weakness — pair ki muscles lagatar kamzor ho rahi hon
  • 3+ mahine conservative treatment se koi farq na ho

Surgery types: Microdiscectomy — sabse common, chhota cut, disc ka nikla hua hissa nikaaltay hain. Laminectomy — spinal stenosis mein. Success rate 85-90%.

Sciatica Se Bachao (Prevention)

  • Core strengthening — pet aur kamar ke muscles mazboot karna sabse zaroori hai
  • Sahi lifting technique — ghutne jhuka kar uthayein, kamar nahi
  • Ergonomic chair — office mein lumbar support wali kursi
  • Weight control — extra wazan spine pe burden hai
  • Regular exercise — swimming, walking, yoga
  • Har 30-45 minute baad uth kar chalna agar desk job hai
  • Smoking chorna — smoking disc degeneration barhata hai

Pakistan Mein Sciatica Ka Masla

Pakistan mein physiotherapy ki awareness bohat kam hai. Log pehle hakeem, chiropractor ya self-medication try karte hain. Prolonged bed rest karna — galat hai. NSAIDs zyada dose mein bina doctor ke — pet kharab hota hai. MRI ke baghair surgery karwa lena — galat hai. Sahi approach: doctor se diagnosis, physiotherapy exercises, aur medicines saath mein. Surgery last option hai.

Conclusion

Sciatica ka dard bohat takleef deh hai magar 90% cases surgery ke baghair theek hote hain. Physiotherapy aur exercises sabse important hai — bed rest myth hai. Frequently Asked Questions

Sciatica ka dard kitne din mein theek hota hai?
Zyada tar cases 6-12 hafton mein theek hote hain conservative treatment (medicines + physiotherapy) se. Halke cases 2-4 hafton mein bhi theek ho sakte hain. Agar 3 mahine se zyada dard rahe aur improvement na ho to doctor surgery discuss kar sakta hai. 90% patients ko surgery ki zarurat nahi parti.
Sciatica mein kaunsi exercise best hai?
McKenzie extension exercises, knee-to-chest stretch, bird-dog exercise, aur piriformis stretch sabse effective hain. Walking bhi bohat faaida mand hai. Core strengthening exercises (planks, bridges) long-term prevention ke liye zaroori hain. Exercises physiotherapist ki guidance mein shuru karein.
Kya sciatica mein bed rest karna chahiye?
Prolonged bed rest GALAT hai — yeh muscles ko kamzor karta hai aur recovery slow karti hai. Pehle 1-2 din agar dard bohat shadeed ho to thora rest theek hai. Uske baad halki harkat aur gentle exercises zaruri hain. Active rehna recovery ko tezi deta hai.
Sciatica mein kaunsi dawa lein?
Doctor aksar Naproxen ya Ibuprofen (inflammation ke liye), Pregabalin ya Gabapentin (nerve pain ke liye), aur muscle relaxants recommend karta hai. Apni marzi se dawa na lein — khaaskar NSAIDs zyada dose mein pet kharab karte hain. Doctor se proper prescription lein.
Sciatica ka operation kab zaruri hai?
Surgery sirf tab zaruri hai jab: pesab/potty ka control kho jaye (EMERGENCY — foran hospital), pair ki muscles lagatar kamzor hon, ya 3+ mahine treatment se koi farq na ho. 90% cases bina surgery theek hote hain. Microdiscectomy sabse common surgery hai jis ki success rate 85-90% hai.
