Kya Peshawar mein online doctor legally valid prescription deta hai?
Haan, har prescription ek licensed PMDC doctor review aur approve karta hai — isliye yeh legally valid hoti hai aur Peshawar ki kisi bhi pharmacy se dawai mil sakti hai.
Peshawar mein online consultation kitni jaldi milti hai?
Average 15-20 minute mein poora process complete ho jata hai. Emergency situations ke liye yeh replacement nahi hai.
Kya Hayatabad se bhi Ilaaj AI use ho sakta hai?
Haan, University Town se Hayatabad, Cantt se Gulbahar tak — internet connection ke saath Peshawar ke kisi bhi hisse se use kar sakte hain.
