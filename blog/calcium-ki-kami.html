Calcium Ki Kami — Haddiyon Ki Kamzori Ka Ilaj Pakistan Mein

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Body ka 99% calcium haddiyon aur daanton mein hota hai
  • Pakistan mein khaaskar women mein calcium deficiency bohat aam hai
  • Vitamin D ki kami saath mein hoti hai — calcium absorb nahi hota
  • Doodh, dahi, paneer, channay, til calcium ke ache sources hain
  • Adults ko rozana 1000mg calcium chahiye, 50+ ko 1200mg
  • Osteoporosis khamosh bimari hai — fracture hone tak pata nahi chalta

Calcium Body Mein Kya Karta Hai?

Calcium aap ke jism ka sab se zyada paya jane wala mineral hai. Iska 99% haddiyon aur daanton mein hota hai — yeh unki mazbooti aur structure ka bunyadi hissa hai. Magar calcium ka kaam sirf haddiyan nahi:

  • Muscles contract aur relax hone ke liye calcium zaruri hai — dil ki dharkan bhi isi se chalti hai
  • Heart rhythm regulate karta hai
  • Nerve signals brain se body tak bhejne mein madad karta hai
  • Blood clotting mein zaruri kirdar ada karta hai — zakhm bharne ke liye

Agar aap ki diet mein calcium kam ho to body apni zarurat poori karne ke liye haddiyon se calcium nikalna shuru kar deti hai. Yeh silsila lamba chale to haddiyan andar se khaali aur kamzor ho jati hain — isko osteoporosis kehte hain.

Calcium Ki Kami Ki Wajuhaat

Pakistan mein calcium deficiency ki kai wajuhaat hain:

  • Diet mein calcium kam: khaaskar un logon mein jo dairy products kam khate hain ya lactose intolerant hain
  • Vitamin D ki kami saath mein: Pakistan mein pardah aur indoor lifestyle ki wajah se dhoop kam milti hai — Vitamin D ke bina calcium absorb hi nahi hota chahe kitna bhi khao
  • Post-menopause estrogen drop: women mein menopause ke baad estrogen kam ho jata hai jo calcium absorption mein madad karta tha — isliye 50+ women mein osteoporosis bohat aam hai
  • Pregnancy aur lactation: baby ki haddiyon ke liye maa ke jism se calcium ki demand bohat barh jati hai
  • Thyroid problems: parathyroid gland ka masla calcium regulation bigaar deta hai
  • Steroids ka lamba istemal: jo log asthma ya joint pain ke liye lamba arsa steroids lete hain unki haddiyan kamzor hoti hain

Calcium Ki Kami Ki Alamat (Symptoms)

Calcium ki kami dheere dheere hoti hai — shuru mein pata nahi chalta. Yeh common symptoms hain:

  • Haddiyon aur joro mein dard — khaaskar kamar, ghutno aur ediyon mein
  • Muscle cramps raat ko — tangon mein akran, khaaskar sote waqt
  • Daant kamzor hona — cavity zyada hona, daant tootna
  • Nakhun tootna — kamzor aur bhurbhure nakhun
  • Tingle/numbness haathon pairon mein — ungliyon mein chonkay lagna
  • Thakawat aur kamzori — bina kisi wajah ke sustpan
  • Bachon mein growth slow — qad chhota rehna, haddiyon ka sahi nishwonuma na hona

Agar yeh symptoms hain to doctor se serum calcium aur Vitamin D level ka blood test zarur karwayein.

Osteoporosis — Khamosh Bimari

Osteoporosis ko "khamosh bimari" isliye kehte hain kyunke fracture hone tak pata nahi chalta. Haddiyan andar se khaali ho jati hain — bilkul spunge jaisi — magar bahar se koi nishani nahi hoti.

  • DXA scan (bone density test) se osteoporosis ki tashkhees hoti hai
  • Women 50+ mein bohat common — har 3 mein se 1 woman ko fracture ka khatra
  • Pakistan mein awareness bohat kam hai — aksar log fracture hone ke baad hi jante hain
  • Hip, spine aur wrist fractures sab se aam hain — hip fracture elderly mein khatarnak ho sakta hai
  • T-score -2.5 ya kam ho to osteoporosis confirm hota hai

Calcium Supplements — Kaunsa Lein?

Agar diet se calcium pura na ho to doctor supplement recommend kar sakta hai. Magar sab supplements ek jaise nahi hote.

Calcium Carbonate vs Citrate

  • Calcium Carbonate: sab se sasta aur aam. Khaane ke saath lein — khaali pet absorption kam hoti hai. Acidity ke mareezon mein gas ya bloating ho sakti hai.
  • Calcium Citrate: thora mehnga magar khaali pet bhi le sakte hain. Elderly aur jin ko pet ke masle hain un ke liye better. Acid kam hone wali dawai (PPI) lene walon ke liye zyada suitable.

Dosage Tips

  • Ek waqt mein 500mg se zyada na lein — body ek baar mein itna hi absorb kar paati hai
  • Vitamin D saath mein zaruri: 1000-2000 IU rozana — iske bina calcium absorb nahi hoga
  • Iron ki goli se 2 ghante ka gap rakhein — dono ek saath lene se absorption kam hoti hai

Side Effects

  • Qabz (constipation) sab se common side effect hai — pani zyada piyen aur fiber khayein
  • Kidney stones ka khatra excessive dose se barhta hai — doctor ki batai hui dose se zyada na lein
  • Apni marzi se supplement shuru na karein — doctor se confirm karein ke aap ko kitna chahiye

Exercise Aur Haddiyan

Calcium ke saath exercise bhi haddiyon ki mazbooti ke liye zaruri hai. Weight-bearing exercises sabse zyada faidamand hain:

  • Walking, jogging, stairs charhna — rozana 30 minute chalein
  • Resistance training — halke weights ya resistance bands se muscles aur haddiyan dono mazboot hoti hain
  • Balance exercises — elderly ke liye falls se bachao. Yoga, tai chi ya simple standing exercises

Swimming aur cycling achi exercise hain magar haddiyon pe directly zyada asar nahi dalti kyunke yeh weight-bearing nahi hain. Haddiyon ke liye zameen pe pair rakh ke exercise zyada zaruri hai.

Conclusion

Calcium ki kami ko nazarandaz na karein — yeh khamoshi se haddiyan kamzor karti hai aur fracture hone tak pata nahi chalta. Apni diet mein dairy products, channay, til, aur calcium-rich foods shamil karein. Vitamin D saath mein lein warna calcium absorb nahi hoga. Frequently Asked Questions

Calcium ki kami ke kya symptoms hain?
Haddiyon aur joro mein dard, muscle cramps (khaaskar raat ko), daant kamzor hona, nakhun tootna, haathon pairon mein tingle/numbness, aur thakawat. Agar yeh symptoms hain to doctor se calcium aur Vitamin D ka blood test karwayein.
Calcium ki tablet kab aur kaise lein?
Calcium Carbonate khaane ke saath lein, Calcium Citrate kisi bhi waqt. Ek waqt mein 500mg se zyada na lein — body absorb nahi kar paati. Vitamin D saath mein zaruri hai warna calcium absorb nahi hoga. Iron ki goli se 2 ghante ka gap rakhein.
Kya sirf doodh se calcium pura ho sakta hai?
Ek glass doodh mein ~300mg calcium hota hai. Adults ko 1000mg chahiye to din mein 3+ glass chahiye — jo mushkil hai. Isliye dahi, paneer, channay, til, aur sabziyan bhi khayein. Agar diet se pura na ho to supplement zaruri hai.
Vitamin D aur calcium ka kya taluq hai?
Vitamin D ke bina calcium body absorb nahi kar sakti — chahe kitna bhi calcium khao. Pakistan mein Vitamin D deficiency bohat aam hai (pardah, indoor lifestyle, kam dhoop). Doctor se Vitamin D level check karwayein — aksar supplement zaruri hota hai.
Osteoporosis ka test kaise hota hai?
DXA scan (bone density test) se osteoporosis ki tashkhees hoti hai. 50+ women aur 70+ men ko yeh test karwana chahiye. Pakistan mein bade hospitals mein available hai. T-score -2.5 ya kam ho to osteoporosis confirm hota hai.
