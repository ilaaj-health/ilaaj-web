Bachon Mein Dast Ka Ilaj — ORS Aur Zaruri Ehtiyat

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Pakistan mein dast bachon ki #2 mortality cause — magar ORS + Zinc se rokna asaan
  • Har potty ke baad ORS dein — 50-100ml (under 2), 100-200ml (over 2)
  • Zinc supplement 10-20mg roz 10-14 din tak — severity kam karta hai
  • Doodh band na karein, masala se zyada cheeni wali drinks (Coke, juice) avoid karein
  • Imodium aur antibiotics bina doctor mashware ke nahi — 12 saal se kam khatarnaak

Bachon Mein Dast Ki Aam Wajuhat

Dast (diarrhea) Pakistan mein bachon ki maut ki doosri sab se badi wajah hai — pneumonia ke baad. Saaf paani ki kami, garmi, aur kharaab khaana sab milkar 5 saal se kam bachon mein dast ki epidemic banate hain. Khushkismati se, sahi ilaj ghareloo level pe possible hai — ORS aur zinc do simple cheezein hain jo zindagi bachati hain.

Bachon Mein Dast — Causes
Viral (Rotavirus)Most common — vaccine se prevention
Bacterial (Shigella, E. coli, Salmonella)Kharaab paani/khaana se
Parasites (Giardia)Persistent dast, weight loss
AntibioticsGut bacteria disturb hone se
Food allergy / lactose intoleranceSpecific khaane se after-effect

Dast Ki Definition — Kab Fikar Karein

Dast yaani 3 ya zyada paani jaisi potty 24 ghante mein. Doodh peene wale bachon mein normal stool semi-loose hota hai — wo dast nahi. Real dast mein stool ki frequency aur paani ki maqdar dono barhti hai. Bohot pasti ya chamri ka rang nila ho jana, sustai, peshab kam — yeh sab serious signs hain.

Dehydration — Sab Se Bara Khatra

Bachon mein dast se asal khatra dehydration (paani ki kami) ka hai, dast khud nahi. Yeh signs check karein:

  • Peshab kam ho gaya — 6+ ghante mein gila diaper nahi
  • Muh aur zubaan khushk
  • Ronay par ansoo nahi aate
  • Ankhein dhans gayi (sunken eyes)
  • Fontanelle (sir ka mulayim hissa) andar dhansa
  • Bachay ki chamri pinch karein to slowly wapas aati hai (skin tenting)
  • Bohot sust, kam haraqat, jagana mushkil
  • Saans tezi se ya unusually slow

Yeh sab signs emergency hain — foran hospital lay jaein.

ORS — Sab Se Important Treatment

Oral Rehydration Salts (ORS) bachay ko dehydration se bachata hai. Pakistan mein common brands hain Nimkol, Energil, Hydrol, Pedialyte. Ek sachet 1 litre saaf ubla huwa thanda paani mein ghol lein — 24 ghante mein khatm karein.

Dose:

  • 2 saal se kam: Har potty ke baad 50-100 ml (1/4 - 1/2 cup)
  • 2-10 saal: Har potty ke baad 100-200 ml (1/2 - 1 cup)
  • Chamche se thoda thoda kar ke har 1-2 minute mein — zyada ek baar mein dena ulti la sakta hai
  • Ulti aaye to 10 minute ruk kar dobara shuru karein, dheere dheere

Ghar par ORS agar packet nahi: 1 litre saaf paani + 6 chamche cheeni + 1/2 chamcha namak. Magar packet zyada accurate hai — Pakistan mein hospital aur dukaan dono se Rs. 5-20 mein milta hai.

Zinc Supplement — Doosra Pillar

WHO ki guideline ke mutabiq zinc supplement 10-14 din tak roz dena dast ki severity aur duration kam karta hai, aur aagay 2-3 mahine tak agla episode prevent karta hai:

  • 6 mahine se kam: 10 mg zinc roz
  • 6 mahine se zyada: 20 mg zinc roz

Pakistan mein Zincat, Zinkid syrup aam available hain. Yeh ORS ke sath dein.

Abhi Doctor Se Milein

Khaana Aur Doodh — Continue Karein

Yeh aam galat fehmi hai ke dast mein doodh band karna chahiye — bilkul nahi:

  • Maa ka doodh: Bilkul continue karein — yeh hydration + immunity dono deta hai
  • Formula milk: Continue karein — sirf lactose-free agar pediatrician kahe
  • Soft food (6+ mahine): Khichri, rice water (chawal ka maand), dahi, mashed banana, applesauce, ubla aloo, toast
  • BRAT diet: Banana, Rice, Applesauce, Toast — easy on tummy

Yeh AVOID karein:

  • Coke, Pepsi, Sprite, fruit juice — zyada cheeni dast worse karti hai
  • Kacha doodh, gaye ka unboiled milk
  • Tali huyi cheezein, masaledar khaana
  • Plain water akela (electrolytes nahi)
  • Sports drinks (Gatorade) — bachon ke liye nahi

Imodium Aur Antibiotics — Mat Dein

Imodium (loperamide) 12 saal se kam bachon ke liye sakht mana hai. Yeh aanton ki movement rok deta hai jo serious complications la sakta hai. Daktar bhi rarely prescribe karte hain.

Antibiotics sirf tab dein jab stool culture mein bacterial infection (Shigella, Salmonella) confirm ho. 80% dast viral hote hain — antibiotics se faida nahi balki gut bacteria kharab. Pakistan mein over-the-counter antibiotics dast ke liye dena bohot common hai — yeh galat practice hai.

Probiotics (Saccharomyces boulardii) jaise Bioflor ya Enterol viral dast mein madadgar ho sakte hain — pediatrician ki recommend par.

Prevention — Roz Ki Aadat

  • Haath dhona — bachay ko khilane se pehle, bathroom ke baad
  • 6 mahine tak exclusive breastfeeding
  • Sirf ubla huwa thanda paani — Pakistan ke nal ka paani filter ya boil zaruri
  • Bahar ka khula khaana avoid — khaaskar garmi mein
  • Phal/sabzi dho kar khilayein
  • Rotavirus vaccine (EPI mein free) lagwa lein
  • Typhoid Conjugate Vaccine (TCV) — 9 mahine pe free
  • Ghar ka khaana taza dein, leftover 4 ghante se zyada na rakhain

Foran Hospital Le Jaein Agar:

  • 6 mahine se kam ki umar mein dast
  • Stool mein khoon ya bohot zyada balghami
  • Baar baar ulti — ORS bhi nahi rukti
  • Severe dehydration signs (peshab band, ankhein dhansi, sustai)
  • 39C+ bukhar
  • Bachay ko jagana mushkil
  • Dauray (seizures)
  • 3 din se zyada chalta dast

Conclusion

Bachon mein dast aksar viral hota hai aur 5-7 din mein theek ho jata hai — magar dehydration jaanleva ho sakti hai. ORS + Zinc ka simple formula Pakistan mein har saal hazaron bachon ki zindagi bachata hai. Imodium aur antibiotics ko apni marzi se mat dein. Frequently Asked Questions

Bachay ko dast ho to ORS kitni quantity mein dein?
Har potty (loose motion) ke baad ORS dein: 2 saal se kam bachon ko 50-100 ml, 2 saal se zyada ko 100-200 ml. Pakistan mein Nimkol, Energil ya Hydrol packets aam milte hain — 1 sachet 1 litre saaf paani mein ghol kar 24 ghante tak istemal karein. Chamche se thoda thoda kar ke dein — zyada ek baar dene se ulti aati hai.
Bachay ko dast mein doodh continue karna chahiye?
Ji haan — maa ka doodh aur formula bilkul continue karein, dast ke dauran bhi. Yeh ghalat fehmi hai ke doodh band karna chahiye. Maa ka doodh bachay ko hydration aur immunity dono deta hai. ORS doodh ke beech mein dein. Lactose-free formula sirf agar pediatrician kahe.
Dehydration ki nishaniyaan kya hain?
Dehydration ki bari nishaniyaan: peshab kam (6+ ghante mein wet diaper nahi), zubaan aur muh khushki, ronay par ansoo nahi aate, ankhein dhans gayi, fontanelle (sir ka mulayim hissa) dhans gaya, sustai, chamri pinch karein to wapas slow ataa hai. Yeh sab signs hospital lay jaane ki indications hain — derh nahi karein.
Bachay ko Imodium ya antibiotic dena chahiye?
Nahi — 12 saal se kam bachon ko Imodium (loperamide) bilkul mat dein, khatarnaak ho sakta hai. Antibiotics sirf tab dein jab stool culture mein bacterial infection confirm ho (jaise Shigella). Zyadatar dast viral hote hain — antibiotics se faida nahi, gut bacteria kharab. WHO ka standard treatment ORS + Zinc hai, antibiotics nahi.
Bachay ko foran hospital kab lay jaein?
Foran hospital lay jaein agar: 6 mahine se kam ki umar mein dast, stool mein khoon ya bohot zyada balghami, persistent ulti (ORS bhi nahi rukti), severe dehydration signs, 39C se zyada bukhar, bachay ko jagana mushkil ho, dauray (seizures), peshab 8+ ghante mein nahi. Pakistan ke har bare hospital mein ORS unit hota hai — Rescue 1122 bhi available.
