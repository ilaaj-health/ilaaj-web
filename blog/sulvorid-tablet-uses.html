Sulvorid Tablet Uses — Anxiety, IBS Aur Schizophrenia Ki Dawa Pakistan

Mukhtasar — Ahem Baatein
  • Sulvorid = sulpiride 200mg — Pakistan mein atypical antipsychotic
  • Dose-dependent dual action: low dose (100-200mg) anxiety/IBS; high dose (400-800mg) schizophrenia
  • Prolactin badhati hai — weight gain, galactorrhoea (dudh aana), menstrual changes possible
  • Prescription zaroori — bina doctor ke band nahi karni, slowly taper karein
  • Sudden band karna dangerous — rebound symptoms aa sakte hain

Sulvorid Kya Hai?

Sulvorid sulpiride 200mg ki tablet hai — Pakistan mein available atypical antipsychotic jo selective dopamine D2/D3 receptor blocker hai. Iska unique feature yeh hai ke dose ke hisaab se alag alag conditions treat karta hai:

  • Low dose (100-200mg/day): Presynaptic D2 receptors block karta hai → dopamine release badhta hai → antidepressant-like aur anti-anxiety effects
  • High dose (400-800mg/day): Postsynaptic D2 receptors block karta hai → antipsychotic effect

Sulvorid Ke Uses

1. Anxiety Aur Psychosomatic Symptoms (Low Dose)

Pakistan mein psychiatrists low-dose sulpiride (50-100mg din mein 2 baar) anxiety aur somatic complaints ke liye prescribe karte hain — khaas kar jab SSRIs se faida nahi hua ya patient GI symptoms ke saath anxiety rakhta hai.

2. IBS Aur Functional GI Disorders (Low Dose)

Anxiety-related irritable bowel syndrome mein low-dose sulpiride helpful hai — gut motility normalize karta hai aur gut-brain axis pe kaam karta hai. Psychosomatic gastritis mein bhi use hoti hai.

3. Schizophrenia (High Dose)

High dose (400-800mg/day) mein sulpiride schizophrenia ke positive symptoms (hallucinations, delusions) treat karta hai. Aaj kal newer antipsychotics (olanzapine, risperidone) zyada prefer hote hain magar sulpiride still use hoti hai.

Zaroori: Sulvorid antipsychotic hai — yeh OTC (bina prescription) lena dangerous hai. Bina doctor ke shuru ya band nahi karni. Khud se dose change bhi nahi karni.

Khulaasa

Sulvorid (sulpiride 200mg) ek versatile magar serious dawa hai — low dose mein anxiety aur IBS, high dose mein schizophrenia ke liye Pakistan mein use hoti hai. Prolactin increase karne wali side effects (weight gain, galactorrhoea) common hain. Prescription aur regular monitoring zaroori hai. Kabhi bhi achanak band nahi karni — doctor ki supervision mein slowly taper karein.

Aksar Pooche Jane Waale Sawaalat

Sulvorid anxiety ke liye safe hai?

Low dose mein use hoti hai magar first-line nahi — SSRIs prefer hote hain. Doctor ki prescription aur monitoring zaroori hai kyunke yeh antipsychotic hai.

Sulvorid se weight kyun barhta hai?

Prolactin barhaata hai jo appetite aur metabolism affect karta hai. Regular weight monitoring aur diet control zaroori hai.

Sulvorid band karne ka sahi tarika kya hai?

Achanak band mat karein — rebound symptoms aate hain. Doctor ki supervision mein slowly dose kam karein.

Sulvorid se dudh kyun aata hai?

Prolactin dramatically barhaata hai — galactorrhoea, menstrual changes (khawateen mein), gynecomastia (mard mein) possible. Doctor ko foran btaayein.

Sulvorid IBS ke liye kaam karti hai?

Haan — anxiety-related IBS-D mein effective hai. Gut dopamine receptors block karta hai, GI motility normalize karta hai. Doctor supervision zaroori hai.
