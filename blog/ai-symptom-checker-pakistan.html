Common Symptoms — Quick Examples

Sir Dard

AI yeh dimensions check karta hai: location (one side / both / front / back), severity, duration, triggers, associated symptoms (vision changes, nausea), aur red flags (sudden severe, neurological symptoms).

Pet Dard

Location (upper / lower / right / left), nature (constant / colicky), associated symptoms (fever, vomiting, blood), eating relationship, aur red flags (rigid abdomen, blood in stool).

Bukhar

Duration, peak temperature, pattern, associated symptoms (rash, joint pain, eye pain — dengue suspect), recent travel, aur red flags (neck stiffness, rash, breathing difficulty).

Khansi

Type (dry / productive), duration, blood, fever, weight loss (TB suspect), smoking history, aur red flags (hemoptysis, severe shortness of breath).

Skin Rash

Location, characteristics, itching, fever, recent medications, contact history, aur red flags (extensive blistering, mouth involvement — Stevens-Johnson).

AI Symptom Checker Ke Limitations

Honestly, koi bhi symptom checker — Ilaaj AI included — yeh nahi kar sakta:

  • Physical examination
  • Lab tests
  • Imaging (X-ray, ultrasound, MRI)
  • Direct emergency diagnosis
  • Complex chronic disease management alone

Ilaaj AI is liye doctor consultation ka direct path integrate karta hai — symptom analysis ke baad PMDC doctor se prescription le sakte hain.

Frequently Asked Questions

Online AI symptom checker kitna accurate hai?

Modern AI symptom checkers (Ilaaj AI, Ada Health, Symptomate) common conditions ke liye 70-80% accurate hain. Magar yeh diagnostic tools nahi hain — yeh "first-pass triage" tools hain. Ilaaj AI ka approach behtar hai kyunke yeh symptom check ke baad PMDC doctor verification ka direct path deta hai. Sirf symptom checker pe rely karna galat hai.

AI symptom checker free hai Pakistan mein?

Haan, Ilaaj AI ka symptom checker bilkul free hai. Aap unlimited times symptoms check kar sakte hain, AI ke recommendations le sakte hain, aur self-care guidance hasil kar sakte hain. Sirf doctor-verified prescription ke liye nominal payment hai. WebMD aur similar international tools bhi free hain magar Roman Urdu support nahi.

Ilaaj AI WebMD se behtar kyun hai?

WebMD English-only hai aur US disease patterns pe based hai — Pakistan mein dengue, typhoid, smog ki specific consideration nahi. WebMD aksar har symptom pe rare/scary conditions dikhata hai ("cyberchondria"). Ilaaj AI: 1) Roman Urdu native, 2) Pakistan-specific diseases, 3) Focused likely diagnoses, 4) PMDC doctor verification path, 5) Local OTC medications recommend karta hai (Panadol, Brufen, Calpol).

Kya AI symptom checker doctor ki jagah le sakta hai?

Bilkul nahi. AI symptom checker doctor ka replacement nahi — yeh triage tool hai. Doctor physical examination, lab tests, imaging, aur experience-based judgment provide karte hain jo AI nahi kar sakta. Ilaaj AI is liye AI assessment + PMDC doctor verification ka combination provide karta hai — best of both worlds. Serious conditions mein direct doctor consultation zaruri hai.

Symptom checker ke results pe medication shuru kar sakte hain?

OTC medications (Panadol, Calpol, Strepsils) ke liye Ilaaj AI ki recommendations safe hain. Magar prescription medications (antibiotics, BP dawai, blood thinners) ke liye doctor verification zaruri. Ilaaj AI symptom check ke baad one-click doctor consultation provide karta hai jo proper prescription dega. Apni marzi se antibiotics ya prescription drugs shuru karna khatra hai.
