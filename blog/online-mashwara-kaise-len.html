Online Mashwara Kaise Len — Ilaaj AI Ka Step-by-Step Guide

  • Ilaaj AI pe online mashwara lena bilkul aasan hai — 5 simple steps
  • Roman Urdu, Urdu, ya English mein apni takleef bata sakte hain
  • AI structured questions puchta hai, phir doctor final approve karta hai
  • Poora process 15-20 minute mein complete ho jata hai
  • Prescription kisi bhi pharmacy se dawai lene ke liye valid hai

Ilaaj AI Se Mashwara Kyun Lein?

Pakistan mein clinic jana time-consuming aur mehnga ho sakta hai — travel, waiting rooms, aur consultation fees sab mil kar ek simple health issue ko bhi mushkil bana dete hain. Ilaaj AI ghar baithe, apni zubaan mein, aur affordable rate pe doctor-verified prescription deta hai.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Account Banayein

app.ilaaj.ai pe jayein aur quick sign-up karein. Basic information dein — naam, umar, contact number. Sirf ek baar karna hai, phir family members bhi add kar sakte hain.

Step 2: Apni Takleef Batayein

Jis tarah aap doston se baat karte hain, waise hi apne symptoms type karein — Roman Urdu, Urdu, ya English mein. Example: "Mujhe 2 din se bukhar hai aur khansi bhi hai" — bas itna kaafi hai shuru karne ke liye. Typing mushkil ho to voice message bhi bhej sakte hain.

Step 3: AI Ke Sawalat Ka Jawab Dein

AI aapse 5-7 focused questions puchega — tap-to-select options ke saath, taake jawab dena aasan ho:

  • Symptom kitne shadeed hain
  • Kab se shuru hue
  • Kya kuch associated symptoms bhi hain
  • Purani bimariyan ya allergies
  • Currently koi dawai le rahe hain

Step 4: AI Draft Prescription Banata Hai

Saari info collect karne ke baad AI ek structured draft prescription banata hai — possible diagnosis, recommended medications, aur lifestyle advice ke saath.

Step 5: Payment Aur Doctor Approval

Verified prescription unlock karne ke liye ek nominal payment karein — yeh clinic visit se kaafi sasta hai. Payment ke baad ek licensed PMDC doctor aapki poori chat history aur AI draft review karta hai, phir approve ya modify karta hai.

Apni pehli online consultation try karein — 15-20 minute mein doctor-verified prescription paayein.

Mashwara Shuru Karein

Step 6: Prescription Mil Jati Hai

Doctor ka digital signature aur PMDC number ke saath prescription aapki chat mein deliver hoti hai. Yeh legally valid hai aur Pakistan ki kisi bhi pharmacy se dawai le sakte hain.

Tips — Behtareen Consultation Ke Liye

  • Specific rahen — "pet dard" ki jagah "pet ke upper right side mein 2 din se dard" batayein
  • Timeline batayein — symptom kab shuru hua, kitni der se hai
  • Purani medical history mention karein — allergies, chronic conditions
  • Current dawaiyan batayein — interactions avoid karne ke liye
  • Photos attach karein agar skin issue ho — visual assessment mein madad karta hai

Kin Cases Mein Ilaaj AI Best Hai?

  • Bukhar, khansi, zukam
  • Pet dard, acidity
  • Skin issues, allergies
  • Follow-up consultations
  • Medication refills
  • Lab test interpretations

Kab Ilaaj AI Kafi Nahi — Emergency Mein Kya Karein

Chest pain, severe bleeding, breathing mushkil, stroke symptoms, ya kisi bhi life-threatening situation mein foran 1122 (Rescue) call karein ya nearest hospital ER jayein — online consultation emergency ka replacement nahi hai.

Conclusion

Ilaaj AI se online mashwara lena bohat simple hai — describe karein, sawalat ka jawab dein, aur prescription paayein, sab kuch 15-20 minute mein. Roman Urdu mein comfortable rahen, PMDC-verified doctor har prescription check karta hai. Apna pehla mashwara aaj hi try karein.

Frequently Asked Questions

Ilaaj AI use karne ke liye kya chahiye?
Sirf ek smartphone ya computer aur internet connection. app.ilaaj.ai pe jayein, quick account banayein, aur apni takleef batana shuru karein — koi complex setup ki zarurat nahi.
Kya Ilaaj AI par Roman Urdu mein baat kar sakte hain?
Bilkul, Ilaaj AI specifically Pakistan ke liye banaya gaya hai aur Roman Urdu, Urdu script, aur English teeno mein naturally samajhta hai — jaise aap apne doston se baat karte hain.
Poora consultation process kitna waqt leta hai?
Average 15-20 minute mein symptoms batana, AI ke sawalat ka jawab dena, aur final doctor-approved prescription paana — sab kuch complete ho jata hai.
Prescription kis pharmacy se valid hai?
Har prescription ek licensed PMDC doctor approve karta hai, is liye yeh legally valid hai aur Pakistan ki kisi bhi pharmacy — chahi chain ho ya local medical store — se dawai le sakte hain.
Kya family members ke liye alag se consultation le sakte hain?
Haan, ek hi account mein family profiles add kar sakte hain — spouse, bachon, ya parents ke liye — har member ki separate health history maintain hoti hai.
