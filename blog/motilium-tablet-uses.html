Motilium Tablet Uses, Dosage and Side Effects in Pakistan

TL;DR — Aham Baatein
  • Motilium = domperidone 10mg, anti-matli aur prokinetic dawai
  • Matli, ulti, bloating, khaana hazam na hona
  • 10 mg din mein 3 baar, khaane se 30 min pehlay
  • Zyada dose se dil ka khatra (QT prolongation)
  • 12 saal se kam bachon mein dawaktar ki rahnumai zaruri

Motilium Kya Hai?

Motilium Janssen Pharmaceuticals ka brand naam hai domperidone ke liye — ek dopamine D2 receptor antagonist jo do tarah kaam karta hai:

  1. Antiemetic: Chemoreceptor trigger zone mein dopamine receptors block karta hai (blood-brain barrier ke bahar), jis se matli aur ulti ki khwahish kam hoti hai
  2. Prokinetic: Pait khaali hone ki raftaar tez karta hai — khaana jaldi hazam hota hai, bloating, bhaari pait, aur reflux kam hoti hai

Metoclopramide ke muqablay mein, domperidone blood-brain barrier asaani se cross nahi karta, isliye dimaag se mutalliq side effects kam hain.

Motilium Tablet Uses

1. Matli aur Ulti

Yeh iski bunyadi use hai. Gastroenteritis, surgery ke baad, dawaiyon ki wajah se, aur safar ki matli mein kaam aati hai.

2. Gastroparesis

Pait deri se khaali hona — khaana andar hi reh jaata hai (diabetic patients mein aam).

3. Bloating aur Khaane ke Baad Bhaari Pait

Khaane ke baad pait bhaari lagni, bloating, dakar, aur upar pait mein takleef.

4. GERD aur Reflux

Antacids/PPIs ke saath pait khaali karne mein madad ke liye prokinetic ke taur par.

5. Hamal ki Matli

Pakistan mein morning sickness ke liye istemal hoti hai — doctor se risk aur faayda discuss karein.

6. Chemotherapy ki Matli

Halki chemo matli ke liye (shadeed matli mein mazboot dawaiyan hain).

7. Maan ka Dudh Barhana (Off-label)

Domperidone prolactin secretion badhata hai jis se maan ka dudh ziyada hota hai. Kafi maaon mein off-label istemal hoti hai.

Dil Ka Khatra — Cardiac Safety Warning

2014–2016 mein dunya bhar ki regulatory agencies (EMA, MHRA, Health Canada) ne domperidone ki pabandiyaan lagayin — QT interval prolongation ke khauf se — ek anormal dil ki taal jo achanak maut (torsades de pointes) ka sabab ban sakti hai. Aham baatein:

  • Khatra zyada hota hai jab dose 10 mg se ziyada ho ek baar mein
  • Khatra zyada hota hai chronic lambe arsey tak istemal mein
  • Khatra zyada hota hai dil ki bimari, electrolyte kharabi, ya QT prolonging dawaiyon ke saath
  • Standard dose (10 mg TDS) pe thodi muddat ke liye sehatmand balighan mein risk kam samjha jaata hai

Pakistan mein yeh khulam khula milti hai — lekin is risk se aaghah rehna zaroori hai.

Side Effects

Aam:

  • Munh ka sokhna
  • Sar dard
  • Dast
  • Pait mein marora

Kam aam lekin aham:

  • Galactorrhea — ghair hamal aurat aur mard mein bhi anormal taur par dudh aana — prolactin barhnay ki wajah se
  • Gynecomastia — mardaanon mein seenay ki rasoli
  • Maahwari ki khirabi — prolactin barhnay se
  • QT prolongation — dil ka khatra (upar dekhen)
  • Extrapyramidal symptoms — bohat kam, kyunki yeh blood-brain barrier mushkil se cross karta hai

Motilium Kise Nahi Leni Chahiye

  • Pehle se QT prolongation ya dil ki taal kharab ho
  • QT prolonging dawaiyan le raha ho (azithromycin, fluoroquinolones, amiodarone, antipsychotics)
  • Jagar ki shadeed bimari
  • Prolactinoma (pituitary tumor jo prolactin banata hai — domperidone aur barha deta hai)
  • Andhron mein rukaawat ya saraakh
  • 12 saal se kam bacche: bohat ehtiyaat se; suspension behtar hai
  • Pituitary tumor

Hamal mein Motilium

FDA ne domperidone US mein approve nahi kiya. Pakistan mein hamal ki matli ke liye istemal hoti hai, lekin saboot mehdood hain aur cardiac safety profile wajah-e-ehtiyaat hai. Halki morning sickness mein pehlay adrak, vitamin B6, aur choti choti ghizayen try karein — yeh ziyada mehfooz hain. Shadeed qai mein apni gynaecologist se options discuss karein.

Dudh Pilane Mein Motilium (Lactation)

Domperidone prolactin badhata hai aur maan ke dudh ki kami mein off-label istemal hoti hai. Thodi miqdar dudh mein bhi aati hai. Standard dose cardiac risk standard doses par low maani jaati hai, lekin istemal se pehlay doctor se zaroor baat karein. Lactation ke liye dose: 10 mg din mein 3 baar.

Drug Interactions

  • QT prolonging dawaiyan: Azomax (azithromycin), moxifloxacin, amiodarone, haloperidol — khatarnaak combination
  • Ketoconazole / itraconazole (antifungals): Domperidone ka blood level kaafi barhate hain — sath na lein
  • Erythromycin: Yeh bhi domperidone level barhata hai — avoid karein
  • Anticholinergics (Buscopan, antipsychotics): Domperidone ka prokinetic asar kam ho sakta hai
  • Antacids / H2 blockers: Absorption kam hoti hai — antacid khaane ke baad lein, domperidone pehlay

Khulaasa

Motilium (domperidone 10 mg) Pakistan ki sabse zyada istemal hone wali matli ki dawaiyon mein se ek hai — matli, ulti, bloating, aur gastroparesis mein faydamand. Standard dose (10 mg din mein 3 baar) thodi muddat ke liye aam taur par mehfooz hai. Lekin dose se zyada lena dil ke liye khatarnaak ho sakta hai (QT prolongation), aur dil ki bimari waalon ya QT prolonging dawaiyan lene waalon ko avoid karna chahiye. Lagi rahi matli ke liye doctor se zaroor milein — yeh kisi aham bimari ki alamat ho sakti hai. Ghar bethe assessment ke liye Ilaaj AI istemal karein.

Aksar Poochhe Jaane Wale Sawalat

Motilium 10mg kis kaam aati hai?

Motilium 10mg (domperidone) matli aur ulti ke liye, khaane ke baad pait bhari lagni, bloating, gastroparesis (pait deri se khaali hona), aur GERD mein prokinetic ke taur par istemal hoti hai. Dudh barhane ke liye bhi off-label use hoti hai. 10mg din mein 3 baar, khaane se 30 minute pehlay lein.

Motilium kitni der mein asar karti hai?

Motilium tablet lene ke 30 se 60 minute mein asar shuru ho jaata hai. Matli mein ek ghante mein aaraam milta hai. Gastroparesis aur bloating mein kuch din regular khaane se pehlay lene par gastric motility behtar hoti hai aur zyada faayda hota hai.

Kya Motilium rozana lambe arsey tak lena safe hai?

Nahi — zyada tar international guidelines matli/ulti ke liye Motilium sirf 7 din tak recommend karti hain. Lambe arsey tak istemal se QT prolongation (dil ka risk) aur hormonal side effects (prolactin ziyada hona, galactorrhea, maahwari ki khirabi) ka khatra barhta hai. Agar aapko lambe arsey tak anti-nausea dawai chahiye toh doctor se milein aur sahi wajah pata karein.

Kya hamal mein Motilium subah ki matli ke liye le sakte hain?

Pakistan mein hamal ki matli ke liye Motilium istemal hoti hai, lekin hamal mein safety ka saboot mehdood hai aur cardiac risk bhi hai. Halki morning sickness mein pehlay adrak, choti choti ghizayen, aur vitamin B6 azmayen — yeh ziyada mehfooz hain. Shadeed qai mein apni gynaecologist se mashwara karein aur domperidone se pehlay doosre options dekhein.

Kya Motilium maan ka dudh barha sakti hai?

Haan — domperidone prolactin secretion badhati hai aur maan ke dudh ki kami mein off-label istemal hoti hai. Lactation ke liye standard dose: 10mg din mein 3 baar. Thodi miqdar dudh mein bhi jaati hai. Doctor se mashwara karein, aur dudh pilane ki frequency aur sahi latch ke saath milakar istemal karein — sirf dawai se zyada faayda nahi hota.
