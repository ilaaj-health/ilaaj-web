Bukhar Ka Ilaj — Wajah, Symptoms aur Treatment Pakistan Mein

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Bukhar 100°F (37.8°C) se upar count hota hai
  • Mosmi viral fever 3-5 din mein khud theek hota hai
  • Dengue mein platelets check zaruri hain — Karachi/Lahore mein common
  • Typhoid ka antibiotic course pura karna zaruri
  • Bachon mein 102°F+ ya 3 din se zyada bukhar = doctor

Bukhar Kya Hota Hai?

Bukhar (fever) jab hota hai jab aap ke jism ka temperature 100°F (37.8°C) se upar chala jaye. Yeh bimari nahi — yeh aap ke immune system ki defense response hai infection ke khilaf. Magar bukhar ki wajah samajhna zaruri hai kyunke har ek ka ilaj alag hai.

Bukhar Ki Aam Symptoms

  • Jism garam, sardi lagna (chills)
  • Sir dard, badan dard
  • Bhook na lagna
  • Sustpan aur thakawat
  • Pasina aana, pyaas zyada lagna
  • Bachon mein chirchirapan, doodh kam peena

Mosmi Viral Fever Ka Ilaj

Pakistan mein mausam badalne pe (March-April aur September-October) mosmi bukhar bohat aam hai. Yeh aksar khud-ba-khud 3-5 din mein theek ho jata hai.

Ghareloo Ilaj:

  • Bohat zyada pani aur fluids piyo — supe, juice, ORS
  • Aaram karein, kaam pe na jaein (dusron ko bhi infection lag sakti hai)
  • Maathay pe thanda gila kapra rakhein (sponging)
  • Halka khaana — daal, khichri, yakhni
  • Adrak/shahed/nimbu wali chai

Medicine:

  • Paracetamol (Panadol): 500-1000 mg har 6 ghante baad — sab se safe
  • Brufen 400 mg: agar Panadol kafi nahi, magar khaali pet na lein
  • Antibiotics: viral fever mein useless hain — sirf doctor ki taqseem pe lein

Dengue Bukhar — Khaaskar Pakistan Mein

Dengue mein achanak shadeed bukhar (104°F+), ankhon ke peeche dard, joro mein dard ("breakbone fever"), aur jism pe sukh chakatte aate hain. Karachi, Lahore aur Rawalpindi mein August-November mein cases zyada hote hain.

Dengue mein zaruri:

  • CBC (complete blood count) test karwaein — platelet count check
  • Sirf Paracetamol lein — Brufen, Aspirin, Disprin bilkul mat lein (bleeding ka khatra)
  • Pani aur ORS bohat zyada
  • Platelets 100,000 se kam ho to hospital
  • Naak/masooro se khoon, ya jism pe nile dhabbe = emergency

Doctor Se Foran Mashwara

Typhoid Ka Ilaj

Typhoid Pakistan mein bohat aam hai, khaaskar garmi mein. Wajah aksar kharaab pani ya bahar ka kuch ka khaana hai. Symptoms shuru mein flu jaise hote hain — bukhar, sir dard, kamzori — magar bukhar din ba din barhta jata hai.

Typhoid ki tashkhees: Doctor "Typhidot" ya blood culture test karwata hai. Confirmed ho to antibiotics ka 7-14 din ka course zaruri hai. Course beech mein chodna khatarnak hai — bacteria resistant ho jata hai.

Pakistan mein XDR typhoid ek serious problem hai — mehnat ki batein ke standard antibiotics kaam nahi karte. Isliye doctor se tashkhees zaruri hai, apni marzi se antibiotics na shuru karein.

Malaria Ka Ilaj

Malaria mein bukhar ka pattern regular hota hai — har 2-3 din baad shadeed bukhar, sardi lagna, phir pasina. KP, Balochistan aur dehati Sindh mein zyada aam hai. Blood test (MP slide) se confirm hota hai. Anti-malarial dawai (chloroquine ya artemisinin combinations) doctor ke parchee se lein.

Bachon Mein Bukhar

Bachon ke saath khaaskar khayal rakhein:

  • 3 mahine se kam baby mein 100.4°F+ bukhar = foran ER
  • Calpol syrup bachon ke liye safest hai — dose weight pe depend karta hai
  • Brufen syrup 6 mahine se upar bachon mein ok hai
  • Aspirin bilkul mat dein — Reye's syndrome ka khatra
  • Sponging halki gungune (warm) pani se karein, thande pani se nahi
  • Doodh aur fluids dein
  • Bukhar ke saath dauray (febrile seizure) ho jaein to baby ko side pe lait dein, koi cheez moonh mein na dein, hospital lay jaein

Kab Doctor Ko Foran Milein?

  • Bukhar 102°F+ aur 3 din se zyada
  • Sans lene mein dushwari
  • Gardan akarna, ya light se ankhein chubhna (meningitis)
  • Daur (seizures)
  • Pesab band ho jana ya kam ho jana
  • Jism pe nile/laal dhabbe
  • Confusion ya behoshi
  • Naak ya masooro se khoon
  • Hamla khwateen ya immunocompromised patients mein koi bhi bukhar

Bukhar Se Bachao

  • Filtered ya boiled pani piyo, khaaskar garmi mein
  • Bahar ke open foods aur drinks avoid karein
  • Macharon se bachao — net, repellent, full sleeves
  • Hath dhona aadat banayein
  • Typhoid vaccine baccay ke liye recommended hai
  • Flu shot saalana lo (Pakistan mein available)

Conclusion

Har bukhar antibiotics se theek nahi hota — viral fever mein antibiotics nuksan dete hain. Frequently Asked Questions

Bukhar mein kaunsi tablet best hai?

Adults mein Paracetamol (Panadol) 500-1000 mg har 6 ghante baad sab se safe option hai. Brufen 400 mg bhi effective hai magar khaali pet na lein. Dengue ka shubha ho to sirf Panadol lein — Brufen, Aspirin aur Disprin bleeding ka khatra barhate hain. Apni marzi se antibiotics shuru na karein.

Bachon ko bukhar mein kya dein?

Bachon ke liye Calpol syrup safest hai, dose weight pe depend karta hai (15 mg/kg har 6 ghante baad). 3 mahine se kam baby mein koi bhi bukhar emergency hai — turant doctor lay jaein. Aspirin bachon ko bilkul na dein. Sponging gungune pani se karein, thande pani se nahi. Doodh aur fluids dete rahein.

Dengue bukhar ki kya alamat hai?

Achanak shadeed bukhar (104°F+), sir dard (khaaskar ankhon ke peeche), joron aur muscles mein shadeed dard, jism pe sukh chakatte, aur ulti dengue ki classic symptoms hain. Pakistan mein August-November dengue ka peak season hai. CBC test karwa kar platelets check karein. Sirf Panadol use karein, NSAIDs (Brufen, Aspirin) bilkul nahi.

Typhoid bukhar kitne din mein theek hota hai?

Typhoid antibiotics ke 7-14 din ke course se theek hota hai. Course beech mein chodna khatarnak hai — bacteria resistant ho jata hai aur Pakistan mein XDR typhoid serious problem hai. Doctor se tashkhees (Typhidot ya blood culture) ke baad correct antibiotic shuru karein. Fluids, rest, aur halka khaana zaruri hai.

Bukhar mein khaane mein kya lein?

Halka aur asaan-hazm khaana lein: yakhni (chicken/sabzi soup), khichri, daal, ublay anday, kelay, dahi. Bohat zyada pani, ORS, narangi/mosmi ka juice. Masaledar, fried, aur bhari khaana avoid karein. Bachon ko doodh aur fluids dete rahein. Bhook na lage to thoda thoda kha kar bhi okay hai — fluids zyada zaruri hain.
