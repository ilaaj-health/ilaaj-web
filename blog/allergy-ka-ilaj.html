Allergy Ka Ilaj — Skin, Khaane aur Mausmi Allergy Pakistan

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Pakistan mein smog/dust allergy aur seasonal pollen sab se common
  • Antihistamines (Telfast, Zyrtec) safe aur effective
  • Anaphylaxis (sans phoolna, soojan) emergency hai
  • Skin rash mein steroid creams short-term effective
  • Allergy testing alergist se proper diagnosis ke liye

Allergy Kya Hai?

Allergy aap ke immune system ka over-reaction hai kisi harmless cheez (allergen) ke khilaf — pollen, dust, food, ya dawai. Body chemical "histamine" release karti hai jo symptoms paida karta hai.

Pakistan Mein Common Allergens
Mausmi pollenMarch-April aur Sept-Oct, paper mulberry tree (Islamabad mein famous)
Dust mitesBedding, takiya, carpets
SmogLahore, Karachi mein winter
FoodMungphali, anday, doodh, machli, gehun

Allergy Ki Types aur Symptoms

1. Allergic Rhinitis (Naak Allergy)

  • Naak band ya behte rehna
  • Cheekein
  • Aankhon mein khujli aur paani
  • Gala mein khujli

2. Skin Allergy (Eczema, Hives, Contact Dermatitis)

  • Daane (urticaria/hives)
  • Khujli
  • Red, sukha skin
  • Soojan
  • Burning sensation

3. Asthma

  • Sans mein dushwari
  • Sans ki seeti (wheezing)
  • Khansi (raat ko zyada)
  • Chest mein tightness

4. Food Allergy

  • Munh aur lips mein soojan
  • Pet dard, ulti, dast
  • Skin par daane
  • Severe: Anaphylaxis

5. Anaphylaxis (Emergency!)

  • Sans lene mein bohat dushwari
  • Pure jism par soojan
  • Gala band hona
  • BP girna, behoshi
  • 1122 turant call karein

Allergy Ka Ilaj

1. Trigger Avoid Karein

Sab se important step. Identify karein ke kis cheez se allergy hoti hai aur exposure kam karein.

2. Antihistamines (First Line)

Non-Sedating (Naya, Day-Time Use):

  • Fexofenadine (Telfast 120/180 mg): Pakistan mein most prescribed
  • Cetirizine (Zyrtec, Cetzine 10 mg): Cheap aur effective, halki sleepiness
  • Loratadine (Claritin 10 mg): Day-time safe
  • Desloratadine (Aerius 5 mg)

Sedating (Raat Ke Liye):

  • Diphenhydramine (Benadryl)
  • Hydroxyzine (Atarax 25 mg) — strong, sleep ke liye bhi use
  • Chlorpheniramine (Polaramine)

3. Nasal Sprays

  • Steroid sprays: Nasonex, Flonase, Beconase — long-term safe, allergic rhinitis ke liye best
  • Saline rinse: Salt water naak mein — Pakistan ki dust mein bohat helpful
  • Decongestant: Otrivin (xylometazoline) — sirf 3-5 din, addictive ho sakta

4. Eye Drops

  • Antihistamine: Patanol, Zaditen
  • Mast cell stabilizer: Cromolyn

5. Topical Steroids (Skin Allergy)

  • Hydrocortisone 1% (mild) — OTC
  • Betamethasone (Betnovate) — moderate, doctor parchee
  • Clobetasol (Dermovate) — strong, short-term only

Caution: Steroid creams chehre pe long-term use bohat nuksan deh. Pakistan mein "Faiza" jaisi steroid creams cosmetic use ke liye bilkul avoid karein.

6. Severe Cases

  • Oral steroids (Deltacortril, Solu-medrol): Acute flare-ups, doctor parchee
  • Immunotherapy (allergy shots): Long-term solution, allergist se
  • Biologics (Xolair, Dupixent): Severe asthma ya eczema

Specialist Se Mashwara

Mausmi Allergy (Hay Fever) — Pakistan

Pakistan mein March-April mein paper mulberry, plane trees aur grass pollen peak hota hai. September-October mein ragweed aur wheat pollen.

Tips:

  • Subah aur shaam outdoor activities kam karein (peak pollen)
  • Bahar se aane pe shower aur kapde change
  • Windows band rakhein peak season mein
  • AC use karein with HEPA filter
  • Daily antihistamine (Telfast/Zyrtec) season mein
  • Nasal saline rinse

Dust Allergy

Karachi/Lahore mein dust allergy bohat aam. Dust mites bedding mein pelte hain.

Tips:

  • Hafte mein 1 baar bedsheet/pillow cover hot wash
  • Anti-allergen pillow covers
  • Carpet kam, hard floor better
  • HEPA filter air purifier
  • Vacuum cleaner with HEPA filter
  • Pet bed/carpet outdoor mein dhup mein

Skin Allergy / Eczema

  • Moisturize: Cetaphil, Cerave, Eucerin — daily
  • Mild soaps: Dove, Cetaphil — sulphate-free
  • Hydrocortisone 1% halki cases mein
  • Stronger steroids doctor parchee se, short-term
  • Cool compress khujli mein
  • Nails short rakhein (scratch nuksan)
  • Cotton clothes (synthetic se irritation)

Food Allergy

Pakistan mein common: mungphali, anday, machli, gehun (celiac), doodh.

Diagnosis:

  • Food diary maintain karein
  • Skin prick test ya IgE blood test
  • Elimination diet

Severe food allergy: Always carry EpiPen (epinephrine auto-injector) — Pakistan mein limited availability. Doctor consultation zaruri.

Drug Allergy

Common culprits: penicillin/amoxicillin, sulpha drugs, aspirin/NSAIDs, contrast dye.

Symptoms: rash, hives, severe cases anaphylaxis. Pakistan mein medical alert bracelet recommended hai.

Pakistan Mein Smog Aur Allergy

  • N95 mask outdoor
  • Air purifier with HEPA filter ghar mein
  • Windows seal smog season mein
  • Vitamin C aur omega-3 inflammation kam karte hain
  • Daily hydration

Kab Foran Doctor / ER?

  • Sans lene mein bohat dushwari
  • Gala ya zaban mein soojan
  • Pure jism par hives aur weakness
  • BP gir jaye, behoshi
  • Bukhar ke saath skin rash
  • Bachon mein new food reaction
  • 2 hafte se zyada chronic symptoms

Conclusion

Allergy treatable hai magar prevention sab se important. Antihistamines safe aur effective hain, magar trigger avoid karna long-term solution hai. Frequently Asked Questions

Allergy ki best dawai kaunsi hai?
Sab se common antihistamines: Fexofenadine (Telfast 120/180 mg) — non-sedating, day-time. Cetirizine (Zyrtec, Cetzine 10 mg) — cheap aur effective, halki sleepiness. Loratadine (Claritin) day-time safe. Raat ke liye Hydroxyzine (Atarax 25 mg) ya Benadryl. Allergic rhinitis mein Nasonex spray bohat effective. Skin rash mein Hydrocortisone 1% cream.
Skin allergy ka ilaj kya hai?
Pehlay trigger identify aur avoid. Daily moisturize karein (Cetaphil, Cerave). Mild cases mein Hydrocortisone 1% cream. Stronger reactions mein Betnovate ya Dermovate doctor parchee se — short-term only. Khujli ke liye antihistamine (Telfast, Zyrtec). Cool compress, cotton clothes, mild soaps (Dove). Steroid creams chehre pe long-term bilkul nahi.
Mausmi allergy ka ilaj?
Pakistan mein March-April aur Sept-Oct peak pollen season. Daily antihistamine (Telfast 120 mg) season ke shuru se shuru karein. Nasonex nasal spray bohat effective. Saline rinse naak. Subah-shaam outdoor activities kam karein. Bahar se aane pe shower. Windows band, HEPA filter air purifier. Severe ho to allergist se immunotherapy ki guidance.
Dust allergy se kaise bachen?
Hafte mein 1 baar bedding hot wash. Anti-allergen pillow covers. Carpet kam karein, hard floor better. HEPA filter air purifier ghar mein. Vacuum cleaner with HEPA filter. Daily antihistamine. Smog ke dino mein N95 mask outdoor. Window seals winter mein. Pakistan mein dust mites bohat common — preventive routine zaruri.
Anaphylaxis (severe allergy reaction) mein kya karein?
Yeh medical emergency hai! Symptoms: sans lene mein bohat dushwari, gala/zaban soojan, pure jism par hives, BP gir jaye, behoshi. Foran 1122 call karein ya nearest ER lay jaein. Agar EpiPen (epinephrine) hai to thigh mein inject karein. Person ko lait dein, pair upar uthayein. Severe food allergy wale logon ko EpiPen always carry karna chahiye.
