Myteka Tablet Uses — Dama, Allergy Aur Breathing Ka Ilaj Pakistan

Mukhtasar — Ahem Baatein
  • Myteka = montelukast 10mg by Sanofi Pakistan — leukotriene receptor antagonist (LTRA)
  • Dama aur allergic rhinitis ki maintenance dawa — inhaler nahi; acute attack mein kaam nahi karta
  • Raat ko ek baar lein — dama raat ko sab se zyada hota hai
  • FDA warning: rare magar serious — depression, suicidal thoughts, bure khwaab possible
  • Bachon ke liye safe hai — alag chewable doses available hain

Myteka Kya Hai?

Myteka montelukast sodium 10mg ki tablet hai jo Sanofi Pakistan banaati hai. Yeh ek leukotriene receptor antagonist (LTRA) hai — dama aur allergic rhinitis ki oral (tablet wali) dawa jo inhaler ki jagah ya saath use hoti hai.

Leukotrienes aise chemicals hain jo allergy aur dama mein cells release karte hain aur airways ko tandoor (bronchospasm) aur sujan dete hain. Myteka CysLT1 receptors block karti hai — in chemicals ka asar rokti hai.

Myteka Ke Uses

1. Dama Ki Maintenance (Asthma)

Myteka dama ke attacks ki taqaadud aur shaddat kam karti hai — raat ko attacks, inhaler ki zaroorat aur exercise-induced bronchospasm reduce karti hai. Yeh inhaled corticosteroids (ICS) ke saath add-on therapy hai — ICS ki jagah nahi.

2. Allergic Rhinitis (Naak Ki Allergy)

Seasonal allergy (hay fever) aur perennial allergy (dust allergy) ke naak ke symptoms mein — bhare naak, chheenk, aur naak se paani — Myteka helpful hai. Antihistamine ke saath lena zyada faida deta hai.

3. Exercise-Induced Bronchoconstriction

Jo log exercise karte waqt saans lene mein takleef feel karte hain — Myteka daily lene se lasting protection milti hai. Salbutamol inhaler ke mutabiq tolerance develop nahi hota.

Khulaasa

Myteka (montelukast 10mg) dama aur allergic rhinitis ke liye Pakistan ki effective oral maintenance dawa hai. Raat ko ek baar leni hai — dama raat ko zyada hota hai isliye evening dosing best hai. Acute attack mein kaam nahi karta — Ventolin inhaler hamesha paas rakhein. FDA black-box warning ke liye neuropsychiatric symptoms pe nazar rakhen.

Aksar Pooche Jane Waale Sawaalat

Kya Myteka inhaler ki jagah le sakti hai?

Nahi — Myteka maintenance hai, emergency inhaler nahi. Acute attack mein Ventolin (salbutamol) use karein. Myteka attacks ki taqaadud kam karti hai — roki nahi sakti.

Myteka kab leni chahiye — din mein ya raat ko?

Raat ko — sone se 1-2 ghante pehle. Dama raat aur subah zyada hota hai. Evening dosing peak levels us waqt deta hai.

Myteka se mood ya neend ka masla ho sakta hai?

Haan — FDA black-box warning: depression, anxiety, suicidal thoughts, bure khwaab possible hain. Rare hai magar real. Koi bhi change hone pe foran doctor ko btaayein.

Myteka kaam karne mein kitna waqt lagti hai?

Kuch din mein shuru, 2-4 hafte mein poora faida. Roz lena zaroori hai — miss karne se progress ruk jaati hai.

Kya Myteka bachon ke liye safe hai?

Haan — 4mg (2-5 saal), 5mg (6-14 saal), 10mg (15+). Neuropsychiatric warning bachon pe bhi lagoo hai — neend ya mood change pe doctor se baat karein.
