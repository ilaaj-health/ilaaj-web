Panadol Extra — Uses, Dosage, Side Effects Pakistan

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Panadol Extra = Paracetamol 500 mg + Caffeine 65 mg
  • Migraine aur strong headache ke liye normal Panadol se zyada effective
  • Maximum 8 tablets per day, max 6 if also taking other caffeine sources
  • Hypertension aur heart issues mein doctor mashware se
  • Raat ko avoid karein — caffeine neend mein khalal

Panadol Extra Kya Hai?

Panadol Extra GSK ka combination product hai jo do active ingredients pe based hai:

  • Paracetamol 500 mg — pain killer aur antipyretic
  • Caffeine 65 mg — analgesic effect ko 40% tak barhata hai

Panadol Extra Kis Liye?

1. Migraine aur Strong Headache

Caffeine ki wajah se normal Panadol se zyada effective. Migraine attack ke shuru mein lena best.

2. Tension Headache

Stress aur thakawat wala sir dard mein faster relief.

3. Strong Body Aches

Flu jaisi conditions mein body pain.

4. Period Cramps

Brufen alternative as caffeine + paracetamol.

5. Post-Surgical Pain

Mild-to-moderate pain mein.

6. Toothache

Dant dard mein extra strength chahiye.

Dosage

Adults aur 12+ Saal Bachon Ke Liye:

  • Single dose: 1-2 tablets (500-1000 mg paracetamol + 65-130 mg caffeine)
  • Frequency: Har 4-6 ghante baad
  • Maximum per 24 hours: 8 tablets (4 grams paracetamol + 520 mg caffeine)
  • Empty stomach OK

Important:

Agar aap din mein chai/coffee/Coke bhi peete hain to caffeine total dekhna chahiye. Daily caffeine limit 400 mg hai (2-3 cups coffee).

Side Effects

Common (Caffeine ki wajah se):

  • Ghabrahat ya restlessness
  • Neend mein dushwari
  • Dil ki dharkan tez
  • Jaldi peshab
  • Halki acidity
  • Hath kanpna (high dose)

Serious:

  • Liver damage (paracetamol overdose)
  • Severe allergic reaction
  • Heart palpitations (caffeine-sensitive)

Kab Avoid Karein?

  • High blood pressure (uncontrolled)
  • Heart problems — arrhythmia, angina
  • Anxiety disorders
  • Insomnia / sleep issues
  • Pregnancy / breastfeeding — caffeine ki wajah se normal Panadol better
  • Liver disease
  • Bachon (12 saal se kam)
  • Caffeine sensitivity

Drug Interactions

  • Other caffeine sources — chai, coffee, energy drinks
  • Theophylline (asthma medicine) — caffeine effect barhata
  • Warfarin — bleeding risk
  • Antibiotics (Ciprofloxacin) — caffeine slowly metabolize
  • Alcohol — liver damage risk
  • MAO inhibitors (depression dawai) — BP spike

Pakistan Mein Common Use Cases

  • Office workers — afternoon headache
  • Students — exam stress headache
  • Drivers — long drives, fatigue + pain
  • Migraine sufferers — first response
  • Roza ke baad — caffeine withdrawal headache

Tips for Best Results

  1. Headache ke shuru mein lein — wait na karein
  2. Paani bohat piyo — dehydration sir dard ki badi wajah
  3. Andhera kamra aur 30 minute aaram
  4. Sleep adequate — caffeine cycle break karein
  5. Migraine triggers identify karein

Overdose

Panadol Extra ka overdose do alag dangers hain:

  1. Paracetamol toxicity: Liver damage 10 grams se
  2. Caffeine toxicity: 1 gram caffeine (~15 tablets) se serious

Symptoms: ulti, dil ki dharkan tez, behoshi, seizures. Foran 1122 ya nearest ER.

  • Panadol Extra (24 tablets pack): ~70-90 PKR

Conclusion

Panadol Extra Pakistan mein migraine aur strong headache ke liye preferred OTC option hai — caffeine analgesic effect barhata hai aur faster onset deta hai. Magar caffeine sensitive logon, hypertensives, pregnant women, aur sleep issues mein normal Panadol behtar hai. Maximum 8 tablets per day, raat ko avoid. Frequent migraine ke liye Ilaaj AI pe doctor se prescription strength options (Sumatriptan, etc.) ki rehnumai.

Frequently Asked Questions

Panadol Extra ka use kya hai?

Panadol Extra mein paracetamol 500 mg + caffeine 65 mg hai — strong headache, migraine, tension headache, body aches, period cramps, aur toothache mein normal Panadol se zyada aur faster effective. Caffeine analgesic effect 40% tak barhata hai. Adults aur 12+ saal bachon ke liye, maximum 8 tablets per day.

Panadol Extra ki ek din mein kitni tablet?

Adults ke liye 1-2 tablets har 4-6 ghante baad, maximum 8 tablets per 24 hours. Iss mein 4 grams paracetamol aur 520 mg caffeine hota hai. Agar aap chai/coffee bhi peete hain to total caffeine 400 mg se zyada nahi honi chahiye — yeh dose calculate karein. Long-term use 3 din se zyada bina doctor consultation nahi.

Panadol Extra raat ko le sakte hain?

Caffeine ki wajah se raat ko Panadol Extra avoid karein — 6-8 ghante neend mein dushwari ho sakti hai. Sone se 6 ghante pehlay last dose. Raat ke pain ke liye normal Panadol 1000 mg ya Brufen better hai. Caffeine sensitive logon ko evening mein bhi avoid karna chahiye.

Pregnancy mein Panadol Extra safe hai?

Pregnancy mein normal Panadol better hai. Panadol Extra ki caffeine bachay tak pohnchti hai — small amount safe magar zyada caffeine miscarriage aur premature birth ka risk barha sakti hai. Daily caffeine pregnancy mein 200 mg se kam honi chahiye. Pain ke liye plain paracetamol use karein. Brufen aur Voltral 3rd trimester mein bilkul nahi.

Panadol Extra aur Brufen mein kya farq hai?

Panadol Extra (paracetamol + caffeine): khaali pet safe, pregnancy mein avoid, dengue mein safe, halki to medium pain ke liye. Brufen 400 (ibuprofen): anti-inflammatory bhi hai, khaane ke baad lein, period cramps aur joint pain ke liye stronger, dengue mein BILKUL nahi (bleeding risk), ulcer/kidney issues mein avoid. Doctor situation ke mutabiq choose karta hai.
