Aankh Aana (Conjunctivitis) Ka Ilaj — Pink Eye Treatment Pakistan Mein

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Conjunctivitis mein aankh ki safed membrane inflamed ho jati hai
  • Teen types: viral (sabse common), bacterial (thick pus), allergic (khujli)
  • Viral conjunctivitis 1-2 hafton mein khud theek hoti hai
  • Bacterial mein antibiotic drops zaruri hain
  • Kisi ko dekhne se NAHI phailta — contact/droplets se phailta hai
  • Nazar kamzor ho ya shadeed dard ho to ophthalmologist zaruri

Conjunctivitis (Aankh Aana) Kya Hai?

Aankh aana ya conjunctivitis tab hota hai jab aankh ki safed jhilli (conjunctiva) mein soojhan aa jaye. Is soojhan ki wajah se aankh ki choti blood vessels phoolt jati hain aur aankh laal ya pink nazar aati hai. Yeh bimari Pakistan mein bohat aam hai — khaaskar bachon aur school jaane walon mein. Garmi aur monsoon ke mausam mein cases bohat barh jate hain. Aankh aana aksar serious nahi hota magar sahi ilaj na ho to takleef barh sakti hai aur doosron mein bhi phail sakta hai.

Conjunctivitis Ki Types

Viral Conjunctivitis

Sabse common type hai. Aankh se paani jaisa paatla discharge aata hai. Aksar ek aankh se shuru hota hai phir doosri aankh mein phail jata hai. Sardi ya flu ke saath bhi ho sakta hai. Yeh self-limiting hai — 1-2 hafton mein khud theek ho jata hai bina kisi khaas dawai ke.

Bacterial Conjunctivitis

Is mein aankh se gaahra peela ya hara pus nikalta hai. Subah uthne par palken chipak jati hain — aankh kholna mushkil hota hai. Dono aankhen affect ho sakti hain. Antibiotic drops se yeh jaldi theek hota hai — 3-5 din mein behtar hona shuru ho jata hai.

Allergic Conjunctivitis

Dono aankhen ek saath affect hoti hain. Khujli (itching) sabse dominant symptom hai. Seasonal hoti hai — pollen, dhool mitti, jaanwaron ke baalon se trigger hoti hai. Yeh contagious NAHI hai — doosron mein nahi phailti.

Pakistan Mein Phailna

Monsoon aur garmi ke mausam mein conjunctivitis ke cases bohat barh jate hain. Schools mein ek bachay se doosray mein rapidly spread hota hai. Shared towels, pillows, aur makeup se phailta hai. Public transport mein surfaces chhone se bhi infection lag sakta hai. Swimming pools mein bhi risk hota hai.

Symptoms — Type Ke Mutabiq

Conjunctivitis Symptoms by Type
ViralWatery discharge, laal aankh, ek aankh phir doosri, mild discomfort
BacterialThick yellow/green discharge, eyelids chipakna subah, dard, swelling
AllergicItching (dominant), watery eyes, dono aankhen, naak behna saath mein

Ghareloo Ilaj — Ghar Pe Kya Karein

  • Cold compress — saaf kapra thande pani mein bheego kar aankhon pe rakhein, 10-15 minute.
  • Artificial tears/lubricant drops — dryness aur irritation mein relief dete hain.
  • Discharge gently saaf karein — saaf cotton ka tukra, andar se bahar ki taraf.
  • Aankh RUBLE ya RAGRAIN MAT — mazeed irritation hoga aur infection barh sakta hai.
  • Contact lenses nikaal dein jab tak puri tarah theek na ho.
  • Aankhon ko haath mat lagayein — agar lagayein to foran haath dho lein.
  • Apna towel, pillow cover, rumaal alag rakhein ghar mein.

Conjunctivitis Ki Medicines

Bacterial Conjunctivitis Ka Ilaj

  • Antibiotic eye drops: Chloramphenicol ya Tobramycin doctor likhe ga.
  • 5-7 din ka course pura karein chahe pehle theek bhi lag raha ho.
  • Subah pehle discharge saaf karein phir drops daalein.

Allergic Conjunctivitis Ka Ilaj

  • Antihistamine drops: Olopatadine (Patanol) bohat effective hain.
  • Oral antihistamine: Cetirizine bhi le sakte hain agar naak aur aankh dono affected hain.
  • Mast cell stabilizers lambe arse ke liye doctor de sakta hai.

Viral Conjunctivitis Ka Ilaj

  • Koi specific medicine nahi hai — yeh khud theek hota hai.
  • Supportive ilaj: artificial tears aur cold compress se aaram milta hai.
  • 1-2 hafton mein recovery hoti hai.
  • Steroid drops apni marzi se BILKUL na daalein.

Prevention — Aankh Aana Se Kaise Bachein

  • Haath dhona sabse zaruri hai — sabun se 20 second.
  • Towel, rumaal, makeup share na karein.
  • Aankh chhuna aur ragarna avoid karein.
  • Infected person ke kapray alag dho lein.
  • Bachon ko school mein ehtiyat sikhayein — haath dhona, doosron ki cheezein na use karna.
  • Swimming ke baad aankhein saaf pani se dho lein.

Myths Vs Facts

MYTH: Kisi infected shakhs ko dekhne se conjunctivitis phailta hai.

FACT: Yeh bilkul galat hai. Conjunctivitis contact se phailta hai — infected haathon se aankh chhona, shared towels ya surfaces se. Hawa ya nazar se nahi phailta.

MYTH: Kaala chashma lagane se aankh aana theek ho jata hai.

FACT: Chashma dhoop aur irritation se protect karta hai magar yeh ilaj nahi hai. Doctor ki dawai zaruri hai.

MYTH: Surma ya kajal lagao — theek ho jayega.

FACT: Surma lagane se aur infection barh sakti hai. Lead-based surma waise bhi harmful hai. Jab tak theek na ho koi cheez aankh mein na daalein.

Kab Ophthalmologist (Eye Doctor) Zaruri Hai?

  • Nazar kamzor ho jaye ya blurry vision ho.
  • Shadeed dard — halka discomfort se zyada.
  • 5-7 din mein koi improvement na ho.
  • Newborn baby mein aankh laal ya discharge = EMERGENCY — foran hospital.
  • Light se bohat zyada takleef (photophobia).
  • Contact lens users mein — corneal ulcer ka risk hota hai.

Conclusion

Aankh aana (conjunctivitis) aam bimari hai magar sahi ilaj zaruri hai. Type samajhna zaruri hai — viral, bacterial, ya allergic — kyunke har ek ka ilaj alag hai. Haath dho kar aur personal cheezein share na kar ke phailne se rok sakte hain. Frequently Asked Questions

Kya aankh aana dekhne se phailta hai?
Nahi, yeh bilkul galat hai. Conjunctivitis dekhne se NAHI phailta. Yeh contact se phailta hai — infected haathon se aankh chhona, shared towels, ya droplets. Haath dhona aur infected person ke samanaat alag rakhna sabse zaruri ehtiyat hai.
Aankh aana kitne din mein theek hota hai?
Viral conjunctivitis 7-14 din mein khud theek hoti hai. Bacterial conjunctivitis antibiotic drops se 3-5 din mein behtar hoti hai — magar poora course (5-7 din) zaruri hai. Allergic conjunctivitis allergen se door rahein to theek hoti hai, magar seasonal pattern mein bar bar aa sakti hai.
Aankh aana mein kaunse drops daalein?
Bacterial mein doctor Chloramphenicol ya Tobramycin drops deta hai. Allergic mein Olopatadine (anti-allergy drops). Viral mein koi specific drops nahi — artificial tears se relief milta hai. Apni marzi se steroid drops BILKUL na daalein — side effects khatarnak hain.
Bachon mein aankh aana ka ilaj kya hai?
Bachon mein cold compress aur discharge saaf karna zaruri hai. Bacterial ho to doctor antibiotic drops dega. School jaana avoid karein jab tak discharge band na ho — doosre bachon ko lag sakta hai. Haath dhulwayein baar baar. Newborn mein aankh aana EMERGENCY hai — foran doctor ko dikhayein.
Kya surma ya kajal lagana safe hai aankh aana mein?
Bilkul NAHI. Jab aankh infected ho tab koi bhi cheez (surma, kajal, kohl) lagana mazeed infection barha sakta hai. Lead-based surma waise bhi harmful hai. Sirf doctor ki di hui drops/ointment use karein. Theek hone ke baad bhi purana makeup/kajal discard karein.
