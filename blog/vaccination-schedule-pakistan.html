Pakistan Mein Bachon Ka Vaccination Schedule (EPI Guide)

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • EPI (Expanded Programme on Immunization) Pakistan ke government centers par FREE hai
  • 10+ jaanleva bemariyon se bachao — TB, polio, hepatitis B, measles, typhoid wagaira
  • Schedule: Birth, 6 hafte, 10 hafte, 14 hafte, 9 mahine, 15 mahine
  • Side effects aksar mamoli — bukhar 24-48 ghante, injection site par lali
  • Polio campaign workers se "qatre" zaroor lein — additional doses bilkul safe hain

EPI Kya Hai Aur Kahan Milta Hai

EPI ka matlab hai Expanded Programme on Immunization — Pakistan government ka national immunization program jo bachon ko 10+ khatarnaak bimariyon se bachata hai. Yeh sab vaccines bilkul free milti hain government ke Basic Health Units (BHU), Rural Health Centres (RHC), Civil Dispensaries, Tehsil aur District hospitals, aur Lady Health Worker visits par. Apna pink immunization card sambhal kar rakhna zaruri hai — har visit pe stamp lagwana.

Pakistan EPI Schedule — Memorize This
Birth (Paida hote hi)BCG (TB), OPV-0, Hepatitis B
6 hafteOPV-1, Penta-1, PCV-1, Rota-1
10 hafteOPV-2, Penta-2, PCV-2, Rota-2
14 hafteOPV-3, Penta-3, PCV-3, IPV (injection)
9 mahineMeasles-1, Typhoid conjugate
15 mahineMeasles-2
Pregnant womenTetanus Toxoid (TT-1 to TT-5)

Har Vaccine Kis Bimari Se Bachata Hai

  • BCG: Tuberculosis (TB) — Pakistan mein TB common hai, isliye paida hote hi lagti hai
  • OPV (oral polio): Polio ke qatre — Pakistan abhi bhi polio endemic hai, har dose zaruri
  • Hepatitis B: Liver ki bimari aur liver cancer
  • Pentavalent (Penta): 5-in-1 — Diphtheria, Pertussis (whooping cough), Tetanus, Hep B, Hib (meningitis)
  • PCV (Pneumococcal): Pneumonia aur meningitis
  • Rota: Rotavirus dast — Pakistan mein bachon ki #2 mortality cause
  • IPV (injectable polio): Additional polio protection
  • Measles: Khasra — jaanleva ban sakta hai
  • Typhoid Conjugate (TCV): 2019 se EPI mein shamil — Pakistan mein typhoid bohot zyada

Birth Doses — Paida Hote Hi

Paida hote hi 3 vaccines: BCG (bain bazu pe injection — chota nishan hamesha ke liye reh sakta hai, yeh normal hai), OPV-0 (muh mein qatre), aur Hepatitis B (injection). Aksar hospital mein paida hone par yeh do days mein lag jaati hain. Agar ghar par paida hua to 14 din ke andar nearest BHU lay jaein.

6, 10, 14 Hafte Ki Visits

Yeh routine visits sab se important hain — har visit pe 3-4 vaccines lagti hain. Multiple vaccines ek saath safe hain — research ne prove kiya hai immune system pe bojh nahi parta. Penta thigh (jangh) mein lagti hai, OPV muh mein qatre, Rota bhi muh mein liquid.

Abhi Doctor Se Milein

9 Aur 15 Mahine — Measles Aur Typhoid

9 mahine pe Measles-1 (khasra) aur Typhoid Conjugate Vaccine lagti hai. 15 mahine pe Measles-2 booster. Measles ke 2 doses 95% protection dete hain. Typhoid conjugate vaccine 2019 se Pakistan ke routine schedule mein add hua — yeh XDR typhoid jo Pakistan mein zyada hai us se bachata hai.

Vaccine Ke Side Effects — Kya Normal Hai

Mamoli side effects bilkul normal hain aur batate hain ke vaccine kaam kar rahi hai:

  • Halka bukhar 24-48 ghante (Calpol/paracetamol wazn ke hisab se dein)
  • Injection wali jagah lali, sujan, ya halka dard
  • Halki sustai, zyada sona, kam khaana 1-2 din
  • Halki fussiness ya rona

Serious reactions bohot rare hain — 30 minute clinic mein ruk kar dekh lena ideal hai. Allergic reaction ki nishani: tezi se rash, chehre/zubaan sujan, saans phoolna — foran emergency.

Foran Doctor Se Milein Agar:

  • Bukhar 39C (102.2F) se zyada
  • Persistent rona 3+ ghante
  • Daura (seizure)
  • Bachay ki sustai bohot zyada, jagana mushkil
  • Allergic reaction — sujan, rash spreading, breathing trouble
  • Injection site par badhti hui sujan ya pus

Aam Galat Fehmiyaan Door Karein

"Vaccines se autism hota hai" — Ghalat. 1998 ki ek study jhooti thi aur withdraw ho chuki hai. Lakhon bachon par research ne saabit kiya: vaccines aur autism mein koi taluq nahi.

"Polio qatre se bachay ko nuksan hota hai" — Bilkul ghalat. Polio Pakistan se khatm karne ki koshish jari hai. Campaign mein additional drops bilkul safe hain — extra protection dete hain. Workers ko gate par mat rokein.

"Vaccines mein haraam ajzaa hote hain" — Ghalat. Pakistan mein use hone wali EPI vaccines Islamic Advisory Group se halal-certified hain.

"Multiple vaccines ek hi din mein khatarnaak hain" — Ghalat. Bachon ka immune system roz lakhon germs handle karta hai. 4-5 vaccines ek saath bilkul safe aur efficient hain.

"Vaccines bandiyaan banane ke liye hain" — Bilkul jhooti propaganda. Yeh khatarnaak afwah hai jis se Pakistan mein bachay marte hain.

Pink Card Sambhal Kar Rakhain

Immunization card school admission, Pakistan se bahar travel (yellow fever certificate ya polio certificate), aur catch-up doses ke liye zaruri hai. Photo bhi mobile mein rakhain backup ke liye. Khaarij ho jaye to nearest health centre se duplicate ban sakti hai.

Conclusion

Vaccination Pakistan ke bachon ki sab se sasti, asaan aur asar daar protection hai — bilkul free, sirf ehsas chahiye. Schedule yaad rakhain ya phone mein reminder lagayein. Frequently Asked Questions

Pakistan mein EPI vaccines kahan se free milti hain?
EPI (Expanded Programme on Immunization) ke tahat saari vaccines bilkul muft milti hain — government Basic Health Units (BHU), Rural Health Centres (RHC), Tehsil/District hospitals, Civil Dispensaries, aur Lady Health Worker visits par. Sirf pink immunization card sambhal kar rakhain. Private hospital se bhi yahi vaccines available hain magar fee lagti hai.
Penta vaccine mein kaun kaun si beemariyaan cover hoti hain?
Pentavalent vaccine ek hi shot mein 5 beemariyon se bachao karta hai: Diphtheria, Pertussis (whooping cough), Tetanus, Hepatitis B, aur Haemophilus influenzae type b (Hib). Yeh 6, 10, aur 14 hafton par lagti hai. Pehle alag alag shots lagte the, ab ek injection mein sab — kam dard, behtar coverage.
Vaccine ke baad bukhar aaye to kya karein?
Vaccination ke 24-48 ghante mein halka bukhar, injection wali jagah lal hona aur sujan, halki sustai — sab normal hain aur 2 din mein theek ho jate hain. Calpol (paracetamol) wazn ke hisab se dein. Injection site par thandi patti rakhain. 39C se zyada bukhar, persistent rona, dauray, ya sujan barhti rahe to foran doctor ko dikhain.
Kya vaccine ki khurak miss ho jaye to dobara start karna parta hai?
Nahi — catch-up possible hai aur dobara shuru karne ki zarurat nahi. Jahan se miss hui wahin se continue kar sakte hain magar pediatrician se mashwara zaruri. Polio campaign ke dauran additional OPV drops bilkul safe hain — routine doses se extra protection. Pink card sambhal kar rakhain taake doctor schedule track kar sake.
Kya vaccines safe aur halal hain? Autism wali baat sach hai?
Vaccines bilkul safe hain aur Pakistan mein use hone wali vaccines halal-certified hain (Islamic Advisory Group ne approve kiya). Autism ka vaccines se taluq science mein bilkul disprove ho chuka hai — yeh ek discredited 1998 ki study thi jo wapas le li gayi thi. Polio drops, MMR, sab safe hain. Multiple vaccines ek hi visit par dena bhi safe hai — immune system pe bojh nahi parta.
