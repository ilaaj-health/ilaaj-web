Periods Irregular Ka Ilaj — Wajah, PCOS aur Treatment Pakistan

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Normal cycle 21-35 din ka hota hai
  • PCOS Pakistan mein 1 in 5 women mein common
  • Weight, thyroid, stress sab se common reasons
  • Birth control pills cycle regularize karne mein effective
  • 3+ mahine missed periods ya bohat heavy bleeding mein doctor zaruri

Normal Periods Cycle

Normal menstrual cycle 21 se 35 din ka hota hai (average 28). Bleeding 3-7 din chalti hai, total 30-80 ml blood loss. Cycle har mahine 7 din se zyada agay peeche jaaye to "irregular" count hota hai.

Irregular Periods Ki Aam Wajuhat

1. PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome)

Pakistan mein bohat common — 5 mein se 1 aurat. Symptoms:

  • Periods late ya skip karna
  • Chehre par baal (chin, upper lip)
  • Acne
  • Wazan barhna, khaaskar pet pe
  • Sir ke baal jhadna
  • Conceive karne mein dushwari

2. Thyroid Issues

Hypothyroid (slow) periods heavy aur frequent kar deti hai. Hyperthyroid (fast) light aur irregular. TSH test zaruri.

3. Weight

  • Bohat zyada wazan — periods skip ya heavy
  • Bohat kam wazan ya crash diet — periods ruk jate hain
  • Healthy BMI 18.5-25

4. Stress

High cortisol GnRH (period hormone) suppress karta hai. Exam stress, family issues, kaam — sab impact karte hain.

5. Pregnancy

Pehla check zaruri agar period miss ho — even if you think it's not possible.

6. Breastfeeding

Postpartum 6 mahine tak periods kam ya na hona normal hai (lactational amenorrhea).

7. Perimenopause

40s mein periods irregular hona shuru ho sakta hai — menopause ki shuru'aat.

8. Medications

  • Birth control pills (chodne ya start karne pe)
  • Steroids
  • Anti-psychotics
  • Chemotherapy

9. Uterine Fibroids ya Polyps

Heavy aur prolonged bleeding ki wajah.

Periods Regularize Karne Ke Ghareloo Tareeqe

1. Healthy Weight

Sab se bara factor. 5-10% wazan kam karne se PCOS-related irregular periods improve hote hain.

2. Anti-Inflammatory Diet

  • Whole grains (atta, oats)
  • Phal, sabziyaan
  • Daal, anday, machli
  • Avoid: sugar, white bread, fast food

3. Exercise

Roz 30 minute walk ya yoga. Bohat intense exercise ulta periods ruk sakta hai (athlete amenorrhea).

4. Stress Management

Namaz, meditation, deep breathing, hobbies. 7-8 ghante neend.

5. Iron-Rich Foods

Periods se anemia common — palak, gosht, khajoor, anjeer. Vitamin C ke saath absorption barhti hai.

6. Specific Foods

  • Dalcheeni (cinnamon): Studies suggest helps with PCOS regularity
  • Adrak chai: Cramps aur regularity
  • Methi (fenugreek): Insulin sensitivity (PCOS mein faida)
  • Spearmint tea: Hairsutism (PCOS) reduce
  • Pineapple, papaya: Period induce karne ka roohani belief — actually mild bromelain effect

Lady Doctor Se Milein

Medical Treatments

1. Birth Control Pills

Sab se common treatment. Cycle regularize karti hain, acne aur hair growth bhi reduce. Pakistan mein common: Yasmin, Diane-35, Nordette.

2. Metformin (PCOS Mein)

PCOS mein insulin resistance address karta hai aur cycle regular karta hai. Wazan kam karne mein bhi madad. Doctor prescription se.

3. Progesterone

Norethisterone (Primolut N) — period induce karne ke liye, ya cycle regulate. 5-10 din ka course.

4. Thyroid Medication

Agar TSH abnormal ho — Eltroxin (thyroxine) periods normalize karta hai.

5. Fertility Treatments

Conceive karne ke liye Letrozole, Clomiphene — fertility specialist se.

Heavy Periods Ka Ilaj

Agar pads/cloth har 1-2 ghante mein change karna parhe ya 7+ din chale:

  • Iron supplements (anemia ka khatra)
  • Tranexamic acid (Cyklokapron) — bleeding kam karta
  • Birth control pills
  • Mirena IUD — heavy bleeding ke liye effective
  • Fibroids ho to surgical options

Painful Periods (Dysmenorrhea)

  • Brufen 400 mg ya Ponstan 250 mg — period start hone se 1 din pehlay shuru karein
  • Garam patti pet pe
  • Hot bath
  • Birth control pills agar severe
  • Endometriosis check (severe pain mein)

Period Skip Karwane Ke Liye (Travel, Wedding)

Norethisterone (Primolut N) — period expected date se 3 din pehlay shuru karein, jab tak chahiye continue karein, then band karein. 2-3 din mein period aa jata hai. Sirf doctor consultation ke baad — har situation mein safe nahi.

Important Tests

Gynecologist aksar yeh karta hai:

  • Pregnancy test
  • Pelvic ultrasound (cysts, fibroids)
  • TSH (thyroid)
  • FSH, LH, prolactin
  • Testosterone, DHEA-S (PCOS)
  • HbA1c (PCOS mein insulin)
  • CBC (anemia check)

Kab Foran Doctor Ko?

  • 3+ mahine periods miss
  • Pad 2 ghante se kam mein bhar jaye
  • Khoon ke clots large
  • Bleeding 7+ din
  • Cycle 21 din se chota
  • Periods ke beech bleeding
  • Sex ke baad bleeding
  • Shadeed pelvic dard
  • Pregnancy mein bleeding
  • Periods ke saath behoshi

Conclusion

Irregular periods sirf cosmetic issue nahi — yeh hormonal aur health imbalance ki nishani hain. PCOS untreated rahe to long-term diabetes aur heart disease ka khatra. Frequently Asked Questions

Periods irregular hone ka kya matlab hai?

Cycle 21 din se chota ya 35 din se bara, har mahine 7+ din ka farq, ya periods skip honay irregular count hote hain. Common wajuhat PCOS (Pakistan mein bohat common), thyroid issues, weight changes, stress, pregnancy, breastfeeding, ya perimenopause hain. Blood tests aur ultrasound se sahi diagnosis hota hai.

Periods regular karne ka qudrati tarika?

Healthy weight maintain karein (BMI 18.5-25), anti-inflammatory diet (whole grains, phal, sabziyaan), roz 30 minute exercise, stress management, aur 7-8 ghante neend. PCOS mein dalcheeni aur methi insulin sensitivity barhati hain. Iron-rich foods (palak, anday, gosht) anemia roknay ke liye. 6 mahine consistency ke baad farq nazr aata hai.

PCOS ka ilaj kya hai?

PCOS ka mukammal ilaj nahi magar manageable hai. First line: weight management (5-10% kam karne se 50% improvement), birth control pills (Yasmin, Diane-35) cycle regulate karne ke liye, Metformin insulin resistance ke liye. Conceive karna ho to Letrozole ya Clomiphene fertility specialist se. Long-term diabetes aur heart disease prevention zaruri.

Period 2 mahine nahi aaya kya karein?

Pehlay pregnancy test karein — chahe shubha na bhi ho. Negative ho to wajuhat: PCOS, thyroid, stress, weight changes, perimenopause. Doctor se milein — woh pelvic ultrasound, TSH, prolactin, aur hormonal tests karwa kar sahi diagnosis dega. Period induce karne ke liye Norethisterone (Primolut N) prescribe ho sakti hai. Self-medicate na karein.

Heavy periods ka ilaj kya hai?

Pad har 1-2 ghante mein bharna ya 7+ din bleeding heavy count hoti hai. Wajuhat: hormonal imbalance, fibroids, polyps, thyroid, ya bleeding disorders. Treatment options: iron supplements (anemia roknay), Tranexamic acid (bleeding kam), birth control pills, Mirena IUD, ya severe cases mein surgery. Hemoglobin check zaruri hai.
