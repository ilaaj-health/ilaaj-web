Loprin 75 mg — Heart Protection, Stroke Prevention Pakistan

TL;DR — Key Takeaways
  • Loprin 75 = low-dose aspirin (75 mg)
  • Pichla heart attack/stroke wale patients mein zaruri
  • Healthy adults mein routinely nahi (bleeding risk)
  • Khaane ke baad lein — stomach safety
  • Surgery se 5-7 din pehlay band karein

Loprin 75 Kya Hai?

Loprin Highnoon Laboratories ka brand name hai low-dose aspirin (75 mg) ke liye. Yeh enteric-coated tablet hai jo stomach mein nahi ghulti — intestines mein dissolve hoti hai, jisse stomach upset kam hota hai.

Loprin 75 Kis Liye?

1. Heart Attack Prevention (Secondary)

Pichla heart attack ho chuka logon mein future events rokne ke liye.

2. Stroke Prevention (Secondary)

Pichla ischemic stroke ya TIA (mini-stroke) wale patients mein.

3. Coronary Artery Disease

Confirmed CAD, angina patients mein.

4. After Stent Placement

Heart stent ke baad zindagi bhar Loprin (saath mein Plavix 1 saal).

5. After Bypass Surgery

CABG (coronary bypass) ke baad.

6. Atrial Fibrillation (Some Cases)

Anticoagulation chahiye magar Warfarin nahi le sakte.

7. High-Risk Diabetes Patients

Multiple risk factors ke saath (high BP, high cholesterol, smoking history).

8. Pre-eclampsia Prevention

High-risk pregnancies mein 12+ weeks se shuru.

Loprin Kab Lena Chahiye? (Secondary Prevention)

Definite indications:

  • Pichla heart attack (MI)
  • Pichla stroke (ischemic) ya TIA
  • Confirmed coronary artery disease
  • Stent placement (PCI)
  • Coronary bypass (CABG)
  • Peripheral artery disease (PAD)

Primary Prevention — Routinely NAHI

Pichla heart event nahi tha to routinely Loprin recommend nahi — bleeding risk benefit se zyada ho sakti hai. Pakistan mein log apni marzi se Loprin shuru kar dete hain — yeh galat hai.

Aap se discuss karein doctor agar:

  • 50+ saal aur multiple risk factors (diabetes + high BP + cholesterol + smoking)
  • Strong family history of early heart disease
  • 10-year cardiovascular risk > 20%
  • Bleeding risk low

Side Effects

Common:

  • Halki bleeding (cuts mein khoon zyada deer)
  • Pet kharab (kam with enteric coating)
  • Bruising aasaan
  • Heartburn

Serious:

  • GI bleeding — sab se bara concern (1-2% per year)
  • Hemorrhagic stroke — rare
  • Severe allergic reaction
  • Tinnitus (high dose)

Bleeding Signs — Foran Doctor

  • Khoon ki ulti
  • Kaali (tarry) potty
  • Naak se beqaboo khoon
  • Pesab mein khoon
  • Achanak severe sir dard (intracranial bleed)
  • Skin pe purple bruises bina injury

Cardiologist Se Mashwara

Kab Avoid Karein?

  • Active bleeding (anywhere)
  • Stomach ulcer (active)
  • Bleeding disorders (hemophilia)
  • Severe asthma (NSAID-sensitive)
  • Aspirin allergy
  • Children under 16 (Reye's syndrome)
  • Pregnancy 3rd trimester (high dose)
  • Severe liver/kidney disease

Surgery Se Pehlay

5-7 din pehlay Loprin band karein elective surgery se. Emergency surgery mein doctor decision karega.

Stent placement ke baad aspirin band na karein bina cardiologist mashware — clot ka risk bohat zyada.

Drug Interactions

  • Warfarin, DOACs: Bleeding risk significantly badha
  • NSAIDs (Brufen, Voltral): Stomach ulcer risk doubles, aspirin effect kam
  • SSRIs (depression dawai): GI bleeding
  • Steroids: Bleeding risk badha
  • Methotrexate: Toxicity
  • ACE inhibitors: BP control reduce
  • Alcohol: Stomach bleeding

Stomach Protection

Long-term Loprin users mein PPI (Risek 20, Pantop) add karna common practice hai jab risk factors hon:

  • 60+ years
  • Pichla GI bleed
  • NSAIDs ya steroids saath
  • H. pylori positive

Aspirin Resistance

5-10% patients mein aspirin effective nahi hoti — "aspirin resistance". Cardiologist platelet function test karwa sakta hai. Alternative: Plavix (clopidogrel).

Dental Procedures

Routine dental work (extractions, cleanings) mein Loprin continue rakhein — bleeding manageable hai. Major surgery se hi band karein.

Conclusion

Loprin 75 (low-dose aspirin) heart attack/stroke survivors aur coronary artery disease patients mein life-saving hai. Magar healthy logon mein routinely recommend nahi — bleeding risk benefit se zyada. Khaane ke baad lein, surgery se 5-7 din pehlay band, GI bleeding signs pe doctor. Frequently Asked Questions

Loprin 75 kis ke liye zaruri hai?

Loprin 75 mg secondary prevention ke liye zaruri hai un patients mein jinhein: pichla heart attack, pichla stroke ya TIA, confirmed coronary artery disease, heart stent placement (PCI), coronary bypass surgery (CABG), ya peripheral artery disease ho. High-risk pregnancies mein pre-eclampsia prevention bhi. Sirf doctor parchee se shuru karein.

Healthy adults Loprin daily le sakte hain?

Routinely nahi recommend. Pichla heart event nahi tha to bleeding risk (GI bleeding, hemorrhagic stroke) heart attack prevention ke benefit se zyada ho sakta hai. Sirf 50+ saal aur multiple high risk factors (diabetes + high BP + cholesterol + smoking) ke saath cardiologist se discuss karein. Pakistan mein log apni marzi se Loprin shuru kar dete hain — yeh galat practice hai.

Loprin ki best time leni ki?

Khaane ke baad, preferably evening/dinner ke saath. Enteric coating ki wajah se stomach upset kam magar phir bhi khaali pet avoid karein. Same time daily — routine maintain. Achanak band na karein especially stent placement ke baad — clot ka risk badha.

Loprin ke side effects kya hain?

Common: halki bleeding (cuts mein khoon zyada deer), pet kharab (enteric coating se kam), bruising aasaan, heartburn. Serious: GI bleeding (1-2% per year, sab se bara concern), hemorrhagic stroke (rare), severe allergic reaction, tinnitus (high dose). Khoon ki ulti, kaali potty, achanak severe sir dard pe foran doctor.

Surgery se pehlay Loprin band karna chahiye?

Elective (planned) surgery se 5-7 din pehlay Loprin band karein. Magar stent placement ke baad bina cardiologist mashware kabhi band na karein — clot ka risk bohat zyada. Emergency surgery mein doctor decision karega. Routine dental work (extractions, cleanings) mein Loprin continue rakhein — bleeding manageable hai.
